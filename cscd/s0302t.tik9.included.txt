

ti sāvakānaṃ sabrahmacāriṃ uddissa ālāpo ti buddhāciṇṇaṃ samudācāraṃ akathentehi sāvakehi āvuso bhikkhaveti ālapitā bhikkhū āvusoti paṭivacanaṃ denti na bhanteti  tesaṃ nirodheneva tassa jhānassa uppādetabbato ti yesaṃ vacīsaṅgārānaṃ vitakkavicārānaṃ nirujjhanena suvikkhambhitabhāvena saddāyatanaṃ appavattiṃ gacchati kāraṇassa dūrato passambhitattā ti niddoso parisuddho  na titthiyānaṃ mūgabbataggahaṇaṃ viya aparisuddhoti adhippāyo ti saddena tatiyajjhānādīni saṅgaṇhāti
