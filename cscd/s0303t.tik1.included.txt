
1 paṭhamavaggo

 mārasaddoyaṃ  bhāvasādhanoti dassento ti āha ti maritabbo māraṃ maraṇaṃ etabboti āha ti ti phalavimuttisaṅkhātā arahato arahantatā nāma yāvadeva anupādānibbānatthā ti anupādānibbānādhigamassa abbhantare tato orameva idaṃ maggaṃ brahmacariyaṃ vussati na tato paraṃ ti brahmacariyassa


ti lagganassa bajjhanassa pucchā yadi rūpādīsu sattattā satto khīṇāsavā kathaṃ sattāti sattabhūtapubbāti katvā nti kīḷāya apanayanaṃ oramaṇaṃ yantarajju viya bhavapabandhassa nayanato ti taṇhā vuttā


2 dutiyavaggo

 rūpādīnaṃyeva vibhave maraṇasamaññāti  vināsabhāvo


3-4 āyācanavaggādi

 tenesa thero paṭibhāneyyakānaṃ etadagge ṭhapito  na paṭivedhāvahabhāvena
