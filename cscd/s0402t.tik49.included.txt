

 catutthassa  paṭhamādīni uttānāneva


 chaṭṭhe saṃvaṭṭanaṃ vinassanaṃ  saṃvaṭṭato uddhaṃ tathā ṭhāyī  vivaṭṭanaṃ nibbattanaṃ vaḍḍhanaṃ vā  tejosaṃvaṭṭo āposaṃvaṭṭo vāyosaṃvaṭṭoti evaṃ saṃvaṭṭasīmānukkamena saṃvaṭṭesu vattabbesu tathā avatvā āposaṃvaṭṭo tejosaṃvaṭṭo vāyosaṃvaṭṭoti vacanaṃ saṃvaṭṭamahābhūtadesanānupubbiyāti keci bhāvīsaṃvaṭṭānupubbiyāti apare āpena saṃvaṭṭo ti saṃvaṭṭanamariyādā ti vinassati ti sabbakālaṃ tīsupi saṃvaṭṭakālesūti attho ti ettha buddhakkhettaṃ nāma tividhaṃ hoti jātikkhettaṃ āṇākkhettaṃ visayakkhettañca
tattha  dasasahassacakkavāḷapariyantaṃ hoti tathāgatassa paṭisandhiggahaṇādīsu kampati  koṭisatasahassacakkavāḷapariyantaṃ yattha  (khu pā 61 ādayo su ni 224 ādayo)  (a ni 467 cūḷava 251)  (saṃ ni 1249)  (dī ni 3277-278) nti (jā 1217-18) imesaṃ parittānaṃ ānubhāvo vattati  anantāparimāṇaṃ yaṃ yāvatā vā panākaṅkheyyāti (a ni 381) vuttaṃ tattha yaṃ yaṃ tathāgato ākaṅkhati taṃ taṃ jānāti evametesu tīsu buddhakkhettesu ekaṃ āṇākkhettaṃ vinassati  tasmiṃ pana vinassante jātikkhettaṃ vinaṭṭhameva hoti vinassantañca ekatova vinassati saṇṭhahantañca  ekatova saṇṭhahati sesamettha  (visuddhi mahāṭī 2404) vuttanayeneva gahetabbaṃ sattamaṭṭhamāni uttānatthāneva


 navame ti kudāssu nāmāhampi āsavānaṃ khayātiādinā nayena anuttare vimokkhe pihaṃ uppādentassa uppannataṇhaṃ nissāya kathaṃ pana lokuttaradhamme ārabbha āsā uppajjatīti na kho panetaṃ evaṃ daṭṭhabbaṃ na ārammaṇakaraṇavasena tattha pihā pavattati avisayattā puggalassa ca anadhigatabhāvato anussavūpaladdhe pana anuttaravimokkhe uddissa pihaṃ uppādento tattha pihaṃ uppādeti nāma ti kammapathabhāvaṃ appattatāya paṭisandhiṃ na deti sesaṃ suviññeyyameva


 dasame ti sajjhāyanti ceva vācuggataṃ karontā dhārenti ca avigatataṇhatāya taṃ taṃ parikkhārajātaṃ bahumpi ādiyantīti bahulā te eva  yathā venayikoti (ma ni 1246 a ni 811 pārā 8) te pana yasmā paccayabahulabhāvā yuttappayuttā nāma honti tasmā āha ti sithilaṃ gaṇhantīti  sikkhāya ādaragāravābhāvena sithilaṃ adaḷhaṃ gaṇhantīti attho nti ca bhāvanapuṃsakaniddeso sithila-saddena vā samānatthassa sāthalasaddassa vasena ti padasiddhi veditabbā ti adhogamanato orambhāgiyabhāvatoti attho ti ujjhitussāhā tadadhigamāya ārambhampi akurumānā
