
 catutthassa  paṭhame nti savisevanaṃ adantahatthiassādisadisaṃ nti vaṭṭavasena uppannacittameva
 dutiye nti nibbisevanaṃ dantahatthiassādisadisaṃ imasmimpi suttadvaye vaṭṭavivaṭṭavasena uppannacittameva kathitaṃ yathā cettha evaṃ ito paresupīti
 tatiye nti agopitaṃ satisaṃvararahitaṃ aguttahatthiassādisadisaṃ
 catutthe nti gopitaṃ avissaṭṭhasatisaṃvaraṃ guttahatthiassādisadisaṃ
 pañcamachaṭṭhāni nti padavasena bujjhanakānaṃ ajjhāsayena vuttāni attho panettha purimasadisoyeva
 sattamaṭṭhamesupi  eseva nayo upamā panettha asaṃvutagharadvārādivasena veditabbā
 navamadasamāni catūhipi padehi yojetvā vuttāni imasmimpi vagge vaṭṭavivaṭṭameva kathitanti
