

 aṭṭhamassa  paṭhame ti na sīlesu paripūrakārī ti samantato pasādajanako ti sabbehi samaṇākārehi samaṇadhammakoṭṭhāsehi paripūro dutiye ti paccanīkasantatāya santā ti paccanīkadhammehi vimuttattā ca vimokkhā

 tatiye ti bhāvetha nu nti anusiṭṭhi ti arahattaphalatthāya catutthe ti paṭipannāya ti so mama jīvitantarāyopi puthujjanakālakiriyaṃ karontassa saggantarāyopi maggantarāyopi assa ti satthaṃ viya aṅgamaṅgāni kantantīti satthakā pañcamādīni vuttanayāneva navame ti pañcavidhe saṃsagge ārāmatā

 dasame ti kusītassa alasassa vatthūni patiṭṭhā kosajjakāraṇānīti attho ti cīvaravicāraṇādikammaṃ kattabbaṃ hoti ti duvidhampi vīriyaṃ nārabhati ti jhānavipassanāmaggaphaladhammassa appattassa pattiyā ti tasseva anadhigatassa adhigamatthāya ti tadeva asacchikatassa sacchikaraṇatthāya nti idaṃ handāhaṃ nipajjāmīti evaṃ osīdanaṃ paṭhamaṃ kusītavatthu iminā nayena sabbattha attho veditabbo ti ettha pana māsācitaṃ nāma tintamāso yathā tintamāso garuko hoti evaṃ garukoti adhippāyo  ti gilāno hutvā pacchā vuṭṭhito hoti ti vīriyakāraṇāni tesampi imināva nayena attho veditabbo sesaṃ sabbattha uttānamevāti
