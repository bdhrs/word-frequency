

 āvajjanassa hitaṃ āvajjaniyaṃ taṃ ṭhānaṃ etassāti āvajjaniyaṭhānaṃ gotrabhūti attho taṃ gotrabhucittaṃ  maggacittassa  paṭipadāñāṇadassanaṃ kudācanaṃ na ca bhajati tatheva ñāṇadassanavisuddhiṃ kudācanaṃ na bhajateva  ñāṇānaṃ antarā ekaṃ  vohāre niyuttanti vohārikaṃ na vohārikaṃ abbohārikaṃ vipassanāya sotasmiṃ vipassanāya sote patitattā vipassanā nāma voharitabbā  nibbānato
nti gotrabhucittaṃ sandhāyāha
nti saṅkhārehi vigataṃ ti paṭhamāvajjanaṃ viya ti paṭhamamanasikārā viya maggassa anantarādīhi paccayehi ti maggassa sikhāpattāya tāya vipassanāya  matthake nti anivattaṃ
 ekena  āvajjanena ekissāyeva vīthiyā nti anulomagotrabhucittānaṃ nānārammaṇatā vuttā  gotrabhucittaṃ anāvajjanampi maggassa āvajjanaṭṭhāne ṭhatvā ti ahaṃ nibbānaṃ ārammaṇaṃ karomi tvampi tamevārammaṇaṃ karohi iti saññaṃ datvā viyāti adhippāyo nti saññaṃ  eso maggo pubbe anibbiddhapubbo
ti chindati
 anekesaṃ ānisaṃsānaṃ dāyakena ādimaggena saṃyuttaṃ paṭhamamaggañāṇaṃ
 ekassa cittuppādassa āsevanapaccayo natthi  kāraṇā  cittuppādā anulomakā ti cittuppādehi āsevanapaccayaṃ  labhitvā  cittaṃ gotrabhu hoti
 maggapekkhanahetu yassa āvajjanamanaso taṃ  ti tasmiṃ āvajjanacitte niruddhe maggassa paccavekkhaṇasaññitāni satta javanāni paṭipāṭiyā jāyante paccavekkhaṇañāṇāni ekūnavīsati honti
 tadā vipassanāvīthiṃ otarati dutiyamaggañāṇaṃ
nti mūlaghātanaṃ tatiyamaggañāṇaṃ
ti anivattato catutthamaggañāṇaṃ
