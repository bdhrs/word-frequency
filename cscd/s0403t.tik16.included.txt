

 dutiyassa  paṭhame natthi vattabbaṃ dutiye āghāto paṭivinayati ettha etehīti vā  tenāha tiādi
nti anantakaṃ antavirahitaṃ vatthakhaṇḍaṃ yadi hi tassa anto bhaveyya pilotikāti saṅkhaṃ na gaccheyya
ti bījakaṇṇikakesarādibhedena sevālena ti nīlamaṇḍūkapiṭṭhivaṇṇena udakapiṭṭhiṃ chādetvā nibbattena udakapiṭṭhikena ti ghammena phuṭṭho abhibhūto nti paṭighasampayuttacittuppādaṃ
visabhāgavedanuppattiyā kakaceneva iriyāpathapavattinivāraṇena chindanto ābādhati pīḷetīti ābādho so assa atthīti  taṃsamuṭṭhānena  sañjātadukkho ti adhimattagilāno ti gāmantasampāpakassa
pasannabhāvena  udakassa acchabhāvo veditabboti āha ti sādurasatāya sātatāti āha ti tanukameva salilaṃ visesato sītalaṃ na bahalāti āha ti ti nikkaddamā sacikkhallādivasena hi udakassa vivaṇṇatā sabhāvato pana taṃ setavaṇṇameva tatiyādīni uttānatthāneva


 chaṭṭhe ti asahanatthe nti anāgatasaddappayogo kato attho pana vattamānakālikova akkharacintakā  hi īdisesu ṭhānesu anokappanāmarisanatthavasena atthisadde upapade vattamānakālepi anāgatavacanaṃ karonti


 sattame ti yasmiṃ vatthusmiṃ āpatti tassa sarūpato dassanaṃ ti yaṃ āpattiṃ so āpanno tassā dassanaṃ ti uposathappavāraṇādisaṃvāsassa paṭikkhipanaṃ akaraṇaṃ ti abhivādanādisāmīcikiriyāya akaraṇaṃ ti codentena ti cuditakassa codetabbakālo nti codetabbapuggalaṃ ti ayaṃ cuditakalakkhaṇe tiṭṭhati na tiṭṭhatīti vīmaṃsitvā ti ime maṃ abhūtena abbhācikkhantā ayasaṃ byasanaṃ uppādentīti bhikkhūnaṃ ayasaṃ uppādeti aṭṭhamanavamāni uttānatthāneva


 dasame  ime satteti adhippāyo yasmā pana so pāsaṃsabhāvena uttamabhāvena ca te satte abhibhavitvā ṭhitoti attānaṃ maññati tasmā vuttaṃ ti ti dassane antarāyābhāvavacanena ñeyyavisesapariggāhikabhāvena  ca anāvaraṇadassāvitaṃ paṭijānātīti āha ti
