

 tatiyassa  paṭhame ti sāmakānaṃ ussannattā evaṃladdhanāme gāmake nti pokkharaṇiyānāmake vihāre ti rattiyā paṭhamayāmaṃ atikkamma majjhimayāme sampatte ti abhikkantaatimanāpavaṇṇā nti sakalakappaṃ ti pokkharaṇiyānāmakaṃ mahāvihāraṃ attano obhāsena pharitvā ti samānaanuñño samānacitto ti dubbacabhāvo ti lāmakamittatā  imasmiṃ sutte parihāniyadhammāva kathitā dutiye aparihāniyadhammā lokuttaramissakā kathitā

 tatiye nti kāmarāgaratto ayaṃ ti chandarāgena vinibaddho ti cittutrāsabhayā ti kilesapaṅkato nti saṅgo ca paṅko ca idampi ubhayaṃ ti yasmiṃ saṅge ca paṅke ca puthujjano satto laggo laggito palibuddho ti catubbidhe upādāne ti jātiyā ca maraṇassa ca sambhave paccayabhūte ti anupādiyitvā vimuccanti ti jātimaraṇānaṃ saṅkhayasaṅkhāte nibbāne nibbānārammaṇāya vimuttiyā vimuccantīti attho imasmiṃ ṭhāne vivaṭṭetvā arahattameva patto esa bhikkhu idāni taṃ khīṇāsavaṃ thomento tiādimāha tattha ti khemabhāvaṃ pattā ti lokuttarasukhena sukhitā ti abbhantare kilesābhāvena diṭṭhadhammeyeva abhinibbutā imasmiṃ sutte vaṭṭameva kathetvā gāthāsu vaṭṭavivaṭaṃ kathitaṃ

 catutthe ti bhindeyya ti lāmikāya ti āhārasappāyautusappāyāni pariggahetvā samādhiṃ samāpajjituṃ kusalo hoti cheko samattho paṭibalo ti samādhissa ṭhitiyaṃ kusalo samādhiṃ ṭhapetuṃ sakkotīti attho ti samādhissa vuṭṭhāne kusalo yathāparicchedena vuṭṭhātuṃ sakkotīti attho ti samādhissa kallatāya kusalo samādhicittaṃ hāsetuṃ kallaṃ kātuṃ sakkotīti attho ti samādhissa asappāye anupakārake dhamme vajjetvā sappāye upakārake sevantopi ayaṃ samādhinimittārammaṇo  ayaṃ lakkhaṇārammaṇoti jānantopi samādhissa gocarakusalo nāma hoti ti upariuparisamāpattisamāpajjanatthāya  paṭhamajjhānādisamādhiṃ abhinīharituṃ sakkonto samādhissa abhinīhārakusalo nāma hoti so paṭhamajjhānā vuṭṭhāya dutiyaṃ samāpajjati dutiyajjhānā tatiyajjhānā vuṭṭhāya catutthaṃ samāpajjatīti

 pañcame ti anussatikāraṇāni tiādīni visuddhimagge (visuddhi 1123 ādayo) vitthāritāneva ti idaṃ buddhānussatikammaṭṭhānaṃ ārammaṇaṃ karitvā ti paramavisuddhiṃ nibbānaṃ pāpuṇanti sesaṃ sabbattha uttānatthameva imasmiṃ pana sutte cha anussatiṭṭhānāni missakāni kathitānīti veditabbāni

 chaṭṭhe ti pañcakāmaguṇasambādhe ti ettha okāsā vuccanti cha anussatiṭṭhānāni tesaṃ adhigamo ti visujjhanatthāya ti sokānañca paridevānañca samatikkamatthāya ti atthaṃ gamanatthāya  ti sahavipassanakassa maggassa adhigamanatthāya ti apaccayaparinibbānassa paccakkhakiriyatthāya
ti sabbākārena ti alagganaṭṭhena ceva apalibuddhaṭṭhena ca ākāsasadisena ti na parittakena ti mahantabhāvaṃ gatena mahantehi vā ariyasāvakehi gatena paṭipannenāti attho ti  pharaṇaappamāṇatāya appamāṇena ti akusalaverapuggalaverarahitena ti kodhadukkhavajjitena sabbametaṃ buddhānussaticittameva sandhāya vuttaṃ paratopi eseva nayo ti visujjhanasabhāvā imasmimpi sutte cha anussatiṭṭhānāni missakāneva kathitānīti

 sattame ti ettha manaṃ bhāveti vaḍḍhetīti manobhāvanīyo ti dassanatthaṃ nti niggamanaṃ vūpasamanaṃ ti kāmarāgappajahanatthāya asubhakammaṭṭhānaṃ katheti dutiyavārādīsu byāpādappahānāya mettākammaṭṭhānaṃ thinamiddhappahānāya thinamiddhavinodanakammaṭṭhānaṃ ālokasaññaṃ vā vīriyārambhavatthuādīnaṃ vā aññataraṃ uddhaccakukkuccappahānāya samathakammaṭṭhānaṃ vicikicchāpahānāya tiṇṇaṃ ratanānaṃ guṇakathaṃ kathento dhammaṃ desetīti veditabbo ti ārabbha ti ārammaṇavasena citte karontassa ti anantarāyena āsavānaṃ khayo hoti

 aṭṭhame ti bhojanasālāya ti piṇḍapātacariyāya uppannakilamatho ti bhattadaratho nti vihārapaccante chāyāya ti yaṃ eva tassa tato purimadivasabhāge samathanimittaṃ citte kataṃ hoti ti taṃyeva etassa tasmiṃ samaye divāvihāre nisinnassa manodvāre sañcarati ti ojāya ṭhito patiṭṭhito ti phāsukaṃ assa hoti ti kathentassa sammukhaṭṭhāne nti sotena sutaṃ nti cittena paṭiggahitaṃ

 navame nti lāḷudāyittheraṃ ti āvuso nāhaṃ badhiro suṇāmi bhagavato vacanaṃ pañhaṃ pana upaparikkhāmīti nti samādhivipassanācittaṃ nti idaṃ jhānattayasaṅkhātaṃ anussatikāraṇaṃ ti imasmiṃyeva attabhāve sukhavihāratthāya pavattati nti ālokanimitte uppannasaññaṃ ti divāti saññaṃ ṭhapeti  nti yathānena divā ālokasaññā manasikatā rattimpi tatheva taṃ manasi karoti ti yathā vānena rattiṃ ālokasaññā manasikatā divāpi taṃ tatheva manasi karoti ti pākaṭena ti nīvaraṇehi anonaddhena ti dibbacakkhuñāṇatthāya sahobhāsakaṃ cittaṃ brūheti vaḍḍheti yaṃ pana ālokasaññaṃ  manasi karotīti vuttaṃ taṃ thinamiddhavinodanālokasaññaṃ sandhāya vuttaṃ na dibbacakkhuñāṇālokanti veditabbaṃ ti dibbacakkhusaṅkhātassa ñāṇadassanassa paṭilābhāya
ntiādīsu yaṃ vattabbaṃ siyā taṃ sabbaṃ sabbākārena vitthārato visuddhimagge kāyagatāsatikammaṭṭhāne vuttaṃ ti pañcakāmaguṇikassa rāgassa pahānatthāya ti yathā passeyya nti matasarīraṃ nti susāne apaviddhaṃ ekāhaṃ matassa assāti  dvīhaṃ matassa assāti  tīhaṃ matassa assāti  bhastā viya vāyunā uddhaṃ jīvitapariyādānā yathānukkamaṃ samuggatena sūnabhāvena dhumātattā uddhumātaṃ uddhumātameva  paṭikūlattā vā kucchitaṃ uddhumātanti  vinīlaṃ vuccati viparibhinnavaṇṇaṃ vinīlameva  paṭikūlattā vā kucchitaṃ vinīlanti  maṃsussadaṭṭhānesu rattavaṇṇassa pubbasannicayaṭṭhānesu setavaṇṇassa yebhuyyena ca nīlavaṇṇassa nīlaṭṭhāne nīlasāṭakapārutasseva chavasarīrassetaṃ adhivacanaṃ paribhinnaṭṭhānehi navahi vā vaṇamukhehi vissandamānaṃ pubbaṃ vipubbaṃ vipubbameva  paṭikūlattā vā kucchitaṃ vipubbanti vipubbakaṃ vipubbakaṃ jātaṃ tathābhāvaṃ gatanti 
nti so bhikkhu imaṃ attano kāyaṃ tena kāyena saddhiṃ ñāṇena upasaṃharati upaneti kathaṃ ti idaṃ vuttaṃ hoti  āyu usmā viññāṇanti imesaṃ tiṇṇaṃ dhammānaṃ atthitāya ayaṃ kāyo ṭhānagamanādikhamo hoti imesaṃ pana vigamā ayampi evaṃdhammo evaṃpūtikasabhāvoyevāti ti evamevaṃ uddhumātādibhedo bhavissati ti evaṃ uddhumātādibhāvaṃ anatikkanto
nti  udarādīsu nisīditvā udaramaṃsaoṭṭhamaṃsaakkhikamaṃsādīni luñcitvā luñcitvā khādiyamānaṃ nti sesāvasesamaṃsalohitayuttaṃ nti maṃse khīṇepi lohitaṃ na sussati taṃ sandhāya vuttaṃ  nimmaṃsalohitamakkhitanti ti aññena disābhāgena nti catusaṭṭhibhedampi hatthaṭṭhikaṃ pāṭiyekkaṃ pāṭiyekkaṃ vippakiṇṇaṃ dīsupi eseva nayo ti atikkantasaṃvaccharāni ti abbhokāse ṭhitāni vātātapavuṭṭhisamphassena terovassikāneva pūtīni honti antobhūmigatāni pana cirataraṃ tiṭṭhanti ti cuṇṇavicuṇṇaṃ hutvā vippakiṇṇāni sabbattha ti vuttanayena khajjamānādīnaṃ vasena yojanā kātabbā ti asmīti pavattassa navavidhassa mānassa samugghātatthāya ti anekadhātūnaṃ paṭivijjhanatthāya ti gacchanto satipaññāhi samannāgatova gacchati ti paṭinivattantopi satipaññāhi samannāgatova nivattati sesapadesupi eseva nayo ti satiyā ca ñāṇassa ca atthāya iti imasmiṃ sutte satiñāṇāni missakāni kathitānīti

 dasame nti yaṃ kiñci mahantakhuddakaṃ uccanīcaṃ vā nti nihīnaṃ nti gāmavāsikānaṃ dassanaṃ nti puthujjanānaṃ santakaṃ nti na ariyaṃ na uttamaṃ na parisuddhaṃ nti na atthasannissitaṃ ti na vaṭṭe nibbindanatthāya ti na rāgādīnaṃ virajjanatthāya ti na rāgādīnaṃ appavattinirodhāya ti na rāgādīnaṃ vūpasamanatthāya ti na abhijānanatthāya ti na sambodhisaṅkhātassa catumaggañāṇassa paṭivijjhanatthāya ti na nibbānassa sacchikiriyāya
ti patiṭṭhitasaddho ti  patiṭṭhitapemo ti ekantaṃ gato acalappattoti attho ti ativiya pasanno nti etaṃ anuttaraṃ ti hatthinimittaṃ  sikkhitabbaṃ hatthisippaṃ sikkhati sesapadesupi eseva nayo nti mahantakhuddakaṃ sippaṃ sikkhati
