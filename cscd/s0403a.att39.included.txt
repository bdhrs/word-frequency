

 sattakanipātassa  paṭhame ti abhiññātabhāvakāmo tatiye nti upāyena catusaccadhammaṃ vicināti ti sahavipassanāya maggapaññāya saccadhammaṃ vipassati ti dīpasseva ti tassa imehi balehi samannāgatassa khīṇāsavassa dīpanibbānaṃ viya carimakacittassa vatthārammaṇehi vimokkho hoti gataṭṭhānaṃ na paññāyati catutthe tiādīni pañcakanipāte vaṇṇitāneva pañcame ti adāliddiyakaraṇaṭṭhena dhanāni

 sattame ti pasenadikosalassa mahāamacco ti bhuttapātarāso upasaṅkami ti nidhānagatena dhanena aḍḍho ti rohaṇaseṭṭhino nattāraṃ migāraseṭṭhiṃ sandhāyevamāha ti vaḷañjanadhanena mahaddhano ti upabhogaparibhogabhaṇḍassa mahantatāya mahābhogo ti suvaṇṇasseva suvaṇṇāmeva hissa koṭisaṅkhyaṃ ahosi ti sesassa taṭṭakasarakaattharaṇapāvuraṇādino paribhogaparikkhārassa pamāṇasaṅkhāne vādoyeva natthi

 aṭṭhame nti kāmarāgasaṃyojanaṃ sabbāneva cetāni bandhanaṭṭhena saṃyojanānīti veditabbāni imasmiṃ sutte vaṭṭameva kathitaṃ sesaṃ sabbattha uttānatthamevāti
