Sikkhākaraṇīyakathāvaṇṇanā
2438 . ‘‘sekhiyā pana ye dhammā’’  uddiṭṭhā,
Iti vinayatthasārasandīpaniyā vinayavinicchayavaṇṇanāya
Sikkhākaraṇīyakathāvaṇṇanā niṭṭhitā.
2439-40 . Savibhaṅgānaṃ ubhatopātimokkhānaṃ aṭṭhakathāsāro vutto. Taṃ sabbaṃ kato
2441 . No paṭibhāṇajaṃ imaṃ tu ye jantuno  te janassa hite janassa tāyane vinaye pakataññuno taññuno bhavanti
2442 . bahusāranaye. Parame vinaye visāradataṃ abhipatthayatā buddhimatā yatinā paramā karaṇīyatamā
2443 . ‘‘avagacchatī’’
 imaṃ avagacchati amaraṃ ajaraṃ arajaṃ arujaṃ santipadaṃ  adhigacchati
Iti vinayatthasārasandīpaniyā vinayavinicchayavaṇṇanāya
Bhikkhunivibhaṅgakathāvaṇṇanā niṭṭhitā.
