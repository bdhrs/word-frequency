
1 paṭhamavaggo

 devassa  putto  devānaṃ janakajanetabbasambandhābhāvato kathamayaṃ devaputtoti vuccatīti āha tiādi apākaṭo aññataroti vuccatīti idaṃ yebhuyyavasena vuttaṃ pākaṭopi hi katthaci aññataroti vuccati heṭṭhā devatāsaṃyutte apākaṭā aññatarā devatāti vatvā idha pākaṭo devaputtoti vuttaṃ tathā hissa ti gottanāmaṃ gahitaṃ tañca kho purimajātisiddhasamaññāvasena anusāsanaṃ anusāso taṃ nti bhikkhusaddassa niddesaṃ nti bhikkhubhāvāvahaṃ ovādaṃ yadi pana na assosi kathamayaṃ pañhaṃ kathesīti aññato sutaṃ nissāya pañhaṃ kathesi na pana bhagavato sammukhā sutabhāvena
nti yathāvuttānaṃ tiṇṇaṃ puggalānaṃ tiādinā hi catutthaṃ idha uddhaṭaṃ tattha ādito tiṇṇaṃ bhagavā pañhaṃ bhāraṃ na karoti ekekaṅgavekallato ceva aṅgadvayavekallato ca catutthassa pana ubhayaṅgapāripūrattā bhāraṃ karotīti āha tiādi
gāthāyaṃ ti upayogatthe sāmivacananti āha ti catusaccādinissitaṃ buddhavacanaṃ sikkhanto catubbidhaṃ vacīsucaritaṃ sikkhati nāmāti āha ti avadhāraṇena tappaṭipakkhaṃ paṭinivatteti nti āsevitabbaṃ bhāvetabbaṃ bahulīkātabbaṃ nti idaṃ tassa vipassanāpadaṭṭhānataṃ hadaye ṭhapetvā vuttaṃ tathā hi vuttaṃ ti ye pana dutiyapadena adhicittasikkhā cittavūpasamena adhipaññāsikkhāti  paṭhanti tesaṃ padena aṭṭhatiṃsappabhedakammaṭṭhānaṃ suddhasamathakammaṭṭhānasseva gahaṇaṃ daṭṭhabbaṃ yadi evaṃ aṭṭhasamāpattivasenāti idaṃ kathanti taṃ vipassanādhiṭṭhānānaṃ samāpattīnaṃ vasena  kathitanti vadanti evañca katvā dutiyapadena adhipaññāsikkhāti idaṃ vacanaṃ samatthitaṃ hoti sikkhanaṃ nāma āsevananti āha ti nti payirupāsanaṃ tañca kho assutapariyāpuṇanakammaṭṭhānuggahādivasena dassento tiādimāha  lakkhaṇahāranayena vacīsucaritassa hi sīlasabhāvattā taggahaṇeneva tadekalakkhaṇaṃ kāyasucaritampi itarampi gahitamevāti ettha ca adhisīlasikkhāya cittaviveko adhipaññāsikkhāya upadhiviveko adhicittasikkhāya kāyaviveko kathito kāyaviveko pana sarūpeneva pāḷiyaṃ gahitoti tividhassapi vivekassa pakāsitattaṃ daṭṭhabbaṃ sesaṃ suviññeyyameva


ti ārammaṇalakkhaṇūpanijjhānalakkhaṇehi dvīhi jhānehi ti kammaṭṭhānānuyogaladdhāya vimuttiyā tena tadaṅgavikkhambhanavimuttiyo vadati satthusāsanassa hadayattā abbhantarattā  mānasassa  paṭilābhamānasaṃ taṃ pana atthato aññā evāti āha nti taṃ pattukāmena ekantato vajjetabbataṇhādiṭṭhīnaṃ bhāve tassa anijjhanato tadabhāve ijjhanato ca te uppādanavasena yadaggena nissito tadaggena panāyampi tehi nissito nāma hotīti āha tiādi arahattaṃ ānisaṃsitabbaṭṭhena ānisaṃsaṃ etassāti  arahattaṃ pattukāmassa pubbabhāgapaṭipadā icchitabbā tattha ca sabbapaṭhamo kammaṭṭhānaattānuyogo so idha na gahitoti āha ti tattha ti pariyattidhammo


ti  sakkassetaṃ nāmaṃ purimajātianugataṃ ti sakko eva ti mātāpituupaṭṭhānādicārittadhammena  ti upadhivepakkapāpadhamme abhibhavitvā nti indasattubhūtaṃ asuraṃ


ti devatāsaṃyutte saṃvaṇṇitatthameva tasmā idha na vattabbo atthoti adhippāyo


ti tena padhānena kāraṇabhūtena padhānakaraṇanimittanti attho  khuddakampi mahantampi hīnampi paṇītampi ti dīgharattassa vīriyārambho nti kiccaniṭṭhānaṃ ti yathā arahattuppattito pubbe tato pacchāpi tatheva buddhipaggahoti vīriyaṃ daḷhaṃ karotūti kuppadhammaṃ viya maññamāno vadati diṭṭhadhammasukhavihārādiatthaṃ pana vīriyakaraṇaṃ icchitabbameva
ti avomissā evaṃ aññattha anāgatattā tenāha tiādi yadi evaṃ idheva kasmā etaṃ vuttanti āha tiādi nti nadī nāma anavaṭṭhitatīrā tattha patiṭṭhātabbaṭṭhānaṃ


ti  pubbe yogāvacaro purimattabhāve bhāvanamanuyutto ayaṃ kira kassapassa bhagavato sāsane pabbajitvāva bahūni vassasahassāni samaṇadhammaṃ akāsi na pana visesaṃ nibbattesi tamatthaṃ kāraṇena saddhiṃ dassetuṃ tiādi vuttaṃ ti yathā visesāvaho hoti  evaṃ ekantena parisuddhassa sabbaso anupakkiliṭṭhassa ti yathā sīlaṃ uparūpari visesāvahaṃ nibbedhabhāgiyañca hoti evaṃ sammadeva āhitacittā suṭṭhu sampannacittā tathābhūtā tena samannāgatā hontīti āha ti ti sekkhattā eva yathādhigatadhammena niccalabhāvena adhiṭṭhitasabhāvā mayā tuṭṭhiyā gahitāya devaputto dullabhā tuṭṭhīti vakkhatīti bhagavā tuṭṭhi hoti sukhāvahāti avocāti āha nti pabbajito rukkhamūliko abbhokāsiko vā anagāriyupeto nāma hoti senāsane pana vasanto kathanti āha tiādi ti bhāvanābhiyogasiddho catūsu paccayesu santoso tena cittavūpasamena tuṭṭhi laddhāti dasseti ti cittakilesānaṃ vūpasamakarabhāvanāya manacchaṭṭhānaṃ indriyānaṃ nibbisevanabhāvakaraṇena savisesaṃ cittassa vūpasamakarabhāvanāya rato manoti yojanā
ettha ca indriyūpasamena cittasamādhānaṃ paripuṇṇaṃ hoti indriyabhāvanāya cittasamādhānassa akārakānaṃ dūrīkaraṇato adhicittasamādhānena catupaccayasantoso savisesaṃ parisuddho paripuṇṇo ca hoti paccayānaṃ alābhalābhesu pariccāgasabhāvato vuttanayena pana santuṭṭhassa yathāsamādinnaṃ sīlaṃ visujjhati pāripūriñca upagacchati tathābhūto catusaccakammaṭṭhāne yutto maggapaṭipāṭiyā sabbaso kilese samucchindanto nibbānadiṭṭho hotīti imamatthaṃ dasseti ntiādinā  gamissantevāti ariyamaggabhāvanaṃ pahāya sammāpaṭipattiyā dukkarabhāvaṃ sandhāya sāsaṅkaṃ vadati tenāha ti
tattha  keci ayaṃ panāti devaputto so hi bhagavato ariyā gacchantīti vacanaṃ sutvā duggamo bhagavātiādimāhāti vadanti taṃ na yujjati yasmā saccametanti evamādipi tasseva vevacanaṃ katvā dassitaṃ tasmā yena maggena ariyā gacchantīti tumhehi vuttaṃ ayaṃ pana duggamo bhagavā visamo maggoti āha devaputto ariyamaggo kāmaṃ kadāci atidukkhā paṭipadātipi vuccati tañca kho pubbabhāgapaṭipadāvasena ayaṃ pana atīva sugamo sabbakilesaduggavivajjanato kāyaduccaritādivisamassa rāgādivisamassa ca dūrīkaraṇato na visamo tenāha tiādi ti ariyamaggassa ariyamaggassa hi adhisīlasikkhādīnaṃ paribundhitabbabhāgena bahū parissayā hontīti ti duggamo visamoti ca evaṃ vutto
ti anuṭṭhahanena adhobhūtauttamaṅgā kusalaṅgesu hi sammādiṭṭhi uttamaṅgā sabbaseṭṭhattā  tañca anariyā patanti na uṭṭhahanti micchāpaṭipajjanato tenāha tiādi ti micchāmagge tenāha ti taṃ magganato anariyā ariyānaṃ maggato apāpuṇanena pariccattā hutvā apāye sakalavaṭṭadukkhe ca patanti ti svāyaṃ anariyehi kadācipi gantuṃ asakkuṇeyyo maggo ariyānaṃ visuddhasattānaṃ sabbaso samadhigamena  kāyavisamādīhi samannāgatattā  tesaṃ sabbasova pahānena sabbattha 


ti sampīḷitataṇhākilesādinā sauppīḷatāya paramasambādhe ativiya saṅkāraṭṭhānabhūto hi nīvaraṇasambādho adhippeto so hi duggahano tasmiṃ asati kāmaguṇasambādho anavasaro eva seyyathāpi mahākassapādīnaṃ  nīvaraṇasambādhābhāvato asambādhabhāvena hi jhānaṃ idha okāsoti vuttaṃ tañca kho accantāsambādhaṭṭhānatāya vipassanāpādakatāya  tathā hi pāḷiyaṃ avindītiādi vuttaṃ tattha ti vindi paṭilabhi ti mahāpañño sapaññoti attho ti bujjhi paṭivijjhi paṭilīno hutvā seṭṭho paṭilīnānaṃ vā seṭṭhoti  mānussayavasena unnatabhāvato  ti kāmaguṇasambādhepi ime kāmaguṇā mādisānaṃ kiṃ karissantīti te abhibhuyya nibbānappattiyā sammāsatiṃ paṭilabhiṃsu tena sampayuttena lokuttarasamādhināpi suṭṭhu samāhitā
ayaṃ kira devaputto ito purimavāre attabhāve paṭhamajjhānalābhī hutvā tato cavitvā brahmakāyikāsu nibbattitvā tattha jhānasukhaṃ anubhavitvā tato cuto idāni kāmabhave nibbatto tasmā taṃ jhānaṃ sambhāvento tādisassa nāma jhānasukhassa lābhī bhagavāti tena guṇena bhagavantaṃ abhitthavanto ti gāthaṃ abhāsi athassa bhagavā yathā nāma aṭṭhasaṭṭhiyojanasatasahassubbedhasinerupabbatarājaṃ upādāya sāsapo na kiñci hoti evaṃ anantāparimeyyabuddhaguṇe upādāya rūpāvacarapaṭhamajjhānaṃ na kiñci hotīti dassento ntiādinā anuttaraguṇādhigamaṃ pavedesi tattha nti vipassanāsatiyā saddhiṃ ariyamaggasatiṃ ti lokiyasamādhinā ceva lokuttarasamādhinā ca suṭṭhu samāhitā te  hi accantaṃ susamāhitā na jhānamattalābhino akuppadhammattā keci kammante susamāhitāti pāṭhaṃ vatvā maggasamāhitāti atthaṃ vadanti


 atītajātiyaṃ sayaṃkāravasena tāya diṭṭhiyā uppāditattā  tenāha ti apare āhu ayaṃ me satthāti gahaṇavasena titthakaro assa atthīti  atītattabhāve titthakarasāvakoti te ti  tassa satthuno diṭṭhiṃ ādāya gahetvāti atthoti vadanti  tabbinimuttassa micchāvādassa abhāvato titthe niyuttāti titthikā te eva ti vuttā ka-kārassa ya-kāraṃ katvā ti yathāvuttassa kalyāṇakammassa ti phalabhāvena ti vīriyadīpanāti attho
ti aniyamavidhānaṃ aniyamavasena vidhivacanaṃ nti taṇhāppabandhanaṃ ti samehi pajaha nti ekaggaṃ tenāha nti ti na uppajjati na pāpuṇātīti āha ti ti na saṅkocaṃ āpajjeyya gharāvāsato paribbajanaṃ parito apagamoti  pabbajitavatasamādānassa adaḷhatāya ca tattha ca asakkaccakiriyāya nti pabbajjāya purimakālatopi adhikaṃ ti uparūpari ti duccaritaṃ nāma sabbena sabbaṃ akatameva hitāvahaṃ
ti yaṃ kiñci kammaṃ nti asakkaccakāritāya sithilaṃ katvā pavattitaṃ ti evarūpaṃ parāmaṭṭhasāmaññasadisameva pacchānutāpacariyādipaṭibhāgato  taṇhāsaṃkilesaupakkiliṭṭhattā ntiādito samantatopi parehi saṅkitaṃ brahmacariyassa ādi ādibrahmacariyaṃ tattha niyuttāti  maggabrahmacariyassa pubbabhāgapaṭipadāti attho tenāha ti ti paṭhamārambhabhūtā


 vimāne  gahite taṃnivāsīpi gahito hotīti vuttaṃ ti ti sabbasmā duggaṭṭhānā  bhagavā tvaṃ tasmā mayhampi ito sambādhaṭṭhānato vippamokkhaṃ karohīti adhippāyo tenāha ti ti sabbassa  lokassa anuggahā tasmā tuyhampi etassapi candassa ti samānā eva pamuñcasīti pamuñcittha tenāha nti


ti lokassa andhakaraṇetipi apare tenāha ti ti vijjotati kāmaṃ devaputtavasena paṭhamaṃ devatā uddhaṭā rāhuno pana payogo tassa vimāneti āha ti  tadā mukhena gahitattā mukhena gahaṇañcettha gilīti adhippetaṃ na ca ajjhoharaṇanti āha ti idāni tassa mukhena gahaṇasamatthataṃ dassetuṃ tiādi vuttaṃ tiādi tassa candimasūriyānaṃ gahaṇakāraṇadassanaṃ ti candimasūriyānaṃ paricārakadevatā nti javaṃ so hi kenaci abhimukhaṃ atidunnivāro kammaniyāmasiddho nti kaṇṭhassa uparimadesaṃ keci sīsamatthakamevāti vadanti  vegassa tikkhasīghathāmabhāvato ti attano gamanadisābhimukhaṃ ākaḍḍhitvā nti rāhuṃ uddhaṃ ullaṅghetukāmampi  padadvayenapi so mahāsarīro mahābalo candimasūriyānaṃ pana gamanavego tena sabbathāpi dunnivāriyovāti dasseti ti candaggahe candavimānena sūriyaggahe sūriyavimānena ubhinnampi vimānena saheva amāvāsiyañhi dve vimānāni yojanamattantaritāni hutvā saheva pavattanti yadi dvepi devaputtā sotāpattiphalaṃ pattā atha kasmā sūriyasutte eva pajaṃ mamanti vuttaṃ na candasutteti so ca kira na cirasseva tato cavitvā aññattha nibbatto aññā eva ca devatā tattha vasi yasmiṃ candaggahe bhagavā taṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi na tathā sūriyo aparabhāge pana tatthapi kālena kālaṃ rāhuggaho hotīti vadanti


2 anāthapiṇḍikavaggo

 pabbatataṭā  sandamāno tathārūpo nadīnivattanapadesopi sambādhaṭṭhānatāya kaccho viyāti āha ti paṭipakkhadūrībhāvena seṭṭhaṭṭhena ca eko udetīti ekodi ekaggatā tasmiṃ yogato ekaggacittā idha  paṭipakkhato attānaṃ nipayanti visodhentīti  paññavanto tenāha tiādi kāyādibhedaṃ ārammaṇaṃ sātisayāya satiyā sarantīti  tenāha ti ti yathāvuttapadese magā sotthimanupaddavena vattissanti evaṃ jhānalābhino sotthiṃ kilesehi anupaddutā vattissanti ayaṃ kira devaputto brahmaloke nibbānasaññī tasmā evamāha bhagavā ayaṃ devaputto anibbānagāmī samāno nibbānagāmisaññī handassa nibbānagāmino dassemīti dutiyaṃ gāthamāha catunnaṃ oghānaṃ saṃsāramahoghasseva vā paratīrabhāvato paratīranti  ambuni jāto  maccho suttajālaṃ chinditvā macchā viya kilesajālaṃ bhinditvā gamissantīti


ti payirupāsahetu ti tissopi sikkhā sikkhanti ti kilesānaṃ sāsanato vaṭṭadukkhaparittāsanato ca siṭṭhisaññite yathānusiṭṭhaṃ paṭipajjitabbato pade saddhammeti attho tenāha ti tattha ti saddhammo ti yuttapattakāle nāma samathavipassanābhāvanā


ti dīghalaṭṭhitveva paññāyi tathāsamaññā eva ahosi


 natthi  etassa āvaṭṭaṃ āvaraṇanti  ti sattānaṃ pakaticakkhussa āvaraṇabhūto rukkho viya pabbato viya ca abhibhavituṃ samattho ñeyyāvaraṇo natthi nti kathaṃsaṇṭhitaṃ kathaṃpakāraṃ vā nti vaṭṭadukkhaṃ


ti kāmabhave tattha hi paribbhamantassa patiṭṭhā dullabhā yebhuyyena tattha sattā nimuggā eva honti tasmā heṭṭhā appatiṭṭho saṃsāro ti mahaggatabhave tattha hi nibbattassa nibbānaṃ āruhituṃ ālambanā dullabhā jhānābhiratiyā tattheva nikanti tesaṃ balavatī hoti tasmā upari anālambano saṃsāro ti nibbānaṃ pati pesitacitto ti puññābhisaṅkhārādayo tayo abhisaṅkhārā tena nandīpubbako kammabhavoti vatvā nandiṃ janetvāti vutto kāmasaññāsīsena kāmacchandassa gahaṇaṃ kāmacchandapamukhāni ca orambhāgiyasaṃyojanāni gahitānīti āha ti rūpabhavo rūpaṃ bhavapadalopena rūpabhavaggahaṇena cettha sesabhavassapi gahaṇaṃ tassa saṃyojanaggahaṇena pañca uddhambhāgiyasaṃyojanāni gahitāni ti  saṃsāramahoghe nti kāmabhavādīnaṃ  bhavabhāvena tadekalakkhaṇatāya  lakkhaṇahāranayena sesaṃ suviññeyyameva


 heṭṭhā devatāsaṃyuttavaṇṇanāyaṃ 


ti tassa devaputtassa nāmaṃ tassa satthu santikūpasaṅkamanassa kāraṇaṃ dassento tiādimāha tadeva sokaṃ tassa devaputtassa aṭṭhuppatti ti sahassamattena accharāsaṅghena parivuto nti nandanavanakīḷikaṃ ti hatthagayhupago hoti ti ettha mālāveṭhanampi khiḍḍāpasutatāyāti daṭṭhabbaṃ aññathā pupphāniyeva tāya tāya cittassa vasena mālābhāvena hatthaṃ upagacchantīti ti tasmiṃ devaloke āyusese sati eva tassa pana upaghātakassa laddhokāsassa pāpakammassa vasena pahāroti divasassa tatiyo bhāgo vuccati tasmā ti ekavelāyamevāti attho
ti piyavatthunimittakena sokena ti pīḷiyamāno ti manussagaṇanāya sattame divase ti tasmiṃyeva niraye  iminā tāhi ca saheva pubbe tassa pāpakammassa katattā ti cittasantāsaṃ āpajji nti nīharituṃ apanetuṃ ti tāhi pañcasatāhi accharāhi saddhiṃ bhagavato santikaṃ gantvā
 āsannapaccakkhabhāvato nti sadā svāyaṃ niccattho adhippāyavasena gahetabboti tattha pahātabbaṃ gahetabbañca dassento tiādimāha  hetupavattito pubbe tassa utrāsassa abhāvato ti dukkhesu tāni hi hetupaccayehi kattabbato gāthāyaṃ ti vuttāni ti vā kicchanimittaṃ yāsañhi payogavipattīnaṃ vasenassa tāni dukkhāni uppajjeyyuṃ taṃnimittanti attho tā hi assa payogavipattiyo gativipattiyo satthu santikaṃ upagamanena hāyeyyuṃ ti nibbattānaṃ vasena diṭṭhāni dukkhāni  ca dukkhesu sabbattha nimittatthe bhummaṃ  cittasantāpena
cattāripi saccāni bujjhati paṭivijjhatīti bodhi satiādidhammasāmaggī tasmā  bodhito sā pana bodhi bhāvanākāreneva pavattati aññatrasaddayogena ca bojjhāti nissakkavacananti tadatthaṃ dassento muñcitvāpadaṃ apekkhitvā nti āha nti dhutadhammamāha so hi taṇhāloluppassa tapanato  sayaṃ guṇasabhāvattā guṇasannissayato ca nti tenāha nti sabbe saṅkhāragatā nissajjīyanti etthāti  asaṅkhatadhātūti āha ti
 paṭipattikkamavasena tasseva paṭhamattā taṃ pana paṭipattikkamaṃ dassetuṃ tiādimāha nippariyāyato maggapariyāpannā eva bojjhaṅgāti āha ti ti tathābhāvena tassa yogino yasmā devaputtassa satthā taṃ gāthaṃ vatvā upari ca saccāni pakāsesi tasmā vuttaṃ ti ti kāmaṃ tasseva visesādhigamo idhāgato pañcasatamattāhi pana accharāhi saddhiṃ sotāpattiphale patiṭṭhahīti veditabbaṃ tenāha  (dī ni aṭṭha 2373 ma ni aṭṭha 1106) so desanāpariyosāne pañcahi accharāsatehi saddhiṃ sotāpattiphale patiṭṭhāya taṃ sampattiṃ thāvaraṃ katvā devalokameva agamāsīti


 nandāmīti  vutte nandī nāma pabbajitassa malanti codetukāmo devaputto ti pucchi athassa bhagavā taṃ paṭikkhipanto ti āha tena tayā mama adhippetanandiyā idha paccayo eva natthi kuto sā nandīti dasseti tenāha tiādi atha devaputto nandiyā asati sokena bhavitabbaṃ soko ca pabbajitassa malanti codento āha ti bhagavā tampi paṭikkhipanto tiādimāha kiṃ maṃ jinātīti attho
yadi te nandisokā na santi hāsavatthuno lābhassa jāniyā ca abhāvato ekavihārino pana aratiyā bhavitabbanti āha  ti tasmimpi bhagavatā paṭikkhitte atha nesampi nandisokāratīnaṃ abhāve kāraṇaṃ pucchanto nti gāthamāha athassa bhagavā taṃ kāraṇaṃ pavedento ti gāthamāha tattha ti aghe jātassa tenāha ti  appahīnataṇhassa  kāraṇassa appahīnattā tasseva hi kāraṇassa appahīnataṃ dassento nti āha dukkhappavattiyā sāpi taṇhāppavatti tena dassitā tiādinā vuttamevatthaṃ nigamanavasena dasseti


nti  uttarasuttaṃ heṭṭhā devatāsaṃyuttavaṇṇanāyaṃ vuttatthameva
