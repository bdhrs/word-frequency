

 idāni  evaṃ uppannāya codanāya vinayadharena kattabbakiccaṃ dassetuṃ tiādi āraddhaṃ tattha ti gāthāya ayamattho  ekeneko mātugāmena saddhiṃ ekaṭṭhānato nikkhamanto vā pavisanto vā diṭṭho so taṃ pārājikena codeti itaro tassa dassanaṃ anujānāti taṃ pana dassanaṃ paṭicca pārājikaṃ na upeti na paṭijānāti evamettha yaṃ tena diṭṭhaṃ taṃ tassa diṭṭho mayāti iminā diṭṭhavacanena sameti yasmā pana itaro taṃ dassanaṃ paṭicca dosaṃ na paṭijānāti tasmā asuddhaparisaṅkito hoti amūlakaparisaṅkitoti attho tassa puggalassa suddho ahanti paṭiññāya tena saddhiṃ uposatho kātabbo sesagāthādvayepi eseva nayo sesaṃ sabbattha uttānamevāti


tiādipucchānaṃ vissajjane ti sacce patiṭṭhātabbaṃ akuppe ca yaṃ kataṃ vā na kataṃ vā tadeva vattabbaṃ na ca codake vā anuvijjake vā saṅghe vā kopo uppādetabbo nti otiṇṇañca anotiṇṇañca vacanaṃ jānitabbaṃ tatrāyaṃ jānanavidhi  ettakā codakassa pubbakathā ettakā pacchimakathā ettakā cuditakassa pubbakathā ettakā pacchimakathāti jānitabbā  codakassa pamāṇaṃ gaṇhitabbaṃ cuditakassa pamāṇaṃ gaṇhitabbaṃ anuvijjakassa pamāṇaṃ gaṇhitabbaṃ anuvijjako appamattakampi ahāpento āvuso samannāharitvā ujuṃ katvā āharāti vattabbo saṅghena evaṃ paṭipajjitabbaṃ ti ettha ti bhūtaṃ vatthu ti codanā ceva sāraṇā ca nti ñattisampadā ceva anussāvanasampadā  ca etena hi dhammena ca vinayena ca satthusāsanena ca adhikaraṇaṃ vūpasamati tasmā anuvijjakena bhūtena vatthunā codetvā āpattiṃ sāretvā ñattisampadāya ceva anussāvanasampadāya ca taṃ adhikaraṇaṃ vūpasametabbaṃ anuvijjakena evaṃ paṭipajjitabbaṃ sesamettha uttānameva
tiādipucchāvissajjanampi uttānameva avasānagāthāsu ti avamaññaṃ karonto kiṃ ime jānantīti paribhāsati ti tāya chandādigāmitāya tena ca paribhāsanena attanā attano khatattā  saddhādīnañca indriyānaṃ upahatattā  ti so khato upahatindriyo paññāya abhāvato dummedho tīsu sikkhāsu asikkhanato na ca sikkhāya gāravo kāyassa bhedā nirayameva upagacchati tasmā ti tassattho na ca āmisaṃ nissāya kare cuditakacodakesu hi aññatarena dinnaṃ cīvarādiāmisaṃ gaṇhanto āmisaṃ nissāya karoti evaṃ na kareyya nti ayaṃ me upajjhāyo vā ācariyo vātiādinā nayena chandādīhi gacchanto puggalaṃ nissāya karoti evaṃ na kareyya atha kho  yathā dhammo ṭhito tatheva kareyyāti
ti  evaṃ kathehi mā evaṃ kathayitthāti kaṇṇamūle manteti ti dosameva gavesati ti vinicchayaṃ hāpeti ti āpattiṃ dīpeti
ti anokāse anajjhiṭṭhova codeti ti purimakathañca pacchimakathañca na jānāti
ti kathānusandhivinicchayānusandhivasena vacanaṃ na jānāti sesaṃ sabbattha uttānamevāti
