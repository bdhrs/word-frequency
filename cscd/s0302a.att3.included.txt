


 anamataggasaṃyuttassa  paṭhame ti anu amataggo vassasataṃ vassasahassaṃ ñāṇena anugantvāpi amataggo aviditaggo nāssa sakkā ito vā etto vā aggaṃ jānituṃ aparicchinnapubbāparakoṭikoti attho ti khandhādīnaṃ avicchinnappavattā paṭipāṭi ti purimamariyādā na dissati yadaggena cassa purimā koṭi na paññāyati pacchimāpi tadaggeneva na paññāyati vemajjheyeva pana sattā saṃsaranti ti idaṃ upamāya khuddakattā vuttaṃ bāhirasamayasmiñhi attho paritto hoti upamā mahatī hatthī viya ayaṃ goṇo goṇo viya sūkaro samuddo viya taḷākanti hi vutte na tesaṃ tādisaṃ pamāṇaṃ hoti buddhasamaye pana upamā parittā attho mahā pāḷiyañhi eko jambudīpo gahito evarūpānaṃ pana jambudīpānaṃ satepi sahassepi satasahassepi tiṇādīni tena upakkamena pariyādānaṃ gaccheyyuṃ na tveva purisassa mātu mātaroti nti tumhehi dukkhaṃ anubhūtaṃ nti tasseva vevacanaṃ nti ñātibyasanādianekavidhaṃ ti susānaṃ pathavīyeva vā sā hi punappunaṃ marantehi sarīranikkhepena vaḍḍhitā ti yuttameva paṭhamaṃ

 dutiye nti cakkavāḷapariyantaṃ mahāpathaviṃ ti taṃ pathaviṃ bhinditvā vuttappamāṇaṃ guḷikaṃ karitvā ekamantaṃ ṭhapeyya dutiyaṃ

 tatiye nti sasaddaṃ rudamānānaṃ nti sanditaṃ pavattaṃ ti sinerurasmīhi paricchinnesu catūsu mahāsamuddesu sinerussa hi pācīnapassaṃ rajatamayaṃ dakkhiṇapassaṃ maṇimayaṃ pacchimapassaṃ phalikamayaṃ uttarapassaṃ suvaṇṇamayaṃ pubbadakkhiṇapassehi nikkhantā rajatamaṇirasmiyo ekato hutvā mahāsamuddapiṭṭhena gantvā cakkavāḷapabbataṃ āhacca tiṭṭhanti dakkhiṇapacchimapassehi nikkhantā maṇiphalikarasmiyo pacchimuttarapassehi nikkhantā phalikasuvaṇṇarasmiyo uttarapācīnapassehi nikkhantā suvaṇṇarajatarasmiyo ekato hutvā mahāsamuddapiṭṭhena gantvā cakkavāḷapabbataṃ āhacca tiṭṭhanti tāsaṃ rasmīnaṃ antaresu cattāro mahāsamuddā honti te sandhāya vuttaṃ catūsu mahāsamuddesūti ntiādīsu nti viasanaṃ vināsoti attho ñātīnaṃ byasanaṃ  bhogānaṃ byasanaṃ  rogo pana sayameva ārogyaṃ viyasati vināsetīti byasanaṃ rogova byasanaṃ  tatiyaṃ

 catutthe nti ekanāmikāya manussamātu khīraṃ imesañhi sattānaṃ gaṇḍuppādakipillikādīsu vā macchakacchapādīsu vā pakkhijātesu vā nibbattakāle mātukhīrameva natthi ajapasumahiṃsādīsu nibbattakāle khīraṃ atthi tathā manussesu tattha ajādikāle ca manussesu cāpi devī sumanā tissāti evaṃ nānānāmikānaṃ kucchiyaṃ nibbattakāle aggahetvā tissāti ekanāmikāya eva mātu kucchiyaṃ nibbattakāle pītaṃ thaññaṃ catūsu mahāsamuddesu udakato bahutaranti veditabbaṃ catutthaṃ

 pañcame ti so kira bhikkhu cintesi  satthā anamataggassa saṃsārassa dīghatamattā na sukaraṃ na sukaranti kathetiyeva  kathaṃ nacchindati  sakkā nu kho upamaṃ kārāpetunti tasmā evamāha ti tayo kappāsaṃsū ekato gahetvā  kantitasuttamayena atisukhumavatthena tena pana parimaṭṭhe kittakaṃ khīyeyyāti sāsapamattaṃ pañcamaṃ

 chaṭṭhe nti āyasena pākārena parikkhittaṃ nagaraṃ na pana anto āyasehi ekabhūmikādipāsādehi ākiṇṇanti daṭṭhabbaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ

 sattame nti ekena kappasatasahasse anussarite aparo tassa ṭhitaṭṭhānato aññaṃ satasahassaṃ aññopi aññanti evaṃ cattāropi cattārisatasahassāni anussareyyuṃ sattamaṃ

 aṭṭhame ti yā etasmiṃ āyāmato pañcayojanasatike antare vālikā navame vattabbaṃ natthi aṭṭhamanavamāni

 dasame tiādīni tīṇipi rāsivevacanāneva imesaṃ pana sattānaṃ saaṭṭhikālato anaṭṭhikālova bahutaro gaṇḍuppādakādipāṇabhūtānañhi etesaṃ aṭṭhimeva natthi macchakacchapādibhūtānaṃ pana aṭṭhimeva bahutaraṃ tasmā anaṭṭhikālañca bahuaṭṭhikālañca aggahetvā samaṭṭhikālova gahetabbo ti gijjhakūṭassa uttarapasse ṭhito ti magadharaṭṭhassa giribbaje giriparikkhepe ṭhitoti attho sesaṃ sabbattha uttānamevāti dasamaṃ



 dutiyavaggassa  paṭhame nti daliddaṃ kapaṇaṃ nti dussaṇṭhānehi hatthapādehi upetaṃ paṭhamaṃ

 dutiye nti sukhasamappitaṃ mahaddhanaṃ mahābhogaṃ nti alaṅkatapaṭiyattaṃ hatthikkhandhagataṃ mahāparivāraṃ dutiyaṃ

 tatiye ti pāveyyadesavāsino tiādīsu dhutaṅgasamādānavasena tesaṃ āraññikādibhāvo veditabbo ti sabbe sabandhanā keci sotāpannā keci sakadāgāmino keci anāgāmino tesu hi puthujjano vā khīṇāsavo vā natthi ntiādīsu setakāḷādivaṇṇesu ekekavaṇṇakālova gahetabbo ti paripanthe tiṭṭhanakā panthaghātacorā ti paradāracārittaṃ āpajjanakā tatiyaṃ

 catutthādīsu liṅganiyamena ceva cakkavāḷaniyamena ca attho veditabbo purisānañhi mātugāmakālo mātugāmānañca purisakāloti evamettha liṅganiyamo imamhā cakkavāḷā sattā paracakkavāḷaṃ paracakkavāḷā ca imaṃ cakkavāḷaṃ saṃsaranti tesu imasmiṃ cakkavāḷe mātugāmakāle mātubhūtaññeva dassento ti āha tiādīsupi eseva nayo catutthādīni

 dasame nti atītakāle ekaṃ apadānaṃ āharitvā dasseti ti paññatti ahosi ti idaṃ thāmamajjhime sandhāya vuttaṃ nti uttamaṃ nti sundarayugalaṃ ti ettāvatā kira dvinnaṃ buddhānaṃ antare yojanaṃ pathavī ussannā so pabbato tiyojanubbedho jāto
ti vassasatato uttariṃ appaṃ dasa vā vīsaṃ vā vassāni puna vassasatameva jīvanako nāma natthi uttamakoṭiyā pana saṭṭhi vā asīti vā vassāni jīvanti vassasataṃ pana appatvā pañcavassadasavassādikāle mīyamānāva bahukā ettha ca kakusandho bhagavā cattālīsavassasahassāyukakāle koṇāgamano tiṃsavassasahassāyukakāle nibbattoti idaṃ anupubbena parihīnasadisaṃ kataṃ na pana evaṃ parihīnaṃ vaḍḍhitvā vaḍḍhitvā parihīnanti veditabbaṃ kathaṃ kakusandho tāva bhagavā imasmiṃyeva kappe cattālīsavassasahassāyukakāle nibbatto āyuppamāṇaṃ pañca koṭṭhāse katvā cattāro ṭhatvā pañcame vijjamāneyeva parinibbuto taṃ āyu parihāyamānaṃ dasavassakālaṃ patvā puna vaḍḍhamānaṃ asaṅkheyyaṃ hutvā tato parihāyamānaṃ tiṃsavassasahassāyukakāle ṭhitaṃ tadā koṇāgamano nibbatto tasmimpi tatheva parinibbute taṃ āyu dasavassakālaṃ patvā puna vaḍḍhamānaṃ asaṅkheyyaṃ hutvā parihāyitvā vīsavassasahassakāle ṭhitaṃ tadā kassapo bhagavā nibbatto tasmimpi tatheva parinibbute taṃ āyu dasavassakālaṃ patvā puna vaḍḍhamānaṃ asaṅkheyyaṃ hutvā parihāyitvā vassasatakālaṃ pattaṃ atha amhākaṃ sammāsambuddho nibbatto evaṃ anupubbena parihāyitvā vaḍḍhitvā vaḍḍhitvā parihīnanti veditabbaṃ tattha ca yaṃ āyuparimāṇesu mandesu buddhā nibbattanti tesampi tadeva āyuparimāṇaṃ hotīti dasamaṃ
