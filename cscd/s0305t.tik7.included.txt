


ti ajjhattaṃ kāye samudayadhammānupassī vayo samudayavayo bahiddhā samudayo vayo samudayavayo ajjhattabahiddhā samudayo vayo samudayavayadhammānupassīti nava anupassanā tathā vedanāya citte dhammesūti evaṃ chattiṃsāya ṭhānesu ti kāye ajjhattaṃ bahiddhā ajjhattabahiddhā vedanāya citte dhammesūti evaṃ dvādasasu ṭhānesu

ti imāya hīnattikapāḷiyā  lāmakaṭṭhena  hīnapaṇītānaṃ majjhe bhavāti uttamaṭṭhena attappakaṭṭhena padhānabhāvaṃ nītāti 

 kaṇḍakā etissā atthīti  karamandagaccho osadhibhāvāpekkhāya itthiliṅganiddeso tabbahulatāya taṃ vanaṃ kaṇḍakīvananteva paññāyittha nti sahassacakkavāḷalokaṃ  tathā ālokavaḍḍhanassa katattā
