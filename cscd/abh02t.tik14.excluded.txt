15. Paṭisambhidāvibhaṅgo

1. Saṅgahavāravaṇṇanā
718 . Eseva   nayo ‘‘dhammappabhedassa hī’’
‘‘Yaṃ kiñci paccayasamuppanna’’
Vidahatī ‘‘pavatteti ceva pāpeti cā’’
Dhammaniruttābhilāpe dhamma ‘‘yā sabhāvaniruttī’’ Tassā abhilāpe 
Taṃ sabhāvaniruttiṃ saddaṃ ārammaṇaṃ katvā paccavekkhantassa tasmiṃ sabhāvaniruttābhilāpe pabhedagataṃ ñāṇaṃ niruttipaṭisambhidā  Taṃ sabhāvaniruttiṃ saddaṃ ārammaṇaṃ katvā paccavekkhantassā Sabhāvaniruttī ‘‘aññaṃ panā’’ Byañjana
Kathitaṃ Bodhimaṇḍa Suvaṇṇasalāka na paṭisambhidāyaṃ ṭhitena
Idaṃ kathita Chaddantavāraṇa tittirajātakā
Tatthā  Sesā parivattanti ‘‘ayamevekā na parivattatī’’ Papañco
Sabbatthakañāṇa  ‘‘sabbatthakañāṇa’’
‘‘paripucchā’’ Paṭipattiṃ pūretabbaṃ maññissantī Tissatthero
Pabhedo Adhigamo Na pana tathā ‘‘pariyattisavanaparipucchā hī’’ paṭisambhidā nāma natthī  ‘‘ime panā’’
Saṅgahavāravaṇṇanā niṭṭhitā.
