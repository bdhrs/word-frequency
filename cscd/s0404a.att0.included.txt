





 aṭṭhakanipātassa  paṭhame ti ādarena sevitāya ti vaḍḍhitāya ti punappunaṃ katāya ti yuttayānasadisakatāya ti patiṭṭhānaṭṭhena vatthu viya katāya ti paccupaṭṭhitāya ti samantato citāya upacitāya ti suṭṭhu samāraddhāya sukatāya ti guṇā tiādīsu yaṃ vattabbaṃ taṃ ekādasakanipāte vakkhāma
nti  pharaṇavasena appamāṇaṃ nti mettāpadaṭṭhānāya vipassanāya anukkamena upadhikkhayasaṅkhātaṃ arahattaṃ pattassa dasa saṃyojanā pahīyantīti attho atha vā ti paṭighañceva paṭighasampayuttasaṃyojanā ca tanukā honti nti tesaṃyeva kilesūpadhīnaṃ khayasaṅkhātaṃ mettaṃ adhigamavasena passantassa ti tena mettāyanena kusalo hoti nti  sattasaṅkhātena saṇḍena samannāgataṃ sattabharitanti attho ti adaṇḍena asatthena dhammeneva vijinitvā ti isisadisā dhammikarājāno ti dānāni dadamānā ti vicariṃsu
ntiādīsu porāṇakarājakāle kira sassamedhaṃ purisamedhaṃ sammāpāsaṃ vācāpeyyanti cattāri saṅgahavatthūni ahesuṃ yehi rājāno  lokaṃ saṅgaṇhiṃsu tattha nipphannasassato dasamabhāgaggahaṇaṃ  nāma sassasampādane medhāvitāti attho mahāyodhānaṃ chamāsikaṃ bhattavetanānuppadānaṃ  nāma purisasaṅgaṇhane medhāvitāti attho daliddamanussānaṃ hatthato lekhaṃ gahetvā tīṇi vassāni vinā vaḍḍhiyā sahassadvisahassamattadhanānuppadānaṃ  nāma tañhi sammā manusse pāseti hadaye bandhitvā viya ṭhapeti tasmā sammāpāsanti vuccati tāta mātulātiādinā nayena pana saṇhavācābhaṇanaṃ  nāma piyavācāti attho evaṃ catūhi saṅgahavatthūhi saṅgahitaṃ raṭṭhaṃ iddhañceva hoti phītañca bahuannapānaṃ khemaṃ nirabbudaṃ manussā mudā modamānā ure putte naccentā apārutagharā viharanti idaṃ gharadvāresu aggaḷānaṃ abhāvato nti vuccati ayaṃ porāṇikā paveṇi
aparabhāge pana okkākarājakāle brāhmaṇā imāni cattāri  saṅgahavatthūni imañca raṭṭhasampattiṃ parivattetvā uddhaṃmūlakaṃ katvā ntiādike pañca yaññe nāma akaṃsu tesu assamettha medhanti vadhentīti  dvīhi pariyaññehi yajitabbassa ekavīsatiyūpassa ekasmiṃ pacchimadivaseyeva sattanavutipañcapasusataghātabhiṃsanassa ṭhapetvā bhūmiñca purise ca avasesasabbavibhavadakkhiṇassa yaññassetaṃ adhivacanaṃ purisamettha medhantīti  catūhi pariyaññehi yajitabbassa saddhiṃ bhūmiyā assamedhe vuttavibhavadakkhiṇassa yaññassetaṃ adhivacanaṃ sammamettha pāsantīti  divase divase yugacchiggaḷe  pavesanadaṇḍakasaṅkhātaṃ sammaṃ khipitvā tassa patitokāse vediṃ katvā saṃhārimehi yūpādīhi sarassatīnadiyā nimuggokāsato pabhuti paṭilomaṃ gacchantena yajitabbassa satrayāgassetaṃ adhivacanaṃ vājamettha pivantīti  ekena pariyaññena sattarasahi pasūhi yajitabbassa beluvayūpassa sattarasakadakkhiṇassa yaññassetaṃ adhivacanaṃ natthi ettha aggaḷāti  navahi pariyaññehi yajitabbassa  saddhiṃ bhūmiyā ca purisehi ca assamedhe vuttavibhavadakkhiṇassa sabbamedhapariyāyanāmassa assamedhavikappassetaṃ adhivacanaṃ
nti te sabbepi mahāyāgā ekassa mettācittassa vipākamahantatāya soḷasiṃ kalaṃ na agghanti soḷasamaṃ bhāgaṃ na pāpuṇantīti attho ti na attanā parassa jāniṃ karoti ti na parena parassa jāniṃ kāreti ti mettāyamānacittakoṭṭhāso hutvā nti sabbasattesu ti tassa kenaci saddhiṃ akusalaveraṃ vā puggalaveraṃ vā natthi

 dutiye ti maggabrahmacariyassa ādibhūtāya ti vipassanāya nti gāravuppattipaccayabhūtaṃ garubhāvanīyaṃ nti bahalaṃ ti atthapāḷianusandhipubbāparaṃ pucchati ti pañhaṃ karoti idañcidañca paṭipucchissāmīti vitakketi ti duvidhena ti anānattakathiko hoti ti nānāvidhaṃ tiracchānakathaṃ na katheti nti ariyatuṇhībhāvo nāma catutthajjhānaṃ sesakammaṭṭhānamanasikāropi vaṭṭati ti jānitabbakaṃ jānāti ti passitabbakaṃ passati ti  piyabhāvatthāya ti garubhāvatthāya ti bhāvanatthāya guṇasambhāvanāya vā ti samaṇadhammatthāya ti nirantarabhāvatthāya

 tatiye ti appiyajanassa pasaṃsako vaṇṇabhāṇī ti piyajanassa  nindako garahako catutthe ti aho vata maṃ aññena avajāneyyunti anavajānanakāmo ti kathākālaṃ na jānāti akāle katheti ti asucīhi kāyakammādīhi samannāgato

 pañcame lokassa dhammāti  etehi muttā nāma natthi buddhānampi honti tenevāha  ti anubandhanti nappajahanti  lokato na nivattantīti attho ti ayañca loko ete anubandhati na pajahati tehi dhammehi na nivattatīti attho
ti lābhe āgate alābho āgatoyevāti veditabbo dīsupi eseva nayo ti vipariṇāmadhammā imeti evaṃ avekkhati ti  vidhamitā viddhaṃsitā ti nibbānapadaṃ jānitvā ti bhavassa pāraṃ gato nipphattiṃ matthakaṃ patto nibbānapadaṃ ñatvāva taṃ pāraṃ gatabhāvaṃ sammappajānātīti imasmiṃ sutte vaṭṭavivaṭṭaṃ kathitaṃ

 chaṭṭhe ti kiṃ visesakāraṇaṃ ti ko adhikappayogo ti gahetvā pariniṭṭhapetvā idhāpi vaṭṭavivaṭṭameva kathitaṃ

 sattame ti saṅghaṃ bhinditvā na cirapakkante ti āgamma paṭicca sandhāya nti attano vipattiṃ vipannākāraṃ sesapadesupi eseva nayo ti abhibhavitvā madditvā

 aṭṭhame nti evaṃnāmake vihāre so kira vaṭavane niviṭṭhattā vaṭajālikāti saṅkhaṃ gato ti imamatthaṃ devarañño ārocessāmīti gantvā pākaṭo ahosi ti sikkhattayasaṅgahassa sakalasāsanabrahmacariyassa ādibhūto

 navame ti jātikulaputto ti thāmasampanno ti  rūpasampattiyā pasādajanako ti bahalarāgo tiādīsu ayamattho  kiṃ aññena kāraṇena  kathitena ayaṃ nando indriyesu guttadvāro bhojane mattaññū jāgariyamanuyutto satisampajaññena samannāgato yehi nando sakkoti paripuṇṇaṃ parisuddhaṃ brahmacariyaṃ carituṃ sace imehi kāraṇehi samannāgato nābhavissa na sakkuṇeyyāti ti evaṃ tattha imasmiṃ sutte vaṭṭameva kathitaṃ

 dasame ti aññena kāraṇena vacanena vā aññaṃ kāraṇaṃ vacanaṃ vā paṭicchādeti ti bāhirato aññaṃ āgantukakathaṃ otāreti ti apaneyyo nīharitabbo esa ti samaṇadūsako ti vīhīsu vīhipalāpo viya nissāratāya samaṇesu samaṇapalāpo ti samaṇakacavaro ti bahi nīharanti ti yavakhette ti ucce ṭhāne ṭhatvā mahāvāte opuniyamānassa ti  sāradhaññānaṃ ekato dubbaladhaññānaṃ ekato karaṇatthaṃ punappunaṃ apasammajjanti apasammajjanisaṅkhātena vātaggāhinā suppena vā vatthena vā nīharanti nti daddarasaddaṃ
nti saṃvāsena ayaṃ ti jāneyyātha ti saṇhavāco ti janamajjhe nti karaṇaṃ vuccati pāpakammaṃ taṃ raho paṭicchanno hutvā  karoti ti saṃsappitvā musāvādī musā bhaṇanto saṃsappati phandatīti attho imasmiṃ sutte vaṭṭameva kathetvā gāthāsu vaṭṭavivaṭṭaṃ kathitanti
