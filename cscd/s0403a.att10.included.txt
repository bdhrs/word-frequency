

 tatiyassa  paṭhame ti visāradabhāvāvahā ti domanassaṃ hoti

 dutiye ti ussaṅkito ca parisaṅkito ca ti api akuppadhammo khīṇāsavo samānopi parehi pāpabhikkhūhi ussaṅkitaparisaṅkito hotīti attho tiādīsu vesiyā vuccanti rūpūpajīviniyo tā gocaro assāti vesiyāgocaro tāsaṃ gehaṃ abhiṇhagamanoti attho sesapadesupi eseva nayo tattha pana ti matapatikā ti mahallikakumārikāyo

 tatiye ti ito mama sāpateyyato bhogaṃ gahetvā tena paṭisanthāraṃ karissāmi tassa ca mama ca antaraṃ pidahissāmīti attho ti  parasantakānaṃ bhaṇḍānaṃ gahaṇāni ti guhitabbamantā ti sassataṃ vā ucchedaṃ vā gahetvā ṭhitāya sesamettha uttānatthameva catutthe sabbaṃ heṭṭhā vuttanayameva

 pañcame nti mettacittena pavattitaṃ kāyakammaṃ ti  sammukhe ceva parammukhe ca itaresupi eseva nayo tiādi catupārisuddhisīlavasena vuttaṃ ti  maggasamādhiphalasamādhinibbattakāni ti samānasīlataṃ gato ekasadisasīlo hutvāti attho ti yo naṃ karoti tassa iti imasmiṃ sutte sīlaṃ missakaṃ kathitaṃ diṭṭhi vipassanāsammādiṭṭhīti

 chaṭṭhe ti paraṃ sīlabhāvena na garahati na upavadati ti attanova katākataṃ jānanavasena attānaṃ anupekkhitā ti parassa katākatesu abyāvaṭo ti apākaṭo appapuñño ti apaññātabhāvena apākaṭatāya mandapuññatāya ti paritāsaṃ nāpajjati iti imasmiṃ sutte khīṇāsavova kathito

 sattame sīlasamādhipaññā missikā kathitā vimutti arahattaphalaṃ  vimuttiñāṇadassanaṃ paccavekkhaṇañāṇaṃ lokiyameva aṭṭhamepi eseva nayo paccavekkhaṇañāṇaṃ panettha asekhassa pavattattā asekhanti vuttaṃ

 navame ti catūsu disāsu appaṭihatacāro imasmimpi sutte khīṇāsavova kathito dasame nti yutto idhāpi khīṇāsavova kathito
