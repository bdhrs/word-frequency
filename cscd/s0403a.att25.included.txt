

 chaṭṭhassa  paṭhame nti upajjhāyena hutvā upasampādetabbaṃ dutiye ti ācariyena hutvā nissayo dātabbo tatiye ti upajjhāyena hutvā sāmaṇero gahetabbo iti imāni tīṇipi suttāni paṭhamabodhiyaṃ khīṇāsavavasena vuttāni catutthādīni anupadavaṇṇanāto uttānatthāneva

 bhattuddesakādīnaṃ  vinicchayakathā samantapāsādikāya vinayaṭṭhakathāyaṃ  vuttanayena veditabbāti ti pakatiyā sammato gaccha bhattāni uddisāhīti na pesetabbo
ti vassikasāṭikāya gāhāpako ti yo ca tassā bhikkhuparisāya pattapariyanto so tassa bhikkhuno padātabboti ettha vuttapattaggāhāpako
