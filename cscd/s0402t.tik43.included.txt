

 pañcamassa  paṭhamadutiyāni uttānatthāneva


 tatiye ti bhāvappadhāno niddesoti āha ti appaṭivānitāti vattabbe appaṭivānīti vuttaṃ tathā hi vīriyappavāhe vattamāne antarā eva paṭigamanaṃ nivattanaṃ paṭivānaṃ taṃ assa atthīti paṭivānī na paṭivānī appaṭivānī tassa bhāvo  anivattanāti attho ti vā itthiliṅgavasenevāyaṃ niddeso antarāyeva paṭigamanaṃ nivattanaṃ paṭivānī na paṭivānī  anivattanāti attho


 catutthe nti sammadeva ṭhapetabbaṃ yathā pana ṭhapitaṃ saṇṭhapitaṃ hoti taṃ dassetuṃ ntiādi vuttaṃ tattha nti samādhippaṭipakkhe kilese sannisīdāpentena cittaṃ gocarajjhatte sannisīdāpetabbanti nti abyaggabhāvāpādanena ekaggaṃ kātabbaṃ nti yathā ārammaṇe suṭṭhu appitaṃ hoti evaṃ sammā sammadeva ādahitabbaṃ suṭṭhu āropetabbaṃ samāhitaṃ kātabbanti attho


 pañcame nti chavānaṃ daḍḍhaṭṭhāne alātaṃ tenevāha nti nti pamāṇena aṭṭhaṅgulamattaṃ dvīsu ṭhānesu ādittaṃ majjhe gūthamakkhitaṃ nti kaṭṭhena kātabbakiccaṃ sesamettha uttānameva chaṭṭhaṃ uttānameva


 sattame ti khippapañño tenāha tiādi nti lokuttarādhigamassa anucchavikasabhāvaṃ kalyāṇā sundarā parimaṇḍalapadabyañjanā vācā assāti  tenāha ti kalyāṇaṃ madhuraṃ vākkaraṇaṃ udāhāraghoso assāti  guṇaparipuṇṇabhāvena pūre puṇṇabhāve bhavāti porī tāya  tenāha ti pure bhavattā poriyā nāgarikitthiyā sukhumālattanena sadisāti porī tāya  sukhumālattanenāti iminā tassā vācāya mudusaṇhabhāvo vutto ti pittasemhādīhi na paliveṭhitāya ti sandiddhavilambitādidosarahitāya ti apatitapadabyañjanāya avirahitapadabyañjanāya ubhayametaṃ anelagalāyāti imasseva atthavacanaṃ ti hi anelāya ceva agalāya cāti attho ti ādimajjhapariyosānaṃ pākaṭaṃ katvā bhāsitatthassa viññāpanasatthāya aṭṭhamanavamadasamāni uttānatthāneva
