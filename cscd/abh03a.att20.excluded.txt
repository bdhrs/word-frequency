5. Pañcamavaggo
1. Vimuttikathāvaṇṇanā
418 .   vimuttiñāṇaṃ yaṃkiñcī Paṭipannassā sotāpannassā
Vimuttikathāvaṇṇanā.
2. Asekhañāṇakathāvaṇṇanā
421 .   Jānāti passatī Gotrabhuno Nanu āyasmā ānando sekho ‘‘uḷāro bhagavā’’ti jānātī
Asekhañāṇakathāvaṇṇanā.
3. Viparītakathāvaṇṇanā
424 .  anicce nicca
Kusala Atthi arahato Sabbeva pathavī  Nanu pathavī atthi, atthi ca koci pathaviṃ pathavito samāpajjatī  Pathavī atthī
Viparītakathāvaṇṇanā.
4. Niyāmakathāvaṇṇanā
428-431 . aniyatassā niyāmagamanāyā
 niyatassā  atthi niyāmo atthi niyāmo Satipaṭṭhānā  Gotrabhuno Bhagavā jānātī
Niyāmakathāvaṇṇanā.
5. Paṭisambhidākathāvaṇṇanā
432-433 . Sammutiñāṇa Sabbā paññā Pathavīkasiṇasamāpatti Tena  hi sabbaṃ ñāṇa 
Paṭisambhidākathāvaṇṇanā.
6. Sammutiñāṇakathāvaṇṇanā
434-435 .  na vattabba Sammutisaccamhī Sammutiñāṇaṃ saccārammaṇaññevā tena ñāṇenā
Sammutiñāṇakathāvaṇṇanā.
7. Cittārammaṇakathāvaṇṇanā
436-438 . nanu atthi kocī Phassārammaṇe  Vedanārammaṇe phassārammaṇe ñāṇaṃ na vattabba  Kiṃ panetaṃ phassapariyāye ñāṇa nanu cetopariyāye ñāṇa
Cittārammaṇakathāvaṇṇanā.
8. Anāgatañāṇakathāvaṇṇanā
439-440 .  anāgataṃ mūlato mūlato mūlaṃ hetu nidānaṃ sambhavo pabhavo samuṭṭhānaṃ āhāro ārammaṇaṃ paccayo samudayo  na heva Anāgataṃ hetupaccayata Gotrabhuno Pāṭaliputtassā 
Anāgatañāṇakathāvaṇṇanā.
9. Paṭuppannañāṇakathāvaṇṇanā
441-442 . paṭuppanne  tenā Tena ñāṇena taṃ ñāṇaṃ jānātī Tena phassena taṃ phassa  nanu sabbasaṅkhāre
Paṭuppannañāṇakathāvaṇṇanā.
10. Phalañāṇakathāvaṇṇanā
443-444 .  sāvakassā sāvako phalassa kataṃ paññapetī
Atthi sāvakassa phalaparopariyattī 
Atthi sāvakassa khandhapaññattī  Sāvako jino Sāvako anuppannassā Aññāṇī
Phalañāṇakathāvaṇṇanā.
Pañcamo vaggo.
Mahāpaṇṇāsako samatto.
