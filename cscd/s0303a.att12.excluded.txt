13. Jhānasaṃyuttaṃ
1. Samādhimūlakasamāpattisuttavaṇṇanā
662 .  samādhikusalo Na samādhismiṃ samāpattikusalo
2-55. Samādhimūlakaṭhitisuttādivaṇṇanā
663-716 . na samādhismiṃ ṭhitikusalo Na samādhismiṃ vuṭṭhānakusalo  Na samādhismiṃ kallitakusalo Na samādhismiṃ ārammaṇakusalo Na samādhismiṃ gocarakusalo Na samādhismiṃ abhinīhārakusalo Na samādhismiṃ sakkaccakārī Na samādhismiṃ sātaccakārī Na samādhismiṃ sappāyakārī  
Jhānasaṃyuttavaṇṇanā niṭṭhitā.
Iti sāratthappakāsiniyā saṃyuttanikāya-aṭṭhakathāya
Khandhavaggavaṇṇanā niṭṭhitā.
Saṃyuttanikāya-aṭṭhakathāya dutiyo bhāgo.
