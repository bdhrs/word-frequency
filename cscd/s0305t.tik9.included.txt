-saddo pucchāyaṃ tasmā nūti iminā samānatthoti āha ti ti vatthukāmesu icchāti āha ti  saparasantatipariyāpannesu  kāmacchandappahānakittanena paṭighasaññāpaṭivinayakittanena ca  pañcorambhāgiyasaññojanasamucchedassa byākatattā ti vipassanaṃ anuyuñjathāti dassento

ti aṭṭhakathādhippāye ṭhatvā vuttaṃ parato āgatena theravādena so anicchito na  hi tārakarūpamuttāvaḷikādisadisaṃ nimittūpaṭṭhānākāramattaṃ khaṇamattaṭṭhāyinaṃ kasiṇanimittesu viya ugghāṭanaṃ kātuṃ sakkoti tenāha ti  ānāpānassatisamādhismiṃ siddhe ayaṃ guṇo sukheneva ijjhatīti nti yathā purimavāre bhikkhu cepi ākaṅkheyyāti  āgataṃ evaṃ idha bhāvite kho bhikkhave ānāpānassatisamādhimhīti āgatavāre bhikkhuggahaṇamakataṃ tasmā soti na vuttaṃ

ti pākāraparikkhepena bhūmiyā vaḍḍhanena rājagahasāvatthiyo viya ti ettha saddo kāraṇavacanoti āha ti ayaṃ kāyo aviññāṇakopi saviññāṇakopi evampi asubho evampi asubhoti nānāvidhehi kāraṇehīti attho nti kesādivasena tatthāpi vaṇṇādito asubhākārassa sabbaso dassanavasena pavattaṃ nti attano parassa ca karajakāye vicchandanuppādanakathaṃ tiādinā byatirekamukhena kāyassa amanuññataṃ dasseti tattha ādito tīhi padehi adassanīyatāya asārakatāya ca majjhe catūhi duggandhatāya ante ekena lesamattenapi manuññatābhāvamassa dasseti tiādinā anvayato sarūpeneva amanuññatāya dassanaṃ ti saṅkhepato vuttamatthaṃ vibhāgena dassetuṃ tiādi vuttaṃ
ti vitthārento ti asubhamātikāya nti bahulīkāro kilesacorehi anabhibhavanīyattā jhānaṃ nti vuttaṃ ti pādakaṃ katvā

ti ettha kāraṇaṃ nāma kāyassa asuciduggandhajegucchapaṭikūlatāva ti puthujjanā nāma sāvajjepi tattha anavajjasaññino hutvā karaṇakārāpanasamanuññatābhedaṃ  sabbaṃ pāpaṃ akaṃsu  kāmaṃ dasānussatiggahaṇeneva ānāpānassati gahitā sā pana tattha sannipatitabhikkhūsu bahūnaṃ sappāyā sātthikā ca tasmā puna gahitā tathā hi bhagavā tameva kammaṭṭhānaṃ imasmiṃ sutte kathesi āhāre paṭikūlasaññā asubhakammaṭṭhānasadisā cattāro pana āruppā ādikammikānaṃ ayogyāti tesaṃ idha aggahaṇaṃ daṭṭhabbaṃ
ti vesālīnagaraṃ gocaragāmaṃ katvā ti satthari saddhamme ca gāravena upagatabhikkhūnaṃ vacanasamanantarameva uṭṭhahiṃsūti katvā vuttaṃ buddhakāle kira bhikkhū bhagavato sandesaṃ sirasā sampaṭicchituṃ ohitasotā viharanti
ti assāsapassāse pariggaṇhanavasena pavattāya satiyā ti tāya sampayuttaaññamaññapaccayabhūtāya uppanno samādhi ti iminā upanissayapaccayasabhāvampi dasseti ubhayatthāpi sahajātādīnaṃ sattannampi paccayānaṃ vasena paccayabhāvaṃ dasseti yathāpaṭipannā me sāvakā cattāro satipaṭṭhāne bhāventītiādīsu uppādanavaḍḍhanaṭṭhena bhāvanāti vuccatīti tadubhayavasena atthaṃ dassento ti āha tattha bhāvaṃ vijjamānataṃ ito gatoti  uppādito paṭiladdhamattoti attho uppanno pana laddhāsevano  paguṇabhāvaṃ āpādito vaḍḍhitoti attho ti bahulaṃ pavattito tena āvajjanādivasībhāvappattimāha yo hi vasībhāvamāpādito so icchiticchitakkhaṇe samāpajjitabbato punappunaṃ pavattissati tena vuttaṃ ti yathā idheva bhikkhave samaṇo  vivicceva kāmehīti  ca evamādīsu paṭhamapade vutto eva-saddo dutiyādīsupi vuttoyeva hoti evamidhāpīti āha ti ubhayattha niyamena laddhaguṇaṃ dassetuṃ tiādi vuttaṃ
nti asubhārammaṇaṃ jhānamāha tañhi asubhesu yogakammabhāvato yogino sukhavisesānaṃ kāraṇabhāvato ca nti vuccati nti iminā ārammaṇaṃ nivatteti ti  jhānapaṭivedhavasena jhānañhi bhāvanāvisesena ijjhantaṃ attano visayaṃ paṭivijjhantameva pavattati yathāsabhāvato paṭivijjhiyati cāti paṭivedhoti vuccati ti  bībhacchārammaṇattā ti jigucchitabbārammaṇattā ti kāraṇena lesantarena vā ti anukkamena vicitabbataṃ pattārammaṇassa paramasukhumataṃ sandhāyāha santehi sannisinne ārammaṇe pavattamāno dhammo sayampi sannisinnova hoti tenāha ti nibbutasabbapariḷāhoti attho ārammaṇasantatāya tadārammaṇānaṃ dhammānaṃ  lokuttaradhammārammaṇāhi paccavekkhaṇāhi veditabbā
nāssa santapaṇītabhāvāvahaṃ kiñci secananti  asecanakattā  anāsittakattā eva  asammisso parikammādinā tato eva  visuṃyeveko  asādhāraṇo sabbametaṃ sarasato eva santabhāvaṃ dassetuṃ vuttaṃ parikammaṃ vā santabhāvanimittaṃ nti ca kasiṇakaraṇādinimittuppādapariyosānaṃ tādisaṃ ettha natthīti adhippāyo tadā hi kammaṭṭhānaṃ nirassādattā asantaṃ appaṇītaṃ siyā upacāre vā natthi ettha santatāti yojanā yathā upacārakkhaṇe nīvaraṇādivigamena aṅgapātubhāvena ca paresaṃ santatā hoti na evamimassa ayaṃ pana ādisamannā paṇīto cāti yojanā ti uttaravihāravāsino ti upasecanena anāsittako tenāha  ti ojavantasadisoti attho ti iṭṭho cetasikasukhapaṭilābhasaṃvattanaṃ tikacatukkajjhānavasena upekkhāya vā santabhāvena sukhagatikattā sabbesampi jhānānaṃ vasena veditabbaṃ jhānasamuṭṭhānapaṇītarūpaphuṭṭhasarīratāvasena pana kāyikasukhapaṭilābhasaṃvattanaṃ daṭṭhabbaṃ tañca kho jhānato vuṭṭhitakāle imasmiṃ pakkhe ti idaṃ hetumhi bhummavacanaṃ daṭṭhabbaṃ
ti jhānena sakasantānato anīhate appahīne ti akosallaṃ vuccati avijjā tato sambhūte avijjāpubbaṅgamā hi sabbe pāpadhammā ti attano pavattikkhaṇeneva ti ettha antaradhāpanaṃ vināsanaṃ taṃ pana jhānakattukaṃ  idhādhippetanti pariyuṭṭhānappahānaṃ hotīti āha  ti ti visesena upasameti visesena upasamanaṃ pana sammadeva upasamanaṃ hotīti āha ti sāsanikassa jhānabhāvanā yebhuyyena nibbedhabhāgiyā hotīti āha ti ariyamaggassa pādakabhūto ayaṃ samādhi anukkamena vaḍḍhitvā ariyamaggabhāvaṃ upagato viya hotīti āha ti ayaṃ panattho virāganirodhapaṭinissaggānupassanānaṃ vasena sammadeva yujjati sesaṃ suviññeyyameva

ti  ānandatthero ti kathaṃ vibhāvito nu kho kimilātiādinā pavattā desanā nti rūpakāye aññatarakoṭṭhāse nti yathā ānāpānaṃ anussaranto bhikkhu kāye kāyānupassī jāto evaṃ sabbattha vāresu vedanānupassītiādi attho veditabbo
nti desanāpadesaṃ manasikārapadesena vedanā vuttāti taṃ sabbaṃ sarūpato aññāpadesato apadisati tiādinā tattha nidassanaṃ dasseti ti cittasaṅkhārapaṭisaṃvedī passambhayaṃ cittasaṅkhāranti etasmiṃ padadvaye
ti pītimanasikāracittasaṅkhārapadesena yadi vedanā vuttā evaṃ santepi  yathāvuttā  vedanārammaṇā ca anupassanā  yadi evaṃ mahāsatipaṭṭhānādīsu vedanā vediyatīti vuttaṃ taṃ kathanti āha  mahāsatipaṭṭhānādīsupītiādi tattha nti sukhādīnaṃ uppattiyā vatthubhūtaṃ rūpasaddādiṃ ārammaṇaṃ katvā vedanā vediyati na puggalo puggalasseva abhāvato  nissāya yathā puggalo vedanaṃ vediyatīti  evaṃ idhāpi vedanāya assāsapassāse ārabbha pavatti tathā pavattamanasikārasīsena vedanāsu vedanānupassī bhikkhu tasmiṃ samaye viharatīti vutto ti taṃ vedanāya ārammaṇabhāvaṃ sandhāya   padānaṃ  vedanānupassanā na yujjatīti vuttaanuyogassa
ti pītisahagatāni paṭhamadutiyajjhānāni paṭipāṭiyā samāpajjati ti tena paṭisaṃviditasaddāpekkhāya hi kattuatthe etaṃ sāmivacanaṃ ti samāpajjanakkhaṇe ti jhānena samaṅgībhāvena ti ārammaṇamukhena tadārammaṇajhānapariyāpannā  ārammaṇassa paṭisaṃviditattāti vuttaṃ hoti yathā nāma sappapariyesanaṃ carantena tassa āsaye paṭisaṃvidite sopi paṭisaṃvidito hoti mantāgadabalena tassa gahaṇassa sukarattā evaṃ pītiyā āsayabhūte ārammaṇe paṭisaṃvidite sā pīti paṭisaṃviditāva hoti salakkhaṇato sāmaññalakkhaṇato ca tassā gahaṇassa sukarattā ti vipassanāpaññāya tikkhavisadabhāvappattāya  visayato dassanakkhaṇe ti pītiyā salakkhaṇassa sāmaññalakkhaṇassa ca paṭivijjhanena yañhi pītiyā visesato sāmaññato ca lakkhaṇaṃ tasmiṃ vidite sā yāthāvato viditā eva hoti tenāha ti
