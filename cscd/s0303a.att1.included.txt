
1 paṭhamavaggo

 rādhasaṃyuttassa  paṭhame ti maraṇaṃ vā bhaveyya ti māretabbo vā ti yo vā pana marati nti nibbidāñāṇatthaṃ ti phalavimutti nāmesā anupādānibbānatthāti attho ti  atikkantosi nti nibbāne patiṭṭhitaṃ idaṃ maggabrahmacariyaṃ nāma nibbānabbhantare vussati na nibbānaṃ atikkamitvāti attho nti nibbānaṃ assa pariyosānaṃ nipphatti niṭṭhāti attho paṭhamaṃ

 dutiye ti laggapucchā ti tatra laggo tatra vilaggo ti paṃsugharakehi ti kīḷanti ti dhanaṃ viya maññanti ti mama idaṃ mama idanti mamattaṃ karonti aññassa phusitumpi na denti ti niṭṭhitā kīḷāti te bhindamānā kīḷāvigamaṃ karonti tatiye ti bhavarajju catutthaṃ uttānameva pañcamādīsu catūsu cattāri saccāni kathitāni dvīsu kilesappahānanti dutiyādīni

2 dutiyavaggo

 dutiyavaggassa  paṭhame ti maraṇaṃ pucchati yasmā pana rūpādivinimuttaṃ maraṇaṃ nāma natthi tenassa bhagavā tiādimāha  dutiye ti maraṇadhammo etenupāyena sabbattha attho veditabboti

3-4 āyācanavaggādi

 tato  paraṃ uttānatthameva ayañhi rādhatthero paṭibhāniyatthero nāma tathāgatassa imaṃ theraṃ disvā sukhumaṃ kāraṇaṃ upaṭṭhāti tenassa bhagavā nānānayehi dhammaṃ deseti evaṃ imasmiṃ rādhasaṃyutte ādito dve vaggā pucchāvasena desitā tatiyo āyācanena catuttho upanisinnakakathāvasena sakalampi panetaṃ rādhasaṃyuttaṃ therassa vimuttiparipācanīyadhammavaseneva gahitanti veditabbaṃ
