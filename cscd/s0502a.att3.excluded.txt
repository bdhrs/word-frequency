3. Cittavaggo
1. Meghiyattheravatthu
Phandanaṃ    capalaṃ citta
meghiyasuttantaṃ
33 .


34 .


 phandana Capala Citta Dūrakkha Dunnivāraya Usukārova tejana   
Vārijovā thale khitto Okamokataubbhato okamokato Ubbhato Paripphandatidaṃ citta māradheyyaṃ pahātave,

Meghiyattheravatthu paṭhamaṃ.
2. Aññatarabhikkhuvatthu
Dunniggahassa  lahuno
 

  

 
   
35 .


dunniggahassa lahuno. Yatthakāmanipātino  cittassa damatho sādhu cittaṃ dantaṃ sukhāvahaṃ

 
 
Aññatarabhikkhuvatthu dutiyaṃ.
3. Aññataraukkaṇṭhitabhikkhuvatthu
Sududdasa
   

36 .


 sududdasa  Sunipuṇa Yatthakāmanipātina Cittaṃ rakkhetha medhāvī cittaṃ guttaṃ sukhāvahaṃ

Aññataraukkaṇṭhitabhikkhuvatthu tatiyaṃ.
4. Saṅgharakkhitabhāgineyyattheravatthu
Dūraṅgama
  


 
 
37 .


dūraṅgama ekacaraṃ asarīraṃ guhāsayaṃ  Ye citta cittaṃ saṃyamessanti Mokkhanti mārabandhanā 

Saṅgharakkhitabhāgineyyattheravatthu catutthaṃ.
5. Cittahatthattheravatthu
Anavaṭṭhitacittassā 

 cittahatthatthero
 
 

38 .


39 .


 anavaṭṭhitacittassā  Saddhammaṃ avijānato pariplavapasādassa paññā na paripūrati Anavassutacittassā Ananvāhatacetaso  Puññapāpapahīnassā Natthi jāgarato bhaya 

 
 



  

Cittahatthattheravatthu pañcamaṃ.
6. Pañcasatabhikkhuvatthu
Kumbhūpama
  




mettasuttaṃ   
40 .


kumbhūpama  Nagarūpamaṃ cittamidaṃ ṭhapetvā Jitañca rakkhe
Anivesano  siyā  

Pañcasatabhikkhuvatthu chaṭṭhaṃ.
7. Pūtigattatissattheravatthu
Aciraṃ  vatayaṃ kāyo
Pūtigattatissatthero 
 
41 .


aciraṃ vatā ayaṃ kāyo pathaviṃ adhisessati,  Chuddho  Niratthaṃva kaliṅgaraṃ

 
Pūtigattatissattheravatthu sattamaṃ.
8. Nandagopālakavatthu
Diso disa
   
42 .


diso disa Yaṃ taṃ kayirā veri vā pana micchāpahiṇitaṃ cittaṃ pāpiyo naṃ tato kare 

Nandagopālakavatthu aṭṭhamaṃ.
9. Soreyyattheravatthu
Na  taṃ mātā pitā kayirā

  

   

 soreyyatthero  

43 .


na ta Sammāpaṇihita Seyyaso  naṃ tato kare

Soreyyattheravatthu navamaṃ.
Cittavaggavaṇṇanā niṭṭhitā.
Tatiyo vaggo.
