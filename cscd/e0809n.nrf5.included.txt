


 abyayaṃ vuccate dāni cirassaṃ tu ciraṃ tathā
cirena cirarattāya saha saddhiṃ samaṃ amā

 punappunaṃ  abhiṇhañcā sakiṃ cābhikkhaṇaṃ muhuṃ
 tu vinā nānā antarena rite puthu

 balavaṃ suṭṭhu cātīvā  kimuta svati
aho tu kiṃ kimū dāhu vikappe kimutoda ca

 bho are ambho
hambho re jeṅga āvuso
he hare tha kathaṃ kiṃsu nanu kacci nu kiṃ samā

 adhunetarahīdāni sampati aññadatthu tu
tagghe  sasakkañcā ddhā kāmaṃ jātu ve have

 yāvatā tāvatā yāva tāva kittāvatā tathā
ettāvatā ca kīveti 

 yathā tathā yathevevaṃ yathānāma yathāhi ca
seyyathāpyevamevaṃ vā tatheva ca yathāpi ca

 ca seyyathāpi nāma yatharivāpi ca
 yathāca viya tatharivāpi ca

 saṃ sāmañca sayaṃ cātha āma sāhu lahūpi ca
opāyikaṃ patirūpaṃ sādhvevaṃ 

 yaṃ taṃ yato tato yena
teneti kāraṇe siyuṃ
asākalye tu  nipphale tu  bhave

 kadāci jātu tulyātha sabbato ca samantato
parito ca samantāpi atha micchā musā bhave

 na anomālaṃ nahi cetu sace yadi
anukulye tu ñca
nattaṃ  doso  tvahe

 īsaṃ kiñci manaṃ  tu atakkite
pure  tu bhavantare

 vimhaye 
tu mone thāvi pātu ca
taṅkhaṇe sajju sapadi balakkāre  ca

 sudaṃ kho  assu yagghe vehādayo 
antarenantarā anto  ca nicchaye

 ānande ñca  tha virodhakathane 
kāmappavedane  usūyopagame ca

vadhāraṇe ñeyyaṃ yathāttaṃ tu yathātathaṃ
 appe mahatyu atha pāto page samā

 nicce 
bāhulye bāhiraṃ bahi
bahiddhā bāhirā bāhye sīghetu  bhave

 adassane  nindāyaṃ vandane 
 pasaṃsāyaṃ atho sattāya  ca

 sāye atrāhe
 tu  anāgate
tato pare tu divase gate

 yattha yatra yahiṃcātha tattha tatra tahiṃtahaṃ
atho uddhañca upari heṭṭhā tu ca adho samā

 codane tha ārādūrā ca ārakā
parammukhā tu ca raho sammukhā tvāvi pātu ca

 appeva appevanāma nūti ca
 ititthañca evaṃ kicche  ca

 khede  paccakkhe
 thirevadhāraṇe
tiro tu tiriyaṃ cātha kucchāyaṃ ccate

 āsiṭṭhatthamhi nindāyaṃ tu  kathyate
kuhiñcanaṃ kuhiṃ kutra kuttha kattha kahaṃ kva tha

 ihedhātra  tu etthāttha
atha sabbatra sabbadhi
kadā kudācanaṃ cātha tadāni ca tadā samā

 ādikamme bhusatthe ca sambhavotiṇṇa tittisu
niyogissariyappīti dāna pūjāgga santisu

 dassane tappare saṅge pasaṃsā gati suddhisu
hiṃsā pakārantobhāva viyogāvayavesu ca
pasaggo disāyoge patthanā dhitiādisu

saddo parihāni parājaya gatīsu ca
bhusatthe paṭilomatthe vikkamāmasanādisu

 nissesābhāva sanyāsa bhusattha mokkha rāsisu
gehādesopamāhīna pasādaniggatāccaye

 dassanosānanikkhantā dhobhāgesvavadhāraṇe
sāmīpye bandhane cheka ntobhāgoparatīsu ca

 pātubhāve viyoge ca nisedhādo  dissati
atho nīharaṇe cevā varaṇādo ca  siyā

 uddhakamma viyoga tta lābha titti samiddhisu
pātubhāvaccayābhāva pabalatte pakāsane
dakkhaggatāsu kathane sattimokkhādike  ca

 kucchitesadatthesu virūpatte pyasobhane
siyābhāvāsamiddhīsu kicche cānandanādike

 samodhāna saṅkhepa samantatta samiddhisu
sammā bhusa saha ppatthā bhimukhatthesu saṅgate
vidhāne pabhave pūjā punappunakriyādisu

 vividhātisayābhāva bhusattissariyāccaye
viyoge kalahe pātu bhāve bhāse ca kucchane

 dūrānabhimukhattesu mohānavaṭṭhitīsu ca
padhāna dakkhatā kheda sahatthādo  dissati

 viyoge  jānane cādhobhāga nicchaya  suddhisu
īsadatthe paribhave desa byāpana hānisu
vacokriyāya theyye ca ñāṇappattādike 

 pacchā bhusatta sādisyā nupacchinnānuvattisu
hīne ca tatiyatthādho bhāgesvanugate hite
dese lakkhaṇa vicchettha mbhūta bhāgādike 

 samantatthe paricchede pūjāliṅgana vajjane
dosakkhāne nivāsanā vaññādhāresu bhojane
soka byāpana tatvesu lakkhaṇādo siyā 

 ābhimukhya visiṭṭhuddha kammasāruppavuddhisu
pūjādhika kulāsacca lakkhaṇādimhi cāpya

 adhikissara pāṭhādhi ṭṭhāna pāpuṇanesva
nicchaye coparittādhi bhavane ca visesane

 paṭidānanisedhesu vāmādānanivattisu
sādise paṭinidhimhi ābhimukhyagatīsu ca

 patibodhe patigate tathā punakriyāya ca
sambhāvane paṭiccatthe ti lakkhaṇādike
 sobhane sukhe sammā bhusa suṭṭhu samiddhisu

 ābhimukhya samīpādi kammāliṅgana pattisu
mariyāduddhakammicchā bandhanābhividhīsu 

 nivāsāvhāna gahaṇa kicchesattha nivattisu
appasādāsi saraṇa patiṭṭhāvimhayādisu

 antobhāva bhusattāti saya pūjāsvatikkame
bhūtabhāve pasaṃsāyaṃ daḷhatthādo siyā 

 sambhāvane ca garahā pekkhāsu ca samuccaye
pañhe saṃvaraṇe ceva āsīsatthe ritaṃ

 niddese vajjane pūjā pagate vāraṇepi ca
padussane ca garahā corikādo siyā 

 samīpapūjā  sādissa dosakkhānopapattisu
bhusattopagamādhikya pubbakammanivattisu
gayhākāroparittesu tya nasanādike

 nidassanākāro pamāsu sampahaṃsane
upadese ca vacana paṭiggāhevadhāraṇe
garahāyedamatthe ca parimāne ca pucchane

 samuccaye samāhāre nvācaye cetarītare
padapūraṇamatte ca saddo avadhāraṇe

 hetupakāresu ādimhi cāvadhāraṇe
nidassane padatthassa vipallāse samāpane

 samuccaye copamāyaṃ saṃsaye padapūraṇe
vavatthitavibhāsāyaṃ vassagge vikappane

 bhūsane vāraṇe cā vuccate pariyattiyaṃ
nantarārambha pañhesu padapūraṇe

 pasaṃsāgarahāsaññā sīkārādo  pi  tha
nicchaye cānumānasmiṃ siyā  vitakkane

 pucchāvadhāraṇānuññā sāntvanālapane 
kaṃsa dayā hāsa khedālapana vimhaye

 vākyārambha visādesu  hāsenukampane
 tu  sākalya mānāvadhyavadhāraṇe

 pācī pura ṅgatotthesu  paṭhame pyatha
pabandhe ca cirātīte nikaṭāgāmike

sanne tu -
bhimukhobhayatodike

 yadyapisaddatthe ekaṃsatthe ca dissati
atho  visesasmiṃ tatheva padapūraṇe

 hetuvajje tatthātha  pāpesatthakucchane

 saṃsaye ca pañhe tha  nekattha vajjane
 tu pucchājigucchāsu  tu vārimhi muddhani

 sahasamīpe tha bhede appaṭhame 
nussavārucisu pyatthe vikappane

 patīcī carime  tvaddhe jigucchane
pakāse sambhave 
aññoññe tu raho 

 khedasokadukkhesu khede tva vimhaye
bhiṃsāpane  nindāyaṃ pidhāne tiriyaṃ
