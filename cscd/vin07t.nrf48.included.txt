

ti pattacīvarādiatthāya alaṃ bhaṇḍanādikaraṇanti vivādādīsu dosadassanamattena saññāpetvā aññamaññaṃ khamāpetvā vūpasamenti na pana aññamaññaṃ āpattānāpattidassanavasenāti adhippāyo tenāha ti
visamāni kāyakammādīni nissito bhikkhu  nāma micchādiṭṭhinissito  balavante purise nissito  nāmāti dassento tiādimāha


ti paresaṃ attano ca āpattiṃ paṭicchādentānaṃ vajjapaṭicchādīnaṃ pārājikādiāpattimeva sandhāya vuttaṃ na sabbāpattiyo nti ukkhepanīyādikammaṃ nissāya uppajjanakānaṃ tadanuvattikāya bhikkhuniyā yāvatatiyānussāvanānaṃ kiccānaṃ vasena na sabbesaṃ kiccānaṃ vasenāti
 pāḷiyaṃ katihi adhikaraṇehīti pucchāya ekena adhikaraṇena kiccādhikaraṇenāti vuttaṃ katisu ṭhānesūti pucchāya tīsu ṭhānesu saṅghamajjhe gaṇamajjhe puggalassa santiketi vuttaṃ katihi samathehīti pucchāya tīhi samathehīti vuttaṃ tīhipi etehi eko vūpasamanappakārova pucchito vissajjito cāti veditabbo
ntiādīsu vivādādhikaraṇameva anuvādādhikaraṇādipi hotīti pucchāya vivādādhikaraṇaṃ vivādādhikaraṇameva hoti anuvādādayo na hotīti vissajjanaṃ


 adhikaraṇapucchāvissajjane  pāḷiyaṃ ti virūpavipākāya aññamaññaṃ dukkhuppādanāya kāyavacīvohāro nti vuddhippatto kalaho
ti vipatticodanāya anu anu saṃyujjanavasena ninnatā
ti ativiya sañjātussāhatā nti codakānampi upatthambhakaraṇaṃ ti saṅghasammukhatādimattassa sammukhāvinayassa saṅghādīhi kattabbakiccesu saṅgahābhāvā




 dutiyagāthāsaṅgaṇikāya  codanā kimatthāyātiādikā pucchā upālittherena katā codanā sāraṇatthāyātiādivissajjanaṃ bhagavatā vuttaṃ upālitthero sayameva pucchitvā vissajjanaṃ akāsītipi vadanti
nti tesaṃ vicāraṇāgahaṇaṃ suttantikattherānaṃ vinayadharattherānañca adhippāyagahaṇanti  attho nti tesaṃ paccekaṃ adhippāyaṃ ñatvā tehi samuṭṭhāpitanayampi gahetvā vinicchayapariyosāpanatthanti adhippāyo
tiādinā abhimukhe ṭhitaṃ kañci anuvijjakaṃ ovadantena viya therena anuvijjakavattaṃ kathitaṃ
nti anuyujjanakkamaṃ taṃ pana yasmā sabbasikkhāpadavītikkamavisayepi taṃtaṃsikkhāpadānulomena kattabbaṃ tasmā sikkhāpadānulomikanti vuttaṃ ti attano sugatigamanaṃ vināseti
anusandhita-saddo bhāvasādhanoti āha ti ti etthāpi eseva nayo ti ācārānusandhinā ācārena saddhiṃ samentiyā paṭiññāyāti attho tenāha tiādi
pāḷiyaṃ ntiādi alajjilajjilakkhaṇaṃ bhikkhubhikkhunīnaṃ vasena vuttaṃ tesaññeva sabbappakārato sikkhāpadādhikārattā sāmaṇerādīnampi sādhāraṇavasena pana sañcicca yathāsakaṃ sikkhāpadavītikkamanādikaṃ alajjilajjilakkhaṇaṃ veditabbaṃ
nti cuditakaanuvijjakānaṃ kathāya anusandhiyuttaṃ vacanaṃ na jānāti tehi ekasmiṃ kāraṇe vutte sayaṃ taṃ asallakkhetvā attano abhirucitameva asambandhitatthanti attho ti anuvijjakena katassa āpattānāpattivinicchayassa anuguṇaṃ sambandhavacanañca
