

 ariyamaggañca  vo bhikkhave desessāmi anariyamaggañca taṃ suṇātha katamo ca bhikkhave anariyo maggo pāṇātipāto micchādiṭṭhi  ayaṃ vuccati bhikkhave anariyo maggo
katamo ca  bhikkhave ariyo maggo pāṇātipātā veramaṇī sammādiṭṭhi  ayaṃ vuccati bhikkhave ariyo maggoti paṭhamaṃ

 kaṇhamaggañca vo bhikkhave desessāmi sukkamaggañca taṃ suṇātha katamo ca bhikkhave kaṇho maggo pāṇātipāto micchādiṭṭhi  ayaṃ vuccati bhikkhave kaṇho maggo
katamo ca bhikkhave sukko maggo pāṇātipātā veramaṇī sammādiṭṭhi  ayaṃ vuccati bhikkhave sukko maggoti dutiyaṃ
