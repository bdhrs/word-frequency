

bhikkhunīnaṃ  saṅghādisesaṃ patvā vuṭṭhānavidhayo sandassanatthaṃ ti puggalaniyamaṃ katvā pārājikato adhippāyanti nti āpattināmaggahaṇañca kataṃ ti pāṭho


katthaci agantvā nisinnaṭṭhāne eva nisīditvā karontiyā  khaṇḍasīmādigatāya 


ti vuttattā ābhoge sati anāpatti imasmiṃ pana sikkhāpade  upacārātikkame āpatti vuttā idha okkame dvīsupi vuttaṃ atthato ekameva gāmantaragamanasaṅghādisesaṃ upacārassa sandhāya vuttattā gaṇamhā ohīyanassa virodho araññeti idaṃ atthavasena vuttaṃ gāmantarepi hoti eva
ti gāthāya vasena aṭṭhakathāyampi gāmantarapariyāpannaṃ nadipāranti vuttaṃ

ti idaṃ 
āpajjati garukaṃ sāvasesaṃ
chādeti anādariyaṃ paṭicca
na bhikkhunī no ca phuseyya vajjaṃ
