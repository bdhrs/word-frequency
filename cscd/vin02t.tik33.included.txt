

ti  sabbaso parisuddhakhīṇāsavaparivārassa ti sīlasamādhipaññāvimuttivimuttiñāṇadassanasaṅkhātadhammakkhandhasarīrassa sāsaneti sambandho ti parivāroti yo saṅgahaṃ āruḷho tassa nti pubbe āgataṃ vinicchayaṃ
 pakatatthapaṭiniddeso tasaddoti tassa bhagavatātiādīhi padehi samānādhikaraṇabhāvena vuttatthassa yāya vinayapaññattiyā bhagavā pakato adhikato supākaṭo ca taṃ vinayapaññattiṃ  saddhiṃ yācanāya atthabhāvena dassento ti āha tattha nti vinayabhūtaṃ paññattiṃ
jānatā passatāti imesaṃ padānaṃ vinayassa adhikatattā tattha vuttanayena tāva atthaṃ yojetvā idāni suttantanayena dassento satipi ñāṇadassanasaddānaṃ paññāvevacanabhāve tena tena visesena tesaṃ visayavisesapavattidassanatthaṃ vijjattayavasena abhiññānāvaraṇañāṇavasena sabbaññutaññāṇamaṃsacakkhuvasena paṭivedhadesanāñāṇavasena ca atthaṃ yojetvā dassento tiādimāha tattha ti pubbenivāsāsavakkhayañāṇehi ti ariyamaggapaññāya ti desetabbadhammānaṃ desetabbappakāraṃ bodhaneyyapuggalānañca āsayānusayacaritādhimuttiādibhedaṃ dhammaṃ desanāpaññāya yāthāvato passatā ti arīnaṃ arānañca hatattā paccayādīnañca arahattā arahatā ti sammā sāmañca saccānaṃ buddhattā sammāsambuddhena atha vā antarāyikadhamme  niyyānikadhamme  kilesārīnaṃ hatattā  sammā sāmaṃ sabbadhammānaṃ buddhattā ti evaṃ catuvesārajjavasenapettha yojanā veditabbā
apica ṭhānāṭṭhānādivibhāgaṃ  yathākammūpage satte  savāsanaāsavānaṃ chinnattā  abhiññeyyādibhede dhamme abhiññeyyādito aviparītāvabodhato  atha vā tīsu kālesu appaṭihatañāṇatāya  kāyakammādivasena tiṇṇampi kammānaṃ ñāṇānuparivattito sammā kāritāya  davādīnaṃ abhāvasādhikāya pahānasampadāya  chandādīnaṃ ahānihetubhūtāya akkhayapaṭibhānasādhikāya sabbaññutāya ti evaṃ dasabalaaṭṭhārasāveṇikabuddhadhammavasenapi yojanā kātabbā
ti pucchāya vissajjane ti etasmiṃ pucchāvissajjane ti tasmiṃyeva dese yathāvuttavatthuvītikkame āpattisambhavato ti vinītavatthukathā ayameva vā pāṭho
