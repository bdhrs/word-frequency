


ti  kakkhaḷo aniṭṭhassa padānato ti catunnaṃ paccayānaṃ paṭilābho ti tehiyeva paccayehi susaṅkhatehi pūjanā so pana atthato sampattiyevāti āha ti ti guṇakittanā antarāyassa anativattanato  anatthāvahattā


 baḷisena carati tena vā jīvatīti  tenāha ti nti āmisūpagataṃ āmisapatitaṃ tenāha nti bhinnādhikaraṇānampi bāhiratthasamāso hotevāti āha nti ayo vuccati sukhaṃ tabbidhuratāya  dukkhanti āha ti ti etena kattuatthe hi etaṃ sāmivacanaṃ yathā kilesā vattanti evaṃ pavattamāno puggalo kilesavippayogo na hotīti vuttaṃ ti


 aṭṭhikacchapā vuccanti yesaṃ kapālamatthake tikhiṇā aṭṭhikoṭi hoti tesaṃ samūho  macchakacchapādīnaṃ sarīre lambantī papatatīti  vuccamānākāro  ayakaṇṭako ti  ayomayakosake ti attanikāpanasallasaṇṭhāno ti ayomayavaṅkakaṇṭako  ettha athāvarato pavesitamatto hi so  amhākanti na vattabbo ti catutthasuttamāha


ti evaṃ itthiliṅgavasena vuccamānā ti gūthabhakkhapāṇakā ti kucchiyaṃ


 ime lābhasakkāraṃ anāharantā jighacchādidukkhaṃ pāpuṇantūti evaṃ na sattānaṃ dukkhakāmatāya evamāhāti ānetvā sambandho  aparāparaṃ uppajjanakaakusalacittānaṃ bahubhāvato


 acchavisayuttāti vā diddhe gatena  tenāha ti


tveva  vuccati sarīrasobhāya abhāvato sarīrassa uggatakaṇṭakattā ti phalati bhijjati


 chabbīsatiyā sāruppānaṃ pariccajanato  rāgasāmantā ca kodhasāmantā ca yāva nicchāraṇato


ti ettha nti asakkārena ca ubhayañca kadāci sakkārena kadāci asakkārena kadāci ubhayenāti attho tenāha tiādi satatavihārānaṃ sampattiyā ti āha tiādi tathā hi sā vajirūpamañāṇanti vuccati ti phalasamāpattiṃ samāpajjituṃ tassā pubbaparikammaṃ upagatattā




 sīlapabbatasannissitattā  pageva sīlassa chaḍḍitattā tatiyādīsu apubbaṃ natthi




 yaṃ  visabhāgavatthu purisassa cittaṃ pariyādāya ṭhātuṃ sakkotīti vuccati tato visesato lābhasakkārova sattānaṃ cittaṃ pariyādāya ṭhātuṃ sakkotīti dassento bhagavā na tassa bhikkhavetiādimavocāti dassento tiādimāha


 tasseva atthassa kevalaṃ janapadakalyāṇīvasena vuttaṃ


ti ariyamaggena āgatasaddhā adhippetāti āha ti


ti saddena bāhusaccasaṃvarasīlādīnaṃ saṅgaho daṭṭhabbo  uppattikāraṇaṃ samudayasaccavasena dukkhasaccassa uppattihetutāvasena


ti idaṃ tannissayaṃ kilesagaṇaṃ sandhāyāha ti nibbattāpento  manussa manussattaṃ puna nibbattituṃ appadānavasena ti duggatinibbattāpanato idha maraṇadukkhāvahanato ca


 chaviādīni chindane samatthā


ti diṭṭhadhammasukhavihārassa okāsābhāvaṃ sandhāya




tiādi parikappavacanaṃ na hi paccekabodhiyaṃ niyatagatiko antarā maggaphalāni adhigantuṃ bhabboti ti anavajjadhammo ti devadattassa  katūpacitassa mahato pāpadhammassa balena tasmiṃ attabhāve samucchedabhāvato na accantāya akusalaṃ nāmetaṃ abalaṃ kusalaṃ viya na mahābalaṃ tasmā tasmiṃyeva attabhāve tādisānaṃ puggalānaṃ atekicchatā aññathā sammattaniyāmo viya micchattaniyāmo accantiko  siyā yadi evaṃ vaṭṭakhāṇukajotanā kathanti āsevanāvasena tasmā  yathā sakiṃ nimuggo nimuggo eva bāloti vuttaṃ evaṃ vaṭṭakhāṇukajotanā yādise hi paccaye paṭicca puggalo taṃ dassanaṃ gaṇhi tathā ca paṭipanno puna acintappativatte paccaye patitato sīsukkhipanamassa na hotīti na vattabbaṃ


ti gabbhassa paripākagatattā vijāyanakāle sampatte nti assatariyā puttaṃ nti gabbho assatariṃ yathāti etaṃ vacanaṃ


 abhiharīyatīti abhihāro bhattaṃyeva abhihāro ti āha nti nti vāḷamacchapittaṃ nti uragādinā osiñceyyuṃ


ti attano mātuyā uppannaanatthāvahassa pahānahetupi ti pitupi hetūti evamādīsu
