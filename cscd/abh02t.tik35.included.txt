

ti ettha khandhādīnaṃ kāmadhātuādidhātūsu sambhavabhedabhinnānaṃ niravasesato saṅgahitattāti vibhāgena yojanā tathā sesesupi pariyāpannapabhedabhinnānantiādinā tattha ti iminā sabbasaṅgāhikavāroti ayamassa atthānugatā samaññāti dasseti yasmā cettha khandhādīnaṃ dvādasannaṃ koṭṭhāsānaṃ anavasesasaṅgaho tasmā evaṃ dutiyavārādīnañcettha anuppaveso veditabbo khandhādīnameva hi tesaṃ sambhavādivicāro uppattānuppattidassanavāroti vattuṃ yuttoti yojanā anuppattidassanañcettha atthāpattisiddhaṃ veditabbaṃ na hi tattha kati khandhā na pātubhavantītiādipāḷi atthi
nti yenādhippāyena pucchā katā tadanurūpaṃ  nāma buddhānaṃ eva āveṇikaṃ aññesaṃ taṃ yādicchikaṃ sutakkharasadisanti āha ti


 kāmabhaveti  idaṃ okāsavasena vatvā puna sattasantānavasena vattuṃ kāmadhātusambhūtānañcāti vuttanti tamatthavisesaṃ dassento ti āha na vattabbaṃ siyāti kasmā vuttaṃ yadipi asaññasattānaṃ acakkhukattā rūpāyatanaṃ accantasukhumattā heṭṭhimabhūmikānañca agocaro samānabhūmikānaṃ pana vehapphalānaṃ uparibhūmikānañca suddhāvāsānaṃ cakkhāyatanassa gocaro hotīti āyatanādikiccaṃ karotiyevāti sakkā vattuṃ yaṃ panettha vitthārato vattabbaṃ taṃ heṭṭhā vuttameva heṭṭhato avīcinirayaṃ pariyantaṃ katvā uparito paranimmitavasavattideve  antokaritvā yaṃ etasmintiādinā  vuttapadesā   samudāyassa avayavādhārabhāvato yathā māsapuñjo māsoti sattā uppajjanti etthāti sattuppatti uppajjanaṭṭhena sattāva uppatti sattuppattīti evaṃ okāsasattalokadvayassa sattuppattipariyāyo veditabbo  na anupādinnakkhandhā viya saṅkhārabhāvenevāti adhippāyo ke pana teti āha ti nti taṃtaṃsattāvāsapariyāpannānaṃ sattānaṃ saṅkhārānameva vā ti sadisākārena pavattamānānaṃ khandhānaṃ patiṭṭhānabhāvena yebhuyyena hi tasmiṃ sattāvāse dhammā samānākārena pavattanti


ti tasmiṃ aññasmiñca bhave okāse ca paricchedakārikāya kāmāditaṇhāya paricchijja āpannā gahitāti pariyāpannāti taṃtaṃbhavādiantogadhā 



ti  kāmādidhātuttaye nibbattadevānaṃ vasena


 suparimajjitakañcanādāsaṃ  viya sobhati vijjotatīti subho sarīrobhāso tena subhena kiṇṇā vikiṇṇāti 
nti parittapathavīkasiṇādigatamanasikāraṃ ti saddena pubbabhāgaṃ sampiṇḍeti chandanaṃ ārammaṇapariyesanaṃ  kattukamyatāchando paṇidhānaṃ cittaṭṭhapanā  saññāvirāgādīhi ārammaṇassa visesanaṃ tathāpavattāya bhāvanāya ārammaṇakaraṇameva
vipulaṃ vuccati mahantaṃ santabhāvopi mahanīyatāya mahantamevāti āha ti
 yaṃ cātumahārājikānaṃ āyuppamāṇaṃ  eso eko rattidivo tāya rattiyā tiṃsa rattiyo māso tena māsena dvādasamāsiko saṃvaccharo tena saṃvaccharena pañca vassasatāni sañjīve āyuppamāṇaṃ yaṃ tāvatiṃsānaṃ āyuppamāṇaṃ eso  eko rattidivo tena saṃvaccharena vassasahassaṃ kāḷasutte āyuppamāṇaṃ yaṃ yāmānaṃ āyuppamāṇaṃ eso  eko rattidivo tena saṃvaccharena dve vassasahassāni saṅghāte  āyuppamāṇaṃ yaṃ tusitānaṃ āyuppamāṇaṃ  eso eko rattidivo tena saṃvaccharena cattāri vassasahassāni roruve āyuppamāṇaṃ yaṃ nimmānaratīnaṃ āyuppamāṇaṃ  eso eko rattidivo tena saṃvaccharena aṭṭha vassasahassāni mahāroruve āyuppamāṇaṃ yaṃ paranimmitavasavattīnaṃ devānaṃ āyuppamāṇaṃ  eso eko rattidivo tena saṃvaccharena soḷasa vassasahassāni tāpane āyuppamāṇaṃ  upaḍḍhantarakappo  eko antarakappo ca āyuppamāṇanti vadanti devānaṃ adhimuttakālakiriyā viya tādisena puññabalena antarāpi maraṇaṃ hotīti nti vuttanti veditabbaṃ evañca katvā abbudādiāyuparicchedopi yuttataro hotīti
nti aṭṭhasu jhānesu kataraṃ jhānaṃ ti bhavaggāni puthujjanabhavaggaṃ ariyabhavaggaṃ sabbabhavagganti vehapphalādīnaṃ samaññā ti puthujjanassa ti sotāpannassa sakadāgāmino ca ti anāgāmino vasena vuttaṃ tasmā so brahmakāyikādīhi cuto arūpaṃ upapajjanto veditabbo navasu brahmalokesu nibbattaariyasāvakānaṃ tatrūpapattiyeva hoti na heṭṭhūpapattīti  ayaṃ aṭṭhakathāpāṭhoti adhippāyena tiādi vuttaṃ tatrūpapattipi hoti uparūpapattipi na heṭṭhūpapattīti pana pāṭhoti tena heṭṭhūpapattiyeva nivāritātiādivacanena payojanaṃ natthi arūpadhātūpapatti ca na nivāritāti sambandho arūpadhātūpapatti na nivāritā matthake ṭhitova parinibbātīti niyamassa anicchitattā
ti kāmaloke ti rūpaloke ti paṭhamajjhānabhūmiyaṃ nibbatto parinibbātīti evaṃ pavattā aṭṭhakathā ti yasmā rūpadhātuyaṃ upapanno adhippeto na upapajjanāraho teneva kāraṇena ti yathāvuttassa rūpadhātuyaṃ upapannassa ariyasāvakassa yena rūparāgena tattha rūpabhave upapanno tasmiṃ arūpajjhānena vikkhambhite sammadeva diṭṭhādīnavesu yathā kāmarūpabhavesu āyatiṃ bhavābhilāso na bhavissati evaṃ arūpabhavepīti dassento āha ti ti rūpadhātuyaṃ upapanno ti heṭṭhimaṃ ariyamaggaṃ sampādetvā ti tasmiṃ rūpabhave nibbattopi tattheva ādīnavadassanavasena ti upari arūpabhave avissaṭṭhabhavapatthano yato arūpadhātuyaṃ upapajjanāraho ti tādisassa ariyasāvakassa  vasena kassaci pañca kassaci tayo anusayā anusentīti ayaṃ   


 ruppanalakkhaṇaṃ  rūpaṃ anubhavanalakkhaṇā vedanātiādinā  phusanalakkhaṇo phasso sātalakkhaṇaṃ sukhantiādinā 
cattāro khandhā siyā kusalātiādīsu idha yaṃ vattabbaṃ taṃ khandhavibhaṅgādīsu vuttaṃ tasmā tattha vuttanayeneva gahetabbanti adhippāyo sesaṃ yadettha na vuttaṃ taṃ suviññeyyamevāti
