 aṭṭhame nti ratanattayaguṇe yāthāvato ñatvā pasannānaṃ so panassa pasādo maggenāgatattā kenaci akampanīyo ti maggādhigameneva adhigatena avigatenāti vā pāṭho tassattho kadāci avigacchantenāti so appadhaṃsiyo ca hoti tasmā vuttaṃ  ti tattha ti pamukhaṃ katvā vacanatthato pana nāmakāyena desanāya sampaṭicchanavasena sakkhibhūtaṃ katvāti attho ti gharasandhicchedanena antopavisanamaggaṃ khanitvā nti dullabhasaddhammassavanaṃ ti avīcippavesanaṃ vadati

 navame vissāsakathaneneva nakulamātā nakulapitā ca satthuvissāsikā nāma jātāti vuttaṃ  nti ti gehasāminī ti upāsakapāḷiyaṃ nakulapitukathāyaṃ vuttanayameva

 dasame ti paccakkhato rūpadassanena satthu sammukhā dhammassavanena ca vinā kevalaṃ anussavaneneva parassa vacanaṃ anugatassavaneneva uppannena pasādena nti anussavato āgatappasādaṃ
