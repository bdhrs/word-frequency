

 dasamassa paṭhame ti majjhimadese nti manacchaṭṭhānaṃ dutiye ti sāsanasaddhamme niyato ti puthujjanehi asādhāraṇena

 sattame ti ettakāva saṅkhārā aniccā na ito pareti evaṃ sīmaṃ mariyādaṃ akatvā ti avatthitāya rahitā bhijjamānāva hutvā upaṭṭhahissantīti  attho ti sakale tedhātuke ti  samaṇabhāvena ariyamaggenāti attho

 aṭṭhame ti mettāyuttāya pāricariyāya satta hi sekhā tathāgataṃ mettāvatāya paricaranti khīṇāsavo pariciṇṇasatthuko

 navame ti tammayā vuccanti taṇhādiṭṭhiyo tāhi rahito ti ahaṃkāradiṭṭhi ti mamaṃkārataṇhā sesaṃ sabbattha uttānamevāti
