
ti omadditvā abhibhavitvā kārako ti sāmaggivināsāya anubalaṃ na dātabbaṃ ti na metaṃ khamatīti diṭṭhiṃ āvi katvāpi

 kāyappayogena  āpajjitabbā  vacīpayogena āpajjitabbā ti osāraṇādīsu navasu ṭhānesu ti ñattidutiyañatticatutthakammesu ti yasmā mahāaṭṭhakathāyaṃ vuttanayena ubhatovibhaṅgā asaṅgahitā tasmā yaṃ kurundiyaṃ vuttaṃ taṃ gahetabbanti sambandho

 catūhi  pañcahīti vacanato dvīhi vā tīhi vā ekato desetuṃ vaṭṭati tato paraṃ na vaṭṭati ti khaṇḍasīmāya ti mahāsīmāya

ti yassa kassaci jānantassa pariyāyena vuttamusāvādoti attho
ti tena vuttaṃ anādiyitvā tuṇhī bhavitabbanti attho

ti maggapaccavekkhaṇādivasena ekūnavīsatibhedāya

ti idaṃ atthanipphādanakāni tesaṃ pubbabhāgāni ca kāraṇabhāvasāmaññena ekajjhaṃ gahetvā vuttaṃ na pana sabbesaṃ pañcannaṃ samānayogakkhemattā ti bhedassa anurūpasāvanena yathā bhedo hoti evaṃ bhinditabbānaṃ bhikkhūnaṃ attano vacanassa sāvanena viññāpanenāti attho tenāha tiādi ti etena pākaṭaṃ katvā bhedakaravatthudīpanaṃ voharaṇaṃ tattha attanā vinicchitamattaṃ  rahassavasena viññāpanaṃ anussāvananti dasseti nti tehi saṅghabhedasiddhito pamāṇaṃ itare pana tesaṃ sambhārabhūtā tenāha tiādi ti voharaṇe

ti tañhi vandantassa mañcapādādīsupi nalāṭaṃ paṭihaññeyyātiādinā vuttakāraṇena
