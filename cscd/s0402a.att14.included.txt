
 pañcamassa  paṭhame ti āhārasappāyaṃ utusappāyaṃ pariggaṇhitvā samāpattisamāpajjane chekatā ti yathāparicchedena gate kāle viyatto hutvā uṭṭhahanto vuṭṭhānakusalo nāma hoti evaṃ kusalatā
 dutiye nti ujubhāvo nti mudubhāvo
 tatiye ti adhivāsanakhanti nti susīlyabhāvena suratabhāvo
 catutthe nti saṇhavācāvasena sammodamānabhāvo ti āmisena vā dhammena vā paṭisantharaṇaṃ
 pañcame ti karuṇāpubbabhāgo nti sīlavasena sucibhāvo chaṭṭhasattamāni uttānatthāneva
 aṭṭhame nti paccavekkhaṇabalaṃ
 navame  muṭṭhassacce akampanena satiyeva  uddhacce akampanena samādhiyeva 
 dasame ti cittekaggatā ti saṅkhārapariggāhakaññāṇaṃ
 ekādasame ti dussīlyaṃ ti micchādiṭṭhi
 dvādasame ti paripuṇṇasīlatā ti sammādiṭṭhikabhāvo tena  kammassakatasammādiṭṭhi jhānasammādiṭṭhi vipassanāsammādiṭṭhi maggasammādiṭṭhi phalasammādiṭṭhīti sabbāpi pañcavidhā sammādiṭṭhi saṅgahitā hoti
 terasame ti visuddhisampāpakaṃ sīlaṃ ti visuddhisampāpikā catumaggasammādiṭṭhi pañcavidhāpi vā sammādiṭṭhi
 cuddasame ti visuddhisampāpikā sammādiṭṭhiyeva nti heṭṭhimamaggasampayuttaṃ vīriyaṃ tañhi tassā diṭṭhiyā anurūpattā yathādiṭṭhissa ca padhānanti vuttaṃ
 pannarasame ti aññatra arahattamaggā kusalesu dhammesu asantuṭṭhibhāvo
 soḷasame nti muṭṭhassatibhāvo nti aññāṇabhāvo
 sattarasame apilāpanalakkhaṇā  sammā pajānanalakkhaṇaṃ nti
