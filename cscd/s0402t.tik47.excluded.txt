(14) 4. Puggalavaggo
1. Saṃyojanasuttavaṇṇanā
131 . upapattipaṭilābhiyāni. Bhavapaṭilābhiyānī Yehī ‘‘sakadāgāmissāti idaṃ appahīnasaṃyojanesu ariyesu uttamakoṭiyā gahita’’ appahīnasaṃyojanesū Uttamakoṭiyā gahita ‘‘orambhāgiyesu ca appahīnaṃ upādāyā’’ ‘‘orambhāgiyāni saṃyojanāni appahīnānī’’
Saṃyojanasuttavaṇṇanā niṭṭhitā.
2. Paṭibhānasuttavaṇṇanā
132 .   yuttappaṭibhāno nomuttappaṭibhāno tipiṭakacūḷanāgatthero catunikāyikapiṇḍatissatthero tipiṭakacūḷābhayatthero lāḷudāyitthero
Paṭibhānasuttavaṇṇanā niṭṭhitā.
3-4. Ugghaṭitaññūsuttādivaṇṇanā
133-4 . ugghaṭitaññū Saha udāhaṭavelāyā Dhammābhisamayo hotī Ayaṃ vuccatī vipañcitaññū. Ayaṃ vuccatī  neyyo. Anupubbena dhammābhisamayo padaparamo. Na tāya jātiyā dhammābhisamayo hotī
Ugghaṭitaññūsuttādivaṇṇanā niṭṭhitā.
