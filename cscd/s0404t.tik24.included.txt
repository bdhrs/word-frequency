

 dutiyassa  paṭhamadutiyāni uttānatthāni ti sabbaññutaññāṇena jānitabbaṃ sabbaṃ jānāti eva na hi padesañāṇe  ṭhito jānitabbaṃ sabbaṃ jānāti ukkaṭṭhaniddesena hi avisesaggahaṇena ca jānanti iminā niravasesaṃ ñeyyajātaṃ pariggayhatīti tabbisayāya jānanakiriyāya sabbaññutaññāṇameva karaṇaṃ bhavituṃ yuttaṃ pakaraṇavasena bhagavāti saddantarasannidhānena ca ayamattho vibhāvetabbo ti dibbacakkhupaññācakkhudhammacakkhubuddhacakkhusamantacakkhusaṅkhātehi ñāṇacakkhūhi passitabbaṃ passati eva atha vā ti yathā aññe savipallāsā kāmarūpapariññāvādino jānantāpi vipallāsavasena jānanti na evaṃ bhagavā bhagavā pana pahīnavipallāsattā jānanto jānāti eva diṭṭhidassanassa abhāvā passanto passatiyevāti attho ti dassanapariṇāyakaṭṭhena cakkhu viya bhūto yathā hi cakkhu sattānaṃ dassanatthaṃ pariṇeti sādheti evaṃ lokassa yāthāvadassanasādhanatopi dassanakiccapariṇāyakaṭṭhena cakkhu viya bhūto paññācakkhumayattā vā sayambhuñāṇena paññācakkhuṃ bhūto pattoti vā cakkhubhūto
ti viditakaraṇaṭṭhena ñāṇasabhāvo aviparītasabhāvaṭṭhena pariyattidhammappavattanato vā hadayena cintetvā vācāya nicchāritadhammamayoti  tenāha ti dhammā vā bodhipakkhiyā tehi uppannattā lokassa ca taduppādanato anaññasādhāraṇaṃ vā dhammaṃ patto adhigatoti  seṭṭhaṭṭhena brahmabhūtoti āha ti atha vā brahmā vuccati maggo tena uppannattā lokassa ca taduppādanato tañca sayambhuñāṇena pattoti  catusaccadhammaṃ vadatīti  ciraṃ saccappaṭivedhaṃ pavattento vadatīti ti dukkhādiatthaṃ tatthāpi pīḷanādiatthaṃ uddharitvā paramatthaṃ vā nibbānaṃ pāpayitā  amatādhigamapaṭipattidesanāya amatasacchikiriyaṃ sattesu uppādento amataṃ dadātīti  bodhipakkhiyadhammānaṃ tadāyattabhāvato  catutthe natthi vattabbaṃ


 pañcame ti thokā na bahū ti yā panāyaṃ avasesā pajā sakkāyadiṭṭhitīrameva anudhāvati ayameva bahutarāti attho ti sammā akkhāte sukathite ti tava desanādhamme ti taṃ dhammaṃ sutvā tadanucchavikaṃ paṭipadaṃ pūretvā maggaphalasacchikaraṇena dhammānuvattino nti kilesamārasaṅkhātassa maccuno nivāsaṭṭhānabhūtaṃ ti ye janā dhammānuvattino te etaṃ suduttaraṃ duratikkamaṃ māradheyyaṃ taritvā atikkamitvā nibbānapāraṃ gamissanti
ti kāyaduccaritādibhedaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ jahitvā ti paṇḍito bhikkhu abhinikkhamanato paṭṭhāya yāva arahattamaggā kāyasucaritādibhedaṃ sukkaṃ dhammaṃ bhāveyya ti okaṃ vuccati ālayo anokaṃ vuccati anālayo ālayato nikkhamitvā anālayasaṅkhātaṃ nibbānaṃ paṭicca ārabbha
ti yasmiṃ anālayasaṅkhāte viveke nibbāne imehi sattehi durabhiramaṃ tatrābhiratimiccheyya ti vatthukāmakilesakāme ti pañcahi nīvaraṇehi  vodāpeyya parisodheyyāti attho
ti sambojjhaṅgesu nti sammā hetunā nayena cittaṃ suṭṭhu bhāvitaṃ vaḍḍhitaṃ ti ānubhāvavanto arahattamaggañāṇajutiyā khandhādibhede dhamme jotetvā ṭhitāti attho ti te imasmiṃ khandhādiloke parinibbutā nāma arahattappattito paṭṭhāya kilesavaṭṭassa khepitattā saupādisesena carimacittanirodhena khandhavaṭṭassa khepitattā anupādisesena cāti dvīhi parinibbānehi parinibbutā anupādāno viya padīpo apaṇṇattikabhāvaṃ gatāti attho




 catutthassa  paṭhamavaggo uttānatthoyeva

 dutiye ca paṭhamādīni uttānatthāni dasame ti paccantadesavāsino ti pātova udakaṃ orohitvā sevālañceva uppalādīni ca gahetvā attano udakasuddhikabhāvajānanatthañceva lokassa ca udakena suddhi hotīti imassa atthassa jānanatthañca mālaṃ katvā pilandhanakā ti pāto majjhanhe sāyanhe ca udakaorohaṇakā tenāha ti ekādasamādīni uttānatthāni catutthe paṇṇāsake natthi vattabbaṃ
