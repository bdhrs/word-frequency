

 navamassa paṭhamaṃ heṭṭhā vuttanayameva dutiye ti duvidhāya saddhāya samannāgato ti na upaṭṭhahati ti atthānatthaṃ kāraṇākāraṇaṃ paripucchitā na hoti ti  sāmiatthe paccattaṃ samannāgatassāti vuttaṃ hoti ti tathāgatassa ekantapaṭibhānā dhammadesanā hoti ekanteneva paṭibhāti upaṭṭhātīti attho

 tatiye ti pañcakkhandhā ti ajjhāsayacchando kattukamyatāchando taṃ mūlaṃ etesanti chandamūlakā manasikārato sambhavantīti  phassato samudenti rāsī bhavantīti  vedanāya samosarantīti  samādhi etesaṃ pamukhoti  jeṭṭhakaṭṭhena sati adhipati etesanti  satijeṭṭhakāti attho paññā uttarā etesanti  vimutti eva sāro etesanti  ettha ca chandamūlakādayo cattāropi lokiyā kathitā sesā lokiyalokuttaramissakāti

 catutthe ti rajjantare dubbhituṃ samattho mahācoro ti pariyādānaṃ gacchati ti addhānaṃ pālento ṭhātuṃ na sakkoti ti attano averine appaharante guṇasampanne ca mahallake ca taruṇadārake ca appaharitabbayuttake paharati ti nissesaṃ gaṇhāti byattacorānañhi idaṃ vattaṃ  parassa dvīsu sāṭakesu eko gahetabbo ekasmiṃ sante dubbalaṃ datvā thiro gahetabbo puṭabhattataṇḍulādīsu ekaṃ koṭṭhāsaṃ datvā eko gahetabboti ti gāmanigamarājadhānīnaṃ āsannaṭṭhāne corikakammaṃ karoti ti yaṃ laddhaṃ taṃ dakkhiṇeyye nidahituṃ cheko na hoti paralokamaggaṃ na sodheti

 pañcame ti yaṃ guṇajātaṃ samaṇena pattabbaṃ ti brahmacariyavāsaṃvutena ti ahaṃ sabbabandhanehi mutto hutvā mahājanampi rāgādibandhanato mocemi ti aññena kenaci asikkhāpito acodito sayambhuñāṇena paṭivijjhitvā paramadamathena dantattā paramadanto nāma ti kilesaparinibbānena parinibbuto

 chaṭṭhe ti yaso ca mayā saddhiṃ mā gañchi ti vipulalābhī nti sīlañceva ñāṇañca ti sannipatitvā ti samāgantvā nti gaṇasaṅgaṇikavihāraṃ ti na hi nūna ime ti tathā hi pana ime ti aṅgulipatodayaṭṭhiṃ katvā vijjhanena ti mahāhasitaṃ hasante ti keḷiṃ karonte

 sattame ti tena dinnassa deyyadhammassa appaṭiggahaṇatthaṃ pattanikkujjanakammavācāya nikujjeyya na adhomukhaṭhapanena ti  catunnaṃ paccayānaṃ alābhatthāya ti upaddavāya avaḍḍhiyā ti ukkujjanakammavācāya ukkujjeyya

 aṭṭhame nti appasannabhāvaṃ jānāpeyyuṃ appasādaṃ pavedentena pana kiṃ kātabbanti nisinnāsanato na uṭṭhātabbaṃ na vanditabbaṃ na paccuggamanaṃ kātabbaṃ na deyyadhammo dātabbo ti pañcavidhe agocare

 navame nti imaṃ temāsaṃ idheva vasitabbanti vutto evaṃ hotūtiādinā nayena paṭissavaṃ ti vuttaṃ na saccaṃ karoti visaṃvādeti

 dasame ti adhipatiṭṭhāne jeṭṭhakaṭṭhāne tañhi jeṭṭhakaṃ katvā kiñci saṅghakammaṃ kātuṃ na labhati ti taṃ mūlaṃ katvā abbhānakammaṃ kātuṃ na labhati sesaṃ sabbattha uttānamevāti
