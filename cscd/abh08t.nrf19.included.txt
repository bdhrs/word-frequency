

ti paccuppannassa nāmarūpadhammassa
 uppajjantassa nāmarūpadhammassa  nicayato āgamanañca natthi nti disāya gamanaṃ   nāmarūpadhammassa ekasmiṃ ṭhāne nicayoti ca natthi
 avijjāsamudayā rūpasamudayo avijjānirodhā rūpanirodho taṇhāsamudayā rūpasamudayo taṇhānirodhā rūpanirodho kammasamudayā rūpasamudayo kammanirodhā rūpanirodho āhāranirodhā rūpanirodho ti saṅkhāradhamme
ti sampattalokuttarasaṅkhātamaggaphalassa sotāpannādipuggalassa ti paṭipattiyaṃ appaṭipannassa viruddhavasena paṭipannassa vā
ti ti vedanāsaññāsaṅkhārā nti viññāṇaṃ
 paccayādito yaṃ  nibbānaṃ  vijānantānaṃ pītipāmojjaṃ tehi labhitabbaṃ amataṃ amataṃ viya pītipāmojjaṃ vā
ti attani maggaphalasmiṃ appatte ti maggaphale ahaṃ patto asmi iti saññito hoti
ti sārajānanasīlā nti taṃ maggāmaggañāṇadassanaṃ  ñāṇaṃ  maggāmaggañāṇadassanaṃ nāma
