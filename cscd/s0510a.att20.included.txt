

vīsatime  vagge paṭhamattherāpadānaṃ uttānameva
 dutiyattherāpadāne ti isipabbajjaṃ pabbajitvā vane vasanabhāvena ti saṅkhaṃ gato attano anukampāya taṃ vanaṃ upagatassa siddhatthassa satthuno vasanamaṇḍapacchādanatthāya yaṃ tiṇaṃ taṃ  chindatīti attho dabbachadanaṃ katvā anekehi khuddakadaṇḍakehi maṇḍapaṃ katvā taṃ tiṇena chādetvā  bhagavato ahaṃ  pūjesinti attho
ti taṃ maṇḍapaṃ tattha ṭhitā devamanussā sattāhaṃ nirodhasamāpattiṃ samāpajjitvā nisinnassa  dhāresunti attho sesaṃ uttānatthamevāti
tatiyattherassa  apadāne ti ettha nti kaṭṭhānaṃ jiṇṇattā chinnabhinnaṭṭhānaṃ nti kaṭṭhānaṃ jiṇṇaṭṭhāne kaṇṇakitamahicchattakādipupphanaṃ khaṇḍañca phullañca khaṇḍaphullāni khaṇḍaphullānaṃ paṭisaṅkharaṇaṃ punappunaṃ thirakaraṇanti khaṇḍaphullapaṭisaṅkharaṇaṃ imassa pana therassa sambhārapūraṇakāle phussassa bhagavato cetiye chinnabhinnaṭṭhāne sudhāpiṇḍaṃ makkhetvā thirakaraṇaṃ khaṇḍaphullapaṭisaṅkharaṇaṃ nāma tasmā so khaṇḍaphulliyo theroti pākaṭo ahosi tatiyaṃ
 catutthattherassāpadāne nti rañño paricārako kammakārako ahosinti attho
ti  jale udake jātaṃ jalajaṃ kiṃ taṃ padumaṃ padumena samānanāmattā  padumuttarassa bhagavatoti attho uttamapadumanāmassa bhagavatoti vā attho catutthaṃ
pañcamaṃ uttānatthameva
 chaṭṭhe ti mahādvāravātapānakavāṭaphalakāhi vatipākāraṭṭālagopurakaddamodakaparikhāhi ca sampannaṃ nagaranti dvāravatīnagaraṃ dvāraṃ vatiñca padhānaṃ katvā nagarassa upalakkhitattā dvāravatī nagaranti voharantīti ti vuttaṃ mālāvaccho pupphārāmo mama ahosīti attho
ti te asokapallavā chaṭṭhaṃ
sattamaṭṭhamanavamāni uttānatthāneva dasamepi apubbaṃ natthīti
