 pañcame ti uttamapaññattiyo nti attabhāvavantānaṃ ti yo esa rāhu asurindo ayaṃ aggoti ettha rāhu kira asurindo cattāri yojanasahassāni aṭṭha ca yojanasatāni ucco bāhantaramassa dvādasayojanasatāni hatthatalapādatalānaṃ puthulatā tīṇi yojanasatāni aṅgulipabbāni paṇṇāsa yojanāni bhamukantaraṃ paṇṇāsayojanaṃ nalāṭaṃ tiyojanasataṃ sīsaṃ navayojanasataṃ ti yo esa rājā mandhātā nāma ayaṃ dibbepi mānusakepi kāme paribhuñjanakānaṃ  sattānaṃ aggo nāma esa hi asaṅkheyyāyukesu manussesu nibbattitvā icchiticchitakkhaṇe hiraññavassaṃ vassāpento mānusake kāme dīgharattaṃ paribhuñji devaloke  pana yāva chattiṃsāya indānaṃ āyuppamāṇaṃ tāva paṇīte kāme paribhuñjīti kāmabhogīnaṃ aggo nāma jāto nti adhipatiṭṭhānaṃ jeṭṭhakaṭṭhānaṃ karontānaṃ ti lokiyalokuttarehi guṇehi tathāgato aggo seṭṭho uttamo akkhāyati
nti dibbasampattisamiddhiyā ca parivārasaṅkhātena yasasā ca jalantānaṃ nti upari ca majjhe ca heṭṭhā ca ti yattakā lokanipphatti

 chaṭṭhe ti sukhumalakkhaṇapaṭivijjhanakāni ñāṇāni ti rūpe saṇhasukhumalakkhaṇapariggāhakena ñāṇena samannāgato hoti ti uttamena ti tena yāva anulomabhāvaṃ pattena sukhumalakkhaṇapariggāhakañāṇena ti natthibhāveneva na passati ti natthibhāveneva na pattheti dīsupi eseva nayo
