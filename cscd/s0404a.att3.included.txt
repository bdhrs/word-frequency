 dutiye ti yāya saddhāya dānaṃ deti sā saddhā nti yāya hiriyā dānaṃ deti sāva adhippetā nti anavajjañca dānaṃ nti divaṅgamaṃ

 tatiye ti dānakāraṇāni ti pemena dānaṃ deti ti  dosena kuddho hutvā yaṃ atthi taṃ vegena gaṇhitvā deti ti mohena mūḷho deti ti garahabhayena vā apāyabhayena vā tassa tasseva vā pana bhayena deti nti kulapaveṇiṃ

 catutthe ti dhaññaphalena mahapphalaṃ na hoti nti yampissa phalaṃ hoti tassa assādo na mahā hoti mandassādaṃ na madhuraṃ nti seyyāpissa na hoti vuḍḍhi tassa mahantaṃ vīhithambhasannivesaṃ na hotīti attho ti thalaninnavasena visamatalaṃ tattha thale udakaṃ na saṇṭhāti ninne atibahu tiṭṭhati nti pattharitvā ṭhitapiṭṭhipāsāṇehi ca khuddakapāsāṇehi ca sakkharāhi ca samannāgataṃ nti ubbhinnaloṇaṃ nti thaddhabhūmitāya gambhīrānugataṃ naṅgalamaggaṃ katvā kasituṃ na sakkā hoti uttānanaṅgalamaggameva hoti nti na udakāgamanasampannaṃ nti pacchābhāge udakaniggamanamaggasampannaṃ na hoti nti na khuddakamahantīhi udakamātikāhi sampannaṃ hoti  nti na kedāramariyādāhi sampannaṃ ntiādīni sabbāni vipākaphalavaseneva veditabbāni
ti paripuṇṇe sampattiyutte ti sampannaṃ bījaṃ ropitaṃ ti deve sammā vassante ti kīṭakimiādipāṇakaītiyā abhāvo ekā sampadā hoti ti vaḍḍhi dutiyā sampadā hoti nti vipulabhāvo tatiyā sampadā hoti nti paripuṇṇaphalaṃ catutthī sampadā hoti ti paripuṇṇasīlesu ti sampannaṃ vividhabhojanaṃ nti tividhaṃ kusalasampadaṃ ti sā bhojanasampadā upanayati kasmā  yasmāssa taṃ katakammaṃ sampannaṃ paripuṇṇanti attho ti sampanno atthu idha ti tīhi vijjāhi ca pañcadasahi caraṇadhammehi ca samannāgato ti evarūpo puggalo cittassa sampadaṃ avekallaparipuṇṇabhāvaṃ labhitvā nti paripuṇṇakammaṃ karoti nti atthañca paripuṇṇaṃ labhati nti vipassanādiṭṭhiṃ nti sotāpattimaggaṃ ti paripuṇṇacitto hutvā arahattaṃ  yāti ti sā sabbadukkhehi vimutti sabbasampadā nāma hotīti

 pañcame ti dānapaccayā upapattiyo ti ṭhapeti ti tasseva vevacanaṃ ti vaḍḍheti nti hīnesu pañcasu kāmaguṇesu vimuttaṃ nti tato uttarimaggaphalatthāya abhāvitaṃ ti yaṃ ṭhānaṃ patthetvā kusalaṃ kataṃ tattha nibbattanatthāya saṃvattati ti maggena vā samucchinnarāgassa samāpattiyā vā vikkhambhitarāgassa dānamatteneva hi brahmaloke nibbattituṃ na sakkā dānaṃ pana samādhivipassanācittassa alaṅkāraparivāraṃ hoti tato dānena muducitto brahmavihāre bhāvetvā brahmaloke nibbattati tena vuttaṃ  vītarāgassa no sarāgassāti

 chaṭṭhe  puññakiriyāni ca tāni tesaṃ tesaṃ ānisaṃsānaṃ vatthūni cāti  dānādīnañhi lakkhaṇe cittaṃ ṭhapetvā evarūpaṃ nāma amhehi dānaṃ dātabbaṃ sīlaṃ rakkhitabbaṃ bhāvanā bhāvetabbāti sattā puññāni karonti dānameva  dānacetanāsu vā purimacetanāto nipphannā sanniṭṭhāpakacetanā  sīlādīhi sīlamayādīni viya sesadvayesupi eseva nayo ti thokaṃ mandaṃ kataṃ hoti ti na nipphajjati ti bhāvanāyayogoyeva anāraddho hotīti attho nti  manussesu sampattirahitaṃ pañcavidhaṃ nīcakulaṃ ti paṭisandhivasena upagacchati tattha nibbattatīti attho nti pamāṇena kataṃ thokaṃ na bahu nti manussesu subhagabhāvaṃ tividhakulasampattiṃ nti adhikappamāṇaṃ balavaṃ vā ti abhibhavitvā gaṇhanti visiṭṭhatarā jeṭṭhakā hontīti attho

 sattame nti parisuddhaṃ vaṇṇasampannaṃ deti nti rasūpapannaṃ ti yuttapattakālena nti yaṃ kappiyaṃ taṃ deti ti imassa dinnaṃ mahapphalaṃ bhavissati imassa na mahapphalanti evaṃ paṭiggāhakapariyesanavasena dānaṃ vā paṇidhāyavasena dānaṃ vā vicinitvā deti

 aṭṭhame ti atthatthāya ti hitatthāya sukhatthāya nti paralokagatānaṃ ñātīnaṃ imasmiṃ sutte anuppanne buddhe cakkavattirājāno bodhisattā paccekabuddhā labbhanti buddhakāle buddhā ceva buddhasāvakā ca yathāvuttānañhi etesaṃ atthāya hitāya sukhāya saṃvattanti nti sappañño ghare vasanto bahūnaṃ vata atthāya hoti ti paṭhameva nti  mātāpitūnaṃ pubbakāraguṇe anussaranto ti sakāraṇena paccayapūjanena pūjeti ti brahmacārino apacayati nīcavuttitaṃ nesaṃ āpajjati ti piyasīlo

 navame ti cetanādānāni tiādīnaṃ attho heṭṭhā vuttoyeva

 dasame ti pāṇātipātacetanā ti sabbalahuko ti tena parittakena kammavipākena appāyuko hoti dinnamattāya vā paṭisandhiyā vilīyati mātukucchito nikkhantamatte vā evarūpo hi na aññassa kassaci nissando pāṇātipātasseva gatamaggo esoti ti yathā kākaṇikāmattampi  hatthe na tiṭṭhati evaṃ bhogabyasanaṃ saṃvatteti ti saha sapattehi veraṃ saṃvatteti tassa hi sapattā ca bahukā honti yo ca naṃ passati tasmiṃ verameva uppādeti na nibbāyati evarūpo hi parassa rakkhitagopitabhaṇḍe aparādhassa nissando
ti abhūtena abbhakkhānaṃ saṃvatteti yena kenaci kataṃ tasseva upari patati ti mittehi bhedaṃ saṃvatteti yaṃ yaṃ mittaṃ karoti so so bhijjatiyeva ti amanāpasaddaṃ saṃvatteti yā sā vācā kaṇṭakā kakkasā kaṭukā abhisajjanī mammacchedikā gatagataṭṭhāne tameva suṇāti manāpasaddasavanaṃ nāma na labhati evarūpo pharusavācāya gatamaggo nāma ti aggahetabbavacanataṃ saṃvatteti tvaṃ kasmā kathesi ko hi tava vacanaṃ gahessatīti vattabbataṃ āpajjati ayaṃ samphappalāpassa gatamaggo ti ummattakabhāvaṃ  saṃvatteti tena hi manusso ummatto vā khittacitto vā eḷamūgo vā hoti ayaṃ surāpānassa nissando imasmiṃ sutte vaṭṭameva kathitanti
