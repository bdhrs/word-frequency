14. Sikkhāpadavibhaṅgo

703 .   ‘‘sampayogavasena, upanissayavasena ca okāsabhāvenā’’
704 . ‘‘asabbasādhāraṇesū’’ Kammapathakoṭṭhāsikā eva, Kammapathabhāvena āgatanti vadanti, Assa Upakārakattaṃ Sabhāgattaṃ
Tathāgahitasaṅkhārārammaṇatāyā ‘‘Pañca sikkhāpadā’’
Tassa tassā ‘‘nissaggiya…pe… dve eva gahitā’’
 ‘‘viratisīlaṃ pana maggakoṭṭhāsika’’ Sesasīlāna
712 . Abhabbaṭṭhānā ‘‘taṃsabhāgatāya verabhūtāna’’ ‘‘Akusalasamuṭṭhitāni cā’’  tehi tesaṃ Yato Naphalabhūtassāpī aṭṭhaṅgikatā hoti,
Abhidhammabhājanīyavaṇṇanā niṭṭhitā.

714 . Yathāviramitabbato
Pañhapucchakavaṇṇanā niṭṭhitā.
Sikkhāpadavibhaṅgavaṇṇanā niṭṭhitā.
