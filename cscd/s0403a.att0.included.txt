


1 paṭhamapaṇṇāsakaṃ


 pañcakanipātassa  paṭhame sattannaṃ sekhānaṃ balānīti  saddhābalādīsu assaddhiye na kampatīti  ahirike na kampatīti  anottappe na kampatīti  kosajje na kampatīti  avijjāya na kampatīti ti yasmā imāni sattannaṃ sekhānaṃ balāni tasmā

 dutiye tiādīsu upayogatthe karaṇavacanaṃ hirīyitabbāni kāyaduccaritādīni hirīyati jigucchatīti attho ottappaniddese hetvatthe karaṇavacanaṃ kāyaduccaritādīhi ottappassa hetubhūtehi ottappati bhāyatīti attho
ti  paggahitavīriyo anosakkitamānaso ti pahānatthāya ti paṭilābhatthāya ti vīriyathāmena samannāgato ti thiraparakkamo ti kusalesu dhammesu anoropitadhuro anosakkitavīriyo
ti pañcannaṃ khandhānaṃ udayavayagāminiyā udayañca vayañca paṭivijjhituṃ samatthāya ti vipassanāpaññāya ceva maggapaññāya ca samaṅgibhūto ti vikkhambhanavasena ca samucchedavasena ca kilesehi ārakā ṭhitāya parisuddhāya ti sā ca abhinivijjhanato nibbedhikāti vuccati tāya samannāgatoti attho tattha maggapaññā samucchedavasena anibbiddhapubbaṃ appadālitapubbaṃ lobhakkhandhaṃ dosakkhandhaṃ mohakkhandhaṃ nibbijjhati padāletīti nibbedhikā vipassanāpaññā tadaṅgavasena nibbedhikā maggapaññāya paṭilābhasaṃvattanato tabbipassanā nibbedhikāti vattuṃ vaṭṭati ti idhāpi maggapaññā sammā hetunā nayena vaṭṭadukkhañca kilesadukkhañca khepayamānā gacchatīti sammā dukkhakkhayagāminī nāma vipassanāpaññā tadaṅgavasena vaṭṭadukkhañca kilesadukkhañca khepayamānā gacchatīti dukkhakkhayagāminī dukkhakkhayagāminiyā vā maggapaññāya paṭilābhāya saṃvattanatopesā dukkhakkhayagāminīti veditabbā iti imasmiṃ sutte pañca balāni missakāneva kathitāni tathā pañcame

 chaṭṭhe ti akusaladhammassa samāpajjanā tena saddhiṃ samaṅgibhāvoti attho ti pariyonandhitvā tiṭṭhati

 sattame ti vatthukāmakilesakāmesu laḷitā abhiratā nti  tiṇalāyanaasitañceva tiṇavahanakājañca ti ācārakulaputto ti pahāya ti yuttaṃ vacanāya ti sulabhā sakkā labhituṃ ti pañcannaṃ nīcakulānaṃ kāmā ti majjhimasattānaṃ kāmā ti rājarājamahāmattānaṃ kāmā ti kāmanavasena kāmetabbavasena ca kāmāicceva saṅkhaṃ gacchanti ti mahallako hoti ti yuttapañño ti attanāva gutto rakkhito attānaṃ vā gopetuṃ rakkhituṃ samattho ti na yutto pamajjituṃ ti yaṃ saddhāya kusalesu dhammesu kātuṃ yuttaṃ taṃ na kataṃ hoti sesapadesupi eseva nayo ti evaṃ saddhādīhi kātabbaṃ katvāva sotāpattiphale patiṭṭhite tasmiṃ puggale anapekkho homīti dasseti imasmiṃ sutte sotāpattimaggo kathito

 aṭṭhame ti sāsanasaddhamme ti okappanasaddhāya ca pakkhandanasaddhāya cāti dvīhipi saddhāhi virahito ti imasmiṃ sāsane guṇehi cavati patiṭṭhātuṃ na sakkoti iti imasmiṃ sutte appatiṭṭhānañca patiṭṭhānañca kathitaṃ

 navame  nāssa gāravoti  nāssa patissoti  ajeṭṭhako anīcavutti sesamettha purimasadisameva

 dasame ti abhājanaṃ nti vaḍḍhiṃ nti virūḷhamūlatāya niccalabhāvaṃ nti mahantabhāvaṃ sesaṃ sabbattha uttānamevāti
