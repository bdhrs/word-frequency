

 ekādasakanipātassa  paṭhamādīni heṭṭhā vuttanayāneva kevalañcettha ādito pañcasu nibbidāvirāgaṃ dvidhā bhinditvā ekādasaṅgāni katāni chaṭṭhe sikkhāpaccakkhānaṃ adhikaṃ

 sattame ti atthena saddhimattho nti byañjanena saddhiṃ  byañjanaṃ ti sampavattissati ti samānaṃ bhavissati ti na virajjhissati nti nibbāne aṭṭhame paccavekkhaṇā kathitā

 navame ti yavasassadoṇiyā samīpe baddho ti abbhantare katvā ti cinteti ti ito cito ca nānappakārakaṃ jhāyati ti nirantaravasena nibaddhaṃ jhāyati ti samāpattiyaṃ sanikantikavasenetaṃ  vuttaṃ samāpattiyañhi sanikantikattā esa khaḷuṅko nāma kato dīsupi eseva nayo
ti kathaṃ kāraṇākāraṇaṃ jānantassa sindhavassa jhāyitaṃ hoti ntiādīsu iṇasadisaṃ bandhanasadisaṃ dhanajānisadisaṃ kalisaṅkhātamahāparādhasadisañca katvā attano abhimukhassa patodassa ajjhoharaṇasaṅkhātaṃ patanaṃ vipassatīti attho ti samāpattisukhanikantiyā abhāvena pathaviārammaṇāya  catukkapañcakajjhānasaññāya na jhāyati niyantiyā abhāveneva so ājānīyo nāma hotīti ti nibbānārammaṇāya phalasamāpattiyā jhāyati ti pathavārammaṇe uppannā catukkapañcakajjhānasaññā vibhūtā pākaṭā hoti vibhūtā bhante rūpasaññā avibhūtā aṭṭhikasaññāti imasmiñhi sutte samatikkamassa atthitāya vibhūtatā vuttā idha pana vipassanāvasena aniccadukkhānattato diṭṭhattā vibhūtā nāma jātā dīsupi eseva nayo evamettha heṭṭhā viya samāpattivasena samatikkamaṃ avatvā vipassanācāravasena samatikkamo vutto ti evaṃ vipassanāpaṭipāṭiyā āgantvā uppāditāya phalasamāpattiyā jhāyanto

 dasame ti antaṃ atītattā accantasaṅkhātaṃ avināsadhammaṃ nibbānaṃ niṭṭhā assāti accantaniṭṭho iminā nayena sesapadāni veditabbāni nti janitasmiṃ  pajāyāti attho ti ye janā tasmiṃ gotte paṭisaranti ahaṃ gotamo ahaṃ kassapoti tesu loke gottapaṭisārīsu khattiyo seṭṭho ti mama sabbaññutaññāṇena saddhiṃ saṃsandetvā desitā mayā anuññātā sesaṃ sabbattha uttānatthamevāti
