

 catutthassa  paṭhame yuddhaṃ upajīvatīti ti rañño anucchaviko ti rañño upabhogaparibhogo ti hattho viya pādo viya ca avassaṃ icchitabbattā aṅganti saṅkhyaṃ gacchati ti udake usabhamattaṃ  thale aṭṭhusabhamattaṃ tato vā uttarinti dūre kaṇḍaṃ pāteti duṭṭhagāmaṇiabhayassa hi yodhājīvo navausabhamattaṃ kaṇḍaṃ pātesi pacchimabhave bodhisatto yojanappamāṇaṃ ti  avirādhitavedhī akkhaṇaṃ vā vijju vijjantarikāya vijjhituṃ samatthoti attho ti ekatobaddhaṃ phalakasatampi mahiṃsacammasatampi aṅguṭṭhapamāṇabahalaṃ lohapaṭṭampi caturaṅgulabahalaṃ asanapadarampi vidatthibahalaṃ udumbarapadarampi dīghantena vālikasakaṭampi vinivijjhituṃ samatthoti attho ntiādi visuddhimagge vitthāritameva tiādi taṇhāmānadiṭṭhipaṭikkhepavasena vuttaṃ ti sammā hetunā kāraṇena sahavipassanāya maggapaññāya passati ti arahattamaggena padāleti

 dutiye ti appaṭipucchitvā vinītā dubbinītaparisā ti pucchitvā vinītā suvinītaparisā ti pamāṇavasena vinītā pamāṇaṃ ñatvā vinītaparisāti attho yāvatajjhāti pāḷiyā pana yāva ajjhāsayāti attho ajjhāsayaṃ ñatvā vinītaparisāti vuttaṃ hoti tatiyaṃ uttānameva

 catutthe ti sabhāvaṭṭhitatā ti sabhāvaniyāmatā ti catubhūmakasaṅkhārā ti hutvā abhāvaṭṭhena aniccā ti sampaṭipīḷanaṭṭhena dukkhā ti avasavattanaṭṭhena anattā iti imasmiṃ sutte tīṇi lakkhaṇāni missakāni kathitāni

 pañcame nti paccatte sāmivacanaṃ tantehi vāyitavatthānīti attho ti  manussakesehi vāyitakambalo  nti idampi paccatte sāmivacanaṃ ti pacchimako lāmako ti tucchapuriso ti paṭisedheti ti kuminaṃ oḍḍeyya chaṭṭhasattamāni uttānatthāneva

 aṭṭhame ti assapoto ti ayamassa ñāṇajavoti vadāmi ti ayamassa guṇavaṇṇoti vadāmi nti ayamassa uccabhāvo parimaṇḍalabhāvoti vadāmīti

 navame ti assesu parasse ti purisesu parasse purisapuriseti attho imasmiṃ sutte tīṇi maggaphalāni kathitāni tattha ayaṃ tīhi maggehi ñāṇajavasampannoti veditabbo

 dasame ti bhaddake ti kāraṇākāraṇaṃ jānanake asse pi eseva nayo imasmiṃ sutte arahattaphalaṃ kathitaṃ tatrāyaṃ arahattamaggena ñāṇajavasampannoti veditabbo

 ekādasame ti antaṃ atikkantaniṭṭho akuppaniṭṭho dhuvaniṭṭhoti attho sesaṃ sadisameva

 dvādasame ti ijjhanakapāṭihāriyena ti  ādisitvā apadisitvā kathanaanukathanakathāpāṭihāriyena

 terasame ti phalasamāpattatthāya sammādiṭṭhiyā ti phalañāṇena ti sesehi phalasamāpattidhammehi imesu tīsupi suttesu khīṇāsavova kathitoti
