 Namo tassa bhagavato arahato sammāsambuddhassa
Khuddakanikāye
Khuddakapāṭha-aṭṭhakathā
Ganthārambhakathā
Buddhaṃ    saraṇaṃ gacchāmi;
Dhammaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi;
Saṅghaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmīti












Khuddakavavatthānaṃ
 ‘‘khuddakānaṃ karissāmi, kesañci atthavaṇṇana’’


 dīghanikāyo majjhimanikāyo saṃyuttanikāyo aṅguttaranikāyo  khuddakanikāyo



 








Nidānasodhanaṃ
 ‘‘buddhaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi, dhammaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi, saṅghaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmī’’









 kena bhāsita Katthā Kadā

Kasmā  cidhādito vutta
Kataṃ nidānasodhanaṃ.
