

 tatiyassa  paṭhame nti ettha -saddo anavasesattho -saddo samantabhāvattho tasmā kevalakappaṃ pokkharaṇiyanti evamattho daṭṭhabbo anavasesaṃ pharituṃ samatthassapi obhāsassa kenaci kāraṇena ekadesapharaṇampi siyā ayaṃ pana sabbasova pharatīti dassetuṃ samantattho kappa-saddo gahito ti vatthālaṅkārasarīrasamuṭṭhitena obhāsena pharitvā candimā  viya ekobhāsaṃ ekapajjotaṃ karitvāti attho ti sammadeva katamanuñño tenāha ti samānajjhāsayoti attho dukkhaṃ vaco etasminti dubbaco tassa kammaṃ dovacassaṃ tassa puggalassa anādariyavasena pavattā cetanā tassa bhāvo atthitā  atha vā dovacassameva  sā atthato saṅkhārakkhandho hoti cetanāpadhāno hi saṅkhārakkhandho catunnaṃ vā khandhānaṃ apadakkhiṇaggāhitākārena pavattānaṃ etaṃ adhivacananti vadanti pāpā assaddhādayo puggalā etassa mittāti pāpamitto tassa bhāvo  sāpi atthato dovacassatā viya daṭṭhabbā yāya hi cetanāya puggalo pāpamitto pāpasampavaṅko nāma hoti sā cetanā pāpamittatā cattāropi vā arūpino khandhā tadākārappavattā pāpamittatā dutiyaṃ uttānameva


 tatiye sambhavati jātimaraṇaṃ etenāti  upādānanti āha ti ti anupādāya tenāha ti jātimaraṇāni sammā khīyanti etthāti  nibbānanti āha ti nti sakalampi vaṭṭadukkhaṃ atikkantā carimacittanirodhena vaṭṭadukkhalesassapi asambhavato


 catutthe ti samāpajjanakusalo hoti tenāha ti tattha antogatahetuattho ṭhiti-saddo tasmā ṭhapanakusaloti atthoti āha ti tattha ti sattaṭṭhaaccharāmattaṃ  khaṇaṃ jhānaṃ ṭhapetuṃ sakkoti adhiṭṭhānavasibhāvassa nipphāditattā ti yathāparicchinnakālena  vuṭṭhānavasibhāvassa nipphāditattā kallaṃ sañjātaṃ assāti kallitaṃ tasmiṃ kallite kallitabhāve kusalo  tosetuṃ sampahaṃsetuṃ nti samādhānassa paṭipakkhadhammānaṃ dūrīkaraṇena sahakārīkāraṇānañca samappadhānena samāpajjane cittaṃ samatthaṃ kātuṃ ti samādhismiṃ nipphādetabbe tassa gocare kammaṭṭhānasaññite pavattiṭṭhāne bhikkhācāragocare satisampajaññayogato kusalo cheko tenāha tiādi nti paṭhamajjhānādisamādhiṃ visesabhāgiyatāya abhinīharituṃ upanetuṃ


 pañcame ti anussatiyo eva diṭṭhadhammikasamparāyikādihitasukhānaṃ hetubhāvato kāraṇāni nti nissaṭaṃ nti vissaṭṭhaṃ nti apetaṃ sabbametaṃ vikkhambhanameva sandhāya vadati ti pañcakāmaguṇato ti buddhānussativasena laddhaṃ upacārajjhānamāha ti paccayaṃ karitvā pādakaṃ katvāti attho


 chaṭṭhe ti vā taṇhāsaṃkilesādīnaṃ sampīḷe saṅkare gharāvāse ti maggaphalasukhādhigamāya okāsabhāvato okāsāti vuccanti ti lokuttaradhammassa  adhigamāya adhigantabbaokāso ti rāgādīhi malehi abhijjhāvisamalobhādīhi  ca upakkiliṭṭhacittānaṃ visuddhatthāya sā panāyaṃ cittassa visuddhi sijjhamānā yasmā sokādīnaṃ anupādāya saṃvattati tasmā vuttaṃ tiādi tattha socanaṃ ñātibyasanādinimittaṃ cetaso santāpo antotāpo antonijjhānaṃ  ñātibyasanādinimittameva socikatā kahaṃ ekaputtakātiādinā (ma ni 2353-354 saṃ ni 263) paridevanavasena lapanaṃ ti pahānāya āyatiṃ anuppajjanañhi idha samatikkamo ti kāyikadukkhassa ca cetasikadomanassassa cāti imesaṃ dvinnaṃ atthaṅgamāya nirodhāyāti attho ñāyati nicchayena kamati nibbānaṃ taṃ vā ñāyati paṭivijjhati etenāti  ariyamaggo idha pana saha pubbabhāgena ariyamaggo gahitoti āha ti ti anupādisesanibbānaṃ sandhāya vadati paccayavasena anuppannaṃ asaṅkhataṃ amatadhātumeva sesamettha uttānameva


 sattame ti manaso vivaṭṭanissitaṃ vaḍḍhiṃ āvahati ti vā manasā bhāvito sambhāvito yañca āvajjato manasi karoto cittaṃ vinīvaraṇaṃ hoti imasmiṃ pakkhe kammasādhano sambhāvanattho bhāvanīya-saddo nti vatvā tadeva vibhāvento tiādimāha ti ettha -saddena idha avuttānaṃ atibhojane nimittaggāhādīnaṃ saṅgaho daṭṭhabbo vuttañhetaṃ cha dhammā thinamiddhassa pahānāya saṃvattanti atibhojane nimittaggāho iriyāpathasamparivattanatā ālokasaññāmanasikāro abbhokāsavāso kalyāṇamittatā sappāyakathāti (itivu aṭṭha 111) antarāyasaddapariyāyo idha -saddoti āha ti


 aṭṭhame  maṇḍalasaṇṭhānamāḷasaṅkhepena katā bhojanasālā ti adhippetāti āha ti sesamettha suviññeyyameva


 navame diṭṭhadhammo vuccati paccakkho attabhāvoti āha ti ti nikkilesatāya nirāmisena sukhena vihāratthāya ti sūriyacandapajjotamaṇiukkāvijjuādīnaṃ āloko divā rattiñca upaladdho yathāladdhavaseneva ālokaṃ manasi karoti citte ṭhapeti tathā ca naṃ manasi karoti yathāssa subhāvitālokakasiṇassa viya kasiṇāloko yathicchakaṃ yāvadicchakañca so āloko rattiyaṃ upatiṭṭhati yena tattha divāsaññaṃ ṭhapeti divāriva vigatathinamiddho hoti tenāha nti ti vuttanayena manasi katvā divāriva saññaṃ uppādeti ti yathānena divā upaladdho sūriyāloko evaṃ rattimpi divā diṭṭhākāreneva taṃ ālokaṃ manasi karoti ti yathā rattiyaṃ candāloko upaladdho evaṃ divāpi rattiṃ diṭṭhākāreneva taṃ ālokaṃ manasi karoti citte ṭhapeti ti thinamiddhena apihitattā vivaṭena ti asañchāditena nti saññāṇobhāsaṃ dibbacakkhuñāṇaṃ rūpagatassa dibbassa itarassa ca dassanaṭṭhena idha ñāṇadassananti adhippetanti āha tiādi
ti jīvitakkhayato upari maraṇato paraṃ ti uṭṭhitena ti uddhaṃ uddhaṃ dhumātattā sūnattā  setarattehi viparibhinnaṃ vimissitaṃ nīlaṃ purimavaṇṇavipariṇāmabhūtaṃ vā nīlaṃ vinīlaṃ vinīlameva nti ka-kārena padavaḍḍhanamāha anatthantarato yathā pītakaṃ lohitakanti ti jigucchanīyattā kucchitaṃ vinīlaṃ nti kucchanattho vā ayaṃ ka-kāroti dassetuṃ vuttaṃ yathā pāpako kittisaddo abbhuggacchatīti (dī ni 3316 a ni 5213)  kākadhaṅkādīhi  nti vissavantapubbaṃ tahaṃ tahaṃ paggharantapubbanti attho nti vissandamānapubbataṃ
ti yo passeyya sarīraṃ sīvathikāya chaḍḍitanti vutto so bhikkhu  sadisataṃ tiādi upasaṃharaṇākāradassanaṃ ti rūpajīvitindriyaṃ arūpajīvitindriyaṃ panettha viññāṇagatikameva ti kammajatejo ti evaṃ ativiya pūtisabhāvo āyuādivigame viyāti adhippāyo ediso bhavissatīti evaṃbhāvīti āha ti
ti uppāṭetvā uppāṭetvā nti sabbaso akkhāditattā tahaṃ tahaṃ sesena appāvasesena maṃsalohitena yuttaṃ nti avisesena hatthaṭṭhikānaṃ vippakiṇṇatā jotitāti anavasesato tesaṃ vippakiṇṇataṃ dassento tiādimāha ti tirovassagatāni tāni pana saṃvaccharaṃ vītivattāni hontīti āha ti purāṇatāya ghanabhāvavigamena vicuṇṇatā idha pūtibhāvo so yathā hoti taṃ dassento tiādimāha nti cakkhudhātuādīnaṃ kāmadhātuādīnaṃ vā ti abhikkante paṭikkante sampajānakārī hotītiādinā (dī ni 1214 2376 ma ni 1109) vuttāya sattaṭṭhānikāya satiyā ceva taṃsampayuttañāṇassa ca atthāya
