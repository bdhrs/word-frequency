
1 paṭhamavaggo

ti  yuddhasajjābhimukhā hutvā gacchiṃsu ti tasmiṃ asurānaṃ abhiyāne  dāni vuccamānā anupubbato kathā ti tettiṃsa purise puññakiriyāya sahāyabhūte gahetvā yāvajīvaṃ mātāpitubharo assantiādinā samādinnāni  abhibhavantaṃ ti hataputtāya sā surā na hoti na suraṃ pivimhāti adhippāyo ti na surāti vuttakālato paṭṭhāya na suranti na dibbantīti asurāti keci  antobhūmiyaṃ āyāmato dasayojanasahassaṃ
uragādisahacaritāni ṭhānāni uragādīnīti āha ti ti paṭhame paribhaṇḍe pañcayojanasahassavitthāraputhubahalāhi sinerussa catūsupi passesu cattāro paribhaṇḍā sinerussa hi tasmiṃ tasmiṃ passe yugandharādīsu pañcasataparittadīpaparivāre mahādīpe ca labhitabbassa mahato atthassa vasena ti kupitena kopena ākulacittā ti yuddhesino ti sesesu paribhaṇḍesu ti supaṇṇādayo
ti sapakkhikaupacikā ti piṭṭhibhāgena nivattitvā
ti sakkassa āṇāya pamādaṃ āpajji  vitthārena nti suvaṇṇamayabhūmijagativīthiṃ
ti  uṭṭhānaṃ kāyikavīriyaṃ akaronto  vāyāmaṃ cetasikavīriyaṃ akaronto nti kasivāṇijjādibhedaṃ aññataraṃ kiccaṃ kattabbakammaṃ nti pavaraṃ tenāha nti tañca kho kasitabbaṭṭhānaṃ  adhippetanti āha nti ti kiñcipi jīvitahetubhūtaṃ kammaṃ akatvā ti tassa jīvitassa hetu nāma ti nibbānassa adhigamupāyabhūto maggo


ti abbhantare nti susīmoti evaṃnāmakaṃ  aññataraṃ attano puttameva


ti ubhinnaṃ saha eva samāgamo bhusaṃ vā byūḷhoti attho bhusā panassa byūḷhatā dvinnaṃ senānaṃ samāgantvā sampiṇḍitabhāvenāti āha ti ti rathapañjarassa paranto pacchimanto pacchimakoṭṭhāso ti rathapañjarassa kubbarena saddhiṃ sambandhanaṭṭhānato nti tadeva diyaḍḍhayojanasatāyāmoti vuttappamāṇameva ti pacchimanto satayojanotiādinā diguṇaṃ katvā candamaṇḍalasūriyamaṇḍalakiṅkiṇikajālādibhedassa  devānaṃ ti amhākaṃ rājā devaseṭṭho ti sakke nisinne tassa anantaraṃ dutiyaṃ āsanaṃ labhati tasmā devaseṭṭhatāya sakko viya gāravaṭṭhāniyo yato sakko tassa dhajaggaṃ ullokeyyāthāti āha esa nayo sesesupi ti asurehi parājayaṃ āpādito ti parasenāya upagacchantiyā uṭṭhitarajamattampi disvā ṭhitopi taṃ rajadhajaṃ disvā bhīrubhāvena 
 dhajaggaparittassa  asammukhībhūtāhipi devatāhi sirasā sampaṭicchitabbato ti -saddena rogabhayādīnañceva vaṭṭadukkhassa ca saṅgaho daṭṭhabbo vidhinā bhāvite parittassa atthe upacārajjhānādīnampi ijjhanato
dīghavāpīnāmake  gāme cetiyaṃ  nāma hammiyaṃ parikkhipitvā katavedikā buddhagataṃ satiṃ upaṭṭhapetvā parittarakkhaguttiṃ āha parittassa ānubhāvena  tathā hi ti


 mahānubhāvatāya asurānaṃ cittavepanena vepacittīti vadanti isīhi pana abhayaṃ yācite bhayameva dadāmīti vatvā tehi akkhayaṃ hotu te bhayanti abhisapavasena vuttakālato paṭṭhāya vepanacittatāya ti vuccati yaṃ lokiyā pulomoti ca vadanti nipātapadānipi kānici atthavisesajotakāni hontīti āha nti hetuatthādīnamettha asambhavato nti sakkaṃ devānamindaṃ ti kaṇṭhabandhanapañcamehi vibhattialopena niddeso ti imaṃ bandhāmi ayaṃ bajjhatūti uppannacitteneva  baddho hoti ayaṃ devānubhāvo ti etthāpi eseva nayo ti corosītiādinā idha vuttehi dasahi tenāha ti nibbattitvā cirakālataṃ upādāya khuṃsanavasena vadati ti  akkosaṃ  dīgharattaṃ khantisoraccesu niruḷhaajjhāsayattā nti mahantaṃ upasamabyañjanaṃ ti vepacittassa
ti paṭisattu hutvā saṃyuddhaṃ kareyya tenāha ti  upasameneva paccatthikassa nāyakabhūtassa kodhassa  paṭisedhanato tādise hi kodho paṭikiriyaṃ alabhanto anupādāno viya jātavedo vūpasammati -saddo hetuattho na kālatthoti āha ti ti tasmiṃyeva khaṇe dvīsu goṇesu yujjhantesu
 anantareva assa virodhaṃ anatthaṃ paṭibāhitvā diṭṭhadhammikassa ceva samparāyikassa ca saṃvidhānato  seṭṭhabalaṃ virodhapaccayaṃ abhibhuyya pavattanato bālayogato bālo tassa balaṃ  aññāṇanti āha nti nti taṃ aññāṇabalaṃ yassa puggalassa balaṃ  paññābalena viddhaṃsetabbato ti dhammaṭṭhaṃ  paṭippharitvā abhibhavitvā pavattā natthi paṭivacanamattaṃ pana koci vadeyyāpi taṃ akāraṇanti dassento tiādimāha nti paṭippharitvāti vacanassa kāraṇavacanaṃ  paṭikujjhanakassa nānattāvitakkanato nti anatthapaṭibāhanamukhena paṇḍitakiccakaraṇena paṭisedhentaṃ bālo ayanti evaṃ paññapetuṃ hetuphalānaṃ anavabodhato ti


ti vatvā tassa vattuṃ chekabhāvaṃ dassetuṃ tiādimāha nti laddhiṃ ti yassa puna coroti uttari vattuṃ na sakkoti evaṃ vimocetvāti paṭhamaṃ vattuṃ na sakkā ti bhāriyaṃ dukkaranti attho ti parassāti vutto so kiñci paṭhamaṃ vadanto attano adhippāyaṃ pavedeti nāma taṃ yathāsatti viditamano tassa uttari vattuṃ sakkoti tenāha nti api ca asurindena hotu devānaminda subhāsitena jayoti paṭhamaṃ vuttaṃ viseso ca pubbaṃ upanentaṃ anuvattati vacasi kusalo sakko devarājā taṃ visesaṃ teneva pubbaṃ upanayāpento upalāpanavasena tumhe  khvetthātiādimāha ti sakkapamukhāya devaparisāya loke pubbeva uppannattā pubbadevāti pasaṃsavacanaṃ vepacittiṃ sandhāya tumheti pubbadevāti ca vuttattā ti vuttaṃ gāravaṭṭhāniyattā vepacittino bahuvacanapayogo daṇḍena avacāro avacaraṇaṃ daṇḍāvacaro natthi ettha vutto daṇḍāvacaroti  sakkena vuttā gāthāyo


ti rathālaṅkārabhūtānaṃ kiṅkiṇikajālādīnaṃ saddo tathā ti ājānīyānaṃ hasitasaddo ca ti pāṇānaṃ anuparodhena paṇāmitacitto ti rathakapparamukhena ti uḷāraṃ suvipulaṃ puññaṃ paccayaṃ katvā nibbatto  katthaci appaṭighaṭṭanena gacchanto ti simbalivanamajjhena  nti ito cito vibhaggañceva niravasesato mathitañca hoti


ti suṭṭhu ativiya paccatthiko paṭisattu ti devapāsena bandhitvā gahito asi  sakkassa puññānubhāvena cetiyarājā kira imasmiṃ kappe tato pubbe kenaci avuttapubbaṃ kharamusāvādaṃ abhāsi tāvadeva virajjhitvā mahāpathaviyā vivare dine nipatitvā avīciaggijālānamindanamahosi taṃ sandhāya vuttaṃ ti ti mahantāni garutarāni pāpāni tathā hi vepacittino sapathakaraṇe nidassanabhāvena etāni aṭṭhakathāyaṃ āgatānīti


ti dvārapālākārena katapaṭimāyo viya  na antarā saṃkocaṃ āpajjeyyāti adhippāyo ti nipphannabhāvena sundaresu sabbe hi anipphannā atthā na sobhanti ti vippakatabhāvena sañjātakiccā ti asañjātakicco kiccarahito nāma natthi gamanaṭṭhitasayananisajjādivasena uppajjanakadukkhavinodanabhāvato ti imesaṃ sattānaṃ saṃbhuñjitabbavatthūni nāma pakatiyā virodhasīlānipi asaṃyogena vā asundarānipi tāni abhisaṅkharaṇapacanasaṃyojanaparamāni veditabbāni tathā sati sambhogārahabhāvūpagamanato tenāha tiādi uṇhāpetvā paribhuñjitabbayutte paribhajjitvāti adhippāyo


 jāmātikā vuccati dhītupati sasuro bhariyāya pitā tasmā ime antaravattino  dve janā sakkavepacittino sujāya vasena nti dikkhitvā pabbajitvā cirakālānaṃ vatasamādānavasena ito bāhirakānaṃ pabbajitānanti āha nti ti ito yathāṭhitaṭṭhānato apehi apakkama  guṇe gāravayogato devā hi yebhuyyena mayaṃ pubbe guṇavante payirupāsitvā tesaṃ ovāde ṭhatvā puññāni upacinitvā idhūpapannāti guṇavantesu ādarabhāvaṃ upaṭṭhapenti


nti  cakkavāḷapabbatapādasamantā mahāsamuddatīrapiṭṭhiyaṃ yatheva sinerusamīpe mahāsamuddo anupubbaninno anupubbapoṇo anupubbapabbhāro evaṃ yebhuyyena cakkavāḷapādasamīpepi tenāha ti ti anantarasutte vuttappakārāsu ti assamapadavemajjhena ti yaṃ nūna mayantiādinā yathā pāḷiyaṃ āgataṃ evaṃ mantayiṃsu
ti yadicchitakaraṇaṃ nti durāsayānaṃ te pana duṭṭhajjhāsayā viruddhā hontīti āha nti nti bījaṃ sandhāya vapitaṃ tenāha nti
nti sāyaṃ asitabbabhojanaṃ yathāvāraṃ bhakkhitametaṃ devānaṃ viya sukhumaṃ guruvāsañca na hotīti nti vuttaṃ nti sañjātagelaññaṃ ti kampati pavedhati


2 dutiyavaggo

 samādātabbato  aññamaññaṃ asaṅkarasabhāvena pabbajitabbato  tato eva asaṃkiṇṇabhāgāti katvā ti vuttaṃ ti puññavisesatāya pujjabhavaphalanibbattanena kittisaññānena ca samaṃ attāni samattāni ti akhaṇḍādibhāvena sabbaso puṇṇāni ti tattha sakkaccakāritāya sammā ādinnāni ti pitubhaginī na yā kāci mātulassa bhariyā kulajeṭṭhakānaṃ adhippetattā bhariyāpi vā mātulasambandhato gahetabbā tathā sati mahāpitubhariyādīnampi saṅgaho daṭṭhabbo
-saddena  jeṭṭhabhaginīnaṃ saṅgaho ti tesaṃ paccuṭṭhānakaro yo koci dadantopi sāpekkho deti so muttacāgo na hoti ayaṃ pana na evanti ti vuttaṃ ti nirapekkhapariccāgoti attho yathā pāṇātipātabahulo lohitapāṇīti vuccati tathā dānabahulo payatapāṇīti vuttoti āha ti ti deyyadhammassa pariccajane abhirato ti parehi yācituṃ yutto icchitassa atthassa tāvadeva vissajjanato ti sabbakālaṃ dāneneva yutto abhiṇhaṃ pavattamahādānattā ti parassa sampuṇṇaṃ katvā pariccajanasaṅkhāte dāne ca attanā paribhuñjitabbato saṃvibhajanasaṅkhāte saṃvibhāge ca  abhirato


ti manussesu bhūto manussattaṃ vā patto nti nivāsaṭṭhānaṃ kāretvā adāsi tasmā vāsaṃ adāsīti  attha-saddo idha kāraṇapariyāyoti āha nti svāyamattho heṭṭhā vibhāvitova  tasmā sahassaṃ paññāakkhīni etassāti  maghaṃ vuccati dhanaṃ taṃ pana saddhāsaṅkhātaṃ maghaṃ assa atthīti  pure dānaṃ dadātīti  anunāsikalopaṃ akatvā puññāni kātuṃ sakkotīti 


ti sakko devarājā bahuvacane vattabbe ekavacanaṃ vuttaṃ vuccatīti vacanaṃ attho tasmā ti bahumhi attheti attho  yathā paṭipajjanto anukkamena te dhamme samādiyitvā sakko sakkattaṃ ajjhagā taṃ paṭipattiṃ dassetuṃ tiādi vuttaṃ ti sakkabhāvassa atītānantare attabhāve nti sakkassa maghamāṇavakāle sammāpaṭipattiṃ tāya sakkabhāvūpagamanañcāti taṃ sabbaṃ  tasmā tattha vuttanayeneva veditabboti adhippāyo


ti manussesu duggato nti manussesu paramanihīnataṃ ti manussesu vā paramanihīno ti tassa devaputtassa tasmiṃ uppajjanaṭṭhāne ti tassa purimavatthuṃ nissāya hīnato cintenti ti tameva paresaṃ kathenti ti vuttamatthaṃ vitthārikaṃ karonti sabbadā paricchijja parivārasampanno hutvā aḍḍhaṭṭharatane hatthikkhandhe mahaccarājānubhāvena nisinnattā ti olambanti  aññadatthu paccekabuddhato attano samānādarakiriyaṃ paccāsīsati tena vuttaṃ tiādi nti ko ayanti byāpannavasena vadati kāḷarattehi suttehi sibbitattā vaṇṇavikāraṃ disvā ti āha  mahādukkhaṃ paccanubhoti tadanurūpapāpakammassa vipākāvasesena laddhokāsena  kāmañca ettha paṭisandhiggahaṇaṃ kusalakammeneva tassa pana akusalakammassa vipākino balavabhāvato vuttaṃ ti tenāha ti bhikkhāya carituṃ samatthakālato paṭṭhāya rogassa balavatāya ti vipassanāpaṭipāṭiyā bhāvanāñāṇaṃ nibbānaṃ paṭipesetvā pavattetvā indriyānaṃ paripakkattā  satthu desanāvilāsena nti pādacumbaṭaṃ kuṭṭhino hi sakalapādatalaṃ mā rujīti cumbaṭaṃ  katvā taṃ pādatale bandhitvā gacchanti  tathā nihīnamanussattabhāvato cavitvā  devattabhāvaṃ gaṇhanto cuticittato dutiyacittavāre ādānacittakkhaṇe devaloke nibbatto
ti maggādhigamanena āgatā uppannā nti ariyānaṃ kantaṃ manāpaṃ manoramaṃ sīladhammaṃ ariyānaṃ adhicittaadhipaññāsikkhā viya adhisīlasikkhāpi sabbā ativiya kantā evāti āha tiādi ti imasmiṃ sotāpannassa bhavasaṅkhāte atthe niddhāretvā vuccamāne mpi yasmā diṭṭhi viya bhavantarepi 


 āramanti ettha sattāti  manoramā upavanādayo te eva cetenti ettha saddhāya attano pītisomanassaṃ sandhahantīti ti ca vuccanti ti manussānaṃ āramaṇīyabhāvassa tassa pana sīlādiguṇavasena acinteyyāparimeyyānubhāvatāpi hotīti bhagavā nti avoca acetanāya bhūmiyā ramaṇīyatā nāma guṇavisiṭṭhānaṃ ariyānaṃ sevanavasena veditabbāti vuttaṃ ti


nti dakkhiṇeyyaṃ uddissa dentānaṃ aṭṭhuppattiko suttanikkhepoti dassetvā atthavaṇṇanaṃ kātuṃ tiādi vuttaṃ nti seṭṭhaṃ tehi tehi vā yathāladdhasappiādayo mā nassantu aggabhāvena gahitāni sappiādīni kevalaṃ aggimhi jhāpanena devā  manussā micchāgāhena mā nassantu ti takkamattena kathemāti amhe maññatha idāni passatha paccakkhato ayaṃ vo āgacchatīti āhaṃsūti yojanā
nti  upadhīsu vā vipaccati upadhayo vā vipākā etassāti upadhivipākaṃ  vipulapakkhatāya  sammāsambuddhena mahābrahmunā ca evaṃ ovādo dinnoti


ti uṭṭhānaṃ kāyikavīriyaṃ karohi tenāha ti cetasikavīriyaṃ pana bhagavatā matthakaṃ pāpitameva tenāha ti  uccabhāvena vitthārato pana āyāmato ca anekayojanasatasahassaparimāṇacakkavāḷaṃ atibyāpetvā ṭhitassa ti pātitabhāra nikkhepitabbato bhārāti āha tiādi te hi taṃsamaṅgino puggalassa sampātanaṭṭhena  nāma vuttañhetaṃ bhārā have pañcakkhandhāti (saṃ ni 322) tadekadesā ca nti yathā pannarasapuṇṇamāya rattiyaṃ paripuṇṇakāle upakkilesavimutto cando sobhati evaṃ tava cittaṃ sabbaso upakkilesavimuttaṃ sobhatīti adhippāyo


ti bahudisā kā pana tāti āha ti anudisāgahaṇena cettha uddhaṃ adhopi gayhatīti ca dasseti ti bhūmipaṭibaddhavuttino etena rukkhapabbatanivāsinopi gahitā honti ti upacārappanājhānāni  uppādetvā aparihīnajjhānatāya cirakālaṃ samāhitacitte nti jīvitapariyantaṃ yāvajīvaṃ ti -saddena avasesapuññakiriyavatthūni saṅgaṇhāti niccasīlavasena  niyamasīlavasena  -saddena tato katipayehi uposathasīlavasena aṭṭhahipīti dasseti ti dhammato anapetehi ti pamokkho


ti  dakkhiṇajāṇumaṇḍalaṃ pathaviyaṃ ṭhapetvā vandamāno brahmajāṇuko nāma tathābhūto hutvā yajitabbato  pūjanīyo evaṃ pūjāvisesayogato sakkoti āha ti sakkassa namakkārabhājanabhūtañhi pucchanto mātali ti āha ti guṇahetukehi anantāni hi buddhānaṃ nāmāni tāni ca kho sabbānipi guṇanemittakāneva anantaguṇattā vuttañhetaṃ 
asaṅkhyeyyāni nāmāni saguṇena mahesino
guṇena nāmamuddheyyaṃ api nāmasahassatoti 
tasmā nti paripuṇṇaguṇanāmanti attho ti sammā samucchindanavasena atikkamanena kilesārīnaṃ apetacayoti  so āramitabbaṭṭhena ārāmo etesanti  tenāha ti


 dehe  sayanato  pūtidehaṃ  tasmiṃ ṭhitatāya ti  yojanā ti ete manussā asuciduggandhajegucchapaṭikkūle mātukucchisaṅkhāte kuṇapasmiṃ dasa māse nimuggā tesaṃ kinnāma tvaṃ pihayasīti yojanā ti etesaṃ isīnaṃ etaṃ sammāpaṭipattiṃ vihayāmi idāni taṃ paṭipattiṃ dassetuṃ ti vuttaṃ  pakkhipitabbassa ca abhāvato tenāha nti nti paresaṃ gahitaṃ santakaṃ tesaṃ pākāya niṭṭhitaṃ ti piṇḍācariyāya  pariyesantā  yāpenti na esanti anesanaṃ ti suṭṭhu samādinnasobhanavatā
ti ganthadhuravipassanādhurānaṃ vasena guṇaparimāṇasubhāsitasseva bhāsanasīlā  ariyena tuṇhībhūtena  tato eva manassa sātisayaṃ  gahitadaṇḍesu parāmāsādipayuttesu daṇḍādānādihetu uppajjanakakilesapariḷāhābhāvato  tenāha ti ti sabhavādānesu ti tabbirahitā tenāha tiādi


3 tatiyavaggo

 heṭṭhā devaputtasaṃyuttavaṇṇanāyaṃ


 duddasiko virūpavaṇṇo ti rassabhāvena avarakoṭimako  na pana so tathārūpo koci yakkho tenāha ti yadi evaṃ kasmā so tathārūpo hutvā āgatoti āha ti kathaṃ panettha ñāyati so eko rūpāvacarabrahmā na paneso avaruddhakayakkhoti yuttiṃ dassento tiādimāha  vacanaṃ ti pharusasamācārena ko nāma mayhaṃ āsane sannisinnoti akkhantiṃ anuppādento nti garutaraṃ sakkārabahumānaṃ ti paramanipaccakāre suvūpasamane ca dassiyamāne sakkassa tāya eva ācārasampattiyā  attano ca āvikātuṃ ti khantimettānuddayāsabbhāvato parasmiṃ upahatacittomhīti sakko attano sabhāvaṃ vadati nti kodhena attano vase nibbattetuṃ na sukaromhi atha kho kodhaṃ mayhaṃ vase na vattemīti adhippāyo ti yadi me kadāci kodho uppajjeyya taṃ kodhaṃ anuvattento cirakālaṃ na kujjhāmi  ti anto sace me kodho uppajjeyya khippameva ca naṃ paṭivineyyanti taṃ me pubbeva vataṃ paripūritaṃ


ti ābādho assa atthīti ābādhiko ti sikkhāpesi sambaro nāma asuramāyāya ādipuriso purātano asurindo taṃ sandhāyāha tiādi  aññopi māyāvī māyaṃ payojetvā upavādantarāyo nāma khamāpane sati vigacchati pākatikameva hotīti āha ti ti vepacittinā sambaravijjāya adānena vañcitattā ti isīnaṃ santikaṃ netvā khamāpanavasena kātabbaṃ akatvā


nti aññamaññaṃ vācasikaṃ pharusaṃ payojesuṃ tenāha ti vivādaṃ akaṃsūti attho nti vacīsaṃvaraṃ atikkamitvā vacanaṃ yasmā accaye desiyamāne taṃ khīṇayati aññamaññassa khamamānassa khamanaṃ paṭiggaṇhato tasmā vuttaṃ ti tumhākaṃ vase vattatu visevitaṃ akatvā yathākāmakaraṇīyo hotu mittadhammo idha uttarapadalopena mittoti vuttoti āha ti nti mittehīti karaṇavacanaṃ bhummatthe tenāha ti yathā nibbattasabhāvassa bhāvato aññathattaṃ  evaṃ mittabhāvato vuttavipariyāyo amittadhammo jarāpariyāyena vutto nti


ti ettha kodhena anabhibhavanīyattaṃ khantimettākaruṇāditappaṭipakkhadhammaparibrūhanena  tathā hi taṃsamaṅgino kodho abhibhuyyatīti āha ti ti esā paṭipatti ti appanāppattā mettā tadupacāro  na vihiṃsati kiñci etāyāti ti appanāppattakaruṇā veditabbā tadupacāro nti khantiādīsu yonisomanasikārābhāvena gārayhasamācārasamāyogena ca nihīnaṃ janaṃ paccayaparisuddhiyā  abhimaddamāno puggalaṃ  tassa so paṭisaṅkhānabhāvanābalehi sammadeva pahātabboti
