

 tatiyassa  paṭhame nti abhisamācāre uttamasamācāre bhavaṃ kiṃ pana tanti āha nti sesaṃ suviññeyyameva dutiye natthi vattabbaṃ


 tatiye nti na ca pabhāsampannaṃ nti tāpetvā tāḷakajjanapabhaṅguraṃ nti vuttāvasesaṃ sajātilohaṃ vijātilohaṃ pisācalohaṃ kittimalohanti evaṃpabhedaṃ sabbampi lohaṃ ti ettha nanu lokiyakusalacittassapi suvisuddhassapi uppajjituṃ appadāneneva upakkilesatāti saccametaṃ yasmiṃ pana santāne nīvaraṇāni laddhappatiṭṭhāni tattha mahaggatakusalassapi asambhavo pageva lokuttarakusalassa parittakusalaṃ pana yathāpaccayaṃ uppajjamānaṃ nīvaraṇehi upahate santāne uppattiyā aparisuddhaṃ hontaṃ upakkiliṭṭhaṃ nāma hoti aparisuddhadīpakapallikavaṭṭitelādisannissayo padīpo viya apica nippariyāyato uppajjituṃ appadāneneva tesaṃ upakkilesatāti dassento tiādimāha ārammaṇe vikkhittappavattivasena cuṇṇavicuṇṇatā veditabbā catutthe natthi vattabbaṃ


 pañcame tiādinā aṅguttarabhāṇakānaṃ matena ayaṃ atthavaṇṇanā āraddhā majjhimabhāṇakā panettha aññathā atthaṃ vadanti vuttañhetaṃ   
ti  laddhūpakārā ti arahattamaggasammādiṭṭhi phalakkhaṇe nibbattā cetovimutti phalaṃ assāti  tadeva cetovimuttisaṅkhātaṃ phalaṃ ānisaṃso assāti  dutiyapadepi eseva nayo ettha catutthaphalapaññā  nāma avasesā dhammā ti veditabbā tiādīsu nti catupārisuddhisīlaṃ nti sappāyadhammassavanaṃ ti kammaṭṭhāne khalanapakkhalanacchedanakathā ti vipassanāpādikā aṭṭha samāpattiyo ti sattavidhā anupassanā catupārisuddhisīlañhi pūrentassa sappāyadhammassavanaṃ suṇantassa kammaṭṭhāne khalanapakkhalanaṃ chindantassa vipassanāpādikāsu aṭṭhasu samāpattīsu kammaṃ karontassa sattavidhaṃ anupassanaṃ bhāventassa arahattamaggo uppajjitvā phalaṃ deti
yathā hi madhuraṃ ambapakkaṃ paribhuñjitukāmo ambapotakassa samantā udakakoṭṭhakaṃ thiraṃ katvā bandhati ghaṭaṃ gahetvā kālena kālaṃ udakaṃ āsiñcati udakassa anikkhamanatthaṃ mariyādaṃ thiraṃ karoti yā hoti samīpe valli vā sukkhadaṇḍako vā kipillikapuṭo vā makkaṭakajālaṃ vā taṃ apaneti khaṇittiṃ gahetvā kālena kālaṃ mūlāni parikhaṇati evamassa appamattassa imāni pañca kāraṇāni karoto so ambo vaḍḍhitvā phalaṃ deti evaṃ sampadamidaṃ veditabbaṃ rukkhassa samantato koṭṭhakabandhanaṃ viya hi sīlaṃ daṭṭhabbaṃ kālena kālaṃ udakasiñcanaṃ viya dhammassavanaṃ mariyādāya thirabhāvakaraṇaṃ viya samatho samīpe valliādīnaṃ haraṇaṃ viya kammaṭṭhāne khalanapakkhalanacchedanaṃ kālena kālaṃ khaṇittiṃ gahetvā mūlakhaṇanaṃ viya sattannaṃ anupassanānaṃ bhāvanā tehi pañcahi kāraṇehi anuggahitassa ambarukkhassa madhuraphaladānakālo viya imassa bhikkhuno imehi pañcahi dhammehi anuggahitāya sammādiṭṭhiyā arahattaphaladānaṃ veditabbanti
ettha ca ti yathārahaṃ nissayādivasena laddhapaccayā vipassanāsammādiṭṭhiyā anuggahitabhāvena gahitattā maggasammādiṭṭhīsu ca arahattamaggasammādiṭṭhi  anantarassa hi vidhi paṭisedho vā aggaphalasamādhimhi tapparikkhāradhammesuyeva ca kevalo cetopariyāyo niruḷhoti ti anantaraṃ kālantare cāti duvidhepi phalakkhaṇe paṭippassaddhivasena sabbasaṃkilesehi cetovimuccati etāyāti  aggaphalapaññaṃ ṭhapetvā avasesā phaladhammā tenāha ti sabbakilesehi cetaso vimuccanasaṅkhātaṃ paṭippassambhanasaññitaṃ pahānaṃ phalaṃ ānisaṃso cāti yojanā idha cetovimuttisaddena pahānamattaṃ gahitaṃ pubbe pahāyakadhammā aññathā phaladhammā eva ānisaṃsoti gayhamāne punavacanaṃ niratthakaṃ siyā ti etthāpi evameva attho veditabbo sammāvācākammantājīvā sīlasabhāvattā visesato samādhissa upakārā tathā sammāsaṅkappo jhānasabhāvattā tathā hi so appanāti niddiṭṭho sammāsatisammāvāyāmā pana samādhipakkhiyā evāti āha ti
nti ariyamaggādhigamassa padaṭṭhānabhūtaṃ catupārisuddhisīlaṃ dīsupi eseva nayo attano cittappavattiārocanavasena saha kathanaṃ saṃkathā saṃkathāva  idha pana kammaṭṭhānappaṭibaddhāti āha ti tassa kammaṭṭhānassa ekavāraṃ vidhiyā appaṭipajjanaṃ khalanaṃ anekavāraṃ pakkhalanaṃ tadubhayassa vicchedanī apanayanī kathā ti vivaṭṭasannissitaṃ katvā pālentassa brūhentassa ca ti yathāuggahitakammaṭṭhānaṃ phātiṃ gamissatīti evaṃ suṇantassa teneva hi nti vuttaṃ ti bhāvanānuyogakammaṃ karontassa pañcasupi ṭhānesu anta-saddo hetuatthajotano daṭṭhabbo evañhi yathā hītiādinā vuccamānā ambūpamā yujjeyya
nti jalāvāṭaṃ ti asithilaṃ daḷhaṃ nātimahantaṃ nātikhuddakaṃ katvā yojeti ti udakasiñcanakāle tato tato pavattitvā udakassa anikkhamanatthaṃ jalāvāṭapāḷiṃ thirataraṃ karoti ti tasseva ambagacchassa sukkhako sākhāsīsako  ti tambakipillikapuṭo nti kudālaṃ  sammādiṭṭhiyā vaḍḍhanūpāyassa mūlabhāvato  bhāvanāya paribrūhanato  yathāvuttāya bhāvanādhiṭṭhānāya sīlamariyādāya daḷhībhāvāpādanato samāhitassa hi sīlaṃ thirataraṃ hoti  icchitabbabhāvanāya vibandhanāpanayanato  tassā vibandhassa mūlabhūtānaṃ taṇhāmānadiṭṭhīnaṃ palikhaṇanato ettha ca yasmā suparisuddhasīlassa kammaṭṭhānaṃ anuyuñjantassa sappāyadhammassavanaṃ icchitabbaṃ tato yathāsute atthe sākacchāsamāpajjanaṃ tato kammaṭṭhānavisodhanena samathanibbatti tato samāhitassa āraddhavipassakassa vipassanāpāripūri paripuṇṇā  vipassanā maggasammādiṭṭhiṃ brūhetīti evametesaṃ aṅgānaṃ paramparāya sammukhā anuggaṇhanato ayamānupubbī kathitāti veditabbaṃ


 chaṭṭhe vimuttiyā vaṭṭadukkhato vimuccanassa āyatanāni kāraṇāni ti āha  ti ti idha sīlaṃ āgataṃ idha samādhi idha paññātiādinā taṃtaṃpāḷiatthaṃ yāthāvato jānantassa ti tadatthabodhiniṃ pāḷiṃ yāthāvato upadhārentassa ti sañjātamattā mudukā pīti jāyati kathaṃ jāyati yathādesitaṃ dhammaṃ upadhārentassa tadanucchavikameva attano kāyavācāmanosamācāraṃ pariggaṇhantassa somanassaṃ pattassa pamodalakkhaṇaṃ pāmojjaṃ jāyati ti purimuppannāya pītiyā vasena laddhāsevanattā ativiya tuṭṭhākārabhūtā kāyacittadarathassa passambhanasamatthatāya passaddhiyā paccayo bhavituṃ samatthā balappattā pīti jāyati yasmā nāmakāye passaddhe rūpakāyopi passaddho eva hoti tasmā nāmakāyo passambhaticceva vuttaṃ
ti vakkhamānassa cittasamādhānassa paccayo bhavituṃ samatthaṃ cetasikaṃ nirāmisasukhaṃ paṭilabhati vindati ti ettha pana na yo koci samādhi adhippeto atha kho anuttarasamādhīti dassento ti āha tiādi tassaṃ desanāyaṃ tādisassa puggalassa yathāvuttasamādhipaṭilābhassa kāraṇabhāvavibhāvanaṃ yaṃ tathā vimuttāyatanabhāvo nti dassituṃ nti kammaṭṭhānapāḷiyā āruḷho samādhi eva parato uppajjanakabhāvanāsamādhissa kāraṇabhāvato samādhinimittaṃ tenāha tiādi


 sattame  sabbaso kilesadukkhadarathapariḷāhānaṃ vigatattā sātisayamettha sukhanti vuttaṃ ti purimassa purimassa vasena pacchimaṃ pacchimaṃ laddhāsevanatāya santapaṇītatarabhāvappattaṃ hotīti āha ti ti kilesānaṃ paṭippassambhanena laddhattā nti kilesānaṃ paṭippassambhanabhāvaṃ  pattattā tabbhāvaṃ upagatattā lokiyasamādhissa paccanīkāni nīvaraṇapaṭhamajjhānanikantiādīni niggahetabbāni aññe kilesā vāretabbā imassa pana arahattasamādhissa paṭippassaddhasabbakilesattā na niggahetabbaṃ vāretabbañca atthīti maggānantaraṃ samāpattikkhaṇe ca appayogena adhigatattā appitattā ca aparihānivasena vā appitattā ti etena appavattamānāyapi satiyā satibahulatāya sato eva nāmāti dasseti ti etena paricchinnassatiyā satoti dasseti ti ñāṇesu


 aṭṭhame  karo vuccati pupphasambhavaṃ gabbhāsaye kirīyatīti katvā karato jāto kāyo  tadupanissayo catusantatirūpasamudāyo kāmaṃ nāmakāyopi vivekajena pītisukhena tathāladdhūpakāro abhisandetītiādivacanato pana rūpakāyo idha adhippetoti āha nti ti abhisandanaṃ karoti taṃ pana abhisandanaṃ jhānamayena pītisukhena karajakāyassa tintabhāvāpādanaṃ sabbatthakameva lūkhabhāvāpanayananti āha tiādi tayidaṃ abhisandanaṃ atthato yathāvuttapītisukhasamuṭṭhānehi paṇītarūpehi kāyassa parippharaṇaṃ daṭṭhabbaṃ tiādīsupi eseva nayo sabbaṃ etassa atthīti sabbāvā tassa  avayavāvayavisambandhe avayavini sāmivacananti avayavavisayo -saddo tasmā vuttaṃ ti  yattha yattha kammajarūpaṃ tattha tattha cittajarūpassa abhibyāpanato tenāha tiādi
ti  kusalo taṃ panassa kosallaṃ nahānīyacuṇṇānaṃ karaṇe piṇḍikaraṇe ca samatthatāvasena veditabbanti āha tiādi -saddo mahatiyā kaṃsapātiyātiādīsu  suvaṇṇe āgatokaṃso upahato yathātiādīsu  kittimalohe katthaci paṇṇattimatte upakaṃso nāma rājāsi mahākaṃsassa atrajotiādi  idha pana yattha katthaci loheti āha ti ti udakasinehena anuppavisanavasena gatā upagatā ti udakasinehena parito gatā samantato phuṭā tato eva  etena sabbaso udakena temitabhāvamāha ti etena tintassapi tassa ghanathaddhabhāvaṃ vadati tenāha tiādi
tāhi tāhi udakasirāhi ubbhijjatīti ubbhidaṃ ubbhidaṃ udakaṃ etassāti ti nadītīre khatakūpako viya ubbhijjanakaudako ti dhārāvasena uṭṭhahanaudako kasmā panettha ubbhidodakova  rahado gahito na itaroti āha tiādi ti dhārānipātehi ca udakabubbuḷehi ca pheṇapaṭalehi cāti vattabbaṃ  aparikkhobhatāya niccalameva suppasannamevāti adhippāyo nti abhisandetītiādikaṃ
ti uppalagacchāni ti uppalesu setuppalarattuppalanīluppalesūti attho  sāmaññaggahaṇato nti ettha -saddo bahupariyāyo satagghītiādīsu viya tena anekasatapattassapi saṅgaho siddho hoti loke pana rattaṃ padumaṃ setaṃ puṇḍarīkanti vuccati yāva aggā yāva ca mūlā udakena abhisandanādisambhavadassanatthaṃ ggahaṇaṃ idha uppalādīni viya karajakāyo udakaṃ viya tatiyajjhānasukhaṃ
