

 idāni  asañciccakathā nāma hoti tattha ānantariyavatthūni nāma garūni bhāriyāni tasmā asañciccāpi tesu vatthūsu vikopitesu ānantariko hotīti yesaṃ laddhi seyyathāpi ekaccānaṃ uttarāpathakānaṃ te sandhāya ti pucchā sakavādissa laddhivasena paṭiññā itarassa atha naṃ yasmā ānantariyakammaṃ nāma kammapathappattaṃ yadi ca asañcicca kammapathabhedo siyā avasesā pāṇātipātādayopi asañcicca bhaveyyunti codanatthaṃ tiādimāha itaro tathārūpāya laddhiyā abhāvena paṭikkhipati sesaṃ yathāpāḷimeva niyyāti ti pucchā paravādissa rogapaṭikārādikāle asañcicca ghātaṃ sandhāya paṭiññā sakavādissa ti pañhepi asañcicca voropitaṃ sandhāya paṭiññā sakavādisseva evaṃ adhippāyaṃ pana aggahetvā hañcīti laddhipatiṭṭhāpanaṃ itarassa taṃ ayoniso patiṭṭhāpitattā appatiṭṭhitameva pitughātakādīsupi eseva nayo saṅghabhedake pana dhammasaññiṃ sandhāya ti pucchā sakavādissa saṅghaṃ samaggaṃ bhetvāna kappaṃ  nirayamhi paccatīti vacanaṃ ayoniso gahetvā paṭiññā paravādissa puna ti puṭṭho sakapakkhe dhammasaññiṃ sandhāya paṭikkhipati parapakkhe dhammasaññiṃ sandhāya paṭijānāti ti  pañhadvayepi eseva nayo ti suttaṃ ekanteneva dhammavādissa ānantarikabhāvadassanatthaṃ vuttaṃ ti gāthāyapi adhammavādīyeva adhippeto itaro pana adhippāyaṃ aggahetvā laddhiṃ patiṭṭhapeti sā ayoniso patiṭṭhāpitattā appatiṭṭhitāyevāti


 idāni  ñāṇakathā nāma hoti tattha duvidhaṃ ñāṇaṃ  lokiyañca lokuttarañca lokiyaṃ samāpattiñāṇampi hoti dānādivasena pavattaṃ kammassakatañāṇampi lokuttaraṃ saccaparicchedakaṃ maggañāṇampi phalañāṇampi imaṃ pana vibhāgaṃ akatvā saccaparicchedakameva ñāṇaṃ na itaraṃ tasmā natthi puthujjanassa ñāṇanti yesaṃ laddhi seyyathāpi hetuvādānaṃ te sandhāya pucchā sakavādissa paṭiññā itarassa tiādi ñāṇavevacanadassanatthaṃ vuttaṃ tenetaṃ dīpeti  yadi tassa ñāṇaṃ natthi paññādayopi natthi atha paññādayo atthi ñāṇampi atthi kasmā paññādīnaṃ ñāṇato anaññattāti ntiādi samāpattiñāṇassa dassanatthaṃ vuttaṃ tiādi kammassakatañāṇassa ti lokuttaramaggañāṇameva dīpeti na ca lokuttarameva ñāṇanti


 idāni  nirayapālakathā nāma hoti tattha niraye nerayikakammāneva nirayapālarūpavasena vadhenti natthi nirayapālā nāma sattāti  yesaṃ laddhi seyyathāpi andhakānaṃ te sandhāya pucchā sakavādissa paṭiññā itarassa atha naṃ yadi tattha nirayapālā na siyuṃ kammakāraṇāpi na bhaveyyuṃ kāraṇikesu hi sati kāraṇāti codetuṃ tiādimāha
ti paccakkhena ñāpanatthaṃ yathā hi manussesu sati kāraṇikesu kāraṇā evaṃ tatthāpīti ayamettha adhippāyo ti pucchā paravādissa paṭiññā itarassa ti paravādinā sakasamayato suttaṃ ābhataṃ taṃ pana sāsanāvacarikanti sakavādinā anuññātaṃ tattha ti eko devo ti pettivisaye petamahiddhiko dayo pākaṭā eva idaṃ vuttaṃ hoti  attano kammehi ito paṇunnaṃ  paralokaṃ pattaṃ purisaṃ na ete vessabhūādayo hananti yehi pana so kammehi tattha paṇunno tāni sakāni kammāniyeva naṃ tattha hanantīti kammassakataṃ dīpeti na nirayapālānaṃ abhāvaṃ sakavādinā pana ti ābhatāni suttapadāni nītatthānevāti


 idāni tiracchānakathā nāma hoti tattha devesu erāvaṇādayo devaputtā hatthivaṇṇaṃ assavaṇṇaṃ vikubbanti natthi tattha tiracchānagatā yesaṃ pana tiracchānavaṇṇino devaputte disvā atthi devesu tiracchānagatāti laddhi seyyathāpi andhakānaṃ  te sandhāya pucchā sakavādissa paṭiññā itarassa atha naṃ yadi devayoniyaṃ tiracchānagatā siyuṃ tiracchānayoniyampi devā siyunti codetuṃ tiādimāha tiādi yesaṃ so abhāvaṃ icchati te dassetuṃ vuttaṃ ti pañhe tassa atthitāya paṭiññā sakavādissa  na tiracchānagatassa tiādi yadi tattha hatthiādayo siyuṃ hatthibandhādayopi siyunti codanatthaṃ vuttaṃ tattha ti yavassa dāyakā ti hatthācariyādayo yehi te nānāvidhaṃ kāraṇaṃ kareyyuṃ ti hatthiādīnaṃ bhattarandhakā nti tathā anicchanto paṭikkhipatīti


 idāni maggakathā nāma hoti tattha yesaṃ pubbeva kho panassa kāyakammaṃ vacīkammaṃ ājīvo suparisuddho hotīti (a ni 3431) idañceva suttaṃ sammāvācākammantājīvānañca  cittavippayuttataṃ nissāya nippariyāyena pañcaṅgikova maggoti laddhi seyyathāpi mahisāsakānaṃ te sandhāya ti pucchā sakavādissa paṭiññā itarassa ādi parasamayavasena vuttaṃ parasamayasmiñhi sammāvācādayo maggaṅganti āgatā rūpattā pana maggo na hotīti vaṇṇitā ntiādi maggaṅgassa amaggatā nāma natthīti dassanatthaṃ vuttaṃ ti sutte parisuddhasīlassa maggabhāvanā nāma hoti na itarassāti āgamaniyapaṭipadāya visuddhibhāvadassanatthaṃ kāyakammaṃ vacīkammaṃ ājīvo suparisuddho hotīti vuttaṃ na imehi vinā pañcaṅgikabhāvadassanatthaṃ tenevāha  evamassāyaṃ ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvanāpāripūriṃ gacchatīti sakavādinā ābhatasuttaṃ nītatthamevāti


 idāni ñāṇakathā nāma hoti tattha dhammacakkappavattane dvādasākārañāṇaṃ sandhāya dvādasavatthukaṃ ñāṇaṃ lokuttaranti yesaṃ laddhi seyyathāpi  pubbaseliyānaṃ te sandhāya pucchā sakavādissa paṭiññā itarassa atha naṃ sace taṃ dvādasavatthukaṃ dvādasahi maggañāṇehi bhavitabbanti codetuṃ tiādimāha itaro maggassa ekattaṃ sandhāya paṭikkhipati ekekasmiṃ sacce saccañāṇakiccañāṇakatañāṇānaṃ vasena ñāṇanānattaṃ sandhāya paṭijānāti tiādīsupi eseva nayo ti suttaṃ saddhiṃ pubbabhāgaparabhāgehi ñāṇanānattaṃ dīpeti na ariyamaggassa dvādasa ñāṇataṃ tasmā asādhakanti
