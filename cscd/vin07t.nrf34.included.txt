

 vattakkhandhake nti ātape pattharitabbaṃ pāḷiyaṃ ti vandanatthāya vassaṃ pucchanena navako sayameva vandatīti vuttaṃ ti oloketabbo


 yathābhāganti ṭhapitaṭṭhānaṃ anatikkamitvā mañcapīṭhādiṃ papphoṭetvā pattharitvā upari paccattharaṇaṃ datvā dānampi senāsanapaññāpanamevāti dassento āha ti


ti anumodanatthāya nisinne ti anumodakaṃ āgametuṃ na bhāro ti attanā ajjhiṭṭhehi bhikkhūhi anumodanteyeva nisīditabbanti attho


nti yattha antovihārepi manussā saputtadārā āvasitvā bhikkhū netvā bhojenti ti nisīdanaṭṭhānesu  santesu nti āsanne samabhūmibhāge paññattaṃ therāsanena samakaṃ āsanaṃ sandhāya vuttaṃ therāsanato pana uccatare āpucchitvāpi nisīdituṃ na vaṭṭati yadi taṃ āsannampi nīcataraṃ hoti anāpucchāpi nisīdituṃ vaṭṭati ti āsanena paṭibāhanāpattiyā āpatti ti pārutasaṅghāṭiṃ avattharitvā anukkhipitvāti attho
pāḷiyaṃ ti idaṃ hatthatale vā pacchipiṭṭhiādidussaṇṭhitādhāre vā pattaṃ ṭhapetvā odanassa gahaṇakāle pattassa apatanatthāya vuttaṃ susajjite pana ādhāre pattaṃ ṭhapetvā ekena hatthena taṃ parāmasitvāpi odanaṃ paṭiggahetuṃ vaṭṭati eva nti etthāpi eseva nayo
nti bhojanāvasāne udakaṃ tenāha ti tena pariyosāne dhovanameva paṭikkhittaṃ bhojanantare pana pānīyapivanādinā nayena hatthaṃ dhovitvā puna bhuñjituṃ vaṭṭatīti dasseti potthakesu pana pānīyaṃ pivitvā hatthā na dhovitabbāti likhanti taṃ purimavacanena na sameti pariyosāne udakasseva hatthadhovanaudakanti vuttattā tiādi niṭṭhitabhattaṃ nisinnaṃ theraṃ sandhāya vuttaṃ  dvārasamīpe


 pāḷiyaṃ ti tiṭṭha bhanteti vadanti
ti nakkhattapadavisayāni ñātāni atthi assayujādinakkhattaṃ jānāthāti adhippāyo tenāha ti nti etthāpi eseva nayo nti kena nakkhattena cando yuttoti attho
ti  abbhokāse nti uddesadānādi āpucchitabbanti dasseti
ti attanova nirantaragamanatthāya ūhaditāti ettha hada-dhātussa vaccavissajjanatthatāyāha ti
