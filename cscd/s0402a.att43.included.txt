

 tatiyavaggassa  paṭhame nti aviraddhappaṭipadaṃ ti kāraṇañcassa paripuṇṇaṃ hoti ti arahattatthāya dutiyaṃ uttānameva

 tatiye ti aguṇo ti katheti pākaṭaṃ karoti ti pañhatthāya abhinīto ti aparihīnaṃ alambitaṃ katvā ettha ca asappuriso pāpicchatāya attano avaṇṇaṃ chādeti sappuriso lajjitāya attano vaṇṇaṃ idāni yasmā asappuriso hirottapparahito saṃvāsena avajānāti sappuriso pana hirottappasamannāgato saṃvāsenāpi nāvajānāti tasmā asappurisabhāvasādhakaṃ adhunāgatavadhukopammaṃ dassetuṃ tiādimāha tattha ti suṇisā nti bahalaṃ sesamettha uttānatthamevāti

 catutthe nti aggappattaṃ uttamasīlaṃ eseva nayo sabbattha pañcame nti yaṃ rūpaṃ sammasitvā arahattaṃ pāpuṇāti idaṃ rūpaggaṃ nāma sesesupi eseva nayo nti ettha pana yasmiṃ attabhāve ṭhito arahattaṃ pāpuṇāti etaṃ bhavaggaṃ nāmāti

 chaṭṭhe ti pācīnagatāya sālapantiyā uttarena nivattitvā ṭhitāya vemajjhaṭṭhāne nti dvinnaṃ sālarukkhānaṃ antare ti dveḷhakaṃ ti vinicchituṃ asamatthatā buddho nu kho na buddho nu kho dhammo nu kho na dhammo nu kho saṅgho nu kho na saṅgho nu kho maggo nu kho na maggo nu kho paṭipadā nu kho na paṭipadā nu khoti  yassa saṃsayo uppajjeyya taṃ vo vadāmi pucchatha bhikkhaveti ayamettha saṅkhepattho ti mayaṃ satthu santike pabbajimha cattāro paccayāpi no satthu santakāva te mayaṃ ettakaṃ kālaṃ kaṅkhaṃ akatvā na arahāma ajja pacchime kāle kaṅkhaṃ kātunti sace evaṃ satthari gāravena na pucchatha ti tumhākaṃ yo yassa bhikkhussa sandiṭṭho sambhatto so tassa ārocetu ahaṃ ekassa bhikkhussa kathessāmi tassa kathaṃ sutvā sabbe nikkaṅkhā bhavathāti dasseti ti evaṃ saddahāmi ahanti attho ti nikkaṅkhabhāvapaccakkhakaraṇañāṇaṃyeva ettha tathāgatassa na saddhāmattanti attho ti imesaṃ antosāṇiyaṃ nisinnānaṃ pañcannaṃ bhikkhusatānaṃ ti yo guṇavasena pacchimako ānandattheraṃyeva sandhāyāha

 sattame ti cintetuṃ ayuttāni ti acinteyyattāyeva na cintetabbāni ti yāni kāraṇāni cintento ti ummattakabhāvassa ti dukkhassa ti buddhānaṃ visayo sabbaññutaññāṇādīnaṃ buddhaguṇānaṃ pavatti ca ānubhāvo ca ti abhiññājhānavisayo ti  diṭṭhadhammavedanīyādīnaṃ kammānaṃ vipāko ti kena nu kho candimasūriyā katā kena mahāpathavī kena mahāsamuddo kena sattā uppāditā kena pabbatā kena ambatālanāḷikerādayoti evarūpā lokacintā

 aṭṭhame ti dānasaṅkhātāya dakkhiṇāya visujjhanakāraṇāni ti mahapphalabhāvena visujjhati mahapphalā hotīti attho ti sucidhammo ti lāmakadhammo ti ettha vessantaramahārājā kathetabbo so hi jūjakabrāhmaṇassa dārake datvā mahāpathaviṃ kampesi ti ettha kalyāṇīnadīmukhadvāravāsī kevaṭṭo kathetabbo so kira dīghasumattherassa tikkhattuṃ piṇḍapātaṃ datvā maraṇamañce nipanno ayyassa maṃ dīghasumattherassa dinnapiṇḍapāto uddharatīti āha ti ettha vaḍḍhamānavāsī luddako kathetabbo so kira petadakkhiṇaṃ dento ekassa dussīlasseva tayo vāre adāsi tatiyavāre amanusso dussīlo maṃ vilumpatīti viravi ekassa sīlavato bhikkhuno datvā pāpitakāleyevassa pāpuṇi ti ettha asadisadānaṃ kathetabbanti

 navame ti taṃsadisāva taṃsarikkhakāva ti chedaṃ gacchati yaṃ patthitaṃ taṃ sabbaṃ nassatīti attho ti yathājjhāsayā na hoti ti parajjhāsayā ajjhāsayato adhikataraphalā hoti ti ettha samitapāpabāhitapāpatāhi samaṇabrāhmaṇatā veditabbā ti bhante catubbidhena cīvarādinā paccayena vadeyyāsīti evaṃ pavāreti nimanteti ti paricchinditvā yattakena pavāreti ti taṃ sabbasova na detiti yathā tassa ajjhāsayo evaṃ dātuṃ na sakkoti hāpetvā appakaṃ deti ti yattakaṃ so icchati tattakameva deti ti appakaṃ pavāretvā avattharitvā bahuṃ deti

 dasame ti vinicchayakaraṇatthaṃ vinicchayasabhāyaṃ neva nisīdati  ti kasivaṇijjādimahākammantaṃ nappayojeti ti bhoge sambharaṇatthāya kambojaraṭṭhaṃ na gacchati desanāmattameva cetaṃ yaṃ kiñci tiroraṭṭhaṃ na gacchatīti attho tiādīsu kodhanatāya kodhapariyuṭṭhito atthānatthaṃ na jānāti issukitāya parasampattiṃ na sahati maccharitāya  dhanaṃ datvā kiccaṃ kātuṃ na sakkoti nippaññatāya kiccaṃ saṃvidhātuṃ na sakkoti tasmā etāni sabhānisīdanādīni na karotīti
