

 navamassa paṭhame nti sītalabhāvaṃ ntiādīsu uddhaccasamaye cittaṃ samādhinā niggahetabbaṃ nāma kosajjānupatitakāle vīriyena paggahetabbaṃ nāma nirassādagatakāle samādhinā sampahaṃsitabbaṃ nāma samappavattakāle bojjhaṅgupekkhāya ajjhupekkhitabbaṃ nāma

 dutiye ti pañcānantariyakammehi ti niyatamicchādiṭṭhiyā ti akusalavipākapaṭisandhiyā vā kusalavipākehi ahetukapaṭisandhiyā vāti

 catutthe nti avaḍḍhiṃ ti vaḍḍhiatthaṃ chaḍḍeti ti  sāsanassa ananulomikāya pañcame nti sotāpattimaggaṃ

 aṭṭhame nti anupagantabbaṃ kāraṇaṃ pañcannaṃ verānaṃ dvāsaṭṭhiyā ca diṭṭhigatānametaṃ adhivacanaṃ nti kāmāvacare aṭṭhamaṃ paṭisandhiṃ navame ti diṭṭhasutamutamaṅgalena  dasame ntiādīni attadiṭṭhivasena vuttāni nti ahetunibbattaṃ sesaṃ sabbattha uttānamevāti
