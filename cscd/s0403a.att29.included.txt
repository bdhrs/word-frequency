

 catutthassa  paṭhame ti sattavidhassa sekhassa puthujjane pana vattabbameva natthi ti uparūpariguṇaparihānāya

 dutiye ti satthari garubhāvo ti navavidhe lokuttaradhamme garubhāvo ti saṅghe garubhāvo ti tīsu sikkhāsu garubhāvo ti appamāde garubhāvo ti dhammāmisavasena duvidhe paṭisanthāre garubhāvo satthā garu assāti  dhammo garu assāti ti bahalagāravo paṭisanthāre  gāravo assāti  tatiye ti sajeṭṭhako sagāravo hirottappaṃ panettha missakaṃ kathitaṃ

 catutthe ti therasseva saddhivihāriko ti ijjhanaṭṭhena mahatī iddhi assāti mahiddhiko anupharaṇaṭṭhena mahā ānubhāvo assāti mahānubhāvo ti evarūpaṃ loke pakatiyā piyasamudāhāravacanaṃ hoti lokiyā  hi cirassaṃ āgatampi anāgatapubbampi manāpajātiyaṃ āgataṃ disvā kuto bhavaṃ āgato cirassaṃ bhavaṃ āgato kathaṃ te idhāgamanamaggo ñāto kiṃ maggamūḷhosītiādīni vadanti ayaṃ pana āgatapubbattāyeva evamāha thero hi kālena kālaṃ brahmalokaṃ gacchatiyeva tattha ti vāraṃ akāsi ti yo ayaṃ idhāgamanāya vāro taṃ cirassaṃ akāsīti vuttaṃ hoti nti  mahārahaṃ brahmapallaṅkaṃ paññāpetvā evamāha ti adhigatena acalena maggappasādena imasmiṃ sutte sotāpattimaggañāṇaṃ kathitaṃ

 pañcame ti vijjākoṭṭhāsikā ti aniccānupassanāñāṇe uppannasaññā ti dukkhānupassanāñāṇe uppannasaññā ti anattānupassanāñāṇe uppannasaññā ti pahānānupassanāñāṇe uppannasaññā ti virāgānupassanāñāṇe uppannasaññā ti nirodhānupassanāñāṇe uppannasaññā

 chaṭṭhe ti vivādassa mūlāni ti kujjhanalakkhaṇena kodhena samannāgato ti veraappaṭinissaggalakkhaṇena upanāhena samannāgato nti dvinnaṃ bhikkhūnaṃ vivādo kathaṃ devamanussānaṃ ahitāya dukkhāya saṃvattati kosambakakkhandhake viya dvīsu bhikkhūsu vivādaṃ āpannesu tasmiṃ vihāre tesaṃ antevāsikā vivadanti tesaṃ ovādaṃ gaṇhanto bhikkhunisaṅgho vivadati tato tesaṃ upaṭṭhākā vivadanti atha manussānaṃ ārakkhadevatā  dve koṭṭhāsā honti tathā dhammavādīnaṃ ārakkhadevatā dhammavādiniyo honti adhammavādīnaṃ adhammavādiniyo tato ārakkhadevatānaṃ mittā bhummadevatā bhijjanti evaṃ paramparāya yāva brahmalokā ṭhapetvā ariyasāvake sabbe devamanussā dve koṭṭhāsā honti dhammavādīhi pana adhammavādinova bahutarā honti tato yaṃ bahukehi gahitaṃ taṃ gacchanti dhammaṃ vissajjetvā bahutarāva adhammaṃ gaṇhanti te adhammaṃ purakkhatvā viharantā apāye nibbattanti evaṃ dvinnaṃ bhikkhūnaṃ vivādo devamanussānaṃ ahitāya dukkhāya hoti ti tumhākaṃ abbhantaraparisāya ti paresaṃ parisāya
ti paresaṃ guṇamakkhanalakkhaṇena makkhena samannāgato ti yugaggāhalakkhaṇena  paḷāsena samannāgato ti parassa sakkārādīni issāyanalakkhaṇāya issāya samannāgato ti āvāsamacchariyādīhi samannāgato ti kerāṭiko ti katapaṭicchādako ti asantasambhāvanicchako dussīlo ti natthikavādī ahetuvādī akiriyavādī ti sayaṃ diṭṭhameva parāmasati ti daḷhaggāhī ti na sakkā hoti gahitaṃ vissajjāpetuṃ imasmiṃ sutte vaṭṭameva kathitaṃ

 sattame ti veḷukaṇḍakanagaravāsinī nti chahi guṇaṅgehi samannāgataṃ ti dānaṃ deti ti dānaṃ dassāmīti māsaḍḍhamāsato paṭṭhāya  somanassappatto hoti ettha hi pubbecetanā dassāmīti cittuppādakālato paṭṭhāya ito uṭṭhitena dānaṃ dassāmīti khettaggahaṇaṃ ādiṃ katvā cintentassa labbhati ti evaṃ vuttā muñcacetanā pana dānakāleyeva labbhati ti ayaṃ pana aparacetanā aparāparaṃ anussarantassa labbhati ti vigatarāgā khīṇāsavā ti rāgavinayapaṭipadaṃ paṭipannā ukkaṭṭhadesanā cesā na kevalaṃ pana khīṇāsavānaṃ anāgāmi-sakadāgāmi-sotāpannānampi antamaso tadahupabbajitassa bhaṇḍagāhakasāmaṇerassāpi dinnā dakkhiṇā chaḷaṅgasamannāgatāva hoti sopi hi sotāpattimaggatthameva pabbajito
ti dānassa paripuṇṇatā ti sīlasaññamena saññatā ti attanāva hatthapāde dhovitvā mukhaṃ vikkhāletvā ti attano hatthehi sayehītipi pāṭho ti ratanattayaguṇe saddahanto ti lābhamacchariyādīhi vimuttena cittena nti niddukkhaṃ uḷārasukhasomanassaṃ devalokaṃ

 aṭṭhame ti akkhīni ummīletvā mā addasaṃ asukasmiṃ nāma ṭhāne vasatīti mā assosiṃ kathentassa vā vacanaṃ mā assosiṃ ti kena nāma kāraṇena ti ārabhanavasena pavattavīriyaṃ ti kosajjato nikkhamanasabhāvaṃ vīriyaṃ ti parakkamasabhāvo ti thāmasabhāvo ti ṭhitisabhāvo ti upakkamasabhāvo sabbaṃ cetaṃ tena tenākārena pavattassa vīriyasseva nāmaṃ

 navame nti vaṭṭagāmikammānaṃ ti piṇḍakaraṇatthāya nti paccayo ti lobhato jātena ti evarūpena kammena nibbattāti na dissanti sukkapakkhe nti vivaṭṭagāmikammānaṃ iti imasmiṃ sutte vaṭṭavivaṭṭaṃ kathitaṃ dasame ti mahāmucalindavane ti sāsanasaddhammo

 ekādasame ti cittavasibhāvaṃ patto ti thaddhākāraṃ pathavīdhātūti sallakkheyya ti yaṃ vijjamānaṃ thaddhākāraṃ pathavīdhātuṃ nissāya amuṃ dārukkhandhaṃ pathavītveva adhimucceyya sā ettha pathavīdhātu atthīti iminā nayena sesapadānipi veditabbāni yatheva hi tasmiṃ thaddhākārā pathavīdhātu atthi evaṃ yūsākārā āpodhātu uṇhākārā tejodhātu vitthambhanākārā vāyodhātu rattavaṇṇamhi sāre padumapupphavaṇṇā subhadhātu pūtibhūte cuṇṇe ceva pheggupapaṭikāsu ca amanuññavaṇṇā asubhadhātu taṃ nissāya amuṃ dārukkhandhaṃ asubhantveva adhimucceyya sallakkheyyāti imasmiṃ sutte missakavihāro nāma kathito

 dvādasame nti gāmantasenāsanavāsiṃ nti tasmiṃ gāmantasenāsane samādhiṃ appetvā nisinnaṃ nti idāni imaṃ ti samādhito uṭṭhāpessati ti na sakamano homi nti niddāyamānaṃ nti ekasabhāvaṃ ekaggatābhūtaṃ araññasaññaṃyeva citte karissatīti attho ti anuggaṇhissati ti aññasmiṃ kāle avimuttaṃ cittaṃ idāni pañcahi vimuttīhi vimocayissati ti vajjeti vissajjeti ti  panuditvā vissajjetvā ti uccārapassāvakaraṇatthāya iminā ettakena ṭhānena satthārā araññasenāsanassa vaṇṇo kathito suttassa pana paṭhamakoṭṭhāse yaṃ vattabbaṃ taṃ heṭṭhā vuttamevāti
