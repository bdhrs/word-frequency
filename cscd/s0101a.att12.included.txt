

ti lohiccasuttaṃ tatrāyaṃ anuttānapadavaṇṇanā ti tassa gāmassa nāmaṃ so kira vatiyā viya samantato sālapantiyā parikkhitto tasmā sālavatikāti vuccati ti tassa brāhmaṇassa nāmaṃ
nti parānukampā virahitattā lāmakaṃ na pana ucchedasassatānaṃ aññataraṃ ti jātaṃ hoti na kevalañca citte jātamattameva so kira tassa vasena parisamajjhepi evaṃ bhāsatiyeva ti paro yo anusāsīyati so tassa anusāsakassa kiṃ karissati attanā paṭiladdhaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ attanāva sakkatvā garuṃ katvā vihātabbanti vadati
ti rosikāti evaṃ itthiliṅgavasena laddhanāmaṃ nhāpitaṃ āmantesi so kira bhagavato āgamanaṃ sutvā cintesi  vihāraṃ gantvā diṭṭhaṃ nāmaṃ bhāro gehaṃ pana āṇāpetvā passissāmi ceva yathāsatti ca āgantukabhikkhaṃ dassāmīti tasmā evaṃ nhāpitaṃ āmantesi
ti kathāphāsukatthaṃ pacchato pacchato anubandho hoti ti vimocetu taṃ diṭṭhigataṃ vinodetūti vadati ayaṃ kira upāsako lohiccassa brāhmaṇassa piyasahāyako tasmā tassa atthakāmatāya evamāha ti ettha paṭhamavacanena bhagavā gajjati dutiyavacanena anugajjati ayaṃ kirettha  adhippāyo  rosike etadatthameva mayā cattāri asaṅkhyeyyāni kappasatasahassañca vividhāni dukkarāni karontena pāramiyo pūritā  etadatthameva sabbaññutaññāṇaṃ paṭividdhaṃ na me lohiccassa diṭṭhigataṃ bhindituṃ bhāroti imamatthaṃ dassento paṭhamavacanena bhagavā gajjati kevalaṃ rosike  lohiccassa mama santike āgamanaṃ vā nisajjā vā allāpasallāpo vā hotu sacepi lohiccasadisānaṃ satasahassassa kaṅkhā hoti paṭibalo ahaṃ vinodetuṃ lohiccassa pana ekassa diṭṭhivinodane mayhaṃ ko bhāroti imamatthaṃ dassento dutiyavacanena bhagavā anugajjatīti veditabbo

ti samudayassa sañjāti bhoguppādo tato uṭṭhitaṃ dhanadhaññanti attho ti ye ñātiparijanadāsakammakarādayo janā taṃ nissāya jīvanti ti lābhantarāyakaro ti ettha nti vuḍḍhi ti anukampī icchatīti attho vuḍḍhiṃ icchati vā no vāti vuttaṃ hoti ti sace sā micchādiṭṭhi sampajjati niyatā hoti ekaṃsena niraye nibbattati no ce tiracchānayoniyaṃ nibbattatīti attho
 idāni yasmā yathā attano lābhantarāyena sattā saṃvijjanti na tathā paresaṃ tasmā suṭṭhutaraṃ brāhmaṇaṃ pavecetukāmo taṃ kiṃ maññasīti dutiyaṃ upapattimāha ti ye ca ime tathāgatassa dhammadesanaṃ sutvā ariyabhūmiṃ okkamituṃ asakkontā kulaputtā ti upayogatthe paccattavacanaṃ dibbe gabbheti attho dibbā gabbhāti ca channaṃ devalokānametaṃ adhivacanaṃ ti devalokagāminiṃ paṭipadaṃ pūrayamānā dānaṃ dadamānā sīlaṃ rakkhamānā gandhamālādīhi pūjaṃ kurumānā bhāvanaṃ bhāvayamānā pācenti vipācenti paripācenti pariṇāmaṃ gamenti ti dibbabhavā nāma devānaṃ vimānāni  tesaṃ nibbattanatthāyāti attho atha vā ti dānādayo puññavisesā ti devaloke vipākakkhandhā tesaṃ nibbattanatthāya tāni puññāni karontīti attho ti tesaṃ maggasampattiphalasampattidibbabhavavisesānaṃ antarāyakaro iti bhagavā ettāvatā aniyamiteneva opammavidhinā yāva bhavaggā  uggataṃ brāhmaṇassa mānaṃ bhinditvā idāni codanārahe tayo satthāre dassetuṃ tayo kho me lohiccātiādimāha

 tattha ti tayo satthāre codentassa codanā ti aññāya ājānanatthāya cittaṃ na upaṭṭhapenti ti nirantaraṃ tassa sāsanaṃ akatvā tato ukkamitvā vattantīti attho ti paṭikkamantiyā upagaccheyya anicchantiyā iccheyya ekāya sampayogaṃ anicchantiyā eko iccheyyāti vuttaṃ hoti ti daṭṭhumpi anicchamānaṃ parammukhiṃ ṭhitaṃ pacchato gantvā āliṅgeyya nti imassāpi satthuno mama ime sāvakāti sāsanā vokkamma vattamānepi te lobhena anusāsato imaṃ lobhadhammaṃ evaṃsampadameva īdisameva vadāmi iti so evarūpo tava lobhadhammo yena tvaṃ osakkantiyā ussakkanto viya parammukhiṃ āliṅganto viya ahosītipi taṃ codanaṃ arahati ti yena dhammena pare anusāsi attānameva tāva tattha sampādehi ujuṃ karohi kiñhi paro parassa karissatīti codanaṃ arahati
nti sassarūpakāni tiṇāni uppāṭetvā parisuddhaṃ kātabbaṃ
 tatiyacodanāya ti anusāsanaṃ asampaṭicchanakālato paṭṭhāya paro anusāsitabbo parassa  anusāsakassa kiṃ karissatīti nanu tattha appossukkataṃ āpajjitvā attanā paṭividdhadhammaṃ attanāva mānetvā pūjetvā vihātabbanti evaṃ codanaṃ arahatīti attho

ti ayañhi yasmā paṭhamameva attānaṃ patirūpe patiṭṭhāpetvā sāvakānaṃ dhammaṃ deseti sāvakā cassa assavā hutvā yathānusiṭṭhaṃ paṭipajjanti tāya ca paṭipattiyā mahantaṃ visesamadhigacchanti tasmā na codanārahoti
ti mayā gahitāya diṭṭhiyā ahaṃ narakapapātaṃ papatanto ti taṃ diṭṭhiṃ bhinditvā dhammadesanāhatthena apāyapatanato uddharitvā saggamaggathale ṭhapitomhīti vadati sesamettha uttānamevāti
