

 sattamassa  paṭhamādīni uttānatthāni tatiye abhisamācāre uttamasamācāre bhavaṃ  vattappaṭipattivattaṃ tenāha ntiādi nti sekhiyavasena paññattasīlaṃ


 catutthe gaṇena saṅgaṇaṃ samodhānaṃ gaṇasaṅgaṇikā sā āramitabbaṭṭhena ārāmo etassāti ti vā sakaparisasamodhānaṃ ti nānājanasamodhānaṃ sesamettha suviññeyyameva pañcamaṃ uttānatthameva


 chaṭṭhe  paṭippassambhanaṃ paṭippassaddhīti atthato ekanti āha ti sukkapakkhe tiādīsu aṅgasantatāya ārammaṇasantatāya sabbakilesasantatāya ca santena atappaniyaṭṭhena  kilesappaṭippassaddhiyā laddhattā kilesappaṭippassaddhibhāvaṃ vā laddhattā  passaddhikilesena vā arahatā laddhattā  ekodibhāvena adhigatattā ti evamattho daṭṭhabbo


 sattame ti tasmiṃ tasmiṃ sacchikātabbe visese sakkhibhāvāya paccakkhakāritāya bhabbo sakkhibhabbo tassa bhāvo sakkhibhabbatā taṃ ti sati satikāraṇe kiñcettha kāraṇaṃ abhiññā vā abhiññāpādakajjhānaṃ vā avasāne pana chaṭṭhābhiññāya arahattaṃ vā kāraṇaṃ arahattassa vipassanā vāti veditabbaṃ yañhi taṃ tatra tatra sakkhibhabbatāsaṅkhātaṃ iddhividhapaccanubhavanādi tassa abhiññā kāraṇaṃ atha iddhividhapaccanubhavanādi abhiññā evaṃ sati abhiññāpādakajjhānaṃ kāraṇaṃ arahattampi kudāssu nāmāhaṃ tadāyatanaṃ upasampajja viharissāmīti anuttaresu vimokkhesu pihaṃ upaṭṭhapetvā chaṭṭhābhiññaṃ nibbattentassa kāraṇaṃ idañca sabbasādhāraṇaṃ na hoti sādhāraṇavasena pana arahattassa vipassanā kāraṇaṃ atha vā ti tassa tassa visesādhigamassa upanissayasaṅkhāte kāraṇe satīti evamettha attho daṭṭhabbo
hānabhāgiyādīsu paṭhamajjhānassa lābhiṃ kāmasahagatā saññāmanasikārā samudācaranti hānabhāgiyo samādhi tadanudhammatā sati santiṭṭhati ṭhitibhāgiyo samādhi avitakkasahagatā saññāmanasikārā samudācaranti visesabhāgiyo samādhi nibbidāsahagatā saññāmanasikārā samudācaranti virāgūpasaṃhitā nibbedhabhāgiyo samādhīti (vibha 799) iminā nayena sabbasamāpattiyo vitthāretvā hānabhāgiyādiattho veditabbo tattha nti  yvāyaṃ appaguṇassa paṭhamassa jhānassa lābhī taṃ ti tato vuṭṭhitaṃ ārammaṇavasena kāmasahagatā hutvā saññāmanasikārā samudācaranti tudanti tassa kāmānatītassa kāmānupakkhandānaṃ saññāmanasikārānaṃ vasena so paṭhamajjhānasamādhi hāyati parihāyati tasmā  vutto ti tadanurūpasabhāvo ti idaṃ micchāsatiṃ sandhāya vuttaṃ yassa hi paṭhamajjhānānurūpasabhāvā paṭhamajjhānaṃ santato paṇītato disvā assādayamānā abhinandamānā nikanti hoti tassa nikantivasena so paṭhamajjhānasamādhi neva hāyati na vaḍḍhati ṭhitikoṭṭhāsiko hoti tena vuttaṃ ti ti avitakkaṃ dutiyajjhānaṃ santato paṇītato manasikaroto ārammaṇavasena avitakkasahagatā ti paguṇapaṭhamajjhānato vuṭṭhitaṃ dutiyajjhānādhigamatthāya codenti tudanti tassa upari dutiyajjhānānupakkhandānaṃ saññāmanasikārānaṃ vasena so paṭhamajjhānasamādhi visesabhūtassa dutiyajjhānassa uppattipadaṭṭhānatāya ti vutto
ti tameva paṭhamajjhānalābhiṃ jhānato vuṭṭhitaṃ nibbidāsaṅkhātena vipassanāñāṇena sahagatā vipassanāñāṇañhi jhānaṅgesu pabhedena upaṭṭhahantesu nibbindati ukkaṇṭhati tasmā ti vuccati ti nibbānasacchikiriyatthāya codenti tudanti ti virāgasaṅkhātena nibbānena upasaṃhitā vipassanāñāṇañhi sakkā iminā maggena virāgaṃ nibbānaṃ sacchikātunti pavattito virāgūpasaṃhitanti vuccati taṃsampayuttā saññāmanasikārā virāgūpasaṃhitā eva nāma tassa tesaṃ saññāmanasikārānaṃ vasena paṭhamajjhānasamādhi ariyamaggappaṭivedhassa padaṭṭhānatāya ti vutto hānaṃ bhajantīti  hānabhāgo vā etesaṃ atthīti  parihānakoṭṭhāsikāti attho iminā nayena  veditabbo aṭṭhamādīni uttānatthāneva
