

 sedamocanagāthāsu ti anupasampannehi saddhiṃ kātuṃ paṭikkhitto uposathādisaṃvāso eva vutto ti ettha -kāro padasandhikaro ti ca liṅgavipallāsavasena vuttaṃ pañho esoti attho -saddo vā dviliṅgo daṭṭhabbo tenāha tiādi
ti garubhaṇḍena garubhaṇḍaparivattanaṃ sandhāya ti dasa avandiyapuggale ti abhabbapuggale ti khurabhaṇḍaṃ dhāretuṃ anuññātasikkhāpadena bhikkhūhi asādhāraṇasikkhāpadoti attho
ti akkhato uddhaṃ sīse ṭhitamukhamaggepi pārājikaṃ sandhāya na vadāmi nti nābhito heṭṭhā ṭhitavaccapassāvamaggepi vivajjiya aññasmiṃ sarīrappadese methunadhammapaccayā kathaṃ pārājiko siyāti attho
nti pārājikaṃ


ti -saddena acchataratipupaṭṭādīhi tiṇapaṇṇādīhi ca paṭicchannakuṭiyo saṅgaṇhāti tādisāya hi kuṭiyā bahi ṭhatvā anto ṭhitāya itthiyā magge dussādinā santhataṃ katvā pavesentopi pārājiko siyā ti liṅge parivatte paṭiggahaṇassa vijahanato puna appaṭiggahetvā paribhuñjanāpattiṃ sandhāya vuttaṃ
pāḷiyaṃ ti vīsatiyā saṅkhātāya bhikkhū samāgatā etena sabbakammārahataṃ saṅghassa dasseti
ti  gāthāya antaravāsakena nivattho uttarāsaṅgena diguṇaṃ katvā pārutasaṅghāṭiyo iti tāni tīṇipi cīvarāni kāye gatāneva bhikkhuniyā bindumattaṃ kāḷakaṃ udakena dhovitamatte nissaggiyāni hontīti attho
ti gāthāya na mātubhūtaṃ itthiṃ haneyya na pitubhūtaṃ purisaṃ haneyya nti tañca anarahantameva haneyya etena arahantaghātakopi na hotīti dasseti ti ānantariyaṃ phusatīti attho
nti yojanavitthataṃ rukkhaṃ sandhāya vuttaṃ
ti bhikkhunīnaṃ paññattasattarasasaṅghādisesesu
