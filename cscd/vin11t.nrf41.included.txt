
ti abhabbapuggalaparidīpakesu sabbapadesu
 nassantu eteti padacchedo ti ettha sāmiatthe paccattavacanaṃ bhedapurekkhakassa bhedapurekkhakāyāti attho
ti avasesesu asaṃvāsakādidīpakesu paṭikkhepapadesu
ti vassaṃ anupagamanādipaccayā jātito ekāva dukkaṭāpatti vuttā
ti uposathakkhandhake vuttasadisā jātā āpattiyo matā adhippetā
ti cammakkhandhake vacchatariṃ gahetvā  chabbaggiyānaṃ pācitti vuttāti sambandho nti balasampannaṃ taruṇagāviṃ sā hi vacchakabhāvaṃ taritvā atikkamitvā ṭhitattā ti vuccati
ti gāvīnaṃ aṅgajātaṃ attano aṅgajātena bahi chupantassa ti gāvīnaṃ visāṇādīsu gahaṇe piṭṭhiabhiruhaṇe ca yathāha chabbaggiyā bhikkhū aciravatiyā nadiyā gāvīnaṃ tarantīnaṃ visāṇesupi gaṇhantītiādi 
 bhesajjakkhandhake ti vaccamaggapassāvamaggānaṃ sāmantā dvaṅgulamatte padese satthakammaṃ karontassa thullaccayamudīritanti yojanā yathāha  na bhikkhave sambādhassa sāmantā dvaṅgule satthakammaṃ vā vatthikammaṃ vā kāretabbaṃ yo kāreyya āpatti thullaccayassāti  ettha ca ti idaṃ satthakammaṃyeva sandhāya vuttaṃ vatthikammaṃ pana sambādheyeva paṭikkhittaṃ
na bhikkhave aññatra nimantitena aññassa bhojjayāgu paribhuñjitabbā yo paribhuñjeyya yathādhammo kāretabboti  vuttattā āha ti ettha  ca  nāma bahalayāgu piṇḍaṃ vaṭṭetvā pātabbayāgūti  vuttaṃ ti paramparabhojanapavāraṇasikkhāpadehi pācitti ti antovutthaantopakkasayaṃpakkaparibhogādīsu yathāha na bhikkhave antovutthaṃ antopakkaṃ sāmaṃpakkaṃ paribhuñjitabbaṃ yo paribhuñjeyya āpatti dukkaṭassātiādi 
ti cīvarakkhandhake
ti campeyyakkhandhake ceva kosambakakkhandhake ca ntiādīsupi eseva nayo
ti bhuttassa lahuṃ pākattāya kucchigataṃ mukhaṃ āropetvā saṇhakaraṇavasena anucālane
nti senāsanakkhandhake ti garubhaṇḍassa
ti saṅghabhedakakkhandhake
nti saṅghabhedānuvattakānaṃ ti gaṇabhojane
ti ettha sabbavattesu anādariyena hotīti seso sesaṃ uttānatthameva
