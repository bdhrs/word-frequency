

 cammakkhandhake   nti issarabhāvena ca adhipatibhāvena ca samannāgataṃ nti rājabhāvaṃ raññā kattabbakiccaṃ vā ti ettha ti tassa nāmaṃ ti gottaṃ ti rattesu pādatalesu sukhumāni añjanavaṇṇāni kammacittīkatāni lomāni jātāni honti so kira pubbe asītisahassānaṃ purisānaṃ jeṭṭhapuriso hutvā tehi saddhiṃ paccekabuddhassa vasanaṭṭhāne paṇṇasālaṃ katvā attano sassirikaṃ uṇṇapāvārakaṃ paccekabuddhassa pādehi akkamanaṭṭhāne pādapuñchanikaṃ katvā ṭhapesi temāsaṃ pana sabbeva paccekabuddhaṃ upaṭṭhahiṃsu ayaṃ tassa ca tesañca asītiyā gāmikasahassānaṃ  pubbayogo
ti tesu gāmesu vasantānaṃ kulaputtānaṃ asītisahassāni ti kenaci karaṇīyena viya na panassa kiñci karaṇīyaṃ atthi aññatra tassa dassanā rājā kira tānipi asītikulaputtasahassāni sannipātāpento evaṃ aparisaṅkanto soṇo āgamissatīti sannipātāpesi ti kasivaṇijjādīni dhammena kattabbāni mātāpitaro dhammena positabbāti evamādinā nayena idhalokahite atthe anusāsitvā ti so amhākaṃ bhagavā tumhe samparāyike atthe anusāsissatīti attho
ti bhagavantaṃ jānāpemi ti sopānassa heṭṭhā aḍḍhacandapāsāṇe nimujjitvā ti yassa tesaṃ hitakiriyatthassa bhagavā kālaṃ jānāti nti vihārapaccante chāyāyaṃ ti pasādavasena punappunaṃ manasi karonti ti bhiyyosomattāya puna visiṭṭhataraṃ dassehīti attho ti adassanaṃ hoti

ti lohitena makkhito hoti nti yattha gāvo haññanti tādisoti attho  nāma vīṇāya vādanakusalo ti vīṇāya tantiyā sare ti atiāyatā kharamucchitā ti sarasampannā ti kammakkhamā ti mandamucchanā ti majjhime sare ṭhapetvā mucchitā ti vīriyasampayuttasamataṃ adhiṭṭhāhi vīriyaṃ samathena yojehīti attho ti saddhādīnaṃ indriyānaṃ samataṃ samabhāvaṃ tattha saddhaṃ paññāya paññañca saddhāya vīriyaṃ samādhinā samādhiñca vīriyena yojayamāno indriyānaṃ samataṃ paṭivijjha ti tasmiṃ samathe sati yena ādāse mukhabimbeneva nimittena uppajjitabbaṃ  taṃ samathanimittaṃ vipassanānimittaṃ magganimittaṃ phalanimittañca gaṇhāhi nibbattehīti attho
nti arahā ahanti jānāpeyyaṃ ti cha kāraṇāni ti paṭivijjhitvā paccakkhaṃ katvā ṭhito hoti tiādi sabbaṃ arahattavasena vuttaṃ arahattañhi sabbakilesehi nikkhantattā  teheva pavivittattā  byāpajjābhāvato  upādānassa khayante uppannattā  taṇhākkhayante uppannattā  sammohābhāvato ti ca vuccati
nti paṭivedharahitaṃ kevalaṃ paṭivedhapaññāya asammissaṃ saddhāmattakaṃ nti punappunaṃ karaṇena vuḍḍhiṃ ti maggappaṭivedhena rāgassa vigatattāyeva nekkhammasaṅkhātaṃ arahattaṃ paṭivijjhitvā ṭhito hoti phalasamāpattivihāreneva viharati tanninnamānasoyeva hotīti attho sesapadesupi eseva nayo
nti  catupaccayalābhañca tesaṃyeva sukatabhāvañca vaṇṇabhaṇanañca ti icchamāno patthayamāno ti viveke adhimutto ahanti evaṃ arahattaṃ byākarotīti attho
nti sīlañca vatañca parāmasitvā gahitagahaṇamattaṃ ti  sārabhāvena jānanto ti abyāpajjaṃ arahattaṃ byākarotīti attho imināva nayena sabbavāresu attho veditabbo
ti balavanto ti etassa khīṇāsavassa cittaṃ gahetvā ṭhātuṃ na sakkonti nti amissakataṃ kilesāhi ārammaṇena saddhiṃ cittaṃ missaṃ karonti tesaṃ abhāvā amissīkataṃ nti patiṭṭhitaṃ nti acalanappattaṃ ti  tassa cittassa uppādampi vayampi passati
ti arahattaṃ paṭivijjhitvā ṭhitassa sesapadehipi arahattameva kathitaṃ ti upayogatthe sāmivacanaṃ ti cittassa ca asammohaṃ adhimuttassa nti āyatanānaṃ uppādañca vayañca disvā ti sammā hetunā nayena imāya vipassanāya paṭipattiyā phalasamāpattivasena cittaṃ vimuccati nibbānārammaṇe adhimuccati ti nibbutacittassa ti iṭṭhāniṭṭhe anunayapaṭighehi akampiyattā tādī tassa tādino

ti arahattaṃ byākaronti ti yena arahāti ñāyati so attho vutto suttattho pana suttavaṇṇanatoyeva gahetabbo ti ahaṃ arahāti evaṃ byañjanavasena attā na upanīto ti aññe pana tucchapurisā hasamānā viya asantameva aññaṃ vacanamattena santaṃ katvā byākaronti nti ekapaṭalaṃ  ti ettha dve sakaṭabhārā eko vāhoti veditabbo nti ettha cha hatthiniyo eko ca hatthīti idamekaṃ  īdisāni satta anīkāni sattahatthikaṃ anīkaṃ nāma ti dvipaṭalā ti tipaṭalā ti catupaṭalato paṭṭhāya vuccati

ti sabbāva nīlikā esa nayo dīsupi tattha ca nīlikā umāpupphavaṇṇā hoti pītikā kaṇikārapupphavaṇṇā lohitikā jayasumanapupphavaṇṇā  mañjiṭṭhikā mañjiṭṭhavaṇṇā eva kaṇhā addāriṭṭhakavaṇṇā mahāraṅgarattā satapadipiṭṭhivaṇṇā mahānāmarattā sambhinnavaṇṇā hoti paṇḍupalāsavaṇṇā kurundiyaṃ pana  padumapupphavaṇṇāti vuttā etāsu yaṃkiñci labhitvā rajanaṃ coḷakena puñchitvā vaṇṇaṃ bhinditvā dhāretuṃ vaṭṭati appamattakepi bhinne vaṭṭatiyeva
ti yāsaṃ vaddhāyeva nīlā eseva nayo sabbattha etāpi vaṇṇabhedaṃ katvā dhāretabbā ti paṇhipidhānatthaṃ tale khallakaṃ bandhitvā katā ti yonakaupāhanā vuccati yā yāvajaṅghato sabbapādaṃ paṭicchādeti ti paliguṇṭhitvā katā yā upari pādamattameva paṭicchādeti na jaṅghaṃ ti tūlapicunā pūretvā katā ti tittirapattasadisā vicittabaddhā ti kaṇṇikaṭṭhāne meṇḍakasiṅgasaṇṭhāne vaddhe yojetvā katā dīsupi eseva nayo pi tattheva vicchikanaṅguṭṭhasaṇṭhāne vaddhe yojetvā katā ti talesu vā vaddhesu vā morapiñchehi suttakasadisehi parisibbitā ti vicitrā etāsu yaṃkiñci labhitvā sace tāni khallakādīni apanetvā sakkā honti vaḷañjituṃ vaḷañjetabbā tesu pana sati vaḷañjantassa dukkaṭaṃ  nāma pariyantesu cīvare anuvātaṃ viya sīhacammaṃ yojetvā katā ti pakkhibiḷālacammaparikkhaṭā  etāsupi yā kāci labhitvā taṃ cammaṃ apanetvā dhāretabbā
nti paṭimuñcitvā apanītaṃ ti aparibhuttā

ti yena sippena abhijīvanti jīvikaṃ kappenti tassa kāraṇāti attho ti ettha tanti nipātamattaṃ idha kho bhikkhave sobheyyāthāti  attho ti ye tumhe atha vā yadi tumheti vuttaṃ hoti yadisaddassa hi atthe ayaṃ nipāto tiādimhi pabbajjācariyo upasampadācariyo nissayācāriyo uddesācariyoti ime cattāropi idha ācariyā eva avassikassa chabbasso ācariyamatto so hi catuvassakāle taṃ nissāya vacchati evaṃ ekavassassa sattavasso duvassassa aṭṭhavasso tivassassa navavasso catuvassassa dasavasso imepi ācariyamattā eva  upajjhāyassa sandiṭṭhasambhattā pana sahāyabhikkhū ye vā pana keci dasahi vassehi mahantatarā te sabbepi upajjhāyamattā nāma ettakesu bhikkhūsu anupāhanesu caṅkamantesu saupāhanassa caṅkamato āpatti
 nāma pādato khīlasadisaṃ maṃsaṃ nikkhantaṃ hoti
ti yena kenaci tiṇena katapādukā ti khajjūrīpattehi katapādukā hintālapattehipi na vaṭṭatiyeva ti kamalatiṇaṃ nāma atthi tena katapādukā usīrapādukātipi vadanti ti uṇṇāhi katapādukā ti bhūmiyaṃ suppatiṭṭhitā niccalā asaṃhāriyā
ti aṅgajāteneva aṅgajātaṃ chupanti ti anto udake daḷhaṃ gahetvā mārenti

ti  dhenuyuttena ti purisasārathinā ti goṇayuttena ti itthisārathinā ti gaṅgāmahakīḷikāya nti ettha purisayuttaṃ itthisārathi vā hotu purisasārathi vā vaṭṭati hatthavaṭṭakaṃ pana itthiyo vā vaṭṭentu purisā vā vaṭṭatiyeva ti yānaṃ abhiruhantassa sabbo kāyo calati tappaccayā nti pīṭhakasivikaṃ nti vaṃse laggetvā kataṃ paṭapotalikaṃ
ti  ettha nti pamāṇātikkantaṃ mañcaṃ nti akappiyattharaṇaṃ āsandīādīsu ti pamāṇātikkantāsanaṃ ti pādesu vāḷarūpāni ṭhapetvā kato ti dīghalomako mahākojavo caturaṅgulādhikāni kira tassa lomāni ti vānacitro uṇṇāmayattharaṇo ti uṇṇāmayo setattharaṇo ti ghanapupphako uṇṇāmayalohitattharaṇo yo āmalakapaṭṭotipi vuccati ti pakatitūlikāyeva ti sīhabyagghādirūpavicitro uṇṇāmayattharaṇo ti ekato uggatalomaṃ uṇṇāmayattharaṇaṃ uddhalomītipi pāṭho ti ubhato uggatalomaṃ uṇṇāmayattharaṇaṃ nti ratanaparisibbitaṃ koseyyakaṭṭissamayaṃ paccattharaṇaṃ  nti ratanaparisibbitaṃ kosiyasuttamayaṃ paccattharaṇaṃ suddhakoseyyaṃ pana vaṭṭati
nti soḷasannaṃ nāṭakitthīnaṃ ṭhatvā naccanayoggaṃ uṇṇāmayaattharaṇaṃ ti hatthiassapiṭṭhīsu attharaṇakaattharaṇā eva  eseva nayo ti ajinacammehi mañcappamāṇena sibbitvā katā paveṇī nti kadalīmigacammaṃ nāma atthi tena kataṃ pavarapaccattharaṇaṃ uttamapaccattharaṇanti attho taṃ kira setavatthassa upari kadalīmigacammaṃ pattharitvā sibbitvā karonti nti saha uttaracchadanena uparibaddhena rattavitānena  saddhinti attho  setavitānampi heṭṭhā akappiyapaccattharaṇe sati na vaṭṭati asati pana vaṭṭati nti sīsūpadhānañca pādūpadhānañcāti mañcassa ubhatolohitakūpadhānaṃ etaṃ na kappati yaṃ pana ekameva upadhānaṃ ubhosu passesu rattaṃ vā hoti padumavaṇṇaṃ vā citraṃ vā sace pamāṇayuttaṃ vaṭṭati mahāupadhānaṃ pana paṭikkhittaṃ

ti dīpipotako ti bhittidaṇḍakādīsu veṭhetvā bandhanti
nti abhinissāya nisīdituṃ apassayaṃ katvā nisīditunti attho ti ettha gilāno nāma yo na sakkoti anupāhano gāmaṃ pavisituṃ
ti evaṃnāmake nagare etenassa gocaragāmo vutto ti papatanāmake pabbate etenassa nivāsanaṭṭhānaṃ vuttaṃ ti tassa nāmaṃ koṭiagghanakaṃ pana kaṇṇapiḷandhanakaṃ dhāreti tasmā kuṭikaṇṇoti vuccati koṭikaṇṇoti attho nti pasādajanakaṃ nti idaṃ tasseva atthavevacanaṃ nti uttamaṃ damathañca samathañca paññañca samādhiñca kāyūpasamañca cittūpasamañcātipi attho nti sabbesaṃ visūkāyikavipphanditānaṃ upacchinnattā dantaṃ khīṇakilesanti attho nti saṃvaraguttiyā guttaṃ nti yatindriyaṃ nti āguvirahitaṃ ti  mama pabbajjādivasato paṭṭhāya tiṇṇaṃ vassānaṃ accayena nti ahaṃ upasampadaṃ alabhiṃ  ti kaṇhamattikuttarā upari vaḍḍhitakaṇhamattikāti attho ti gunnaṃ khurehi akkantabhūmito samuṭṭhitehi gokaṇṭakehi upahatā te kira gokaṇṭake ekapaṭalikā upāhanā rakkhituṃ na sakkonti evaṃ kharā honti ti imā catassopi tiṇajātiyo etehi kaṭasārake ca taṭṭikāyo ca karonti ettha ti  erakatiṇaṃ taṃ oḷārikaṃ moragūtiṇaṃ tambasīsaṃ mudukaṃ sukhasamphassaṃ tena katataṭṭikā nipajjitvā vuṭṭhitamatte puna uddhumātā hutvā tiṭṭhati majjārunā sāṭakepi karonti jantussa maṇisadiso vaṇṇo hoti ti bhisiṃ vā kaṭasārakaṃ vā paññapesi paññapetvā ca pana soṇassa āroceti  āvuso satthā tayā saddhiṃ ekāvāse vasitukāmo gandhakuṭiyaṃyeva te senāsanaṃ paññattanti
ti ayaṃ kho kālo bhaveyya ti paridassesi idañcidañca vadeyyāsīti yaṃ me upajjhāyo jānāpesi tassa ayaṃ kālo bhaveyya handa dāni ārocemi taṃ sāsananti ayamettha adhippāyo
ti anussāvanācariyapañcamena  ettha manussacammaṃ ṭhapetvā yena kenaci cammena upāhanā vaṭṭati upāhanakosakasatthakosakakuñcikakosakesupi eseva nayo ti ettha pana yaṃkiñci eḷakacammaṃ ajacammañca attharitvā nipajjituṃ vā nisīdituṃ vā vaṭṭati migacamme eṇīmigo vātamigo pasadamigo kuraṅgamigo migamātuko rohitamigoti  etesaṃyeva cammāni vaṭṭanti aññesaṃ pana 
makkaṭo kāḷasīho ca sarabho kadalīmigo
ye ca vāḷamigā keci tesaṃ cammaṃ na vaṭṭati
tattha ti sīhabyagghaacchataracchā na kevalañca etesaṃyeva yesaṃ pana cammaṃ vaṭṭatīti vuttaṃ te ṭhapetvā avasesā antamaso gomahiṃsasasabiḷārādayopi sabbe imasmiṃ atthe vāḷamigātveva veditabbā etesañhi sabbesaṃ cammaṃ na vaṭṭati ti yāva āharitvā vā na dinnaṃ tumhākaṃ bhante cīvaraṃ uppannanti pahiṇitvā vā nārocitaṃ tāva gaṇanaṃ na upeti sace anadhiṭṭhitaṃ vaṭṭati adhiṭṭhitañca gaṇanaṃ na upetīti attho yadā pana ānetvā vā dinnaṃ hoti uppannanti vā sutaṃ tato paṭṭhāya dasāhameva parihāraṃ labhatīti
