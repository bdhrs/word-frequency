

 pañcamassa  paṭhame ti dibbacakkhuñāṇadassanassa paṭilābhāya ti divāti evaṃ saññaṃ adhiṭṭhāti nti yathā divā ālokasaññā manasi katā tatheva taṃ rattimpi manasi karoti dutiyapadepi eseva nayo nti dibbacakkhuñāṇobhāsena sahobhāsaṃ kiñcāpi  ālokasadisaṃ kataṃ attho panettha na evaṃ sallakkhetabbo dibbacakkhuñāṇāloko hi idhādhippeto
ti pākaṭā hutvā kathaṃ pana vedanā viditā uppajjanti viditā abbhatthaṃ gacchantīti idha bhikkhu vatthuṃ pariggaṇhāti ārammaṇaṃ pariggaṇhāti tassa pariggahitavatthārammaṇatāya tā vedanā evaṃ uppajjitvā evaṃ ṭhatvā evaṃ nirujjhantīti viditā uppajjanti viditā tiṭṭhanti viditā abbhatthaṃ gacchanti nāma pi eseva nayo
ti udayañca vayañca passanto nti evaṃ rūpaṃ ettakaṃ rūpaṃ na ito paraṃ rūpaṃ atthīti ti evaṃ rūpassa uppādo ti pana bhedo adhippeto pi eseva nayo nti bhikkhave yaṃ mayā etaṃ puṇṇakapañhe saṅkhāya lokasmintiādi bhāsitaṃ taṃ idaṃ phalasamāpattiṃ sandhāya bhāsitanti attho
tattha ti ñāṇena jānitvā nti sattaloke ti uccāvacāni uttamādhamāni nti calitaṃ ti  lokasmiṃ katthaci ekakkhandhepi ekāyatanepi ekadhātuyāpi ekārammaṇepi natthi ti paccanīkakilesavūpasamena santo ti  kodhadhūmena vigatadhūmo evamettha suttante maggekaggatampi kathetvā gāthāya phalasamāpattiyeva kathitāti

 dutiye nti yo etesaṃ pañhānaṃ tasmiṃ tasmiṃ ṭhāne byākaraṇaṃ jānāti nti tathāvidhaṃ bhikkhuṃ tesu catūsu pañhesu kusaloti evaṃ vadanti ti parehi ghaṭṭetuṃ vā abhibhavituṃ vā na sakkā ti sattasīdantaramahāsamuddo viya gambhīro ti dummocāpayo gahitaggahaṇaṃ vissajjāpetuṃ na sakkāti attho ti vaḍḍhiyañca avaḍḍhiyañca ti atthasamāgamena ti dhitisampanno puggalo paṇḍito ayanti evaṃ pavuccati

 tatiye ti kodhaṃ gāravena garuṃ katvā gaṇhāti na saddhammaṃ saddhammaṃ pana agāravena lāmakaṃ katvā gaṇhāti sesapadesupi eseva nayo
ti vaḍḍhanti sañjātamūlāya vā saddhāya patiṭṭhahanti acalā bhavanti catutthe ti kodhamhi sagāravatā esa  nayo sabbattha

 pañcame ti cakkavāḷalokassa ekokāse bhummaṃ ti idaṃ aparāparaṃ cutipaṭisandhivasena gahitaṃ ti padagamanena nti satthā saṅkhāralokassa antaṃ sandhāya vadati ntiādīsu ñātabbaṃ daṭṭhabbaṃ pattabbanti attho iti devaputtena cakkavāḷalokassa anto pucchito satthārā saṅkhāralokassa kathito  so pana attano  pañhena saddhiṃ satthu byākaraṇaṃ sametīti saññāya sampahaṃsanto ntiādimāha
ti daḷhadhanu uttamappamāṇena dhanunā samannāgato ti dhanuācariyo ti dvādasa vassāni dhanusippaṃ sikkhito ti usabhappamāṇepi vālaggaṃ vijjhituṃ samatthabhāvena katahattho ti katasarakkhepo dassitasippo ti kaṇḍena ti atikkameyya yāvatā so tālacchādiṃ atikkameyya tāvatā kālena ekaṃ cakkavāḷaṃ atikkamāmīti attano javasampattiṃ dasseti
ti yathā puratthimā samuddā  pacchimasamuddo dūre evaṃ me dūre padavītihāro ahosīti vadati so kira pācīnacakkavāḷamukhavaṭṭiyaṃ ṭhito pādaṃ pasāretvā pacchimacakkavāḷamukhavaṭṭiṃ atikkamati puna dutiyapādaṃ pasāretvā paracakkavāḷamukhavaṭṭiṃ atikkamati nti icchā eva ti nippapañcataṃ dasseti bhikkhācārakāle kiresa nāgalatādantakaṭṭhaṃ khāditvā anotatte mukhaṃ dhovitvā kāle sampatte uttarakurumhi piṇḍāya caritvā cakkavāḷamukhavaṭṭiyaṃ nisinno bhattakiccaṃ karoti tattha muhuttaṃ vissamitvā puna javati ti tadā dīghāyukakālo hoti ayaṃ pana vassasatāvasiṭṭhe āyumhi gamanaṃ ārabhi ti taṃ vassasataṃ anantarāyena jīvanto ti cakkavāḷalokassa antaṃ appatvā antarāva mato so pana tattha kālaṃ katvāpi āgantvā imasmiṃyeva cakkavāḷe nibbatti
ti saṅkhāralokassa antaṃ appatvā ti vaṭṭadukkhassa nti pariyantakaraṇaṃ ti attabhāve ti sasaññe sacittake nti dukkhasaccaṃ nti  samudayasaccaṃ nti nirodhasaccaṃ nti maggasaccaṃ iti nāhaṃ āvuso imāni cattāri saccāni tiṇakaṭṭhādīsu paññapemi imasmiṃ pana catumahābhūtike kāyasmiṃyeva paññapemīti dasseti ti samitapāpo ti na pattheti chaṭṭhaṃ uttānatthamevāti

 sattame ti kenaci pariyāyena anāsannāni hutvā suvidūrāneva vidūrāni ti ākāsañca mahāpathavī ca tattha kiñcāpi pathavito ākāsaṃ nāma na dūre dvaṅgulamattepi hoti aññamaññaṃ alagganaṭṭhena pana suvidūravidūreti vuttaṃ ti sūriyo ti catusatipaṭṭhānādibhedo sattatiṃsabodhipakkhiyadhammo ti dvāsaṭṭhidiṭṭhigatabhedo assaddhammo
ti ālokakaro ti avigacchanasabhāvo hoti ti paṇḍitānaṃ mittasanthavavasena samāgamo ti yattakaṃ addhānaṃ tiṭṭheyya ti tādisova hoti pakatiṃ na jahati ti sīghaṃ vigacchati

 aṭṭhame ti pañcālabrāhmaṇiyā putto ti paripuṇṇavācāya ti apalibuddhāya ti niddosāya ceva agaḷitāya ca apatitapadabyañjanāya ti vivaṭṭapariyāpannāya ti vaṭṭaṃ anissitāya vivaṭṭanissitameva katvā katheti vaṭṭanissitaṃ katvā na kathetīti ayamettha adhippāyo
nti na akathentaṃ nti nibbānapadaṃ ti obhāseyya ti tasseva vevacanaṃ nti abbhuggataṭṭhena navalokuttaradhammo isīnaṃ dhajo nāma vuccati tameva paggaṇheyya ukkhipeyya uccaṃ katvā katheyyāti attho navalokuttaradhammadīpakaṃ subhāsitaṃ dhajo etesanti ti buddhādayo ariyā ti heṭṭhā vuttanayeneva lokuttaradhammo isīnaṃ dhajo nāmāti

 navame ti saññāya vipallatthabhāvā catasso viparītasaññāyoti attho sesapadadvayepi  eseva nayo ti anicce vatthusmiṃ niccaṃ idanti evaṃ gahetvā uppajjanakasaññā saññāvipallāsoti attho iminā nayena sabbapadesu attho veditabbo
ti anattani attāti evaṃsaññinoti attho ti na kevalaṃ saññinova saññāya viya uppajjamānāya micchādiṭṭhiyāpi hatā ti te saññādiṭṭhiyo viya uppajjamānena khittena cittena samannāgatā ti desanāmattametaṃ viparītasaññācittadiṭṭhinoti attho ti te mārassa yoge yuttā nāma honti ti catūhi yogehi khemaṃ nibbānaṃ appattā ti puggalā ti catusaccabuddhā nti catusaccadhammaṃ ti sakaṃ cittaṃ paṭilabhitvā nti aniccabhāvena addasaṃsu nti asubhaṃ asubhatoyeva addasaṃsu ti gahitasammādassanā nti sakalaṃ vaṭṭadukkhaṃ samatikkantā

 dasame ti virocituṃ adatvā upakkiliṭṭhabhāvakaraṇena upakkilesā ti himaṃ ti dhūmo ca rajo ca ti purimā tayo asampattaupakkilesā rāhu pana sampattaupakkilesavasena kathitoti veditabbo ti guṇappatāpena na tapanti guṇobhāsena na bhāsanti guṇavirocanena na virocanti ti pañcavidhāya surāya catubbidhassa merayassa ca pānato aviratā
