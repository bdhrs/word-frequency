
 pañcamassa  paṭhamādīni uttānatthāneva

 chaṭṭhe nti saṃsappanassa kāraṇaṃ desanāsaṅkhātaṃ dhammadesanaṃ ti taṃ kammaṃ karonto āsappati parisappati vipphandati ti tena kammena yaṃ gatiṃ gamissati sā jimhā hoti ti tassa yaṃ  gatiṃ upapajjissati sāpi jimhāva hoti ti saṃsappanasabhāvā ti bhūtasmā sabhāvato vijjamānakammā sattassa nibbatti hoti ti vipākaphassā phusanti

 sattame nti cetetvā pakappetvā katānaṃ nti citānaṃ vaḍḍhitānaṃ ti tesaṃ kammānaṃ vipākaṃ avediyitvā nti vigatantabhāvaṃ tesaṃ kammānaṃ paricchedaparivaṭumatākaraṇaṃ ti tañca kho vipākaṃ diṭṭhadhammavedanīyaṃ diṭṭheva dhamme nti upapajjavedanīyaṃ anantare attabhāve ti aparapariyāyavedanīyaṃ pana saṃsārappavatte sati sahassimepi attabhāveti iminā idaṃ dasseti saṃsārappavatte paṭiladdhavipākārahakamme na vijjati so jagatippadeso yattha ṭhito mucceyya pāpakammāti ti  tippakārā ti kāyakammantasaṅkhātā vipatti iminā nayena sabbapadāni veditabbāni aṭṭhame ti samantato caturasso pāsako

 navame ti vipākadukkhassa vaṭṭadukkhasseva vā imasmiṃ sutte maṇiopammaṃ natthi ti nti nipātamattaṃ yathā vā mettaṃ  bhāventā vigatābhijjhā bhavanti evaṃ vigatābhijjho evamassa vigatābhijjhatādīhi nīvaraṇavikkhambhanaṃ dassetvā idāni akusalanissaraṇāni kathento tiādimāha nti appamāṇasattārammaṇatāya ciṇṇavasitāya vā appamāṇaṃ  nāma kāmāvacarakammaṃ ti taṃ mahogho parittaṃ udakaṃ viya attano okāsaṃ gahetvā ṭhātuṃ na sakkoti atha kho naṃ oghe parittaṃ udakaṃ viya idameva appamāṇaṃ kammaṃ ajjhottharitvā attano vipākaṃ nibbatteti ti daharakālato paṭṭhāya
ti imaṃ kāyaṃ gahetvā paralokaṃ gantuṃ nāma na sakkāti attho  ti cittakāraṇo atha vā citteneva antariko ekasseva hi cuticittassa anantarā dutiye paṭisandhicitte devo nāma hoti nerayiko nāma  hoti tiracchānagato nāma hoti purimanayepi cittena kāraṇabhūtena devo nerayiko vā hotīti attho nti diṭṭhadhammavedanīyakoṭṭhāsavanetaṃ vuttaṃ ti mettāya upapajjavedanīyabhāvassa upacchinnattā upapajjavedanīyavasena na anugataṃ bhavissati idaṃ sotāpannasakadāgāmiariyapuggalānaṃ paccavekkhaṇaṃ veditabbaṃ ti jhānānāgāmitāya ti imasmiṃ sāsane paññā idhapaññā nāma sāsanacaritāya ariyapaññāya ṭhitassa ariyasāvakassāti attho nti arahattaṃ dasamaṃ uttānatthamevāti
