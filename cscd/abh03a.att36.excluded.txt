21. Ekavīsatimavaggo
1. Sāsanakathāvaṇṇanā
878 .   Satipaṭṭhānā
Sāsanakathāvaṇṇanā.
2. Avivittakathāvaṇṇanā
879-880 .   Phassehī
Avivittakathāvaṇṇanā.
3. Saññojanakathāvaṇṇanā
881-882 .  Atthi kiñci sakkāyadiṭṭhī Sabbaṃ buddhavisaya tena hī
Saññojanakathāvaṇṇanā.
4. Iddhikathāvaṇṇanā
883-884 .   atthi adhippāyaiddhī adhippāyaiddhī Āmantā niccapaṇṇā rukkhā hontū suvaṇṇo ca panāsī
Iddhikathāvaṇṇanā.
5. Buddhakathāvaṇṇanā
885 .  atthi buddhāna satipaṭṭhānato
Buddhakathāvaṇṇanā.
6. Sabbadisākathāvaṇṇanā
886 .   Puratthimāyā Kinnāmo so bhagavā
Sabbadisākathāvaṇṇanā.
7. Dhammakathāvaṇṇanā
887-888 .  sabbe dhammā micchattaniyatā Rūpaṃ rūpaṭṭhenā  Rūpaṃ rūpaṭṭhena niyata micchattaniyata  Tena hi rūpa
Dhammakathāvaṇṇanā.
8. Kammakathāvaṇṇanā
889-891 . Diṭṭhadhammavedanīyaṭṭhena niyata
Kammakathāvaṇṇanā.
Ekavīsatimo vaggo.
