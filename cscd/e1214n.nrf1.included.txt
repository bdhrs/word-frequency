

 tuda byathāyaṃ  nuda kkhepaṇe likha lekhaṇe
kuca saṅkocane rica kkharaṇe khaca bandhane

 uca sadde samavāye vijī bhayacalesu 
 bhuja koṭille valañjo  valañjane

 bhaja sevāputhakkāre ruja roge aṭāṭane
kuṭacchede  koṭille agā sajjhāyanādisu

 puṇo subha kiraye vatta vattane cata yācane
putha pāke pūtibhāve kuthasaṃklesane

  putha-patha vitthāre vida jānane
hada uccāra ussagge-cintāyaṃ mida hiṃsane

 nandha vinandhane thīna-puna saṅghātavācino
kapa acchādane vappa vāraṇe khipa peraṇe

 supo saye chupo phasse  capa sāntvane
nabha  vihiṃsāyaṃ rumbha uppīḷanādisu

 sumbha saṃsumbhane jambha jambhane jubha nicchubhe
ṭhubha niṭṭhubhane camu adane chamu hīḷane

 jhamu dāhe chamu adane irīya vattane
kira  vikiraṇe giro nigiraṇādisu

 phura sañcalanādīsu kura saddādanesu 
khuracchede vilikhaṇe ghura bhīme gilādane

 tila snehe cila vāse hila hāve siluñchane
bila bhede thūla caye kusacchedana pūraṇe

 visappavese pharaṇe disātisajjanādisu
phula phasse musa theyye thusa appikirayāya 
guḷa mokkhe guḷa parivattanamhi 
hū  bhuvādayo luttavikaraṇā

 hū-bhū sattāya  i ajjhāne gatimhi 
khā-khyā  pakathane ji jaye ñāvabodhane

 sī-ḷī vehāsagamane ṭhā gatīvinivuttiyaṃ
nī pāpaṇe muna ñāṇe hana hiṃsāgatīsu 

 pārakkhaṇamhi pā pāne brū vācāyaṃ viyattiyaṃ
bhā dittiyaṃ mā pamāṇe  yā pāpuṇe 

  rā-lā ādāne vā gatīgandhanesu 
asa  bhuvi  si saye sā samatthiye
