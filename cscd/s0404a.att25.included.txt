

 tatiyassa  paṭhame ti samaṇānaṃ uppajjanakasaññā ti nirantarakārī ti niddukkho ti idamatthaṃ ime paccayāti evamassa jīvitaparikkhāresu hoti paccavekkhitaparibhogaṃ paribhuñjatīti attho dutiyaṃ uttānatthameva

 tatiye ti saggato maggato ca virajjhanaṃ hoti ti na sampādanā na paripūrakāritā hoti ti pavattati

 catutthe nti diṭṭhānurūpena paripuṇṇaṃ samādinnaṃ sakalaṃ gahitaṃ ti tīsu dvāresu nibbattitacetanāva gahitā ti evarūpo siyanti evaṃ patthanā ti  devo vā bhavissāmi devaññataro vāti cittaṭṭhapanā ti sampayuttakasaṅkhārā pañcame purecārikaṭṭhena ti taṃ anubandhamānameva

 chaṭṭhe ti nijjarakāraṇāni ti ayaṃ heṭṭhā vipassanāyapi nijjiṇṇā eva pahīnā kasmā puna gahitāti asamucchinnattā vipassanāya hi kiñcāpi nijjiṇṇā na pana samucchinnā maggo pana uppajjitvā taṃ samucchindati na puna vuṭṭhātuṃ deti tasmā puna gahitā evaṃ sabbapadesu yojetabbo ettha ca sammāvimuttipaccayā catusaṭṭhi dhammā bhāvanāpāripūriṃ gacchanti katame catusaṭṭhi sotāpattimaggakkhaṇe adhimokkhaṭṭhena  saddhindriyaṃ paripūrati paggahaṭṭhena vīriyindriyaṃ upaṭṭhānaṭṭhena satindriyaṃ avikkhepaṭṭhena samādhindriyaṃ dassanaṭṭhena paññindriyaṃ paripūreti vijānanaṭṭhena manindriyaṃ abhinandanaṭṭhena somanassindriyaṃ pavattasantatiādhipateyyaṭṭhena jīvitindriyaṃ paripūrati arahattaphalakkhaṇe adhimokkhaṭṭhena saddhindriyaṃ pavattasantatiādhipateyyaṭṭhena jīvitindriyaṃ paripūratīti evaṃ catūsu ca maggesu catūsu ca phalesu aṭṭhaṭṭha hutvā catusaṭṭhi dhammā pāripūriṃ gacchanti

 sattame nti aṭṭhidhovanaṃ tasmiñhi janapade manussā ñātake mate na jhāpenti āvāṭaṃ pana khaṇitvā bhūmiyaṃ nidahanti atha nesaṃ pūtibhūtānaṃ aṭṭhīni nīharitvā dhovitvā paṭipāṭiyā ussāpetvā gandhamālehi pūjetvā ṭhapenti nakkhatte patte tāni aṭṭhīni gahetvā rodanti paridevanti tato nakkhattaṃ kīḷanti

 aṭṭhame nti dosanīharaṇabhesajjaṃ ti nīhaṭā hoti panuditā navame nti vamanakaraṇabhesajjaṃ dasame ti niddhamitabbā ti niddhamitā

 ekādasame  aṅgaparipūraṇatthaṃ sammādiṭṭhiyeva nti vuttā evamete sabbepi arahattaphaladhammā asekhā asekhassa pavattattā paccavekkhaṇañāṇampi asekhanti vuttaṃ

 dvādasame ti asekhāyeva asekhasantakā vā iminā suttena khīṇāsavova kathitoti
