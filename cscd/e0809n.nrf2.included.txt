


 vaggā bhūmi purī macca catubbaṇṇa vanādihi
pātālena ca vuccante saṅgopaṅgehidhakkamā

 vasundharā chamā bhūmi pathavī medanī mahī
ubbī vasumatī go ku vasudhā dharaṇī dharā
puthavī jagatī bhūrī bhū ca bhūtadharā vanī

 khārā  tu mattikā  ūsavā tūsaro tisu
thalaṃ thalītthī bhūbhāge thaddhalūkhamhi 

 pubbavideho cāpara goyānaṃ jambudīpo ca
uttarakuru ceti siyuṃ cattārome 

 pumbahutte kurū sakkā kosalā magadhā sivī
kaliṅgāvanti pañcālā vajjī gandhāra cetayo

 vaṅgā videhā kambojā maddā bhaggaṅga sīhaḷā
kasmīrā kāsi paṇḍavādī siyuṃ 

 loko ca bhuvanaṃ vuttaṃ deso tu visayo pyatha
milakkhadeso paccanto majjhadeso tu majjhimo

 anūpo salilappāyo kacchaṃ puma napuṃsake
 harite dese tiṇenā bhinavena hi

 nadyambujīvano deso vuṭṭhinippajjasassako
yo 
 ca kamena so

 tīsvanūpādyatho canda sūrādo rito
raṭṭhaṃ tu vijitañcātha purise setu āliyaṃ

 upāntabhū  goṭṭhaṃ tu gokulaṃ vajo
maggo pantho patho addhā añjasaṃ vaṭumaṃ tathā

 pajjo  yanañca padavī vattanī paddhatitthiyaṃ
tabbhedā jaṅgha sakaṭa maggā te ca 

kapadike kantāro tu ca duggame

 paṭimaggo paṭipatho  dīgha mañjasaṃ
suppatho tu supantho ca uppathaṃ tvapathaṃ bhave

 chattiṃsaparamāṇūna meko  ca chattiṃsa te
 tāpi chattiṃsa  chattiṃsa te

tā satta  tā ti satta te
satta  mamu dviccha  tā duve siyuṃ

 tāni satteva yaṭṭhi tā vīsatū 
 musabhāsīti  catugāvutaṃ

 dhanupañcasataṃ  caturambaṇaṃ
 tu hatthāna maṭṭhavīsa pamāṇato
