nti  udayasuttaṃ tattha nti paññānubhāvanijjhātaṃ vimokkhaṃ pucchati atha bhagavā yasmā udayo catutthajjhānalābhī tasmāssa paṭiladdhajjhānavasena nānappakārato aññāvimokkhaṃ dassento  gāthādvayamāha tattha nti yamidaṃ paṭhamajjhānaṃ nibbattentassa kāmacchandappahānaṃ tampi aññāvimokkhaṃ pabrūmi evaṃ sabbapadāni yojetabbāni
nti catutthajjhānaupekkhāsatīhi saṃsuddhaṃ nti iminā tasmiṃ catutthajjhānavimokkhe ṭhatvā jhānaṅgāni vipassitvā adhigataṃ arahattavimokkhaṃ vadati arahattavimokkhassa hi maggasampayuttasammāsaṅkappādibhedo dhammatakko purejavo hoti tenāha  dhammatakkapurejavanti nti etameva ca aññāvimokkhaṃ avijjāpabhedanasaṅkhātaṃ nibbānaṃ nissāya jātattā kāraṇopacārena avijjāya pabhedananti pabrūmīti
 evaṃ avijjāpabhedanavacanena vuttaṃ nibbānaṃ sutvā taṃ kissa vippahānena vuccatīti pucchanto ti gāthamāha tattha ti kiṃ saṃyojano nti vicaraṇakāraṇaṃ ti kiṃ nāmakassa assa dhammassa vippahānena athassa  bhagavā tamatthaṃ byākaronto ti gāthamāha tattha ti kāmavitakkādiko vitakko assa
 idāni tassa nibbānassa maggaṃ pucchanto ti gāthamāha tattha nti abhisaṅkhāraviññāṇaṃ athassa maggaṃ kathento bhagavā ti gāthamāha tattha ti evaṃ satassa sampajānassa sesaṃ sabbattha pākaṭameva
evaṃ bhagavā imampi suttaṃ arahattanikūṭeneva desesi desanāpariyosāne ca pubbasadiso eva dhammābhisamayo ahosīti



nti posālasuttaṃ tattha ti yo bhagavā attano ca paresañca ekampi jātintiādibhedaṃ atītaṃ ādisati ti samatikkantarūpasaññissa ti tadaṅgavikkhambhanavasena sabbarūpakāyappahāyino pahīnarūpabhavapaṭisandhikassāti  adhippāyo ti viññāṇābhāvavipassanena natthi kiñcīti passato ākiñcaññāyatanalābhinoti vuttaṃ hoti ti sakkāti bhagavantaṃ ālapanto āha tassa puggalassa ñāṇaṃ pucchāmi kīdisaṃ pucchitabbanti ti kathaṃ so netabbo kathamassa uttariñāṇaṃ uppādetabbanti
 athassa bhagavā tādise puggale attano appaṭihatañāṇataṃ pakāsetvā taṃ ñāṇaṃ byākātuṃ gāthādvayamāha tattha ti abhisaṅkhāravasena catasso paṭisandhivasena sattāti evaṃ sabbā viññāṇaṭṭhitiyo abhijānanto tathāgato ti kammābhisaṅkhāravasena tiṭṭhantaṃ  etaṃ puggalaṃ jānāti āyatiṃ ayaṃ evaṃgatiko bhavissatīti nti ākiñcaññāyatanādīsu adhimuttaṃ nti tammayaṃ
ti ākiñcaññāyatanajanakaṃ kammābhisaṅkhāraṃ ñatvā kinti palibodho ayanti ti yā ca tattha arūparāgasaṅkhātā nandī tañca saṃyojanaṃ iti ñatvā ti tato ākiñcaññāyatanasamāpattito vuṭṭhahitvā taṃ samāpattiṃ aniccādivasena vipassati ti etaṃ tassa puggalassa evaṃ vipassato anukkameneva uppannaṃ arahattañāṇaṃ aviparītaṃ ti vusitavantassa sesaṃ sabbattha pākaṭameva
evaṃ bhagavā imampi suttaṃ arahattanikūṭeneva desesi desanāpariyosāne ca pubbasadiso eva dhammābhisamayo ahosīti



nti mogharājasuttaṃ tattha nti dve vāre ahaṃ so hi pubbe ajitasuttassa ca tissametteyyasuttassa ca avasāne dvikkhattuṃ bhagavantaṃ pucchi bhagavā  panassa indriyaparipākaṃ āgamayamāno na byākāsi tenāha  dvāhaṃ sakkaṃ apucchissanti nti yāvatatiyañca sahadhammikaṃ puṭṭho visuddhidevabhūto isi bhagavā sammāsambuddho byākarotīti evaṃ me sutaṃ godhāvarītīreyeva kira so evamassosi tenāha  byākarotīti me sutanti
ti manussaloko ti taṃ ṭhapetvā avaseso ti brahmalokaṃ ṭhapetvā avaseso  upapattidevasammutidevayutto brahmaloko sadevakoti etaṃ vā sadevake loketiādinayanidassanamattaṃ tena sabbopi tathāvuttappakāro loko veditabbo
 evaṃ nti evaṃ aggadassāviṃ sadevakassa lokassa ajjhāsayādhimuttigatiparāyaṇādīni passituṃ samatthanti dasseti

evaṃ bhagavā imampi suttaṃ arahattanikūṭeneva desesi desanāpariyosāne ca vuttasadiso eva dhammābhisamayo ahosīti



ti  piṅgiyasuttaṃ tattha ti so kira brāhmaṇo jarābhibhūto vīsavassasatiko jātiyā dubbalo ca idha padaṃ karissāmīti aññattheva karoti vinaṭṭhapurimacchavivaṇṇo ca  tenāha  jiṇṇohamasmi abalo vītavaṇṇoti ti māhaṃ tuyhaṃ dhammaṃ asacchikatvā antarāyeva avidvā hutvā anassiṃ nti idheva tava pādamūle pāsāṇake vā cetiya jātijarāya vippahānaṃ nibbānadhammaṃ yamahaṃ vijaññaṃ taṃ me ācikkha
 idāni yasmā piṅgiyo kāye sāpekkhatāya jiṇṇohamasmīti gāthamāha tenassa bhagavā kāye sinehappahānatthaṃ ti gāthamāha tattha ti rūpahetu rūpapaccayā ti kammakāraṇādīhi upahaññamāne ti cakkhurogādīhi ca rūpahetuyeva janā ruppanti bādhīyanti
 evaṃ bhagavatā yāva arahattaṃ tāva kathitaṃ paṭipattiṃ sutvāpi piṅgiyo jarādubbalatāya visesaṃ anadhigantvāva puna ti imāya gāthāya bhagavantaṃ thomento desanaṃ yācati athassa bhagavā punapi yāva arahattaṃ tāva paṭipadaṃ dassento ti gāthamāha sesaṃ sabbattha pākaṭameva
imampi suttaṃ bhagavā arahattanikūṭeneva desesi desanāpariyosāne ca piṅgiyo anāgāmiphale patiṭṭhāsi so kira antarantarā cintesi  evaṃ vicitrapaṭibhānaṃ nāma desanaṃ na labhi mayhaṃ mātulo bāvarī savanāyāti tena sinehavikkhepena arahattaṃ pāpuṇituṃ nāsakkhi antevāsino panassa sahassajaṭilā arahattaṃ pāpuṇiṃsu sabbeva iddhimayapattacīvaradharā ehibhikkhavo ahesunti



ito  paraṃ saṅgītikārā desanaṃ thomentā tiādimāhaṃsu tattha ti idaṃ parāyanaṃ avoca nti bāvarissa paricārakena piṅgiyena saha soḷasannaṃ buddhassa vā bhagavato paricārakānaṃ soḷasannanti paricārakasoḷasannaṃ te eva  ca  tattha soḷasaparisā pana purato ca pacchato ca vāmapassato ca dakkhiṇapassato ca cha cha yojanāni nisinnā ujukena dvādasayojanikā ahosi ti yācito ti pāḷiatthamaññāya ti pāḷimaññāya nti evaṃ imassa dhammapariyāyassa adhivacanaṃ āropetvā tesaṃ brāhmaṇānaṃ  nāmāni kittayantā nti āhaṃsu
