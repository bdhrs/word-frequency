

 abhisamayasaṃyuttassa  paṭhame nti maṃsaṭṭhānena vimutte nakhagge nakhasikhā ca nāma lokiyānaṃ mahatīpi hoti satthu pana rattuppalapattakoṭi viya sukhumā kathaṃ panettha paṃsu patiṭṭhitoti adhiṭṭhānabalena bhagavatā hi atthaṃ ñāpetukāmena adhiṭṭhānabalena tattha patiṭṭhāpito nti mahāpathaviyā paṃsuṃ satakoṭṭhāse katvā tato ekakoṭṭhāsaṃ paratopi eseva nayo ti paññāya ariyasaccāni abhisametvā ṭhitassa ti etadeva bahutaraṃ dukkhaṃ yadidaṃ parikkhīṇanti evaṃ paṭhamaṃ vuttaṃ dukkhakkhandhaṃ upanidhāya ñāṇena taṃ tassa santike ṭhapetvā upaparikkhiyamāneti attho katamaṃ panettha purimadukkhaṃ nāma yaṃ parikkhīṇaṃ katamaṃ pana parikkhīṇaṃ yaṃ paṭhamamaggassa abhāvitattā uppajjeyya katamaṃ pana upanidhāya yaṃ sattasu attabhāvesu apāye aṭṭhamañca paṭisandhiṃ ādiṃ katvā yattha katthaci uppajjeyya sabbaṃ taṃ parikkhīṇanti veditabbaṃ nti satta vāre sattasu attabhāvesūti attho ti idamassa paraṃ pamāṇanti dasseti ti mahato atthassa nipphādako paṭhamaṃ

 dutiye ti vāpī ti gambhīratāya ti mukhavaṭṭisamā ti sakkā hoti tīre ṭhitena kākena pakatiyāpi mukhatuṇḍikaṃ otāretvā pātuṃ dutiyaṃ

 tatiye ti yasmiṃ sambhijjaṭṭhāne imā ti samāgantvā sandanti ti samāgacchanti ti dve vā tīṇi vā ti udakabindūni nti aññāhi nadīhi saddhiṃ sambhinnaṭṭhāne udakaṃ catutthaṃ uttānatthameva tatiyacatutthāni
