

 bhikkhunīsaṃyuttassa  paṭhame ti āḷaviyaṃ jātā āḷavinagaratoyeva ca nikkhamma pabbajitā nti kassapasammāsambuddhassa cetiye navakammatthāya dhanaṃ samādapetvā āgacchantassa yasodharassa nāma dhammabhāṇakassa ariyapuggalassa akkhīni uppāṭetvā tattheva akkhibhedappattehi pañcahi corasatehi nivutthattā tato paṭṭhāya andhavananti saṅkhaṃ gataṃ vanaṃ taṃ kira sāvatthito dakkhiṇapasse gāvutamatte hoti rājārakkhāya guttaṃ tattha pavivekakāmā bhikkhū ca bhikkhuniyo ca gacchanti tasmā ayampi kāyavivekatthinī yena taṃ vanaṃ tenupasaṅkami nti nibbānaṃ ti paccavekkhaṇañāṇena nti tvaṃ etaṃ nibbānapadaṃ vā nibbānagāmimaggapadaṃ vā na jānāsi ti vinivijjhanatthena sattisūlasadisā ti khandhā tesaṃ adhikuṭṭanabhaṇḍikā paṭhamaṃ

 dutiye nti arahattaṃ nti duppasahaṃ ti parittapaññāya yasmā vā dvīhi aṅgulehi kappāsavaṭṭiṃ gahetvā suttaṃ kantanti tasmā  itthī dvaṅgulapaññāti vuccati ti phalasamāpattiñāṇe pavattamāne ti catusaccadhammaṃ vipassantassa pubbabhāge vā vipassanāya ārammaṇabhūtaṃ khandhapañcakameva ti aññaṃ vā kiñci ahaṃ asmīti taṇhāmānadiṭṭhivasena yassa siyā dutiyaṃ

 tatiye ti appamaṃsalohitatāya kisā gotamīti panassā nāmaṃ pubbe kira sāvatthiyaṃ ekasmiṃ kule asītikoṭidhanaṃ sabbaṃ aṅgārāva jātaṃ kuṭumbiko aṅgārajātāni anīharitvā   avassaṃ koci puññavā bhavissati tassa puññena puna pākatikaṃ bhavissatīti suvaṇṇahiraññassa cāṭiyo pūretvā āpaṇe ṭhapetvā samīpe nisīdi athekā duggatakulassa dhītā  aḍḍhamāsakaṃ gahetvā dārusākaṃ āharissāmīti vīthiṃ gatā taṃ disvā kuṭumbikaṃ āha  āpaṇe tāva dhanaṃ ettakaṃ gehe kittakaṃ bhavissatīti kiṃ disvā amma evaṃ kathesīti imaṃ hiraññasuvaṇṇanti so puññavatī esā bhavissatīti tassā vasanaṭṭhānaṃ pucchitvā āpaṇe bhaṇḍaṃ paṭisāmetvā tassā mātāpitaro upasaṅkamitvā evamāha  amhākaṃ gehe vayappatto dārako atthi tassetaṃ dārikaṃ dethāti kiṃ sāmi duggatehi saddhiṃ keḷiṃ karosīti mittasanthavo nāma duggatehipi saddhiṃ hoti detha naṃ kuṭumbasāminī bhavissatīti naṃ gahetvā gharaṃ ānesi sā saṃvāsamanvāya puttaṃ vijātā putto padasā āhiṇḍanakāle kālamakāsi sā duggatakule uppajjitvā mahākulaṃ gantvāpi puttavināsaṃ pattāmhīti uppannabalavasokā puttassa sarīrakiccaṃ vāretvā taṃ matakaḷevaraṃ ādāya nagare vippalapantī carati
ekadivasaṃ mahatiyā buddhavīthiyā dasabalassa  santikaṃ gantvā  puttassa me arogabhāvatthāya bhesajjaṃ detha bhagavāti āha gaccha sāvatthiṃ āhiṇḍitvā yasmiṃ gehe matapubbo natthi tato siddhatthakaṃ āhara puttassa te bhesajjaṃ bhavissatīti sā nagaraṃ pavisitvā dhuragehato paṭṭhāya bhagavatā vuttanayena gantvā siddhatthakaṃ yācantī ghare ghare kuto tvaṃ evarūpaṃ gharaṃ passissasīti vuttā katipayāni gehāni āhiṇḍitvā  sabbesampi kirāyaṃ dhammatā na mayhaṃ puttassevāti sālāyaṃ chavaṃ chaḍḍetvā pabbajjaṃ yāci satthā imaṃ pabbājetūti bhikkhuniupassayaṃ pesesi sā khuraggeyeva arahattaṃ pāpuṇi imaṃ theriṃ sandhāya atha kho kisāgotamīti vuttaṃ
ti ekā āsi ti rudamānamukhī viya ti ettha  antaṃ atītaṃ accantaṃ bhāvanapuṃsakametaṃ idaṃ vuttaṃ hoti  yathā puttamaraṇaṃ antaṃ atītaṃ hoti evaṃ mataputtā ahaṃ idāni mama puna puttamaraṇaṃ nāma natthi ti purisāpi me etadantikāva  yo me puttamaraṇassa anto purisānampi me esevanto abhabbā ahaṃ idāni purisaṃ gavesitunti ti sabbesu khandhāyatanadhātubhavayonigatiṭhitinivāsesu mama taṇhānandī vihatā ti avijjākkhandho ti ñāṇena bhinno tatiyaṃ

 catutthe ti ātataṃ vitataṃ ātatavitataṃ ghanaṃ susiranti evaṃ pañcaṅgasamannāgatena ti sabbe tuyhaṃyeva demi ti nāhaṃ tena atthikā ti suvaṇṇavaṇṇopi kāyo niccaṃ uggharitapaggharitaṭṭhena pūtikāyova tasmā evamāha ti bhijjanasabhāvena ti cuṇṇavicuṇṇaṃ āpajjanadhammena ti aṭṭā pīḷitā homi ti lajjāmi ti  aṭṭhavidhā lokiyasamāpatti ārammaṇasantatāya aṅgasantatāya ca santāti vuttā ti sabbesu rūpārūpabhavesu tesaṃ dvinnaṃ bhavānaṃ gahitattā gahite kāmabhave aṭṭhasu ca samāpattīsūti etesu sabbesu ṭhānesu mayhaṃ avijjātamo vihatoti vadati catutthaṃ

 pañcame nti aggato paṭṭhāya suṭṭhu pupphitaṃ sālarukkhaṃ ti tava vaṇṇadhātusadisā dutiyā vaṇṇadhātu natthi tayā sadisā aññā bhikkhunī natthīti vadati nti yathā tvaṃ idhāgatā kiñci santhavaṃ vā sinehaṃ vā na labhasi evamevaṃ tepi tayāva sadisā bhaveyyuṃ nti dvinnaṃ akkhīnaṃ majjhe nāsavaṃsepi tiṭṭhantiṃ maṃ na passasi ti vasībhūtā asmi pañcamaṃ

 chaṭṭhe ti ko nu mandabuddhi bālo taṃ evaṃ gāhāpesi nti aññampi nānappakāraṃ upaddavaṃ idāni yaṃ māro āha   ko nu taṃ idamādapayīti taṃ maddantī  na maṃ andhabālo ādapesi loke pana aggapuggalo satthā dhammaṃ desesīti dassetuṃ tiādimāha tattha ti paramatthasacce nibbāne nivesesi ti nirodhasaccaṃ ajānantā chaṭṭhaṃ

 sattame ti punappunaṃ maraṇamārakilesamāradevaputtamārānaṃ vasaṃ āgacchanti ti santāpito ti yattha tuyhaṃ mārassa agati ti tasmiṃ nibbāne sattamaṃ

 aṭṭhame ti samaṇisadisā dissasi ti kiṃ kāraṇā momūhā viya carasi ti imamhā sāsanā bahi pāsaṃ ḍentīti  sattānaṃ cittesu diṭṭhipāsaṃ khipantīti attho sāsanaṃ pana pāse moceti tasmā pāsaṇḍoti na vuccati ito bahiddhāyeva pāsaṇḍā honti ti saṃsīdanti lagganti
idāni kaṃ nu uddissa muṇḍāsīti pañhaṃ kathentī tiādimāha tattha ti sabbāni khandhāyatanadhātubhavayonigatiādīni abhibhavitvā ṭhito maraṇamārādayo nudi nīharīti ti sabbesu rāgādīsu vā mārayuddhe vā ajito ti sabbesu khandhādīsu mutto ti taṇhādiṭṭhinissayena  anissito ti sabbakammakkhayasaṅkhātaṃ arahattaṃ patto ti upadhisaṅkhayasaṅkhāte nibbāne ārammaṇato vimutto aṭṭhamaṃ

 navame nti kena idaṃ kataṃ nti attabhāvaṃ sandhāya vadati nti dukkhapatiṭṭhānattā attabhāvameva vadati ti hetunirodhena paccayavekallena navamaṃ

 dasame ti imasmiṃ suddhasaṅkhārapuñje paramatthato satto nāma na upalabbhati ti pañcasu khandhesu vijjamānesu tena tenākārena vavatthitesu ti sattoti samaññāmattameva hoti nti pañcakkhandhadukkhaṃ ti ṭhapetvā dukkhaṃ añño neva sambhoti na nirujjhatīti dasamaṃ

saṃyuttanikāyaaṭṭhakathāya
