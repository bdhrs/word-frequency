
ti methunasevanarāgapaṭisaṃyuttena chandena etena chande pana asati balakkārena padhaṃsitāya anāpattīti   sūcitā hoti sā samaṇī pārājikā nāma hotīti pavuccatīti yojanā
 sajīvassa api ajīvassāti padacchedo nti idaṃ aṅgajātanti imassa visesanaṃ ti attano vaccapassāvamukhamaggānaṃ aññatarasmiṃ magge ettha santhate vā asanthate vāti seso ti iminā sambandho yebhuyyaakkhāyitādikanti padacchedo saddena akkhāyitaṃ saṅgaṇhāti
manussapurisādīnaṃ navannaṃ sajīvassapi ajīvassapi yassa kassaci santhataṃ vā asanthataṃ vā yebhuyyakkhāyitādikaṃ aṅgajātaṃ attano santhate vā asanthate vā tividhe magge allokāse tilaphalamattampi pavesentī parājitāti yojanā
nti  bhikkhubhikkhunīnaṃ sādhāraṇasikkhāpadavinicchayaṃ
nti ettha akkhakānaṃ adhoti viggaho nti jāṇumaṇḍalānaṃ ubbhanti viggaho saddo uddhaṃsaddapariyāyo idha attanoti seso ti kāyasaṃsaggarāgena tintassa ti etthāpi eseva nayo ti vuttattā sāti labbhati nti ettha yaṃ kiñcīti seso paropakkamamūlakaṃ pārājikaṃ dassetumāha ti ettha yathāparicchinne kāyeti ca sādiyeyyāti ca vattabbaṃ
yā pana bhikkhunī avassutā avassutassa manussapuggalassa yaṃ kiñci  attano  yaṃ sarīrakaṃ tena sarīrakena chupeyya  manussapurisena yathāparicchinne kāye  sādiyeyya vā sā pārājikā siyāti yojanā
ti iminā kapparato uddhaṃ pārājikakkhettamevāti dīpeti attano yathāvuttappakārena kāyenāti yojanā attano adhakkhakantiādivuttappakārena kāyenāti attho tathā avassutāya avassutassa  kāyapaṭibaddhaṃ phusantiyā thullaccayaṃ hoti attano yathāparicchinnakāyapaṭibaddhena tathā avassutāya avassutassa purisassa kāyaṃ phusantiyā thullaccayaṃ hoti
 attano avasesena kāyena avassutāya avassutassa purisapuggalassa  phusantiyā thullaccayaṃ  hoti evaṃ attano payoge ca purisassa payoge ca tassā bhikkhuniyāyeva thullaccayaṃ hotīti yojanā
 yakkhapetatiracchānapaṇḍakānaṃ kāyaṃ adhakkhakaṃ ubbhajāṇumaṇḍalanti yathāparicchinnaṃ tatheva attano kāyena ubhatoavassave sati phusantiyā  bhikkhuniyā thullaccayaṃ tatheva yakkhādīnaṃ payogepi tassāyeva thullaccayaṃ hotīti yojanā
ti bhikkhuniyā vasena ekatoavassave cāpi nti pārājikakkhettabhūtena attano kāyena manussapurisassa kāyaṃ phusantiyā thullaccayaṃ   vuttanti attho ti yathāvuttapārājikakkhettato avasese thullaccayakkhette sabbattha ekatoavassave sati dukkaṭaṃ hotīti attho kāyapaṭibaddhena kāyapaṭibaddhāmasanādīsu sabbattha ubhatoavassave vā ekatoavassave vā dukkaṭameva hoti
nti  apārājikakkhettaṃ idha dassitaṃ ettha ekatoavassave dukkaṭaṃ hoti kapparassa ca heṭṭhāpi  adhojāṇumaṇḍale saṅgahaṃ gatanti yojanā
 bhikkhu bhikkhuniyā saddhiṃ sace kāyasaṃsaggaṃ  sevatīti yojanā ti sīlavināso pārājikāpatti siyāti attho nti ettha gehasitapemanti vattabbe gāthābandhavasena sitasaddalopo attho panassa bhikkhuvibhaṅge vuttanayova
ti  bhikkhuniyāti vā bhikkhussāti vā visesaṃ akatvā
ti bhikkhussa vā bhikkhuniyā vā ti bhikkhuniyaṃ vā bhikkhusmiṃ vā nti kāyasaṃsaggādirāgarahitaṃ  bhikkhussa vā bhikkhuniyā vā  bhikkhuniyaṃ vā bhikkhusmiṃ vā visaye  anāpattīti attho
ti sīlabhedaṃ katvā bhikkhuniyā apakatattā āha ti
ti vākyārambhe ti ettha bhikkhunīhi kāyasaṃsaggasaṅghādisesamāha
 khetteti vakkhamānaṃ phuṭṭhāti iminā yojetvā ntiādīhi thullaccayaṃ khettetiādīhi ca sambandhitabbaṃ pārājikanti vakkhamānattā ti ettha pārājikakkhetteti seso
ti niccalāpi sādiyati ti thullaccayādīnaṃ khette kāyena niccalāyapi cittena sādiyantiyā āpatti kasmā vuttāti āha ti  bhikkhupātimokkhe viya kāyasaṃsaggaṃ samāpajjeyyāti avatvā idha kāyasaṃsaggaṃ sādiyeyyāti vuttattāti adhippāyo
 āpattiyā nti kiriyāya samuṭṭhānaṃ ti sādiyanamatteneva āpajjitabbabhāve sati nti kiriyasamuṭṭhānamitividhānaṃ ti  kiriyasamuṭṭhānabāhullena nayena khadiravanādivohāro viyāti daṭṭhabbaṃ
 bhikkhuniyā  virajjhitvā āmasantiyā anāpatti ayaṃ purisoti vā itthīti vā  āmasantiyā anāpatti purisassa āmasane sati phassaṃ asādiyantiyā vā anāpattīti yojanā
ti yakkhummattāya ti pittakopena ummādappattāya idañca asucīti vā candananti vā visesataṃ ajānanameva pamāṇaṃ

ti iminā avasesāpattiṃ jānitvā chādentiyā pārājikābhāvaṃ dīpeti ti pabbajjāliṅgeyeva ṭhitāya iti dhure nikkhittamattasminti yojanā saddo nidassane itarāya pubbeyeva āpannattā tamapekkhitvā ti āha
 vuttāvisiṭṭhaṃ sabbaṃ vinicchayaṃ saṅgahetumāha ntiādi ti duṭṭhullapaṭicchādane

ti samaggena saṅghena ti āpattiyā adassanādīsu ukkhittako ti iminā ukkhepanīyakammakatassa anosāritabhāvaṃ dīpeti yā  diṭṭhi etassāti  so ukkhittako bhikkhu yāya diṭṭhiyā samannāgato hotīti adhippāyo  ti iminā anuvattappakāro dassito taṃ ukkhittakaṃ bhikkhunti yojanā sā bhikkhunī  bhikkhunīhi visumpica saṅghamajjhepi eso kho ayye bhikkhu samaggena saṅghena ukkhittotiādinā  nayena tikkhattuṃ vuccamānāti yojanā taṃ vatthuṃ acajantī gahetvā yadi tatheva tiṭṭhatīti yojanā ettha yāvatatiyaṃ samanubhāsitabbāti seso ti tatiyāya kammavācāya yyakārappattavasena assa samanubhāsanakammassa pariyosāne ti asākiyadhītā paccatte karaṇavacanaṃ ti iminā puna teneva ca attabhāvena bhikkhunibhāve paṭisandhātuṃ anarahatā vuttā
nti adhammakamme adhammakammasaññā vematikā dhammakammasaññāti etāsaṃ vasena tikadukkaṭaṃ vuttaṃ samanubhāsane vuttā samuṭṭhānādayo sabbe idha vattabbāti yojanā

 hatthaggahaṇaṃ vā sādiyeyyāti hattho nāma kapparaṃ upādāya yāva agganakhā etassa asaddhammassa paṭisevanatthāya ubbhakkhakaṃ adhojāṇumaṇḍalaṃ gahaṇaṃ sādiyati āpatti thullaccayassāti  vuttattā āha tiādi tanti vakkhamānattā yanti labbhati apārājikakkhettassa yassa kassaci aṅgassa yaṃ gahaṇaṃ taṃ hatthaggahaṇanti pavuccatīti yojanā hatthe gahaṇaṃ 
ti vuttappakārena yassa kassaci cīvarassa yaṃ gahaṇanti yojanā
 asaddhammasaddena  methunassāpi vuccamānattā tato visesetumāha ti bhikkhunī kāyasaṃsaggasaṅkhātassa asaddhammassa kāraṇā purisassa hatthapāsasmiṃ tiṭṭheyya vāti yojanā
ti tassa asaddhammassa kāraṇā ti hatthapāse ti ettha katanti seso saṅketanti iminā sambandho āgamanaṃ assāti padacchedo iccheyyāti vuttepi na gamanicchāmattena atha kho bhikkhuniyā purisassa hatthapāsaṃ purisena ca bhikkhuniyā hatthapāsaṃ okkantakāleyeva vatthupūraṇaṃ daṭṭhabbaṃ yathāha saṅketaṃ vā gaccheyyāti etassa asaddhammassa paṭisevanatthāya purisena itthannāmaṃ āgacchāti vuttā gacchati pade pade āpatti dukkaṭassa purisassa hatthapāsaṃ okkantamatte āpatti thullaccayassāti  ca purisassa abbhāgamanaṃ sādiyati āpatti dukkaṭassa hatthapāsaṃ okkantamatte āpatti thullaccayassāti  ca ettha ca ti itthannāmaṃ ṭhānaṃ āgacchāti attho
ti tasseva kāyasaṃsaggasaṅkhātaasaddhammassa sevanatthāya ti vatthādinā yena kenaci paṭicchannaokāsaṃ purisassa hatthapāse ṭhitā tadatthāya kāyaṃ upasaṃhareyya vāti yojanā
 hatthaggahaṇādīnaṃ vuttappakārānaṃ aṭṭhannaṃ vatthūnaṃ pūraṇena ti saṅkhātā  bhikkhunī  sīlavināsena tatoyeva  abhikkhunī hotīti yojanā
ti hatthaggahaṇādipaṭipāṭiyā vā ti tabbipariyato paṭilomena vā ti ekamekaṃ antaritvā puna tassāpi karaṇavasena ekantarikāya vā anulomena vā paṭilomena vā tathekantarikāya vā aṭṭhamaṃ vatthuṃ paripūrentī cutāti yojanā
 etadeva atthaṃ byatirekamukhena samatthetumāha tiādi ti  bahukkhattumpi saddo hettha bahusaddapariyāyo pārājikā neva siyāti yojanā iminā taṃtaṃvatthumūlakaṃ dukkaṭathullaccayaṃ āpajjatīti vuttaṃ hoti
 yā pana āpattiyo āpannā desetvā tāhi muccatīti yojanā ti na punevaṃ karissāmīti dhuraṃ nikkhipitvā ti desitā desitagaṇanameva upeti pārājikassa aṅgaṃ na hotīti attho tasmā yā ekaṃ āpannā dhuranikkhepaṃ katvā desetvā puna kilesavasena āpajjati puna deseti evaṃ aṭṭha vatthūni pūrentīpi pārājikā na hoti
ti puna āpajjane anikkhittadhurāya bhikkhuniyā desitāpi āpatti desanāgaṇanaṃ na upeti kiṃ hotīti āha ti tasmā pārājikāpattiyā aṅgameva hotīti adhippāyo
ti asaddhammo nāma kāyasaṃsaggoti ayaṃ attho ti pakāsito
 ayamattho  kena vacanena uddisitoti āha nti nti viññū paṭibalo kāyasaṃsaggaṃ samāpajjitunti  idaṃ vacanaṃ  pamāṇaṃ
