

 pañcamassa  paṭhame ti bhogānaṃ ādātabbakāraṇāni ti uṭṭhānasaṅkhātena vīriyena adhigatehi ti bāhubalena sañcitehi ti sedaṃ avakkhipetvā uppāditehi ti dhammayuttehi ti dasakusalakammaṃ akopetvā laddhehi ti pīṇitaṃ  thūlaṃ karoti sesamettha catukkanipāte vuttanayeneva veditabbaṃ dutiyaṃ uttānatthameva

 tatiye ti dānasīlādikā puññapaṭipadā sesesupi eseva nayo ti atthassa abhisamāgamena atthappaṭilābhenāti vuttaṃ hoti

 catutthe ti guṇehi uggatattā evaṃladdhanāmo nti catumadhurayojitena sālipiṭṭhena kataṃ sālapupphasadisaṃ khādanīyaṃ tañhi paññāyamānavaṇṭapattakesaraṃ katvā jīrakādisambhārayutte sappimhi pacitvā sappiṃ vinivattetvā kolumbe pūretvā gandhavāsaṃ gāhāpetvā pidahitvā lañchetvā ṭhapitaṃ  hoti taṃ so yāguṃ pivitvā nisinnassa bhagavato antarabhatte dātukāmo evamāha ti desanāmattametaṃ upāsako pana taṃ bhagavato ca pañcannañca bhikkhusatānaṃ adāsi yathā ca taṃ evaṃ sūkaramaṃsādīnipi tattha nti sampannabadaraṃ nti madhurarasehi badarehi saddhiṃ jīrakādisambhārehi yojetvā pakkaṃ ekasaṃvaccharikasūkaramaṃsaṃ nti vinivattitatelaṃ nti sālipiṭṭhena saddhiṃ madditvā jīrakādisaṃyutte sappimhi pacitvā catumadhurena yojetvā vāsaṃ gāhāpetvā ṭhapitaṃ nāliyasākaṃ ti ettha akappiyaṃ upādāya kappiyampi na kappatīti vuttaṃ seṭṭhi pana sabbampi taṃ āharāpetvā rāsiṃ katvā yaṃ yaṃ akappiyaṃ taṃ taṃ antarāpaṇaṃ pahiṇitvā kappiyaṃ upabhogaparibhogabhaṇḍaṃ adāsi candanaphalakaṃ nātimahantaṃ dīghato aḍḍhateyyaratanaṃ tiriyaṃ diyaḍḍharatanaṃ sāravarabhaṇḍattā pana mahagghaṃ ahosi bhagavā taṃ paṭiggahetvā khaṇḍākhaṇḍikaṃ chedāpetvā bhikkhūnaṃ añjanapisanatthāya dāpesi
ti  kāyavācācittehi ujukesu ti pemena ntiādīsu pariccāgavasena  muttacāgatāya  anapekkhacittatāya cittena na uggahitanti ti  viruhanaṭṭhena khettasadise
nti suddhāvāsesu ekaṃ jhānamanena nibbattaṃ devakāyaṃ ti yathājjhāsayo iminā kiṃ pucchati tassa kira manussakāle arahattatthāya ajjhāsayo ahosi taṃ pucchāmīti pucchati devaputtopi arahattaṃ pattatāya ti āha ti tīsu vā kulasampattīsu chasu vā kāmasaggesu yattha yattha uppajjati tattha tattha dīghāyu yasavā hotīti pañcamaṃ catukkanipāte vuttanayeneva veditabbaṃ chaṭṭhasattamāni uttānatthāneva

 aṭṭhame ti aladdhabbāni na sakkā labhituṃ ti kāraṇāni ti yaṃ mayhaṃ jarāsabhāvaṃ taṃ mā jīratu sesapadesupi eseva nayo ti na rucceyya ti nīhari
ti yasmiṃ kāle ti upaddavesu ti na kampati nānusocati ti kāraṇatthavinicchaye kusalo nti nibbikāratāya porāṇakameva ti vaṇṇabhaṇanena ti mahānubhāvamantaparivattanena ti subhāsitakathanena ti satassa vā sahassassa vā dānena ti kulavaṃsena vā idaṃ amhākaṃ paveṇiyā āciṇṇaṃ idaṃ anāciṇṇanti evaṃ paveṇikathanenāti attho nti etesu jappādīsu yena yena yattha yattha ṭhāne jarādhammādīnaṃ ajīraṇatādiatthaṃ labheyya ti tena tena tasmiṃ tasmiṃ ṭhāne parakkamaṃ kareyya nti vaṭṭagāmikammaṃ mayā thiraṃ katvā āyūhitaṃ svāhaṃ idāni kinti karomīti evaṃ paccavekkhitvā adhivāseyyāti

 navame ti kaṇṇamūle ti duṭṭhumano ti patitakkhandho ti cintayanto ti nippaṭibhāno hutvā sesaṃ heṭṭhā vuttanayameva

 dasame ti adhiomucchito gilitvā pariniṭṭhapetvā gahaṇasabhāvāya atirekamucchāya taṇhāya samannāgato ti mahatā rājānubhāvena aṭṭhārasahi senīhi parivārito mahatiyā rājiddhiyā pāyāsīti attho ti ekaṃsatthe nipāto  ekaṃseneva sokasallaharaṇoti attho iti rājā imaṃ ovādaṃ sutvā tasmiṃ ṭhito dhammena samena rajjaṃ kāretvā saggaparāyaṇo ahosi
