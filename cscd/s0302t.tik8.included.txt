

ti kūṭacchiddassa anupavisanavasena kūṭaṃ gacchanti yā ca gopānasiyo gopānasantaragatā tāpi kūṭaṃ āhacca ṭhānena  duvidhāpi kūṭe samosaraṇā  vināsena bhijjanena ti avijjāya accantameva appavattiyā tena ca mokkhadhammādhigamena tadanurūpadhammādhigamo dassito ti pana iminā tassa upāyo dassito


 yathā nakhasikhāya āropitapaṃsu sugatisaṃvattaniyassa kammassa appakattā ti hīnūdāharaṇavasena vuttaṃ appatarā hi sattā ye devesu jāyanti tañca kho kāmadevesu itaresu pana vattabbameva natthi


 viheṭhayanti ti bhāvanāpāripūrivasena paribrūhitā ti bhāvanāya bahulīkaraṇena aparāparaṃ pavattitā ti yathā yuttaṃ ājaññarathaṃ chekena sārathinā adhiṭṭhitaṃ yathāruci pavattati evaṃ yathāruci pavattiyā gamitā ti adhiṭṭhānaṭṭhena  sabbaso upakkilesavisodhanena suvisodhitamariyādaṃ viya katā ti paṭipakkhadūrībhāvato subhāvitabhāvena avikampaneyyatāya ṭhapitā  ti sabbabhāgena bhāvanūpacayaṃ gamitā tenāha ti  ti mettābhāvanāya matthakappattiyā sammadeva sampāditā


nti mahāmukhānaṃ mahantakoḷumbānaṃ sataṃ nti sappimadhusakkarādīhi upanītapaṇītabhojanehi paripuṇṇānaṃ ti pāṭhassa nti godohanavelāmattaṃ taṃ pana kittakaṃ adhippetanti āha tiādi nti anodhisomettābhāvanamāha  mettacittaṃ appanāppattaṃ bhāvetuṃ sakkotīti adhippāyo tampi tato yathāvuttadānato nti


ti tiṇhaphalasattiyā agge hatthena vā muṭṭhinā vā pahāraṃ datvā ti pahatakappāsapiṇḍaṃ viya ti phalasaṇṭhānaṃ niyyāsapiṇḍaṃ viya ti kalikādibhāvena vīsatiṃsapiṇḍāni ekajjhaṃ katvā  piṇḍaṃ karonto ti paṭilīnayati nāmeti  te dvepi dhārā ekato samphusāpento ti paṭipaharati tattha khaṇḍaṃ viya niyyāso nti vihatassa kappāsassa paṭisaṃharaṇavasena bandhanadaṇḍaṃ ti kappāsassa saṃvellanavasena pavattento


ti  thirataradhanuno idāni tassa thiratarabhāvaṃ paricchedato dassetuṃ tiādi vuttaṃ tattha nti palānaṃ dvisahassathāmaṃ vuttamevatthaṃ pākaṭataraṃ katvā dassetuṃ tiādimāha tattha ti dhanuno ti jiyaṃ āropitassa ti jiyāya baddho pathavito muccati etaṃ dvisahassathāmanti veditabbaṃ  nti kāḷalohatambalohasīsādīnaṃ ti purisabhāro so pana majjhimapurisassa vasena edisaṃ tassa balaṃ daṭṭhabbaṃ  dhanuvedasikkhāvasena  lakkhesu avirajjhanasarakkhepavasena kataṃ rājakulādīsu upagantvā asanaṃ sarakkhepo etehīti ti āha ti
bodhisatto cattāri kaṇḍāni āharīti vatvā tameva atthaṃ vitthārato dassento tiādimāha tattha ti dhāvissāma ti sīghapatanasantāpena ca sūriyarasmisantāpassa āsannabhāvena ca usumā uṭṭhahi ti pakkhajālantarena
ti nippayojanamidaṃ javananti nivattitvā ti pihitapatto viya ahosi vegasā patanena nagarassa upari ākāsassa nirikkhaṇaṃ ahosi sañcāritattā  seyyathāpi bodhisattassa dhanuggahakāle sarakūṭādidassane
nti tassa adassanaṃ sandhāyāha na attano patanaṃ sūriyamaṇḍalañhi atisīghena javena gacchantampi paññāsayojanāyāmavitthataṃ attano vipulatāya pabhassaratāya ca sattānaṃ cakkhussa gocarabhāvaṃ gacchati javanahaṃso pana tādisena sūriyena saddhiṃ javena gacchanto na paññāyeyya tasmā vuttaṃ nti  akkhaṇavedhino gantvā gahite sotuṃ ghaṇḍaṃ piḷandhāpetvā sayaṃ puratthābhimukho nisinno puratthimadisābhimukhaṃ gatakaṇḍaṃ sandhāyāha ti te cattāri kaṇḍāni ekakkhaṇeyeva khipiṃsu
āyuṃ  saṅkharoti etenāti  yathā hi kammajarūpānaṃ pavatti jīvitindriyapaṭibaddhā evaṃ attabhāvassa pavatti tappaṭibaddhāti bahuvacananiddeso pana pāḷiyaṃ ekasmiṃ khaṇe anekasatasaṅkhassa jīvitindriyassa upalabbhanato  jīvitindriyaṃ  yathāvuttadevatānaṃ javato  ittarakhaṇattā vuttañhetaṃ 
jīvitaṃ attabhāvo ca sukhadukkhā ca kevalā
ekacittasamāyuttā lahuso vattate khaṇoti (mahāni 10)
ti  bhaṅgo  tatopi ativiya ittarakhaṇattā


 aññe rājāno ca bhāgaṃ gaṇhantā ime viya dasabhāgaṃ gaṇhantīti tesamayaṃ anugati paññāyati mahājanassa ānayanato ti āha ti idāni taṃ ādito paṭṭhāya āgamanānukkamaṃ dassetuṃ tiādimāha nti kareṇukaṃ hatthininti attho ti anatthapariharaṇavasena pasatthūpahāravasena ca pūjesuṃ ti kuḷīradahaṃ pāvisi  ṭhapanavasena apakkamitvā 
nti kismiñci chidde suvaṇṇamayaṃ kismiñci rajatamayaṃ kismiñci phalikamayaṃ  bandhiṃsu pubbe pharitvā tiṭṭhantassa dvādasa yojanāni pamāṇo etassāti  saddo athassa anekasatakāle gacchante  āṇisaṅghāṭamattattā
ti agādhā dukkhogāḷhā sallasuttañhi animittamanaññātantiādinā pāḷivasena gambhīraṃ na atthagambhīraṃ tathā hi tattha tā tā gāthā duviññeyyarūpā tiṭṭhanti duviññeyyaṃ ñāṇena dukkhogāhanti katvā gambhīranti  vuccati pubbāparampettha kāsañci gāthānaṃ duviññeyyatāya dukkhogāhameva tasmā taṃ pāḷivasena gambhīranti vuttaṃ ti iminā nayena atthavasena gambhīrāti ettha attho veditabbo mahāvedallasuttassa atthavasena gambhīratā pākaṭāyeva lokaṃ uttaratīti lokuttaro navavidhaappamāṇadhammo so atthabhūto etesaṃ atthīti  tenāha ti ti sattena attanā suññataṃ kevalaṃ dhammamattameva nti ca liṅgavacanavipallāsena vuttanti āha ti ti kavino kammaṃ kavikatā yassa pana yaṃ kammaṃ taṃ tena katanti vuccatīti āha ti  kāveyyāti padaṃ kabbanti vuttaṃ hoti kabbanti ca kavinā vuttanti attho tenāha nti ti vicitrākāraakkharā ti na sāsanāvacarā ti buddhānaṃ sāvakāti  apaññātānaṃ yesaṃ kesañci sāvakehi ti na uggayhamānā savanadhāraṇaparicayaatthūpaparikkhādivasena anuggayhamānā  adassanaṃ gacchanti


 kaliṅgaraṃ vuccati khuddakadārukhaṇḍaṃ taṃ upadhānaṃ etesanti  licchavī pana khadiradaṇḍaṃ upadhānaṃ katvā tadā vihariṃsu tasmā vuttaṃ tiādi pakativijjusaññito natthi etesaṃ khaṇo vijjhaneti  tato sīghataraṃ vijjhanato akkhaṇanti vijju vuccati ittarakhaṇattā akkhaṇobhāsena lakkhaṇavedhakā  anekadhā bhinnassa vālassa vijjhanena  vālekadeso hi idha vāloti gahito
bahudeva divasabhāgaṃ padhānānuyogato uppannadarathaparissamavinodanatthaṃ ti te tathārūpe padhānakammikabhikkhū sandhāya  idāni vuccamānaṃ atthajātaṃ  porāṇaṭṭhakathāyaṃ ti  tambapaṇṇidīpamāha padhānānuyuñjanavelāya nivedanavasena tattha tattha ekajjhaṃ pahataghaṇḍinigghoseneva  tattha tattha paṇṇasālādīsu vasantānaṃ bhikkhūnaṃ vasena ekapadhānabhūto nānāmukhoti anurādhapurassa pacchimadisāyaṃ eko vihāro nagarassa puratthimadisāyaṃ ubhayattha pavattaghaṇḍisaddā antarāpavattaghaṇḍisaddehi missetvā osaranti  pavattaghaṇḍisaddo tathā nāgadīpe


nti bhattānumodanaupanisinnakathāvelato atikkantavelaṃ ti sarasampattito asambhinnena sarassa uccāraṇasampattiṃ aparihāpetvāti attho aparisuddhāsayatāya nti taṃ tathā paccayānaṃ paribhuñjanaṃ taṃ tathā micchāpaṭipajjanaṃ


ti gharena gharassa sambandhaṭṭhānaṃ saha malena vattatīti  gehato gāmato ca nikkhamanacandanikaṭṭhānaṃ nti saṅkārakūṭaṃ keci sandhisaṅkārakūṭaṭṭhānanti vadanti nti āpannaāpattito na kilesato vuṭṭhānaṃ suddhante adhiṭṭhānaṃ taṃ pana yathāāpannāya āpattiyā desanātveva vuccatīti āha ti
