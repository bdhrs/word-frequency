 ye te bhikkhave bhikkhū āpattiṃ anāpattīti dīpenti te bhikkhave bhikkhū bahujanaahitāya paṭipannā bahujanaasukhāya bahuno janassa anatthāya ahitāya dukkhāya devamanussānaṃ bahuñca te bhikkhave bhikkhū apuññaṃ pasavanti te cimaṃ saddhammaṃ antaradhāpentīti dutiyaṃ
