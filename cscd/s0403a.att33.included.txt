

 aṭṭhamassa paṭhame nti saupaghātaṃ sopaddavaṃ nti kāyikacetasikena pariḷāhena sapariḷāhaṃ ti icchitabbā avassaṃbhāvinī

 dutiye nti jātiādīhi maññanaṃ nti hīnohamasmīti mānaṃ nti atikkamitvā pavattaṃ accuṇṇatimānaṃ nti adhigatamānaṃ nti kodhamānehi thaddhabhāvaṃ nti hīnassa hīnohamasmīti mānaṃ

 tatiye ti manussadhammato uttari nti ariyabhāvaṃ kātuṃ samatthaṃ ñāṇadassanavisesaṃ cattāro magge cattāri ca phalānīti attho nti tividhaṃ kuhanavatthuṃ nti lābhatthikatāya ukkhipitvā avakkhipitvā vā lapanaṃ

 catutthe ti kāraṇañcassa paripuṇṇaṃ paggahitaṃ hoti ti dhamme ratiṃ vindati bhāvanāya ramati bhāvento vā ramatīti  pahāne ramati pajahanto vā ramatīti  tividhe paviveke ramatīti  abyāpajjhe niddukkhabhāve ramatīti  nippapañcasaṅkhāte nibbāne ramatīti 

 pañcame ti na āgamanakusalo ti na apagamanakusalo nti kattukamyatāchandaṃ ti na rakkhati

 chaṭṭhe ti ñāṇālokabahulo ti yoge bahulaṃ karoti ti pītipāmojjabahulo ti  kusaladhammesu asantuṭṭho ti aṭṭhapitadhuro paggahitavīriyo ti sampati ca uttariñca vīriyaṃ karoteva sattamaṃ uttānameva

 aṭṭhame ti kāyapāgabbhiyādīhi samannāgato navamaṃ uttānatthameva dasame ti mahicchataṃ nissāya uppannena lobhadukkhena dukkhito sesaṃ sabbattha uttānamevāti
