336 .
Rāgānatabbhutasarojamukhaṃ dharāya,
Pādā tilokaguruno’dhikabaddharāgā;
Ādāya niccasarasena karena gāḷhaṃ,
Sañcumbayanti satatāhitasambhamena
336 . ‘‘rāga’’ Dharāya Rāgaṃ ānataṃ rāgānatabbhutasarojamukhaṃ. Tilokaguruno rāgo Nicca sarasena karena ādāya satataṃ sambhamo 
336 . ‘‘rāgā’’ Dharāya rāgānatabbhutasarojamukhaṃ tilokaguruno adhikabaddharāgā niccasarasena karena gāḷhaṃ ādāya satatāhitasambhamena sañcumbayanti 



337 .
Iccā’nugamma purimācariyānubhāvaṃ,
Saṅkhepato nigadito’ya’malaṅkatīnaṃ;
Bhedo’parūpari kavīhi vikappiyānaṃ,
Ko nāma passitu’malaṃ khalu tāsa’mantaṃ
Iti saṅgharakkhitamahāsāmipādaviracite
Subodhālaṅkāre
Atthālaṅkārāvabodho nāma
Catuttho paricchedo.
337 . ‘‘icci’’ Iti  ācariyānaṃ ānubhāvaṃ anugamma alaṅkatīna nigadito ‘‘uparūparī’’ Uparūpari vikappiyānaṃ tāsa antaṃ khalu ekantena ko nāma alaṃ
Iti mahāsāmināmikāyaṃ subodhālaṅkāraṭīkāyaṃ
Catuttho paricchedo.
337 . ‘‘iccānugamma’’ Iti purimācariyānubhāvaṃ saṅkhepato nigadito Uparūpari kavīhi vikappiyānaṃ tāsa antaṃ khalu ko nāma alaṃ
Iti subodhālaṅkāranissaye
Catuttho paricchedo.
