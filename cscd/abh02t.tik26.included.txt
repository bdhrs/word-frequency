
1 suttantabhājanīyavaṇṇanā
 ijjhatīti iddhīti  ijjhanti etāyāti pajjitabbā iddhi vuttā iddhiṃ pajjanti pāpuṇantīti kattusādhanassa iddhisaddassa karaṇasādhanena pādasaddena samānādhikaraṇatāya asambhavato ijjhanakassa ca atthassa karaṇabhūtena pādena pajjitabbattā iddhi eva pādoti saddayojanā na sambhavatīti imamatthamāha nti ti ijjhanakiriyāya karaṇabhūtena atthena sādhetabbā ca iddhi pajjitabbāti yojanā nti ijjhanapajjanakiriyākaraṇānaṃ iddhipādatthānaṃ  paṭisedhadvayaṃ pakatiyaṃ ṭhapetīti ti yasmā paṭhamenatthena samānādhikaraṇasamāso dutiyena sāmivacanasamāso iddhipādasaddānaṃ na yujjati tasmā yathāvuttā vā paṭhamenatthena samānādhikaraṇasamāsavaseneva yojanā yujjati pādassa pajjamānakoṭṭhāsabhāvato dutiyenatthena itarasamāseneva yojanā yujjati pādassa ijjhanakaraṇūpāyabhāvato
ti  sandhāya vadati ti nibbattiatthāya vuddhiatthāya ca nti iddhi eva pādo iddhipādoti imasmā visuṃ ti iddhiyā pādo iddhipādoti evaṃ samāsayojanāvasena ti kattukaraṇatthesu yo yo yutto nti visesādhigamataṃpubbabhāgānaṃ yathākkamaṃ kattiddhikaraṇiddhibhāvaṃ sandhāya vuttoti yojanā uttaracūḷabhājanīye chandoyeva chandiddhipādo cittameva vīriyameva vīmaṃsāva  vīmaṃsiddhipādoti vuttattā āha tiādi
chandacittavīmaṃsiddhipādesu tāva yuttaṃ padhānasaṅkhāraggahaṇaṃ apubbattā vīriyiddhipāde pana kathanti codanaṃ sandhāyāha tiādi yadi dveyeva samannāgamaṅgāni chandādayo kimatthiyāti āha ti  atabbisesanattā yadipi  samādhivisesanasamannāgamaṅgadassanatthaṃ dvikkhattuṃ vīriyaṃ āgatanti vuttaṃ taṃ pana vīriyasamādhisamannāgatanti ettāvatāpi siddhaṃ hoti evaṃ siddhe sati puna vacanaṃ vīriyantarasabbhāvaṃ nu kho dīpetīti kadāci āsaṅkeyyāti tadāsaṅkānivattanatthaṃ tiādimāha ti chandādīnaṃ adhipatipaccayatāvisesena visiṭṭho teneva hi chandādimukheneva iddhipādā desitā tathā ca vuttaṃ chandaṃ ce bhikkhu adhipatiṃ karitvā labhati samādhintiādi ti tassa tassa chandassa samādhino avassayatāvasena paccayavisesatāyāti attho  adhigamūpāyatāpi nissayabhāvoyevāti tiādinā yathāvuttaṃ chandādīnaṃ iddhipādataṃ pāḷiyāyeva vibhāveti tattha ti chandādīnaṃyeva upāyatthabhāveneva iddhipādabhāvassa adhippetattā ti chandādike dhure jeṭṭhake pubbaṅgame katvā nibbattitasamādhi chandādīnaṃ iddhiyā adhigamūpāyatādassanaṃ upāyiddhipādadassanaṃ tadatthameva tathābhūtassa vedanākkhandho viññāṇakkhandhoti tattha tattha pāḷiyaṃ nissayiddhipādadassanaṃ kataṃ chandādivisiṭṭhānaṃyeva vedanākkhandhādīnaṃ adhippetattā evañcetaṃ sampaṭicchitabbaṃ  kevalaṃ iddhisampayuttānaṃyeva khandhānaṃ vasena iddhipādabhāve gayhamāne  visesakāraṇābhāvatoti adhippāyo
 satthu ārādhanaṃ sikkhāya vā ti thirabhāvato kulāpadese jātimā purissaro hotīti ti vuttaṃ vicāraṇāpaññāhetukattā mantassa vīmaṃsāsadisatā suviññeyyāvāti na uddhaṭā
ti chandasamādhipadhānasaṅkhārā vīriyacittavīmaṃsāsamādhipadhānasaṅkhārāti ime tayo tayo dhamme  bhedaṃ akatvā abhinditvā iddhibhāvasāmaññena iddhipādabhāvasāmaññena ca  saṅgaṇhitvā tenāha ti bhedanaṃ vā sambhedanaṃ missīkaraṇanti āha ti tathā hi sesā pana sampayuttakā cattāro khandhā iddhipādāyevāti (vibha aṭṭha 433) iddhiiddhipāde amissetvāpi kathitaṃ ti bhedena catūsu iddhipādesu asammissabhāvena āveṇikattā āveṇikā  hi chandādayo tassa tassa iddhipādassa ti abhedena caturiddhipādasādhāraṇabhāvenāti attho
ti -saddena padhānasaṅkhāraṃ vīriyacittavīmaṃsā ca saṅgaṇhāti ti tehi sampayuttaṃ catukkhandhamāha chandiddhipāde pavisantītiādinā  ti chando samādhi padhānasaṅkhārā taṃsampayuttā khandhāti evaṃ catūsu chandahetuko chandādhiko vā samādhi adhippetoti āha ti ti evaṃ anena pakārena yaṃ samādhibhāvanāmukhaṃ samādhibhāvanānuyogena bhāvitā khandhā 
tiādinā abhinavaṃ natthīti saṅkhepato vuttaṃ vivarati nti chandasamādhipadhānasaṅkhārānaṃ nti ime hi tayotiādivacanaṃ ti chando samādhītiādivacanassa ti sādhanabhāvena ti ime hi tayo dhammātiādivacanena yasmā chandādayo tayo dhammā aññamaññaṃ sampayuttakānañca nissayabhāvena pavattanti tasmā tesampi iddhipādabhāvo vutto so pana nissayabhāvo sampayogāvinābhāvīti āha ti sampayuttakantogadhattāti attho chandādīnaṃ viya sampayuttakkhandhānaṃ sabhāvato iddhibhāvo natthīti āha ti tena vuttaṃ ti ti chandādīnaṃ  khandhānaṃ chandādīnaṃ vā ti sampayuttakā panātiādiṃ sandhāyāha  catukkhandhatadekadesānaṃ iddhibhāvadīpanena
pubbe vuttato vacanakkamena aññanti āha ti kenaṭṭhena iddhi paṭilābho kenaṭṭhena pādo pubbabhāgoti yathākkamaṃ yojanā yadi patiṭṭhānaṭṭhena pādo nissayiddhipādoyeva vutto siyā na upāyiddhipādoti āha tiādi ti suttantabhājanīye abhidhammabhājanīye ca tenāha tiādi ti  chandādhipati chandahetuko chandādhiko vā samādhi chandasamādhītiādinā samādhissa visesanabhāvena  samādhisevanavasenāti ca pāṭho tattha ti chandādhike adhipatiṃ karitvā samādhissa āsevanavasena nti chandavato ce samādhi ijjhati mayheva ijjhatīti samādhiāsevanāya upāyabhūtānaṃ

2 abhidhammabhājanīyavaṇṇanā
 sādhipativārāni aṭṭhasatāni tesu paccekaṃ cattāro iddhipādā na sambhavantīti āha ti tattha kāraṇamāha ti yadi hi adhipatī siyuṃ sādhipatīti iddhipādabhedena dvattiṃsa nayasatāni suddhikāni aṭṭhāti cattāri nayasahassāni bhaveyyuṃ taṃ pana natthīti adhippāyo yattakā pana nayā idha labbhanti taṃ dassetuṃ tiādi vuttaṃ suddhikāni aṭṭha nayasatāni sādhipatikānipi aṭṭhevāti catunnaṃ maggānaṃ vasena soḷasa nayasatāni

3 pañhapucchakavaṇṇanā
sayaṃ jeṭṭhakabhāvena pavattanato cattāro adhipatayo aññamaññaṃ garuṃ na karonti tasmā cattāro iddhipādā na maggādhipatinoti vuttā tenāha ti nti adhipatayo aññamaññassa adhipatī na bhavantīti etamatthaṃ ti adhipati bhavituṃ samatthassa ti adhipatiṃ katvā garuṃ katvā  nappavattanti ti adhipatikiccakaraṇena sahapavattiyaṃ ti idaṃ adhipatitāsamatthā dhammā adhipatibhāveneva pavatteyyunti dosāropanavasenāha na yathādhigatavasena adhipatidhammānañhi pubbābhisaṅkhāre sati adhipatibhāvena pavatti na aññathāti sahabhāvepi tadabhāvaṃ sandhāya visesanaṃ na  kattabbaṃ siyāti sakkā vattuṃ ti aññamaññaṃ adhipatiṃ katvā pavattiyaṃ nti vīmaṃsādhipatikabhāvassa vacanaṃ  sabbesampi adhipatīnaṃ sādhipatikattāti adhippāyo  sādhipatibhāvassa anekaṃsikatāvacanato
