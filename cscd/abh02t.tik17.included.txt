
1 sabbasaṅgāhikavāravaṇṇanā
 pañcakkhandhātiādinā  dhātusambhavapariyāpannapātubhāva bhūmantaratīsu dhātūsuuppādakadānādikusala kammatabbipākaabhiññeyyādiārammaṇadukadvayadiṭṭhādikusalattikāditikapañcakarūpalokiyadukadvayabhedabhinnānaṃ niravasesato saṅgahitattā dutiyavārādīnañca ettha anuppavesato sabbasāmaññena vutto paṭhamo  nāma dutiyo ti vuttaṃ tattha pana kāmadhātuyā kati khandhā kati āyatanānītiādinā (vibha 991) tesaṃ atthitā eva vuttā kiriyāvisesassa appayogo atthi bhavati saṃvijjatīti sāmaññakiriyāya viññeyyabhāvato tenāyaṃ sambhavāsambhavadassanavāroti vattuṃ yutto catuttho ca upapattikkhaṇe uppattānuppattidassanavāroti tattha pātubhāvāpātubhāvavacanato
nti pucchānurūpaṃ avitathabyākaraṇaṃ parehi katampi sabbaññuvacanaṃ viññāya katattā  nāma hoti ko pana vādo sabbaññunā eva kateti adhippāyo
2 uppattānuppattivāravaṇṇanā
ti iddhiyā rūpadhātugatānaṃ kāmāvacarasattānañcāti attho ti ettha yadi tadabhāvena gandhāyatanādīni āyatanādikiccaṃ na karonti  asaññasattesu cakkhāyatanassa abhāvena rūpāyatanaṃ āyatanādikiccaṃ na kareyya tato asaññasattānaṃ devānaṃ upapattikkhaṇe dvāyatanāni pātubhavantītiādivacanaṃ na vattabbaṃ siyā kāmāvacarādiokāsā tattha uppajjamānasattānaṃ tattha pariyāpannadhammānaṃ vā adhiṭṭhānabhāvena dhātūti vuccanti tathā yesu kāmāvacarādisattanikāyesu kāmāvacarādisattā uppajjanti tesaṃ sattānaṃ uppatti etthāti sattuppattīti vuccamānā te  sattanikāyā ca na panettha apariyāpannokāso apariyāpannasattanikāyo ca atthi yo dhātūti vucceyyāti imamatthaṃ dassento ti āha ti iminā vā okāsasattalokadvayaṃ saha gahetvā tādisāya apariyāpannadhātuyā abhāvaṃ dasseti sattabhāvena vā uppatti sattuppatti sattāvāsavasena taṃtaṃbhavavasena uppajjamānā upādinnakakkhandhā taṃtaṃpariyāpannānaṃ sadisādhiṭṭhānabhāvena dhātūti vuccantīti evaṃ apariyāpannadhātu natthīti attho
3 pariyāpannāpariyāpannavāravaṇṇanā
ti tattha aññattha ca uppajjamānā upādinnakakkhandhā taṃtaṃpariyāpannā sabbe daṭṭhabbā
6 uppādakakammaāyuppamāṇavāro

 khandhādīnaṃ dhātusambhavādivasena pabhedaṃ vatvā ye sattā dhātuppabhedavanto yañca tesaṃ uppādakakammaṃ yo ca tassa vipāko tesaṃ vasena pabhedaṃ dassetuṃ tiādiko chaṭṭhavāro āraddho khandhādayo eva hi dhātuttayabhūtadevavasena dānādikammavasena taṃtaṃāyuppamāṇaparicchinnaupādinnakakkhandhavasena ca bhinnāti catudoṇaṃ  chadoṇanti eke


ti tayo janasamūhāti adhippāyo
ti sobhanā pabhā
 kañcanapiṇḍo viya sassirikā  tattha pana sobhanapabhāya kiṇṇā subhākiṇṇāti vattabbe ā-kārassa rassattaṃ  antimaṇa-kārassa ha-kārañca katvā ti vuttā atha pana subhena kiṇṇā subhakiṇṇā purimapadesupi parittaṃ subhaṃ etesanti  appamāṇaṃ subhaṃ etesanti ti subha-saddena samāso yojetabbo hoti
ti jhānakkhaṇe tato pacchā vā parittādikasiṇārammaṇabhāvanāya āvajjanena ca jhānassa ārammaṇamanasikāranānattatā na hoti pubbabhāgabhāvanāya pana pubbabhāgāvajjanena ca hotīti attho pubbabhāgabhāvanāya vasena hi jhānaṃ parittapathavīkasiṇādīsu taṃtadārammaṇaṃ hoti pubbabhāgena taṃtaṃkasiṇāvajjanena taṃtaṃmanasikāranti chandādayo pana appanākkhaṇepi vijjanti tattha ti na taṇhāpatthanā atha kho chandapatthanāva daṭṭhabbā  nicchayo  cittappavattiyeva yadi pana bhavachandabhavapatthanādayo taṃtaṃbhavavisesaniyāmakā adhippetā ti etassa appanāya pavattāya tato pacchāpi vaṭṭantīti attho daṭṭhabbo saññāvirāgādīhi pana visesiyamānaṃ ārammaṇaṃ tathā tathā tattha pavatto manasikāro ca bhavavisesaniyāmako pubbabhāgova vaṭṭatīti ti vuttaṃ
ti vipulasantasukhāyuvaṇṇādiphalā  paññācakkhunā maṃsadibbacakkhūhi ca
 yāva na taṃ pāpakammaṃ byantī hotīti (ma ni 3250) vacanato nti āha abbudādiāyuppamāṇaparicchedo pana kammavaseneva katoti adhippāyo
ti samānajātikena accharāgaṇena sabbadā parivāriyamānassa kāmaguṇākiṇṇassa tabbirahitaṭṭhānassa abhāvāti attho
ti kiṃ jhānaṃ upapattiṃ niyametīti attho ti brahmapārisajjādayo navapi sodhetvā ti vehapphalesūti  attho ti tato paraṃ agamanato uttamabhavāti adhippāyo teneva ti gahitā ti vehapphalādiṭṭhānāni eva sandhāya vuttaṃ vehapphalato pana purimesu navasu nibbattaanāgāmī arūpadhātuṃ upapajjatīti katvā rūpadhātuyā cutassa arūpadhātuṃ upapajjantassa kassaci satta anusayā anusenti kassaci pañca kassaci tayo anusentīti (yama 2anusayayamaka311) idaṃ vuttaṃ na vehapphalādīsu upapannaṃ sandhāyāti ayamettha adhippāyo siyā yaṃ pana vuttaṃ ti etena heṭṭhūpapatti eva nivāritā na tesveva uparūpari vehapphale ca upapatti arūpadhātūpapatti ca ti idampi anupubbena ārohantaṃ sandhāya vuttanti na tena tassa matthakaṃ appattassa arūpadhātuṃ upapatti nivāritāti daṭṭhabbā
yo vā aññattha tattha vā maggaṃ bhāvetvā cavitvā tattha upapanno avikkhambhitarūparāgo ariyasāvako taṃ sandhāya ayaṃ aṭṭhakathā vuttā teneva nti ti ti ca sabbattha nibbattaggahaṇaṃ kataṃ tassa pana yena tattha upapanno tasmiṃ rūparāge vikkhambhite puna bhavābhilāso na bhavissatīti arūparāgupacchedo ca bhavissatiyeva yo pana puthujjano tattha nibbatto ariyamaggaṃ bhāvetvā arūpehi vikkhambhitarūparāgo uppanne magge nibbattabhavādīnavadassanavasena anivattitabhavābhilāso tassa vasena yamakapāḷi pavattāti vā ayamattho adhippeto siyā

7 abhiññeyyādivāravaṇṇanā
 ruppanalakkhaṇaṃ  rūpaṃ phusanalakkhaṇo phassotiādinā sāmaññavisesalakkhaṇapariggāhikā  diṭṭhikaṅkhāvitaraṇavisuddhiyo  tato paraṃ yāva anulomā  udayabbayānupassanato paṭṭhāya yāva maggā 
ti khandhādīnaṃ tāva khandhavibhaṅgādīsu pañhapucchakavāre vattabbaṃ vuttaṃ hetuādīnañca khandhādīsu antogadhattā tattha tattha pañhapucchakavāre vattabbaṃ vuttamevāti daṭṭhabbaṃ
