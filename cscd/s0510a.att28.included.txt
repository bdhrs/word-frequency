

 tiṃsatime  vagge paṭhamāpadāne nti pūjāsakkāraṃ kāretukāmo ahaṃ nti nānā anekavidhaṃ campakasalalādipupphaṃ saṃ suṭṭhu ānayiṃ rāsiṃ akāsinti attho ti sakalalokattayabandhussa ñātakassa sikhissa bhagavato parinibbutassa citakaṃ āḷāhanacitakaṃ dārurāsiṃ jalantaṃ ādittaṃ disvā tañca mayā rāsīkataṃ pupphaṃ  pūjesinti attho
 dutiyāpadāne ti ajinamigacammaṃ uttarāsaṅgaṃ katvā nivāsino acchādanoti attho ti iddhividhādayo pañca abhiññāyo pañca ñāṇāni nibbattā uppāditā nipphāditā ti candamaṇḍalassa samantato majjako phuṭṭho ahosinti attho nti sakalalokattaye padīpasadisaṃ vipassiṃ bhagavantaṃ mama santikaṃ  visesena sampattaṃ āgataṃ ti devalokato pāricchattakapupphāni āharitvā vipassissa satthuno matthake chattākārena ahaṃ dhāresinti attho
 tatiyāpadāne ti mayhaṃ putto saddhāya pabbajito ti tasmiṃ pabbajitakāle kāsāyanivattho  na bāhirakapabbajjāya pabbajitoti attho ti so mayhaṃ putto catūsu buddhesu sāvakabuddhabhāvaṃ saṃ suṭṭhu patto arahattaṃ pattoti attho ti sakalalokehi katasakkāro khandhaparinibbānena parinibbutoti attho nti ahaṃ tassa gatadesaṃ pucchitvā sakaṃ puttaṃ vicinanto pacchato agamaṃ anugato asmīti attho ti mahantehi sīlakkhandhādīhi yuttattā mahantassa tassa mama puttassa arahato ādahanaṭṭhāne  citakaṭṭhānaṃ ahaṃ agamāsinti attho ti tasmiṃ ādahanaṭṭhāne  añjaliṃ dasaṅgulisamodhānaṃ paggahetvā sirasi katvā ahaṃ  dahanadārurāsiṃ  paṇāmaṃ katvā ti na kevalameva vanditvā dhavalacchattañca paggayha ukkhipitvā ahaṃ  patiṭṭhapesinti attho
 catutthāpadāne ti sūriye anuggate anuṭṭhite paccūsakāleti attho ti rogapīḷitassa mayhaṃ cittappasādo vipulo atireko buddhānussaraṇena ahu ahosi mahesino  lokamhi  pākaṭabhāvo ahosīti sambandho ti tasmiṃ pātubhāve sati ahaṃ gilāno buddho uppannoti ghosaṃ assosiṃ nti taṃ jitapañcamāraṃ sammāsambuddhaṃ na passāmi bāḷhagilānattā gantvā passituṃ na sakkomīti attho ti maraṇāsannakālaṃ anuppatto āsannamaraṇo hutvāti attho nti buddhotināmaṃ anussariṃ buddhārammaṇaṃ manasi akāsinti attho
 pañcamāpadāne ti ārāmadvārato saṅghassa nikkhamanadvāramaggehi attho ti tasmiṃ nikkhamanadvāramagge bhagavato bhikkhusaṅghassa ca pādā mā kaddamaṃ akkamantūti akkamanatthāya gosīsaṭṭhiṃ mayā santharitanti attho nti attano gosīsaattharaṇakammassa balena ājānīyā vātajavā sindhavā sīghavāhanādīni vipākaphalāni anubhomīti attho nti  saṅghe mayā suṭṭhu kataṃ kāraṃ appakampi kiccaṃ mahapphaladānato paramakāraṃ uttamakiccaṃ aho vimhayanti attho yathā tiṇadosādivirahitesu khettesu vappitaṃ sālibījaṃ mahapphalaṃ deti evameva rāgadosādidosarahite parisuddhakāyavacīsamācāre saṅghakhette gosīsaattharaṇakammaṃ mayā kataṃ idaṃ mahapphalaṃ detīti vuttaṃ hoti ti aññaṃ bāhirasāsane kataṃ kammaṃ saṅghe  pūjāsakkārassa kalaṃ soḷasiṃ kalaṃ koṭṭhāsaṃ na agghatīti sambandho
