Suttaṭṭhuppatti
 ‘‘nidhiṃ nidheti puriso’’



Paṭhamagāthāvaṇṇanā
1 . nidhiṃ nidheti puriso thāvaro  Jaṅgamo aṅgasamo Anugāmiko
Nidhetī Puriso Gambhīre odakantike  Atthe kicce samuppanne Atthāya me bhavissatī
Dutiyagāthāvaṇṇanā

2 . 





Etadatthāya  nidhi nāma nidhīyati
Tatiyagāthāvaṇṇanā

3 .

 nidhi ṭhānā cavati Saññā vā assa vimuyhati, nāgā vā apanāmenti Yakkhā vāpi haranti  Apassato appiyā vā dāyādā uddharanti
