

 tanu vitthāre saka sattismiṃ-du paritāpe sanu dānasmiṃ
vana yācāyaṃ manu bodhasmiṃ-hi gate apa pāpuṇanasmiṃ 
kara karaṇasmiṃsi bandhe-su abhissavane


 cura theyye loka  dassane aki lakkhaṇe
siyā thaka patighāte  takka vitakkaṇe

 lakkha dassanaaṅkesu  makkha makkhaṇe
bhakkhādane mokkha moce sukha-dukkha  takiraye

 liṅga cittakirayādīsu maga-magga gavesane
 paca vitthāre klese vañca palambhane

 vacca ajjhāyane acca pūjāyaṃ vaca bhāsane
raca patiyatane suca pesuññe ruca rocane

 mucappamocane loca dassane kaca dittiyaṃ
sajjājja ajjane tajja tajjane vajja vajjane

 yuja saṃyamane pūja pūjāyaṃ tija tejane
paja magga saṃvaraṇe gate bhaja vibhājane

  bhāja puthakkāre sabhāja pītidassane
 ghaṭa saṅghāte ghaṭṭa sañcalanādisu

 kuṭa-koṭṭacchedane  kuṭa ākoṭanādisu
naṭa nacce caṭa-puṭa bhede vaṇṭa vibhājane

 tuvaṭṭa ekasayane ghaṭo visaraṇe 
guṇṭha oguṇṭhane heṭha bādhāyaṃ veṭha veṭhane
guḍi veṭhe kaḍi-khaḍi bhedane maḍi bhūsane

 paṇḍa-bhaṇḍa paribhāse daḍi āṇāya 
taḍi saṃtāḷane piṇḍa saṅghāte chaḍḍa chaḍḍane

 vaṇṇa saṃvaṇṇane cuṇṇa cuṇṇane āṇa pesane
gaṇa saṃkalane kaṇṇa savaṇe cinta cintane

 santa saṅkocane manta gutta bhāsana jānane
cita saṃcetanādisu kitta saṃsaddane 

 yata nīyyātane gantha sandabbhe attha yācane
katha vākyappabandhe  vida ñāṇe nude cuda

 chadāpavāraṇe chadda vamane chanda icchayaṃ
vadībhivāda thomesu bhadikalyāṇakammani

 hiḷāda  sukhe gandha sūcane vidha kampane
randha pāke  māna pūjāyaṃ nu tthutimhi 

 thana devasadde ūna parihāne thena coriye
dhana sadde ñapa tosa nisāna māraṇādisu

 lapa vākye jhapa dāhe rupa ropaṇaādisu
pī tappane  kappa vitakke labhi vañcane

  vahi garahāyaṃ samu sāntvana dassane
kamu icchāya kantimhi  thoma silāghane

 timu temana saṅkāsu ama rogagatādisu
saṃgāma yuddhe  īra vācā pakampane

 vara āvaraṇicchāsu yācāyaṃ dhara dhāraṇe
tīra kamma samattimhi pāra sāmatthiyādisu

 tulummāne khala sove sañcaye pālarakkhaṇe
kala saṅkalanādīsu  mīla nimīlane

 sīlūpadhāraṇe mūla rohaṇe lala icchane
dula ukkhepaṇe pūla mahattana samussaye

 ghusa sadde pisa pese bhusālaṅkaraṇe 
rusa pārusiye khuṃsa akkose pusa posane

 disa uccāraṇādīsu vasa acchādane 
rasassāde rave snehe  sisa visesane

 si bandhe missa sammisse kuha vimbhāpane siyā
raha cāge gate  maha pūjāya 

 pihicchāyaṃ siyā vīḷa lajjāyaṃ eḷa phāḷane
hīḷa gārahiye pīḷa bādhāyaṃ taḷa tāḷane
laḷa pasevāyaṃ 


 bhuvādī ca rudhādī ca-divādi svādayo gaṇā
kiyādī ca tanādī ca-curādītīdha sattadhā

 kirayāvācittamakkhātu-mekekattho bahūdito
payogatonugantabbā-anekatthā hi dhātavo

 hitāya mandabuddhīnaṃ-vyattaṃ vaṇṇakkamā lahuṃ
racitā dhātumañjusā-sīlavaṃsena dhīmatā

 saddhammapaṅkeruharājahaṃso
āsiṭṭhadhammaṭṭhiti sīlavaṃso
yakkhaddilenākhya nivāsavāsī
yatissaro soyamidaṃ akāsi



 ekā nekassa rānantū-bhayesaṃ antimā sarā
aṅgānubandhā dhātūnaṃ-vuccantepi yathākkamaṃ

 dhātuno vyāñjanā pubbe-niggahītaṃ samantimā
ivaṇṇenārudhādīna-manubandhena ciṇhitaṃ

 sesānubandhā sabbesaṃ-hontīdhuccāraṇapphalā
uccāvacapphalā bhāsa-ntarampatvā bha vantipi

 nāmadhātuka bhāvopi-kirayāya adhikārato
viruddhantarābhāvā-kvacideva payujjate

 dvandayuttivasā kvāpi-ādeso yovibhattiyā
guṇādibhāva saddopi-takirayatthe vidhīyate
