
 aṭṭhamassa  paṭhame buddhā sāriputtādayo vā ti pāpamittatāti pade vuttassa paṭipakkhanayena
 dutiye ti samaṅgībhāvo ti payuñjanaṃ paṭipatti ti asamaṅgībhāvo ti appayuñjanaṃ appaṭipatti ti anuyogahetu
 catutthe ti catunnaṃ ariyasaccānaṃ paṭivijjhanakapuggalassa ti tasseva paṭivedhassa kāraṇabhūtā ettha ca cattāri ariyasaccāni bujjhati aññāṇaniddāya vāpi bujjhatīti bodhīti laddhanāmo ariyasāvako bujjhanakasatto tassa bujjhanakasattassa ti tassā dhammasāmaggisaṅkhātāya bodhiyā bujjhanaṭṭhena bodhiyo bodhiyo eva saccasampaṭibodhassa aṅgāti vuttaṃ ti vipassanādīnaṃ kāraṇānaṃ bujjhitabbānañca saccānaṃ anurūpaṃ bujjhanato  paṭimukhaṃ paccakkhabhāvena abhimukhaṃ bujjhanato  sammā aviparītato bujjhanato ti evaṃ atthavisesadīpakehi upasaggehi tiādi vuttaṃ bodhisaddo sabbavisesayuttaṃ bujjhanasāmaññena saṅgaṇhāti ti iminā tassā dhammasāmaggiyā bujjhanassa ekantakāraṇataṃ dasseti ti vuttappakārena etaṃ bojjhaṅgāti (paṭi ma 217) padaṃ niddese  vibhattameva
 pañcame ti yāthāvato sakiccakaraṇabhūmi ti yāthāvasarasabhūmi ti balavavipassanā keci bhaṅgañāṇato paṭṭhāyāti vadanti vipassanāya pādakajjhāne ca satiādayo bojjhaṅgapakkhikā eva pariyāyabodhipakkhiyabhāvato tiādi catubbidhānaṃ bojjhaṅgānaṃ bhūmivibhāgadassanaṃ
 chaṭṭhe ti tesaṃ bhikkhūnaṃ antare kāmaṃ saṅgītiāruḷhavasena appakamidaṃ suttapadaṃ bhagavā panettha sannipatitaparisāya ajjhāsayānurūpaṃ vitthārikaṃ karotīti katvā idaṃ vuttaṃ  ti ti tassā kathāya atiracchānakathābhāvamāhu  tathā hi sā pubbe bahuñātikaṃ ahosi bahupakkhaṃ idāni appañātikaṃ appapakkhanti aniccatāmukhena niyyānikapakkhikā jātā ti yathāvuttāya parihāniyā nti nihīnaṃ ti idaṃ kammassakatajjhānapaññānampi visesanameva tadubhayampi hi bāhirakānaṃ tappaññādvayato sātisayameva sabbaññubuddhānaṃ desanāya laddhavisesato vivaṭṭūpanissayato ca
 sattame ti tathā kathentānaṃ tesaṃ bhikkhūnaṃ tattha upagamanena attano desanāya bhājanabhūtaṃ cittappavattiṃ ñatvā ti saddena jhānapaññādīnaṃ catunnampi paññānaṃ gahaṇaṃ
 aṭṭhamādīsu ti yā esa mama sāsanetiādinā heṭṭhā vuttanayeneva sesamettha uttānatthameva

 navame vagge natthi vattabbaṃ
