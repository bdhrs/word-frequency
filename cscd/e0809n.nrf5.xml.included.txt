


 abyayaṃ vuccate dāni cirassaṃ tu ciraṃ tathā
cirena cirarattāya saha saddhiṃ samaṃ amā

 punappunaṃ  abhiṇhañcā sakiṃ cābhikkhaṇaṃ muhuṃ
vajjane tu vinā nānā antarena rite puthu

 balavaṃ suṭṭhu cātīvā tisaye kimuta svati
aho tu kiṃ kimū dāhu vikappe kimutoda ca

avhāne bho are ambho
hambho re jeṅga āvuso
he hare tha kathaṃ kiṃsu nanu kacci nu kiṃ samā

 adhunetarahīdāni sampati aññadatthu tu
tagghe kaṃse sasakkañcā ddhā kāmaṃ jātu ve have

 yāvatā tāvatā yāva tāva kittāvatā tathā
ettāvatā ca kīveti paricchedatthavācakā

 yathā tathā yathevevaṃ yathānāma yathāhi ca
seyyathāpyevamevaṃ vā tatheva ca yathāpi ca

evampi ca seyyathāpi nāma yatharivāpi ca
paṭibhāgatthe yathāca viya tatharivāpi ca

 saṃ sāmañca sayaṃ cātha āma sāhu lahūpi ca
opāyikaṃ patirūpaṃ sādhvevaṃ sampaṭicchane

 yaṃ taṃ yato tato yena
teneti kāraṇe siyuṃ
asākalye tu cana ci nipphale tu mudhā bhave

 kadāci jātu tulyātha sabbato ca samantato
parito ca samantāpi atha micchā musā bhave

nisedhe na anomālaṃ nahi cetu sace yadi
anukulye tu saddhañca
nattaṃ  doso divā tvahe

 īsaṃ kiñci manaṃ appe sahasā tu atakkite
pure ggato tu purato peccāmutrabhavantare

aho  hī vimhaye tuṇhī
tu mone thāvi pātu ca
taṅkhaṇe sajju sapadi balakkāre pasayha ca

 sudaṃ kho  assu yagghe vehādayo padapūraṇe
antarenantarā anto vassaṃ nūna ca nicchaye

 ānande sañca diṭṭhā tha virodhakathane nanu
kāmappavedane kacci usūyopagametthu ca

evāvadhāraṇe ñeyyaṃ yathāttaṃ tu yathātathaṃ
nīcaṃ appe mahatyuccaṃ atha pāto page samā

 nicce sadā sanaṃpāyo
bāhulye bāhiraṃ bahi
bahiddhā bāhirā bāhye sīghetu saṇikaṃ bhave

atthaṃ adassane duṭṭhu nindāyaṃ vandane namo
sammā suṭṭhu pasaṃsāyaṃ atho sattāya matthi ca

sāyaṃ sāyejja atrāhe
suve tu sve anāgate
tato pare parasuve hiyyotu divase gate

 yattha yatra yahiṃcātha tattha tatra tahiṃtahaṃ
atho uddhañca upari heṭṭhā tu ca adho samā

 codane iṅgha handātha ārādūrā ca ārakā
parammukhā tu ca raho sammukhā tvāvi pātu ca

saṃsayatthamhi appeva appevanāma nūti ca
nidassane ititthañca evaṃ kicche kathañci ca

hā khede sacchi paccakkhe
dhuvaṃ thirevadhāraṇe
tiro tu tiriyaṃ cātha kucchāyaṃ duṭṭhu kuccate

suvatthi āsiṭṭhatthamhi nindāyaṃ tu dhī kathyate
kuhiñcanaṃ kuhiṃ kutra kuttha kattha kahaṃ kva tha

 ihedhātra  tu etthāttha
atha sabbatra sabbadhi
kadā kudācanaṃ cātha tadāni ca tadā samā

 ādikamme bhusatthe ca sambhavotiṇṇa tittisu
niyogissariyappīti dāna pūjāgga santisu

 dassane tappare saṅge pasaṃsā gati suddhisu
hiṃsā pakārantobhāva viyogāvayavesu ca
popasaggo disāyoge patthanā dhitiādisu

parāsaddo parihāni parājaya gatīsu ca
bhusatthe paṭilomatthe vikkamāmasanādisu

 nissesābhāva sanyāsa bhusattha mokkha rāsisu
gehādesopamāhīna pasādaniggatāccaye

 dassanosānanikkhantā dhobhāgesvavadhāraṇe
sāmīpye bandhane cheka ntobhāgoparatīsu ca

 pātubhāve viyoge ca nisedhādo ni dissati
atho nīharaṇe cevā varaṇādo ca nī siyā

 uddhakamma viyoga tta lābha titti samiddhisu
pātubhāvaccayābhāva pabalatte pakāsane
dakkhaggatāsu kathane sattimokkhādike u ca

du kucchitesadatthesu virūpatte pyasobhane
siyābhāvāsamiddhīsu kicche cānandanādike

saṃ samodhāna saṅkhepa samantatta samiddhisu
sammā bhusa saha ppatthā bhimukhatthesu saṅgate
vidhāne pabhave pūjā punappunakriyādisu

 vividhātisayābhāva bhusattissariyāccaye
viyoge kalahe pātu bhāve bhāse ca kucchane

 dūrānabhimukhattesu mohānavaṭṭhitīsu ca
padhāna dakkhatā kheda sahatthādo vi dissati

 viyoge  jānane cādhobhāga nicchaya  suddhisu
īsadatthe paribhave desa byāpana hānisu
vacokriyāya theyye ca ñāṇappattādike ava

 pacchā bhusatta sādisyā nupacchinnānuvattisu
hīne ca tatiyatthādho bhāgesvanugate hite
dese lakkhaṇa vicchettha mbhūta bhāgādike anu

 samantatthe paricchede pūjāliṅgana vajjane
dosakkhāne nivāsanā vaññādhāresu bhojane
soka byāpana tatvesu lakkhaṇādo siyā pari

 ābhimukhya visiṭṭhuddha kammasāruppavuddhisu
pūjādhika kulāsacca lakkhaṇādimhi cāpyabhi

 adhikissara pāṭhādhi ṭṭhāna pāpuṇanesvadhi
nicchaye coparittādhi bhavane ca visesane

 paṭidānanisedhesu vāmādānanivattisu
sādise paṭinidhimhi ābhimukhyagatīsu ca

 patibodhe patigate tathā punakriyāya ca
sambhāvane paṭiccatthe patīti lakkhaṇādike
su sobhane sukhe sammā bhusa suṭṭhu samiddhisu

 ābhimukhya samīpādi kammāliṅgana pattisu
mariyāduddhakammicchā bandhanābhividhīsu ā

 nivāsāvhāna gahaṇa kicchesattha nivattisu
appasādāsi saraṇa patiṭṭhāvimhayādisu

 antobhāva bhusattāti saya pūjāsvatikkame
bhūtabhāve pasaṃsāyaṃ daḷhatthādo siyā ati

 sambhāvane ca garahā pekkhāsu ca samuccaye
pañhe saṃvaraṇe ceva āsīsatthe apīritaṃ

 niddese vajjane pūjā pagate vāraṇepi ca
padussane ca garahā corikādo siyā apa

 samīpapūjā  sādissa dosakkhānopapattisu
bhusattopagamādhikya pubbakammanivattisu
gayhākāroparittesu upetya nasanādike

evaṃ nidassanākāro pamāsu sampahaṃsane
upadese ca vacana paṭiggāhevadhāraṇe
garahāyedamatthe ca parimāne ca pucchane

 samuccaye samāhāre nvācaye cetarītare
padapūraṇamatte ca casaddo avadhāraṇe

iti hetupakāresu ādimhi cāvadhāraṇe
nidassane padatthassa vipallāse samāpane

 samuccaye copamāyaṃ saṃsaye padapūraṇe
vavatthitavibhāsāyaṃ vāvassagge vikappane

 bhūsane vāraṇe cālaṃ vuccate pariyattiyaṃ
athothānantarārambha pañhesu padapūraṇe

 pasaṃsāgarahāsaññā sīkārādo  pi nāma tha
nicchaye cānumānasmiṃ siyā nūna vitakkane

 pucchāvadhāraṇānuññā sāntvanālapane nanu
vatekaṃsa dayā hāsa khedālapana vimhaye

 vākyārambha visādesu handa hāsenukampane
yāva tu tāva sākalya mānāvadhyavadhāraṇe

 pācī pura ṅgatotthesu puratthā paṭhame pyatha
pabandhe ca cirātīte nikaṭāgāmike purā

 nisedhavākyālaṅkārā vadhāraṇapasiddhisu
khalvāsanne tu abhito
bhimukhobhayatodike

kāmaṃ yadyapisaddatthe ekaṃsatthe ca dissati
atho pana visesasmiṃ tatheva padapūraṇe

hi kāraṇe visesāva dhāraṇe padapūraṇe
tu hetuvajje tatthātha ku pāpesatthakucchane

nu saṃsaye ca pañhe tha nānā nekattha vajjane
kiṃ tu pucchājigucchāsu kaṃ tu vārimhi muddhani

amā sahasamīpe tha bhede appaṭhame puna
kirānussavārucisu udāpyatthe vikappane

 patīcī carime pacchā sāmi tvaddhe jigucchane
pakāse sambhave pātu
aññoññe tu raho mitho

hā khedasokadukkhesu khede tvahaha vimhaye
bhiṃsāpane dhī nindāyaṃ pidhāne tiriyaṃ tiro

 tuna tvāna tave tvā tuṃ dhā so thā kkhattu meva ca
to tha tra hiñcanaṃ hiṃhaṃ dhi ha hi dha dhunā rahi

 dāni vodācanaṃ dājja thaṃ tattaṃ jjha jju ādayo
samāso cābyayībhāvo
yādeso cābyayaṃ bhaveti
iti abyayavaggo
sāmaññakaṇḍo tatiyo
abhidhānappadīpikā samattā
nigamana

saggakaṇḍo ca bhūkaṇḍo tathā sāmaññakaṇḍiti
kaṇḍattayānvitā esā abhidhānappadīpikā

 tidive mahiyaṃ bhujagāvasathe
sakalatthasamavhayadīpaniyaṃ
iha yo kusalo matimā sa naro
paṭu hoti mahāmunino vacane

parakkamabhujo nāma bhūpālo guṇabhūsano
laṅkāya māsi tejassī jayī kesarivikkamo

 vibhinnaṃ ciraṃ bhikkhusaṅghaṃ nikāya
ttayasmiñca kāresi sammā samagge
sadehaṃva niccādaro dīghakālaṃ
mahagghehi rakkhesi yo paccayehi

 yena laṅkā vihārehi gāmārāmapurīhi ca
kittiyā viya sambādhī katā khettehi vāpihi

 yassāsādhāraṇaṃ patvā nuggahaṃ sabbakāmadaṃ
ahampi ganthakārattaṃ patto vibudhagocaraṃ

 kārite tena pāsāda gopurādivibhūsite
saggakhaṇḍeva tattoyā sayasmiṃ patibimbite

mahājetavanākhyamhi vihāre sādhusammate
sarogāmasamūhamhi vasatā santavuttinā

 saddhammaṭṭhitikāmena moggallānena dhīmatā
therena racitā esā abhidhānappadīpikāti
