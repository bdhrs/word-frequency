3. Vinayapeyyālaṃ
201 . Dveme   bhikkhave, atthavase paṭiccā Sikkhāpadaṃ paññatta Saṅghasuṭṭhutāyā Saṅghaphāsutāyā Dummaṅkūna Pesalāna Diṭṭhadhammikānaṃ āsavāna Saṃvarāyā Samparāyikāna  Paṭighātāyā Verāna Vajjāna Bhayāna Akusalāna Gihīnaṃ anukampāyā Pāpicchānaṃ pakkhupacchedāyā Appasannānaṃ pasādāyā Pasannānaṃ bhiyyobhāvāyā Saddhammaṭṭhitiyā Vinayānuggahāyā
202-230 . Pātimokkhaṃ paññatta Pātimokkhuddeso bhikkhūnaṃ pañca, bhikkhunīnaṃ cattāroti nava pātimokkhuddesā paññattā. Pātimokkhaṭṭhapana Pavāraṇā paññattā Pavāraṇaṭṭhapanaṃ paññatta  tajjanīyakammaṃ   niyassakammaṃ pabbājanīyakammaṃ paṭisāraṇīyakammaṃ ukkhepanīyakammaṃ parivāsadānaṃ mūlāya paṭikassanaṃ mānattadānaṃ abbhānaṃ osāraṇīyaṃ nissāraṇīyaṃ
upasampadā ñattikammaṃ ñattidutiyakammaṃ ñatticatutthakammaṃ apaññatte paññattaṃ paññatte anupaññattaṃ sammukhāvinayo sativinayo  amūḷhavinayo paṭiññātakaraṇaṃ Yebhuyyasikā tassapāpiyasikā tiṇavatthārako
Vinayapeyyālaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
