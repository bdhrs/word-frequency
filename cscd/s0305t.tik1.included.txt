
1 pabbatavaggo

 bujjhati  catusaccaṃ ariyasāvako etāyāti  dhammasāmaggī ariyasāvako pana catusaccaṃ bujjhatīti ti kāraṇā yāya dhammasāmaggiyāti sambandho taṇhāvasena  diṭṭhivasena  sassatadiṭṭhiyā  ucchedadiṭṭhiyā  līnavasena  uddhaccavasena  kāmasukhānuyogavasena  attakilamathānuyogavasena  (saṃ ni 11) 
kilesavasena patiṭṭhānaṃ abhisaṅkhāravasena āyūhanā taṇhādiṭṭhīhi patiṭṭhānaṃ avasesakilesābhisaṅkhārehi āyūhanā sabbākusalābhisaṅkhāravasena patiṭṭhānaṃ sabbalokiyakusalābhisaṅkhāravasena āyūhanāti 
vuttesu pakāresu idha avuttānaṃ vasena veditabbo ti etena sikhāpattavipassanāsahagatānampi satiādīnaṃ bojjhaṅgabhāvaṃ dasseti tiādinā maggaphalena sahagatānaṃ sattahi bojjhaṅgehi bhāvitehi saccapaṭivedho hotīti kathamidaṃ jānitabbanti codanaṃ sandhāyāha tiādi ti etena bodhibojjhaṅgasaddānaṃ samudāyāvayavavisayataṃ dasseti ti etena puggalapaññattiyā avijjamānapaññattibhāvaṃ dasseti
bodhāya saṃvattantīti bojjhaṅgāti vuttaṃ kāraṇattho aṅgasaddoti bujjhatīti bodhi bodhiyā eva aṅgāti ti vuttaṃ ti vipassanādīnaṃ kāraṇānaṃ bujjhitabbānaṃ saccānaṃ anurūpaṃ paccakkhabhāvena paṭimukhaṃ aviparītaṃ sammā bujjhantīti evaṃ vatthuvisesadīpakehi  uparimaggehi anubujjhantītiādinā vuttabodhisaddehi nippadesena vuttaṃ  bujjhanatāsāmaññena saṅgaṇhātīti ettha ca līnapatiṭṭhāna-kāmasukhallikānuyoga-ucchedābhinivesānaṃ dhammavicaya-vīriyapītipadhāna-dhammasāmaggī paṭipakkho uddhaccāyūhanaattakilamathānuyoga-sassatābhinivesānaṃ passaddhisamādhi-upekkhāpadhāna-dhammasāmaggī paṭipakkho sati pana ubhayatthāpi icchitabbā tathā hi sā sabbatthikā vuttā
saṃ-saddo pasaṃsāyaṃ punadeva sundaro ca atthopīti āha ti ti abhivisiṭṭhabhāvena nibbatteti savisesabhāvaṃ vadati ti vatvā tattha yathāvuttavivekattayato aññaṃ vivekadvayaṃ uddharitvā dassetuṃ tiādi vuttaṃ tattha jhānakkhaṇe tāva kiccato vikkhambhanavivekanissitaṃ vipassanākkhaṇe ajjhāsayato paṭippassaddhivivekanissitaṃ bhāvetīti tenāha  anuttaraṃ vimokkhaṃ upasampajja viharissāmīti tattha tattha nicchayatāya kasiṇajjhānaggahaṇena anuppādānampi gahaṇaṃ daṭṭhabbaṃ


 tiṭṭhanti etenāti ṭhiti kāraṇaṃ kammautucittāhārasaññito catubbidho paccayo ṭhiti etassāti  āhārapaccayasaddā hi ekatthā ti kāmacchando paccayo asubhe subhākārena pavattanato subhanti vuccati tena kāraṇena pavattanakassa aññassa kāmacchandassa nimittattā nti ti yathāvuttassa subhassa ti subhākārena iṭṭhākārena vā gayhamānaṃ rūpādiārammaṇampi subhanimittaṃ vuttākārena ti ākaṅkhitassa hitasukhassa anupāyabhūto manasikāro tato eva ti ayonisomanasikāro nti yathāniddhārite kāmacchandabhūte tadārammaṇabhūte ca duvidhepi subhanimitte aṭṭhakathāyaṃ pana subhārammaṇeicceva vuttaṃ ti -saddena kāmacchandanīvaraṇassa āhāradassanapāḷi uttānāti katvā vuttaṃ  ti
 purimuppannassa pacchā uppajjanakassa nimittabhāvato  nāma ekūnavīsati āghātavatthubhūtā sattasaṅkhārā ti pantasenāsanādīsu aramaṇaṃ  ti ukkaṇṭhabhāvo ti dūresu vivittesu vā ti samathavipassanādhammesu  ratipaṭipakkho ti aramaṇākāro ti anabhiratabhāvo ti anabhiramaṇākāro ti ukkaṇṭhanākāro ti ukkaṇṭhanavaseneva paritassanā
 na sabhāvasiddhaṃ nti nāmakāye alasabhāvo  pana tandīti jātiālasiyanti vuttaṃ ti etena atisītādipaccayā saṅkocāpattiṃ dasseti  kilesavatthuvibhaṅge (vibha aṭṭha 857) ti jātiālasiyaṃ ti tandiyanākāro ti tandiyā abhibhūtacittatā alasassa bhāvo  ālasyāyanākāro  ālasyāyitassa bhāvo  iti sabbehipi imehi padehi kilesavasena kāyālasiyaṃ kathitaṃ
ti sammohavasena ti kāyassa virūpato namanā ti phandanā punappunaṃ jambhanā ti purato namanā ti pacchato namanā ti samantato namanā ti yathā hi tantato uṭṭhitapesakāro kiñcideva upariṭṭhitaṃ gahetvā ujuṃ kāyaṃ ussāpeti evaṃ kāyassa uddhaṃ ṭhapanā nti uppannabyādhitā iti sabbehipi imehi padehi kilesavasena kāyaphandanameva kathitaṃ (vibha aṭṭha 858)
ti bhattavasena pariḷāhuppatti ti bhuttavato ti bhattagelaññaṃ atibhuttapaccayā hi mucchāpatto viya hoti ti bhuttapaccayā kilantabhāvo ti bhattadaratho kucchipūraṃ bhuttavato hi pariḷāhuppattiyā upahatindriyo viya hoti kāyo khijjati nti bhuttabhattaṃ nissāya kāyassa akammaññatā
ti ārammaṇe cittassa saṅkocappatti ti cittassa gilānabhāvo ti akallako vuccati tathā cāha nāhaṃ bhante akallakoti ti cittagelaññasaṅkhāto akammaññanākāro ti olīyanākāro iriyāpathikampi cittaṃ yassa vasena iriyāpathaṃ sandhāretuṃ asakkonto olīyati tassa taṃ ākāraṃ sandhāya vuttaṃ ti dutiyapadaṃ upasaggena vaḍḍhitaṃ nti avipphārikatāya  saṅkocappattaṃ itare dve ākāraniddesā nti avipphārikatāya anussāhanā asaṅgahanasaṅgahanaṃ thiyanākāro nti thiyitassa ākāro avipphārikatāti attho
ti cittassa avūpasantatā asannisinnabhāvo tenāha  ti nti svāyaṃ avūpasantākāro vikkhepasabhāvattā vikkhepahetutāya ca atthato etaṃ uddhaccakukkuccameva
 nāma buddhe kaṅkhatītiādinā āgataaṭṭhakaṅkhāvatthubhūtā dhammā yasmā vicikicchā byāpādādayo viya anu anu uggahaṇapaccayā uppajjati tasmā kaṅkhāṭṭhānīyaṃ ārammaṇameva dassitaṃ vicikicchāya ārammaṇadhammāti yasmā purimuppannā vicikicchā pacchā vicikicchāya paccayo hoti tasmā vicikicchāpi vicikicchāṭṭhānīyadhammā veditabbā tatrāyaṃ vacanattho  tiṭṭhanti pavattanti etthāti ṭhānīyā vicikicchā eva ṭhānīyā  aṭṭhakathāyaṃ pana ārammaṇassapi tattha visesapaccayataṃ upādāya ti vuttaṃ subhanimittassa hi paccayabhāvamattaṃ sandhāyetaṃ vuttaṃ tathāpi yathā paṭighampi paṭighanimittanti katvā byāpādo upanissayena kathitoti vuttaṃ evaṃ subhampi subhanimittanti katvā kāmacchando upanissayena kathitoti sakkā viññātuṃ  thinamiddhauddhaccakukkuccāni tattha thinamiddhaṃ aññamaññaṃ sahajātādivasena paccayo tathā uddhaccakukkuccanti ubhayesampi upanissayakoṭiyā paccayabhāve vattabbameva natthīti āha ti
yasmā  sati nāma cattāro satipaṭṭhānātiādinā tesaṃ tesaṃ dhammānaṃ anussaraṇavasena vattati tasmā te dhammā satisambojjhaṅgaṭṭhānīyā nāma lokuttaradhamme ca anussavādivasena gahetvā tathā pavattateva tena vuttaṃ tiādi
kosallaṃ vuccati paññā tato uppannā ti anavajjā hutvā sukhavipākā vipaccanakā padadvayena paccayato sabhāvato kiccato phalato kusaladhammaṃ dasseti akusalaniddesepi eseva nayo ti gārayhā ti agārayhā  lāmakā  seṭṭhā  kāḷakā asuddhā  odātā suddhā  -saddo paṭhame vikappe sadisakoṭṭhāsattho dutiye paṭipakkhakoṭṭhāsattho tatiye niggahetabbapaṭipakkhakoṭṭhāsattho daṭṭhabbo
kusalakiriyāya ādikammabhāvena pavattavīriyaṃ dhitisabhāvatāya dhātūti vuttanti āha nti laddhāsevanaṃ vīriyaṃ balappattaṃ hutvā paṭipakkhaṃ vidhamatīti āha  nti adhimattādhimattatarānaṃ paṭipakkhadhammānaṃ vidhamanasamatthaṃ paṭupaṭutarādibhāvappattaṃ hotīti āha  nti
tiṭṭhati pavattati etthāti ṭhānīyā ārammaṇadhammā pītisambojjhaṅgassa ṭhānīyāti ti ti vuttaṃ yasmā aparāparuppattiyā pītipi tathā vattabbataṃ labhatīti vuttaṃ visuddhimagge pītiyā eva taṃ nāmanti ti daratho kilesapariḷāho so passambhati etāyāti darathapassaddhi kāyapassaddhiyā vedanādikhandhattayassa viya rūpakāyassapi passambhanaṃ hoti cittapassaddhiyā cittasseva passambhanaṃ tato evettha bhagavatā lahutādīnaṃ viya duvidhatā vuttā tathā samāhitākāraṃ sallakkhetvā gayhamāno samathova  tassa ārammaṇabhūtaṃ paṭibhāganimittampi vividhaṃ aggaṃ etassāti byaggo vikkhepo tathā hi so anavaṭṭhānaraso bhantatāpaccupaṭṭhāno vutto ekaggabhāvato byaggapaṭipakkhoti  samādhi so eva nimittanti pubbe viya vattabbaṃ tenāha nti
yo  ārammaṇe iṭṭhāniṭṭhākāraṃ anādiyitvā gahetabbo majjhattākāro yo ca pubbe upekkhāsambojjhaṅgassa bhāvanāvasena uppanno majjhattākāro duvidhopi so upekkhāya ārammaṇadhammoti adhippetoti āha  ti  ārammaṇasseva tesaṃ visesapaccayabhāvato ti vīriyādayo cattāro dhammā tesañhi upanissayova sātisayo icchitabboti


 nāma maggaphalapariyāpannā sammāvācākammantājīvā sīlalakkhaṇappattā  tadaññe cetanādayo  pana kiriyābyākatacittapariyāpannaṃ cārittasīlaṃ vārittasīlassa pana sambhavo eva natthi viramaṇavasena pavattiyā abhāvato pubbabhāgasīlaṃ lokiyasīlanti keci
nti cakkhūhi dassanaṃ nti sabhāvadhammānaṃ saṅkhatānaṃ paccattalakkhaṇassa ñātapariññāya aniccādisāmaññalakkhaṇassa tīraṇapariññāya dassanaṃ pajahantopi hi te pahātabbākārato passati nāma nibbānassa tathalakkhaṇaṃ maggaphalehi dassanaṃ taṃ pana  jhānena pathavīkasiṇādīnaṃ abhiññāhi rūpānaṃ dassanampi  savanapayirupāsanānaṃ parato gahitattā nti pañhapucchanavasena payirupāsanaṃ aññakammatthāya upasaṅkamanassa kevalaṃ upasaṅkamaneneva jotitattā
ariyānaṃ anussati nāma guṇavasena tatthāpi laddhaovādāvajjanamukhena yathābhūtasīlādiguṇānussaraṇanti dassetuṃ tiādi vuttaṃ ti ariyehi aññesaṃ sāsanikānaṃyeva santike tenāha  ti ti sāsanikehi aññesu tāpasaparibbājakādīsu tattha hi pabbajjā ariyānaṃ anupabbajjā nāma na hotīti vuttaṃ
 samantapāsādikattā mahātherassa ti nissayasīsena nissitasallakkhaṇaṃ na hi pabbajjā dīpapaṭiladdhā atha kho dīpanivāsiācariyapaṭiladdhā  tassa parivāratāya pabbajjāyāti
ti taṃ ovādānusāsanidhammaṃ cinteti citte karoti ti punappunaṃ parivitakkanena tadatthaṃ vitakkanipphāditaṃ karoti ti sampādito hoti taṃ pana sampādanaṃ pāripūri evāti āha ti ti yathāvutte dhamme ti ñāṇassa pavattanavasena nti tasmiṃ tasmiṃ ovādadhamme āgatānaṃ rūpārūpadhammānaṃ nti visesalakkhaṇaṃ sāmaññalakkhaṇañca ti idaṃ rūpaṃ ettakaṃ rūpantiādinā vicayaṃ āpajjati ti aniccaṃ calaṃ palokaṃ pabhaṅgūtiādinā ñāṇaṃ pavatteti ti rūpasattakārūpasattakakkamena  vipassanaṃ paccakkhato viya aniccatādīnaṃ dassanaṃ sammasanaṃ āpajjati
nti phalānisaṃsāti vuttadvayaṃ  pariyāyasaddattā ti pageva ti maraṇakālasamīpe samīpatthe hi idaṃ bhummanti āha ti
 ñāṇassa tikkhamajjhamudubhāvena tenāha tiādi  āyuvemajjhaṃ atikkamitvā parinibbāyanato ti avihādīsu yattha katthaci ti vipassanāñāṇasaṅkhārasaṅkhātena payogena saha vipassanāpayogenāti attho suddhāvāsabhūmiyaṃ uddhaṃyeva maggasoto etassāti  paṭisandhivasena akaniṭṭhabhavaṃ gacchatīti 
avihādīsu vattamānopi ekaṃsato uddhaṃgamanāraho puggalo akaniṭṭhagāmī eva nāmāti vuttaṃ ti nti niddhāraṇe sāmivacanaṃ uddhaṃsotabhāvato yadipi heṭṭhimādīsupi ariyabhūmi nibbattateva tathāpi tattha bhūmīsu āyuṃ  aggahetvā akaniṭṭhabhave āyuvaseneva soḷasakappasahassāyukatā daṭṭhabbā satta phalā sattānisaṃsā pāṭikaṅkhāti vuttattā ti vuttaṃ sattannampi sahabhāvo labbhatīti ti vuttaṃ tayidaṃ pāḷiyaṃ tattha tattha tasmiṃ samayeti āgatavacanena viññāyati bojjhaṅgānaṃ pana nānāsabhāvattā ti vuttaṃ


ti satisambojjhaṅgo nāma seṭṭho uttamo pavaro tasmāhaṃ satisambojjhaṅgasīsena phalasamāpattiṃ appetvā viharissāmīti evaṃ ce mayhaṃ pubbabhāge hotīti attho ti svāyaṃ satisambojjhaṅgo sabbaso pamāṇakarakilesābhāvato appamāṇadhammārammaṇato ca appamāṇoti evaṃ mayhaṃ antosamāpattiyaṃ asammohavasena hoti ti bhāvanāpāripūriyā suṭṭhu paripuṇṇoti evaṃ  mayhaṃ antosamāpattiyaṃ asammohavasena hotīti ti yathākālaparicchedasamāpattiyā avaṭṭhānena tappariyāpannatāya satisambojjhaṅgo tiṭṭhati paṭibandhavasena ti uppādassa anāvajjanena asamannāhārena uppādasīsena cettha uppādavantova saṅkhārā gahitā nti nibbānaṃ uppādābhāvato uppādavantehi ca vinissaṭattā nti vipākappavattaṃ nti nibbānaṃ tappaṭikkhepato nti sabbasaṅkhāranimittaṃ nti nibbānaṃ ti uppādādianāmasanena kevalameva saṅkhāragahaṇaṃ nti nibbānaṃ  āvajjitakālato paṭṭhāya ārabbha pavattiyā ti aṭṭhahi kāraṇehi ti samāpattito vuṭṭhitakāle pajānāti ti uppādāvajjanādīhi ceva anuppādāvajjanādīhi ca vuttākāraviparītehi aṭṭhahi ākārehi  samāpattivasena anavaṭṭhānatopi gacchantaṃ ti thero ti
ti  phalasamāpattipariyāpannā bojjhaṅgā kiṃ pana te visuṃ visuṃ pavattantīti āha tiādi ti padhānaṃ seṭṭhaṃ katvā ti tadanugatā satisambojjhaṅgaṃ anugacchanakā tañca kho tathā katvā dhammaṃ paccavekkhaṇavasena keci pana taṃ paccavekkhaṇādikaṃ katvāti vadanti


ti ettha bodho nāma bujjhanaṃ taṃ pana kissa kenāti pucchanto ti vatvā taṃ dassento tiādimāha maggena nibbānaṃ bujjhanatthāya saṃvattanti paccavekkhaṇāya katakiccataṃ bujjhanatthāya saṃvattanti paṭhamavikappe sacchikiriyābhisamayo eva dassitoti tena atuṭṭhe ti dutiyavikappamāha nti padehi sabbaṃ maggakiccaṃ tassa phalañca dassitaṃ nti iminā nibbānasacchikiriyā tiādinā kilesappahānaṃ tiādinā paccavekkhaṇā dassitā


 nibaddhavāsavasena  ārāme nisīdanasīloti  parisaṃ ogāḷho hutvā caratīti ti āha  tiādi nti iminākārena nti niggahaṇaṃ tena pucchāpadassa atthaṃ vivarati nti niggahanibbeṭhanaṃ tena vissajjanapadassa atthaṃ vivarati ti etena itivādoti ettha iti-saddassa atthaṃ dasseti upārambhādhippāyo vadati etenāti  doso ti evaṃ imassa upari vādāropanaṃ hoti ti evaṃ tato pamokkho hoti  evaṃ vādappamokkhānisaṃsaṃ parehi āropitadosassa nibbeṭhanavasena dassetvā idāni dosapavedanavasena dassetuṃ tiādi vuttaṃ
ntiādito paṭṭhāya yāva tīṇi sucaritānīti ettakaṃ ṭhānaṃ nti indriyasaṃvaro khotiādinayappavattaṃ imaṃ desanaṃ ti na abhijjhāyati ti na abhitussati ti kammaṭṭhānārammaṇe samādhānavasena ṭhitaṃ hoti avaṭṭhitaṃ tenāha nti nti sammā avikkhepavasena ṭhitaṃ ti kammaṭṭhānānuyuñjanavasena paṭipakkhato nīvaraṇato vimuttiyā suṭṭhu vimuttanti  amanāparūpadassane  vilakkho na hoti  dosavasena  athaddhacitto kovesena hi cittaṃ thaddhaṃ hoti na mudukaṃ ti domanassavasena yo dīnabhāvo tadabhāvena niddosamānaso ti byāpajjābhāvena sītibhūtacitto

ti indriyasaṃvara-ājīvapārisuddhi-paccayasannissita-sīlāni ti lokiyāpi lokuttarāpi hontīti attho nti lokuttarānaṃ sattannaṃ bojjhaṅgānaṃ  te sandhāya vuttaṃ cattāro kho kuṇḍaliya satipaṭṭhānā bhāvitā bahulīkatā satta bojjhaṅge paripūrentīti ti yathāvuttasatipaṭṭhānā ti lokiyasatipaṭṭhānamūlakā bojjhaṅgāva ti ettha keci pubbabhāgā cāti pāṭhaṃ katvā pubbeva lokuttarā pubbabhāgā cāti atthaṃ vadanti ti satta kho kuṇḍaliya bojjhaṅgā bhāvitā bahulīkatā vijjāvimuttiṃ paripūrentīti evaṃ vuttā bojjhaṅgā lokuttarāva vijjāvimuttisahagatabhāvato

ti daṇḍena abhihaṭappattarūpahetuṃ sattesu uppajjanakadukkhaṃ ñāṇena vīmaṃsantassa tayidaṃ rūpanimittakaṃ sattesu uppajjanakaṃ dukkhaṃ ñāṇena karuṇāvihārissa visesato pakkhandatīti katvā vuttaṃ ti na pana sabbaso arūpe ānisaṃsadassanato
ti idaṃ pāmojjagahaṇamukhena tannissayaviññāṇassa gahaṇaṃ sambhavatīti katvā vuttaṃ nti vuttanayena viññāṇaggahaṇe paricitaṃ
ti  upekkhābrahmavihāraṃ viharato ti sukhādivasena ābhujanābhāvato ti sukhadukkhāti paramatthakammaggahaṇe vimukhatāsambhavato nti paramatthato avijjamānasattapaññattigahaṇaparicitaṃ tassa tassa abhāvamattakassa gahaṇampi dukkhaṃ kusalampi hoti sesaṃ suviññeyyameva


ti puretaraṃyeva asajjhāyakatānaṃ mantānaṃ appaṭibhānaṃ pageva paṭhamaṃyeva siddhaṃ tattha vattabbameva natthīti adhippāyo pariyuṭṭhānaṃ nāma abhibhavo gahaṇanti āha  ti vikkhambheti apanetīti vikkhambhanaṃ paṭipakkhato nissarati etenāti nissaraṇaṃ vikkhambhanañca taṃ nissaraṇañcāti  tenāha  tiādi sesapadadvayepi eseva nayo attanā araṇiyo pattabbo  tathā  veditabbo
aniccato anupassanto niccasaññaṃ pajahatītiādīsu byāpādādīnaṃ anāgatattā abyāpādavāre tadaṅganissaraṇaṃ na gahitaṃ kiñcāpi na gahitaṃ paṭisaṅkhānavasena pana tassa vinodetabbatāya tadaṅganissaraṇampi labbhatevāti sakkā viññātuṃ  upacārappattā appanāppattā vā yo koci kasiṇajjhānādibhedo  upacārappanāppattavasena gahetabbaṃ
ti tatto ti tasseva kuthitabhāvassa usmudakataṃ accuṇhataṃ patto tenāha ti ti tilabījakakaṇṇikakesarādibhedena ti udakapicchilena  ākulatāya  kalaluppattiyā ti ālokarahite ṭhāne


ti ekaṃsato eva bhagavā ete dhammā nīvaraṇā citte kusalappavattiyā  nīvaraṇato ti kāyaparissamo so pana aṭṭhuppattiyā paccayattā darathoti vutto cittakilamatho tappaccayajāto daṭṭhabbo tenāha  tiādi cittadarathopi paṭippassambhīti ānetvā sambandho ti yathādhigatena ariyamaggeneva ti abhayassa rājakumārassa  kāyacittadarathadvayaṃ


7 ānāpānavaggo

ti saññāsīsena upacārajjhānaṃ vadati tenāha ntiādi ti idaṃ saviññāṇakaṃ aviññāṇakampi kāyasāmaññato gahetvā vuttaṃ ti ettha upādiyati attano ārammaṇaṃ gaṇhātīti  upādānaṃ etassa ekadese appahīne satīti attho

8 nirodhavaggo

nti anabhiramaṇaṃ anapekkhitaṃ accantanirodhabhūte nibbāne pavattasaññā  tattha sā maggasahagatā lokuttarā  yā pana nibbāne ninnabhāvena pavattā upasamānussatisahagatā ca sā lokiyāti āha  ti tesaṃ navasūtiādi pamādapāṭho ekādasasu appanā hoti nava upacārajjhānikāti pāṭho gahetabbo ti idampi idhāgatanayo na visuddhimaggādīsu āgatanayo  ettha ca ārammaṇādīsu yathāyogaṃ appanaṃ upacāraṃ vā pāpuṇitvā arahattappattassa pubbabhāgabhūtā vipassanāmaggabojjhaṅgā kathitā
