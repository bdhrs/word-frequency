

 pātimokkhaṭṭhapanakkhandhake   ti aruṇuṭṭhitakālepi hi nandimukhā viya ratti khāyati tenāha nandimukhiyā rattiyāti nti attacittasantāne kilesapūtibhāvena antopūtiṃ nti kilesavassanavasena avassutaṃ nti ākiṇṇadosatāya saṃkiliṭṭhajātaṃ) ti aparisuddhā ānanda parisāti vacanaṃ sutvāyeva hi tena pakkamitabbaṃ siyā evaṃ apakkamitvā yāva bāhāgahaṇāpi nāma so moghapuriso āgamessati acchariyamidanti dasseti
ti na paṭhamameva gambhīro anupubbena gambhīroti attho ti vīcīnaṃ osakkanakandaraṃ mariyādavelaṃ nātikkamati ti tīrato appeti ussāretīti attho ti arahattappatti
ti āpattiṃ āpajjitvā paṭicchādento aññaṃ navaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati idametaṃ sandhāya vuttaṃ ti āpattiṃ āpajjitvā vivaranto aññaṃ nāpajjati idametaṃ sandhāya vuttaṃ


nti ettha pure vā pacchā vā ṭhapitampi aṭṭhapitaṃ hoti khette ṭhapitameva pana ṭhapitaṃ nāma hoti tasmā suṇātu me bhante saṅgho ajjuposatho pannaraso yadi saṅghassa pattakallaṃ saṅgho uposathaṃ kareyyāti ettha yāva rekāraṃ bhaṇati tāva ṭhapetabbaṃ idañhi khettaṃ yyakāre pana vutte ṭhapentena pacchā ṭhapitaṃ nāma hoti suṇātu meti anāraddheyeva ṭhapentena pure ṭhapitaṃ hoti


ti tena puggalena sā vipatti katā vā hotu akatā vā pātimokkhaṭṭhapanakassa saññāamūlikavasena amūlikā hoti ti katañca akatañca ubhayaṃ gahetvā vuttaṃ
ti kammaṃ kopetukāmatāya saṅghassa kamme karīyamāne neva āgacchati na chandaṃ deti sammukhībhūtova paṭikkosati tena dukkaṭaṃ āpajjati iccassāpi sāpattikasseva pātimokkhaṃ ṭhapitaṃ hoti ti puna kātabbaṃ kammanti paccādiyati tena ukkoṭanakena pācittiyaṃ āpajjati iccassāpi sāpattikasseva pātimokkhaṃ ṭhapitaṃ hoti


ti ettha maggenamaggapaṭipādanādīsu ākārādisaññā veditabbā ti ettha diṭṭhañca sutañca pāḷiyaṃ  āgatameva sace pana tehi diṭṭhasutehi parisaṅkaṃ uppādeyya taṃ sandhāya vuttaṃ tāya parisaṅkāyāti


ti ettha sāsanaṃ sodhetukāmo bhikkhu yaṃ adhikaraṇaṃ attanā ādiyati taṃ attādānanti vuccati nti ettha rājabhayaṃ corabhayaṃ dubbhikkhabhayaṃ vassārattoti ayaṃ akālo viparīto kālo
nti asantamidaṃ mayā adhammo vā dhammoti dhammo vā adhammoti avinayo vā vinayoti vinayo vā avinayoti dussīlo vā puggalo sīlavāti sīlavā vā dussīloti gahitoti attho vipariyāyena bhūtaṃ veditabbaṃ nti ettha  yaṃ jīvitantarāyāya vā brahmacariyantarāyāya vā saṃvattati idaṃ anatthasaṃhitaṃ viparītaṃ atthasaṃhitaṃ nāma
ti appekadā hi rājabhayādīsu evarūpā attano pakkhassa upatthambhakā bhikkhū laddhuṃ na sakkā honti taṃ sandhāya vuttaṃ na labhissāmīti  appekadā pana khemasubhikkhādīsu laddhuṃ sakkā honti taṃ sandhāya labhissāmīti vuttaṃ
nti kosambakānaṃ viya bhaṇḍanaṃ kalaho viggaho vivādo saṅghabhedo ca bhavissatīti ti subhaddaṃ vuḍḍhapabbajitaṃ niggahetvā pañcasatikasaṅgītiṃ karontassa mahākassapattherasseva dasavatthuke adhikaraṇe dasabhikkhusahassāni niggahetvā sattasatikasaṅgītiṃ karontassa āyasmato yasasseva saṭṭhibhikkhusahassāni niggahetvā sahassikasaṅgītiṃ karontassa moggaliputtatissattherasseva ca pacchā  samanussaraṇakaraṇaṃ hoti sāsanassa ca vigatupakkilesacandimasūriyasassirikatāya saṃvattati


tiādīsu yena gahaṭṭhapabbajitesu yo koci pahaṭo vā hoti gihīnaṃ gaṇḍaphālanādīni vejjakammāni vā katāni tassa kāyasamācāro upacikāhi khāyitatālapaṇṇamiva chiddo ca paṭimāsituṃ yattha katthaci gahetvā ākaḍḍhituṃ sakkuṇeyyatāya sappaṭimaṃso ca hoti viparīto acchiddo appaṭimaṃsoti veditabboti vacīsamācāro pana musāvādaomasavādapesuññaamūlakānuddhaṃsanādīhi chiddo ca sappaṭimaṃso ca hoti viparīto acchiddo appaṭimaṃso
nti palibodhe chinditvā kammaṭṭhānabhāvanānuyogena adhigataṃ mettacittaṃ nti āghātavirahitaṃ vikkhambhanavasena vihatāghātanti attho ti idaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ katarasmiṃ nagare vuttanti attho


tiādīsu eko ekaṃ okāsaṃ kāretvā codento  nāma saṅghamajjhagaṇamajjhasalākaggayāguaggavitakkamāḷakabhikkhācāramaggaāsanasālādīsu upaṭṭhākehi parivāritakkhaṇe vā codento  nāma tacchena vadanto  nāma ambho mahallaka parisāvacara paṃsukūlika dhammakathika patirūpaṃ tava idanti vadanto  nāma kāraṇanissitaṃ pana katvā bhante mahallakattha parisāvacarā paṃsukūlikā dhammakathikattha patirūpaṃ tumhākaṃ idanti vadanto  nāma kāraṇanissitaṃ katvā vadanto  nāma ti mettacittaṃ upaṭṭhapetvā vakkhāmi na duṭṭhacitto hutvā


ti attano citte uppādetvā ti karuṇābhāvo iminā karuṇañca karuṇāpubbabhāgañca dasseti ti hitagavesanatā ti tena hitena saṃyojanatā dvīhipi mettañca mettāpubbabhāgañca dasseti ti āpattito vuṭṭhāpetvā suddhante patiṭṭhāpanā vatthuṃ codetvā sāretvā paṭiññaṃ āropetvā yathāpaṭiññāya kammakaraṇaṃ  nāma ti ye ete kāruññatātiādinā nayena vuttā ime pañca dhamme ajjhattaṃ manasi karitvā paro codetabboti
ti vacīsacce ca akuppanatāya ca cuditakena hi saccañca vattabbaṃ kopo ca na kātabbo neva attanā kujjhitabbo na paro ghaṭṭetabboti attho sesaṃ sabbattha uttānamevāti
