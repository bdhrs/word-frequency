

 pañcamassa paṭhamādīsu natthi vattabbaṃ chaṭṭhe  pañca aṅgāni etassāti pañcaṅgaṃ pañcaṅgameva pañcaṅgikaṃ tassa  mahatī daddarī vīṇāvisesopi ti ti visesanaṃ kataṃ  kumbhathunadaddarikādi  bherimudiṅgādi cammapariyonaddhaṃ hutvā vinibaddhaṃ   nāma caturassaambaṇaṃ paṇavādi ca gomukhīādīnampi ettheva saṅgaho daṭṭhabbo ti -saddena saṅkhādīnaṃ saṅgaho ti sammatāḷakaṃsatāḷasilāsalākatāḷādi tattha  nāma dantamayatāḷaṃ  lohamayaṃ silāmayaṃ ayopattena ca vādanatāḷaṃ ti suṭṭhu paṭiyattassa ti  nātidaḷhanātisithilasaṅkhāte majjhime mucchanāpamāṇe ti paṭu paṭṭho so cassa paṭubhāvo manoharoti āha ti nti rāgaṃ uppādetuṃ ti rocateva ti na tajjeti sotasukhabhāvato piyāyitabbova hoti
ti sāmikaṃ muñcitvā aññaṃ manasāpi na pattheti ti uṭṭhānavīriyasampannā ti nikkosajjā ti sammānanādīhi ceva chaṇādīsu pesetabba-piyabhaṇḍādipaṇṇākārapesanādīhi ca saṅgahitaparijanā idha  nāma sāmikassa ceva  attano ca ñātijano nti kasivaṇijjādīni katvā ābhatadhanaṃ sattamaṭṭhamāni uttānatthāni


 navame ti idhalokavijinanatthāya abhibhavatthāya yo hi diṭṭhadhammikaṃ anatthaṃ parivajjanavasena abhibhavati tato eva tadatthaṃ sampādeti so idhalokavijayāya paṭipanno nāma hoti paccatthikaniggaṇhanato sadatthasampādanato ca tenāha ti  ti yāgubhattapacanakālādīni anatikkamitvā tassa tassa sādhukaṃ karaṇena suṭṭhu saṃvihitakammanto ti paralokassa vijinanatthāya abhibhavatthāya yo hi samparāyikaṃ anatthaṃ parivajjanavasena abhibhavati tato eva tadatthaṃ sampādeti so paralokavijayāya paṭipanno nāma hoti sesaṃ sabbattha uttānameva
