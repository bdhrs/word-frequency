

 catutthassa paṭhame yujjhanaṃ yodho so ājīvo etassāti  yuddhamupajīvatīti vā etasmiṃ atthe ti niruttinayena padasiddhi veditabbā tenāha ti ti pubbabhāge vipassanāpaññāya sammasanavasena maggakkhaṇe abhisamayavasena attapaccakkhena ñāṇena passati


 dutiye ti taṃ kiṃ maññatha bhikkhave rūpaṃ niccaṃ vā aniccaṃ vātiādinā appaṭipucchitvā kevalaṃ dhammadesanāvaseneva vinītaparisā ti dukkhena vinītaparisā ti taṃ kiṃ maññatha bhikkhavetiādinā pucchitvā anumatiggahaṇavasena vinītā ti sukhena vinītaparisā anumatiggahaṇavasena vinayanañhi na dukkaraṃ hoti


 catutthe nti tathāgatānaṃ uppādepi veneyyapuggalānaṃ maggaphaluppatti viya saṅkhārānaṃ aniccādisabhāvo na tathāgatuppādāyatto atha kho tathāgatānaṃ uppādepi anuppādepi hotimevāti vuttaṃ hoti ti ṭhito eva so aniccasabhāvo byabhicārābhāvato na kadāci saṅkhārā aniccā na hoti kāmaṃ asaṅkhatā viya dhātu na nicco so sabhāvo tathāpi sabbakālikoyevāti adhippāyo
aparo nayo  ti sabbe saṅkhārā aniccāti esā dhātu esa sabhāvo tathāgatānaṃ uppādato pubbe uddhañca appaṭivijjhiyamāno na tathāgatehi uppādito atha kho sabbakālaṃ sabbe saṅkhārā aniccā ṭhitāva sā dhātu kevalaṃ pana sayambhuñāṇena abhisambujjhanato ayaṃ dhammo tathāgatena abhisambuddhoti pavedanato ca tathāgato dhammassāmīti vuccati apubbassa tassa uppādanato tena vuttaṃ ti
ti ñāṇena abhisambujjhati ti ñāṇena abhisamāgacchati ti katheti ti dasseti ti jānāpeti ti ñāṇamukhe ṭhapeti ti vivaritvā dasseti ti vibhāgato dasseti ti pākaṭaṃ karoti
atha vā ti paccakkhakaraṇena abhimukhaṃ bujjhati yāthāvato paṭivijjhati tato eva  abhimukhaṃ samāgacchati ādito kathento  uddisatīti attho tameva uddesaṃ pariyosāpento  yathāuddiṭṭhamatthaṃ nidassanavasena pakārehi ñāpento  pakārehi etamatthaṃ patiṭṭhapento  yathāuddiṭṭhaṃ paṭinidassanavasena  vivaṭaṃ vibhattañca atthaṃ hetūdāharaṇadassanehi pākaṭaṃ karonto 


 pañcame nti idaṃ yāni kānicīti iminā samānādhikaraṇanti āha nti nti vītānaṃ ti nihīno ti ettha ti tassa nāmaṃ taṃ kira sakaddamāya bhūmiyā telaghaṭaṃ gahetvā gacchantaṃ tāta mā khalīti sāmiko āha so pamādena khalitvā patitvā sāmikassa bhayena palāyituṃ āraddho sāmiko upadhāvitvā naṃ dussakaṇṇe aggahesi sāṭakaṃ chaḍḍetvā acelako hutvā palāyi so paṇṇena vā tiṇena vā paṭicchādetumpi ajānanto jātarūpeneva ekaṃ gāmaṃ pāvisi manussā taṃ disvā ayaṃ samaṇo arahā appiccho natthi iminā sadisoti pūvabhattādīni gahetvā upasaṅkamanti so mayhaṃ sāṭakaṃ anivatthabhāvena idaṃ uppannanti tato paṭṭhāya sāṭakaṃ labhitvāpi na nivāsesi tadeva ca pabbajjaṃ aggahesi tassa santike aññepi aññepīti pañcasatā manussā pabbajiṃsu taṃ sandhāyāha makkhali moghapurisoti chaṭṭhādīni uttānatthāni eva


 ekādasame akuppadhammatāya khayavayasaṅkhātaṃ antaṃ atītāti accantā evaṃ aparihāyanasabhāvattā accantā niṭṭhā assāti  tenāha tiādi na hi paṭividdhassa lokuttaradhammassa dassanaṃ kuppanaṃ nāma atthi ti satataniṭṭho accantameva catūhi yogehi khemo etassa atthīti  niccayogakkhemīti attho maggabrahmacariyassa vusitattā tassa avihāyanasabhāvattā accantaṃ brahmacārīti  niccabrahmacārīti attho nti brahmacariyapariyosānaṃ vaṭṭadukkhapariyosānañca  accantaṃ pariyosānamassāti  dvādasamaterasamāni uttānatthāneva
