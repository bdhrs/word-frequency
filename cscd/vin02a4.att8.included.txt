

 dutiyagāthāsaṅgaṇiyaṃ ti vatthuñca āpattiñca dassetvā codanā ti dosasāraṇā ti saṅghasannipāto kimatthāya ti matikammaṃ vuccati mantaggahaṇaṃ taṃ kissa kāraṇāti attho
ti vuttappakārā codanā tena cuditakapuggalena codakadosasāraṇatthāya ti dosasāraṇā pana tassa puggalassa niggahatthāya ti tattha sannipatito saṅgho vinicchayapariggahaṇatthāya dhammādhammaṃ tulanatthāya suvinicchitadubbinicchitaṃ jānanatthāyāti attho nti suttantikattherānañca vinayadharattherānañca mantaggahaṇaṃ  pāṭekkaṃ pāṭekkaṃ vinicchayasanniṭṭhāpanatthaṃ
nti cuditake vā codake vā kopaṃ mā janayi nti sace tvaṃ saṅghamajjhe otiṇṇaṃ adhikaraṇaṃ vinicchituṃ nisinno vinayadharo
nti na tvaṃ imaṃ dhammavinayaṃ ājānāsītiādinayappavattaṃ nti yā anatthaṃ janayati parisaṃ khobhetvā uṭṭhāpeti evarūpiṃ kathaṃ mā abhaṇi tiādīsu  nāma ubhatovibhaṅgo  nāma khandhako  nāma parivāro  nāma sakalaṃ vinayapiṭakaṃ  nāma cattāro mahāpadesā
ti anuyuñjanavattaṃ nisāmehi nti chekena paṇḍitena ñāṇapāramippattena bhagavatā nīharitvā ṭhapitaṃ nti supaññāpitaṃ nti sikkhāpadānaṃ anulomaṃ ayaṃ tāva padattho ayaṃ panettha sādhippāyasaṅkhepavaṇṇanā  sace tvaṃ anuvijjako mā sahasā bhaṇi mā anatthasaṃhitaṃ viggāhikakathaṃ  bhaṇi yaṃ pana kusalena buddhimatā lokanāthena etesu suttādīsu anuyogavattaṃ kathaṃ supaññattaṃ sabbasikkhāpadānaṃ anulomaṃ taṃ nisāmaya taṃ upadhārehīti nti attano samparāye sugatinibbattiṃ anāsento anuyogavattaṃ nisāmaya yo hi taṃ anisāmetvā anuyuñjati so samparāyikaṃ attano gatiṃ nāseti tasmā tvaṃ anāsento nisāmayāti attho idāni taṃ anuyogavattaṃ dassetuṃ tiādimāha tattha ti hitaṃ esanto gavesanto mettañca mettāpubbabhāgañca upaṭṭhapetvāti attho ti yuttapattakālena ajjhesitakāleyeva tava bhāre kate anuyuñjāti attho
ti yo etesaṃ sahasā vohāro hoti sahasā bhāsitaṃ taṃ mā padhāresi mā gaṇhittha
ti ettha nti kathānusandhi vuccati tasmā paṭiññānusandhinā kāraye kathānusandhiṃ sallakkhetvā paṭiññāya kārayeti attho atha vā paṭiññāya ca anusandhitena ca kāraye lajjiṃ paṭiññāya kāraye alajjiṃ vattānusandhināti attho tasmā eva ti gāthamāha tattha ti vattānusandhinā kāraye yā assa vattena saddhiṃ paṭiññā sandhiyati tāya paṭiññāya kārayeti attho
ti jānanto āpajjati ti nigūhati na deseti na vuṭṭhāti
ti yaṃ tumhehi vuttaṃ taṃ saccaṃ ahampi naṃ evameva jānāmi nti aññañca taṃ ahaṃ pucchāmi
ti purekathitañca pacchākathitañca na jānāti ti tasmiṃ pubbāpare akusalo ti kathānusandhivacanaṃ vinicchayānusandhivacanañca na jānāti
