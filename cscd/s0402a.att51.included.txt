

 catutthassa  paṭhame saddhādhurena indaṭṭhaṃ karotīti  sesesupi eseva nayo dutiye assaddhiye akampanaṭṭhena  sesesupi eseva nayo tatiye nti niddosabalaṃ nti saṅgaṇhitabbayuttakānaṃ saṅgaṇhanabalaṃ catutthapañcamāni uttānāneva

 chaṭṭhe ti ettha  āposaṃvaṭṭo tejosaṃvaṭṭo vāyosaṃvaṭṭoti  ābhassarā subhakiṇhā vehapphalāti yadā kappo tejena saṃvaṭṭati ābhassarato heṭṭhā agginā ḍayhati yadā āpena saṃvaṭṭati subhakiṇhato heṭṭhā udakena vilīyati yadā vātena  saṃvaṭṭati vehapphalato heṭṭhā vātena viddhaṃsati vitthārato pana sadāpi ekaṃ buddhakkhettaṃ vinassati ayamettha saṅkhepo vitthārakathā pana visuddhimagge  vuttanayeneva veditabbā

 sattame ti mahicchāpaccayena vighātena dukkhena samannāgato ti catūsu paccayesu tīhi santosehi asantuṭṭho ti parehi anavajānanassa paṭilābhatthāya ti susaṅkhatacatupaccayasaṅkhātassa lābhasakkārassa  ceva vaṇṇabhaṇanasaṅkhātassa silokassa ca paṭilābhatthāya ti iti maṃ ete jānissantīti jānanatthāya kulāni upasaṅkamati sesapadesupi eseva nayo

 aṭṭhame ti atthagambhīresu ti kāraṇākāraṇesu ti nāvagāhati nappavattati ti ettha uggahaparipucchāpaññāpi vaṭṭati sammasanappaṭivedhapaññāpi vaṭṭatiyeva

 navame nti there paṭibaddhacittā taṃ pahiṇituṃ evamāha ti saha sīsena kāyaṃ pārupitvā ti vegena mañcakaṃ paññāpetvā tattha nipajji ti  tassākāraṃ sallakkhetvā lobhappahānatthāya saṇheneva asubhakathaṃ kathetuṃ etaṃ avoca ti āhārena sambhūto āhāraṃ nissāya vaḍḍhito ti paccuppannaṃ kabaḷīkārāhāraṃ nissāya taṃ evaṃ yoniso sevamāno pubbakammasaṅkhātaṃ āhāraṃ pajahati paccuppannepi pana kabaḷīkārāhāre nikantitaṇhā pajahitabbā
ti idāni evaṃ pavattaṃ paccuppannataṇhaṃ nissāya vaṭṭamūlikaṃ pubbataṇhaṃ pajahati ayaṃ pana paccuppannataṇhā kusalā akusalāti akusalā sevitabbā na sevitabbāti sevitabbā paṭisandhiṃ ākaḍḍhati nākaḍḍhatīti nākaḍḍhati etissāpi pana paccuppannāya sevitabbataṇhāya nikanti pajahitabbāyeva nti ettha ti kāraṇaparivitakkanametaṃ idaṃ vuttaṃ hoti  so āyasmā arahattaphalaṃ sacchikatvā viharissati ahaṃ kena kāraṇena na sacchikatvā viharissāmi sopi hi āyasmā sammāsambuddhasseva putto ahampi sammāsambuddhasseva putto mayhampetaṃ uppajjissatīti ti idaṃ evaṃ uppannasevitabbamānaṃ  nissāya ti vaṭṭamūlakaṃ pubbamānaṃ pajahati yaṃ nissāya panesa taṃ pajahati sopi taṇhā viya akusalo ceva sevitabbo ca no ca paṭisandhiṃ ākaḍḍhati nikanti pana tasmimpi pajahitabbāva
ti padaghāto paccayaghāto buddhena bhagavatā kathito iti imehi catūhi aṅgehi there desanaṃ vinivaṭṭente tassā bhikkhuniyā theraṃ ārabbha uppanno chandarāgo apagañchi sāpi theraṃ khamāpetuṃ accayaṃ desesi theropissā paṭiggaṇhi taṃ dassetuṃ tiādi vuttaṃ

 dasame nti uppaṭipāṭiyā gahitaṃ ti vaḷañjenti kathenti ti ettha padameva atthassa byañjanato byañjananti vuttaṃ ti duṭṭhu nikkhittassa uppaṭipāṭiyā ṭhapitassa ti aṭṭhakathā nīharitvā kathetuṃ na sakkā hoti ti mūlabhūtānaṃ bhikkhūnaṃ upacchinnattā chinnamūlako ti appatiṭṭho ti paccayabāhullāya paṭipannā ti tisso sikkhā sithilaggahaṇena gaṇhanakā ti pañca nīvaraṇāni avagamanato okkamananti vuccanti tattha pubbaṅgamāti attho ti tividhe viveke ti nibbīriyā iminā nayena pana sabbattha attho veditabbo
