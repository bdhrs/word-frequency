


ratanattayappaṇāma

 munindavadanambhoja gabbhasambhavasundarī
saraṇaṃ pāṇinaṃ vāṇī mayhaṃ pīṇayataṃ manaṃ
nimitta

dyalaṅkārā santi santo purātanā
tathāpi tu vaḷañjenti suddhamāgadhikā na te
abhidhānādikaṃ

 tenāpi nāma toseyya mete laṅkāravajjite
anurūpenālaṅkāre nesa meso parissamo

 yesaṃ na sañcitā paññā nekasatthantarocitā
sammohabbhāhatā vete nāvabujjhanti kiñcipi

 kiṃ tehi pādasussūsā yesaṃ natthi garūniha
ye tappādarajokiṇṇā teva sādhū vivekino

nikkhitta nettacittā kavijjanā
yaṃkiñci racayantetaṃ na vimhayakaraṃ paraṃ

 teyeva paṭibhāvento sova bandho savimhayo
yena tosenti viññū ye tattha pyavihitādarā

 ca nāma saddatthā sahitā dosavajjitā
 bhedenāyaṃ tidhā bhave

 cā ca puna dvidhā niruppate
taṃ tu pāpentyalaṅkārā vindanīyatarattanaṃ

 anavajjaṃ mukhambhoja  manavajjā ca bhāratī
alaṅkatāva sobhante kiṃ nu te niralaṅkatā

 vinā garūpadesaṃ taṃ bālolaṅkattu micchati
sampāpuṇe na viññūhi hassabhāvaṃ kathaṃ nu so

 ganthopi kavivācāna malaṅkārappakāsako
yāti tabbacanīyattaṃ tabbohārūpacārato

 dvippakārā alaṅkārā tattha bhedato
saddatthā nāmāva taṃsajjita tadāvali

 guṇālaṅkārasaṃyuttā api dosalavaṅkitā
pasaṃsiyā na viññūhi sā kaññā viya tādisī

 tena dosanirāsova mahussāhena sādhiyo
niddosā sabbathā sāyaṃ saguṇā na bhaveyya kiṃ

 sālaṅkāraviyuttāpi guṇayuttā manoharā
niddosā dosarahitā guṇayuttā vadhū viya

 ca dosā ye vividhā matā
sodāharaṇa metesaṃ lakkhaṇaṃ kathayāmyahaṃ
padadosa uddesa

 viruddhatthantarā jhattha kiliṭṭhāni virodhi ca
neyyaṃ visesanāpekkhaṃ hīnatthaka manatthakaṃ
vākyadosa uddesa

 dosā padāna vākyāna mekatthaṃ bhaggarītikaṃ
tathā byākiṇṇa gāmmāni yatihīnaṃ kamaccutaṃ
ativutta mapetatthaṃ sabandhapharusaṃ tathā
vākyatthadosauddesa

 apakkamo  cityahīnaṃ bhaggarīti sasaṃsayaṃ
gāmmaṃ duṭṭhālaṅkatīti dosā vākyatthanissitā
padadosaniddesa

 tañhi yassaññattho virujjhati
adhippete yathā megho  sukhaye janaṃ

 visesya madhikaṃ yenā  metaṃ bhave yathā
 khajjotoyaṃ virājate

 yassatthāvagamo dukkho pakatyādivibhāgato
kiliṭṭhaṃ taṃ yathā tāya soya māliṅgyate 

 yaṃ kiliṭṭhapadaṃ mandā bhidheyyaṃ dikaṃ
kiliṭṭhapadadoseva tampi anto karīyati

 patītasaddaracitaṃ siliṭṭhapadasandhikaṃ
pasādaguṇasaṃyuttaṃ  mata medisaṃ

 māvuttānekavaṇṇajaṃ
yamakaṃ tañca pādāna mādi majjha nta gocaraṃ


 sujanāsujanā sabbe guṇenāpi vivekino
vivekaṃ na samāyanti avivekijanantike


 kusalākusalā sabbe pabalāpabalā thavā
no yātā yāvahosittaṃ sukhadukkhappadā siyuṃ
abyapeta paṭhama dutiya tatiyapādādi yamakaṃ

 sādaraṃ sā daraṃ hantu vihitā vihitā mayā
vandanā vandanāmāna bhājane ratanattaye


 kamalaṃ kamalaṃ kattuṃ vanado vanadombaraṃ
sugato sugato lokaṃ sahitaṃ sa hitaṃ karaṃ

 abyapetādiyamaka  sseso leso nidassito
ñeyyānimāyeva disā yaññāni yamakānipi

 accantabahavo tesaṃ bhedā sambhedayoniyo
tathāpi keci sukarā keci accantadukkarā

 taṃ  ca nekantamadhurāniti
upekkhiyanti sabbāni sissakhedabhayā mayā

 desakālakalāloka ñāyāgamavirodhi yaṃ
taṃ  ceta mudāharaṇato phuṭaṃ

 ya dappatīta mānīya vattabbaṃ  māhu taṃ
yathā sabbāpi  disā rocanti rattiyaṃ

 nedisaṃ bahu maññanti sabbe sabbattha viññuno
dullabhāvagatī sadda sāmatthiyavilaṅghinī

 siyā  yaṃ taṃ patvā visesanaṃ
sātthakaṃ taṃ yathā taṃ so bhiyyo passati 

 hīnaṃ kare visesyaṃ yaṃ taṃ  bhave yathā
 samudeti divākaro

 pādapūraṇamattaṃ yaṃ miti taṃ mataṃ
yathā  vande buddhassa pādapaṅkeruhaṃ 
vākyadosa niddesa

 saddato atthato vuttaṃ yattha bhiyyopi vuccati
ta  yathā


 titthiyaṅkurabījāni jahaṃ diṭṭhigatāniha
pasādeti pasanneso mahāmuni mahājane

 āraddhakkamavicchedā  bhave yathā


 padānaṃ  dubbinikkhepā byāmoho yattha jāyati
taṃ nti viññeyyaṃ tadudāharaṇaṃ yathā




 visiṭṭhavacanāpetaṃ tyabhimataṃ yathā
kaññe kāmayamānaṃ maṃ na kāmayasi kiṃnvidaṃ

 padasandhānato kiñci duppatītikaraṃ bhave
tampi  tyabhimataṃ yathā 

 vuttesu sūcite ṭṭhāne padacchedo bhave yati
yaṃ tāya hīnaṃ taṃ vuttaṃ nti sā pana

 yati  ca visesato
pubbāparānekavaṇṇa pi katthaci


 taṃ name sirasā  tathāgataṃ
sakalāpi disā  soṇṇarasehi yo

 saro sandhimhi pubbanto viya lope vibhattiyā
aññathā tvaññathā tattha yādesādi parādiva

dī pubbapadantāva niccaṃ pubbapadassitā
dayo niccasambandhā parādīva parena tu


 name taṃ sirasā  tathāgataṃ
yassa lokaggataṃ  na hi yujjati

 munindaṃ taṃ sadā  muttamaṃ
yassa ti vijambhati


 mahāmettā  yattha paramodayā
paṇamāmi jinaṃ taṃ  varaguṇālayaṃ

 padatthakkamato muttaṃ  midaṃ yathā


 lokiyattha  matikkantaṃ  mataṃ yathā


 samudāyatthatopetaṃ taṃ  yathā


 bandhe pharusatā yattha taṃ  yathā

vākyatthadosa niddesa

 ñeyyaṃ lakkhaṇa manvattha vasenā
udāharaṇa metesaṃ dāni sandassayāmyahaṃ


 sammā sampāditāniha
 sādhanāni na saṃsayo










ñca vissāso nopapajjate
 ca


 munindacandimā loka saralolavilocano
jano vakkantapanthova padassanapīṇito

 vākyatthato duppatīti karaṃ  mataṃ yathā
poso  soyaṃ  na vissamī

 tetaṃ  yatthālaṅkāradūsanaṃ
tassālaṅkāraniddese rūpa māvi bhavissati

 katotra  saṅkhepanayā mayāyaṃ
dosāna mesaṃ pavaro vibhāgo
esovalaṃ bodhayituṃ kavīnaṃ
tamatthi ce khedakaraṃ parampi
