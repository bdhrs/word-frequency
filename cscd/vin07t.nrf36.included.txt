

 bhikkhunikkhandhake ti idaṃ pañcavassasatato uddhaṃ saddhammassa appavattanakāraṇadassanaṃ ntiādi  mataṃ gahetvā vuttaṃ  pana paṭisambhidāppattehi vassasahassaṃ aṭṭhāsi chaḷabhiññehi vassasahassaṃ tevijjehi vassasahassaṃ sukkhavipassakehi vassasahassaṃ pātimokkhehi vassasahassaṃ aṭṭhāsīti  vuttaṃ   pi  aññathāva vuttaṃ taṃ sabbaṃ aññamaññaviruddhampi taṃtaṃbhāṇakānaṃ matena likhitasīhaḷaṭṭhakathāsu āgatanayameva gahetvā ācariyena likhitaṃ īdise kathāvirodhe sāsanaparihāniyā abhāvato sodhanupāyābhāvā ca paramatthavirodho eva hi suttādinayena sodhanīyo na kathāmaggavirodhoti


 pāḷiyaṃ ti yasmiṃ divase ti tasmiṃyeva divase ti avamānaṃ katvā
ti āpattiṃ āpannāyo ti parivāre kammavibhaṅge 
 pāḷiyaṃ ti dvīhi tīhi bhikkhunīhi ti pātimokkhuddesakassa na ārocenti
ti anekadussapaṭṭe ekato katvā kataveṇiyā
nti  pattalekhādivaṇṇavisesaṃ ti vikkiṇanti nti pāsukaṭṭhinamanakabandhanaṃ
nti kacchaṃ bandhitvā nivāsanaṃ ti rukkhamūlasenāsanassa tāsaṃ alabbhanato vuttaṃ
ti aṭṭha bhikkhuniyo vuḍḍhapaṭipāṭiyāva gaṇhantiyo āgatapaṭipāṭiṃ paṭibāhanti nāññāti attho ti issariyaṃ pavattentīti atthaṃ vadanti
ti bhikkhunā dūtabhūtena ti sikkhamānāya dūtāya
ti nappahoti nti navakammaṃ katvā vasatūti apaloketvā saṅghikabhūmiyā okāsadānaṃ
ti duviññeyyagabbhā ti itthīnaṃ sādhāraṇatitthe
