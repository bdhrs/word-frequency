19. Kappiyabhūmivinicchayakathā
101 .   ‘‘kappiyā catubhūmiyo’’ kappiyā, catubhūmiyo, ‘‘ussāvanantikā…pe… veditabbā’’ ‘‘anujānāmi…pe… vacanato’’ ussāvanantikā gonisādikā, gahapati, sammutī Tatthā Taṃ pana avatvāpī Pi Aṭṭhakathāsu vuttanayena vutte Sādhāraṇalakkhaṇa Caya Yato paṭṭhāyā Paṭhamiṭṭhakādīnaṃ heṭṭhā na vaṭṭantī ‘‘thambhā pana…pe… vaṭṭantī’’
Saṅghasantakamevā  Bhikkhusantaka
102 . Mukhasannidhī
taṃ pana avatvāpī pi Aṭṭhakathāsū Sādhāraṇalakkhaṇa Caya  Yato paṭṭhāyā ‘‘Thambhā pana upari uggacchanti, tasmā vaṭṭantī’’
Ārāmo Senāsanānī Vihāragonisādikā nāmā ‘‘Upaḍḍhaparikkhittopī’’ Etthā Kappiyakuṭi laddhuṃ vaṭṭatī
Tesaṃ gehānī Vihāraṃ  ṭhapetvā Geha ‘‘yaṃ panā’’ ‘‘saṅghikaṃ vā puggalikaṃ vā’’
Mukhasannidhī
Tattha tattha khaṇḍā hontī Sabbasmiṃ chadane vinaṭṭhe  Pakkhapāsakamaṇḍala
103 . ‘‘Anupasampannassa datvā tassā’’  antovuttha Antopakkepi Sāmaṃpakka
Iti vinayasaṅgahasaṃvaṇṇanābhūte vinayālaṅkāre
Kappiyabhūmivinicchayakathālaṅkāro nāma
Ekūnavīsatimo paricchedo.
