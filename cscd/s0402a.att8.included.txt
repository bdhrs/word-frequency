
 catutthassa  paṭhame ti phalasamādhi ti phalapaññā
 dutiye ti vīriyaṃ ti cittekaggatā
 tatiye nti cattāro arūpakkhandhā nti rūpakkhandho iti imasmiṃ sutte dhammakoṭṭhāsaparicchedañāṇaṃ nāma kathitaṃ
 catutthe ti phalañāṇaṃ ti taṃsampayuttā sesadhammā
 pañcame ti sassatadiṭṭhi ti ucchedadiṭṭhi chaṭṭhasattamāni uttānatthāneva
 aṭṭhame ti dubbacabhāvo ti pāpamittasevanabhāvo navamaṃ  vuttavipariyāyena veditabbaṃ
 dasame ti aṭṭhārasa dhātuyo dhātūti jānanaṃ ti tāsaṃyeva dhātūnaṃ aniccādivasena lakkhaṇattayaṃ āropetvā jānanaṃ
 ekādasame ti pañcannañca sattannañca āpattikkhandhānaṃ jānanaṃ ti desanāya vā kammavācāya vā āpattīhi vuṭṭhānajānananti
