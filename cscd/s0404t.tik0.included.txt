





 aṭṭhakanipātassa  paṭhame ti bhāvanāpāripūrivasena paribrūhitāya ti bhāvanāya bahulīkaraṇena aparāparaṃ pavattitāya ti yathā yuttaājaññayānaṃ chekena sārathinā adhiṭṭhitaṃ yathāruci pavattati evaṃ yathāruci pavattārahataṃ gamitāya ti sabbasampattiadhiṭṭhānaṭṭhena ti bhāvanābahulīkārehi pati pati upaṭṭhitāya avijahitāya ti sabbabhāgena bhāvanānurūpaṃ cayaṃ gamitāya tenāha ti ti ativiya sammadeva nibbattigatāya
tiādīsu yo koci gahaṭṭho vā pabbajito vā nti mettājhānaṃ
nti bhāvanāvasena ārammaṇavasena ca appamāṇaṃ asubhabhāvanādayo viya hi ārammaṇe ekadesaggahaṇaṃ akatvā anavasesapharaṇavasena anodhiso pharaṇavasena ca appamāṇārammaṇatāya paguṇabhāvanāvasena ca appamāṇaṃ ti mettaṃ pādakaṃ katvā sammasitvā heṭṭhime ariyamagge adhigacchantassa sukheneva paṭighasaṃyojanādayo pahīyamānā tanū hontīti evamettha attho daṭṭhabbo
evaṃ kilesappahānañca nibbānādhigamañca mettābhāvanāya sikhāppattamānisaṃsaṃ dassetvā idāni aññepi ānisaṃse dassetuṃ tiādi vuttaṃ tattha ti mettābalena suṭṭhu vikkhambhitabyāpādatāya byāpādena adūsitacitto ti hitapharaṇavasena mettaṃ  karoti ti atisayena kusalavā mahāpuñño paṭighādianatthavigamena khemī ti saddo byatireko ti cittena anukampanto idaṃ vuttaṃ hoti  ekasattavisayāpi tāva mettā mahākusalarāsi sabbe pana pāṇe attano puttaṃ viya hitapharaṇena manasā anukampanto pahukaṃ pahuṃ anappakaṃ apariyantaṃ catusaṭṭhimahākappepi attano vipākappabandhaṃ pavattetuṃ samatthaṃ uḷāraṃ puññaṃ ariyo parisuddhacitto puggalova karoti nipphādetīti nti sattehi aviraḷaṃ ākiṇṇamanussanti attho
ti  lokassa saṅgaṇhanakāraṇāni nipphannasassato nava bhāge kassakassa datvā raññaṃ ekabhāgaggahaṇaṃ  evaṃ kassakā haṭṭhatuṭṭhā sassāni sampādentīti āha ti tato orabhāge kira chabhāgaggahaṇaṃ jātaṃ nti channaṃ channaṃ māsānaṃ pahonakaṃ ti pāsagate viya karoti vācāya piyaṃ vācāpiyaṃ tassa kammaṃ  sabbaso raṭṭhassa iddhādibhāvato  coriyābhāvato iddhañhi raṭṭhaṃ acoriyaṃ  apārutagharabhāvato
ti ummūlaṃ katvā ti mahāyaññassa pubbabhāge pacchā ca pavattetabbehi dvīhi parivārayaññehi ti sattanavutādhikānaṃ pañcannaṃ pasusatānaṃ māraṇena bheravassa pāpabhīrukānaṃ bhayāvahassa tathā hi vadanti 
chasatāni  niyujjanti pasūnaṃ majjhime hani
assamedhassa yaññassa ūnāni pasūhi tīhīti 
nti yugacchidde pakkhipitabbadaṇḍakaṃ ti khipanti ti sakaṭehi vahitabbehi pubbe kira eko rājā sammāpāsaṃ yajanto sarassatinaditīre pathaviyā vivare dinne nimuggoyeva ahosi andhabālabrāhmaṇā gatānugatigatā ayaṃ tassa saggagamanamaggoti saññāya tattha sammāpāsaṃ yaññaṃ paṭṭhapenti tena vuttaṃ ti  ayūpo appakadivaso yāgo sayūpo bahudivasaṃ sādheyyo  mantapadābhisaṅkhatānaṃ sappimadhūnaṃ miti samaññā hiraññasuvaṇṇagomahiṃsādi  sāragabbhakoṭṭhāgārādīsu natthi ettha aggaḷāti  tattha kira yaññe attano sāpateyyaṃ anavasesato anigūhitvā niyyātīyati
ti  candimasseva pabhāya ti yathā sabbepi tārāgaṇā candimasobhāya soḷasimpi kalaṃ nāgghanti evaṃ te assamedhādayo yaññā mettassa cittassa vuttalakkhaṇena subhāvitassa soḷasimpi kalaṃ nānubhavanti na pāpuṇanti nāgghantīti attho
idāni aparepi diṭṭhadhammikasamparāyike mettābhāvanāya ānisaṃse dassetuṃ tiādi vuttaṃ tattha ti mettābrahmavihārabhāvanānuyutto puggalo ti teneva mettābhāvanānubhāvena dūravikkhambhitabyāpādatāya na kañci sattaṃ hiṃsati leḍḍudaṇḍādīhi na vibādhati vā ti paraṃ samādapetvā na satte mārāpeti na vibādhāpeti ca ti sārambhaviggāhikakathādivasena na kañci jināti sārambhasseva abhāvato jānikaraṇavasena vā aṭṭakaraṇādinā na kañci jināti tenāha ti ti parehi payojetvā paresampi dhanajāniṃ na kārāpeyya tenāha ti mettāya vā aṃso aviheṭhanaṭṭhena avayavabhūtoti 


 dutiye ti ādibrahmacariyameva ādibrahmacariyikā tenāha ti ti niddoso parisuddho tuṇhībhāvo na titthiyānaṃ mūgabbatagahaṇaṃ viya aparisuddhoti nti ukkaṭṭhaniddesenetaṃ vuttaṃ paṭhamajjhānādīnipi ariyo tuṇhībhāvotveva saṅkhaṃ  gacchanti nti idaṃ kammasādhananti āha ti yathā vā ekacco viparītaṃ gaṇhanto jānantopi na jānāti passantopi na passati na evamayaṃ ayaṃ pana jānanto jānāti passanto passatīti evamettha daṭṭhabbo tatiyādīni suviññeyyāni


 pañcame ti sattalokassa avassaṃbhāvidhammā tenāha tiādi ghāsacchādanādīnaṃ laddhi  tāni eva vā laddhabbato  tadabhāvo  lābhaggahaṇena cettha tabbisayo anurodho gahito alābhaggahaṇena virodho yasmā lohite sati tadupaghātavasena pubbo viya anurodho laddhāvasaro eva hoti tasmā vuttaṃ ti esa nayo yasādīsupi sesaṃ suviññeyyameva


 chaṭṭhe adhikaṃ payasati payujjati etenāti  savisesaṃ itikattabbakiriyā tenāha ti sattamaṭṭhamesu natthi vattabbaṃ


 navame  duvidhā kulaputtā jātikulaputtā ācārakulaputtā ca tattha tena kho pana samayena raṭṭhapālo kulaputto tasmiṃyeva thullakoṭṭhike  aggakulikassa puttoti  evaṃ āgatā uccākulaputtā  saddhāyete kulaputtā agārasmā anagāriyaṃ pabbajitāti  evaṃ āgatā pana yattha katthaci kule pasutāpi  nāma idha pana uccākulappasutataṃ sandhāya kulaputtoti bhikkhave nandaṃ sammā vadamāno vadeyyāti bhagavatā vuttanti āha ti ubhohipi pana kāraṇehi tassa kulaputtabhāvoyeva sesamettha uttānameva


 dasame ti paṭicchādanavasena carati pavattati paṭicchādanaṭṭho eva vā caratisaddo anekatthattā dhātūnanti āha ti nti pana paṭicchādanākāradassananti āha tiādi tattha ti yaṃ codakena cuditakassa dosavibhāvanaṃ kāraṇaṃ vacanaṃ vā vuttaṃ taṃ tato aññeneva kāraṇena vacanena vā paṭicchādeti ti codanāya amūlāya amūlikabhāvadīpaniyā yuttiyā vā ti tadatthabodhakena vacanena ko āpannotiādinā codanaṃ vissajjetvāva vikkhepāpajjanaṃ  nāma itthannāmaṃ āpattiṃ āpannosīti vutte  pāṭaliputtaṃ gatomhītiādinā codanaṃ vissajjetvāti ayameva viseso yo hi āpattiṃ āpannosīti vutto ko āpanno kiṃ āpanno kismiṃ āpannā kaṃ bhaṇatha kiṃ bhaṇathāti vā vadati evarūpaṃ kiñci tayā diṭṭhanti vutte na suṇāmīti sotaṃ vā upaneti ayaṃ  nāma yo pana itthannāmaṃ nāma āpattiṃ āpannosīti puṭṭho pāṭaliputtaṃ gatomhīti vatvā puna na tava pāṭaliputtagamanaṃ pucchāma āpattiṃ pucchāmāti vutte tato rājagahaṃ gatomhi rājagahaṃ vā yāhi brāhmaṇagahaṃ vā āpattiṃ āpannosīti taṃ tattha me sūkaramaṃsaṃ laddhantiādīni vadati ayaṃ  nāma  ti samaṇavesadhāraṇena samaṇappatirūpakatāya samaṇānaṃ kacavarabhūtaṃ
  ti vipannasīlatāya kacavarabhūtaṃ puggalaṃ kacavaramiva anapekkhā apanetha ti kasambubhūtañca naṃ khattiyādīnaṃ majjhagataṃ pabhinnapaggharitakuṭṭhaṃ caṇḍālaṃ viya apakaḍḍhatha kiṃ kāraṇaṃ saṅghārāmo nāma sīlavantānaṃ kato na dussīlānaṃ yato etadeva sandhāyāha ti yathā palāpā antosārarahitā ataṇḍulā bahi thusena vīhī viya dissanti evaṃ pāpabhikkhū anto sīlarahitāpi bahi kāsāvādiparikkhārena bhikkhū viya dissanti tasmā palāpāti vuccanti te palāpe vāhetha opunatha vidhamatha paramatthato assamaṇe samaṇavesamattena samaṇamānine ti kappetha karothāti vuttaṃ hoti ti sappatissā vaṭṭadukkhassa antaṃ karissatha parinibbānaṃ pāpuṇissathāti attho
