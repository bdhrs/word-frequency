


 patiṭṭhānaṃ  idha saṃsāre avaṭṭhānaṃ tassa mūlaṃ kilesāti āha nti patiṭṭhānāya pana byāpārāpatti kammanti vuttaṃ ti yasmā kilesesu taṇhādiṭṭhiyo taṇhādiṭṭhicaritānaṃ visesato saṃsāranāyikā kilesasahitameva ca kammaṃ patiṭṭhānāya hoti na kevalaṃ tasmā vuttaṃ ti tathā taṇhāya bhavassādabhāvato diṭṭhiyā vibhavābhinandanabhūtāya vibhavābhisaṅkharaṇabhāvato ti vuttaṃ diṭṭhīsupi antomukhappavattāya bhavadiṭṭhiyā visesato saṃsāre avaṭṭhānaṃ yato olīyanāti vuccatīti āha nti bahimukhappavattāpi vibhavadiṭṭhi bhavābhisaṅkharaṇaṃ nātivattatīti vuttaṃ ti layāpatti yathāraddhassa ārambhassa aniṭṭhānaṃ antosaṅkocabhāvatoti āha nti uddhatāpatti anupāyabhūtā byāpārāpatti asaṅkocabhāvatoti vuttaṃ ti tathā kosajjapakkhikattā ca kāmasukhānuyogassa uddhaccapakkhikattā ca attakilamathānuyogassa tadubhayavasena patiṭṭhānāyūhanā vuttā itaraṃ vuttanayānusārena veditabbaṃ ti imissā  nti kilesavasena patiṭṭhānantiādīnaṃ vasena veditabbā patiṭṭhānāyūhanāti yojanā
