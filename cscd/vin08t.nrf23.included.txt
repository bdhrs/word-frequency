
 evaṃ upasampadāvinicchayaṃ kathetvā idāni nissayavinicchayaṃ kathetuṃ tiādimāha tattha nissayanaṃ  sevanaṃ bhajanantyattho nipubbasi sevāyanti dhātu bhāvasādhano na nissāya naṃ vasatīti nissayoti idha viya avuttakammasādhano tattha hi sevitabbo puggalo labbhati idha pana sevanakiriyāti idāni taṃ nissayaṃ pucchāpubbaṅgamāya  vissajjanāya vitthārato ṭhapetuṃ tiādimāha tattha ti nissayadāyakaṃ kattāraṃ pucchati ti nissayapaṭiggāhakaṃ sampadānaṃ ti kāraṇaṃ nti nissayapaṭipannakaṃ tato pucchānukkamena vissajjetuṃ tyādimāha na kevalaṃ ettheva atha kho nissayamuccanaṅgepi byattenāti āgato
tattha  ettha ca ko visesoti āha ti idāni parisupaṭṭhāpakalakkhaṇaṃ dassetuṃ tiādimāha tattha ti sakale vinayapiṭake nti sikkhāpetuṃ ti idaṃ paripucchāvasena uggaṇhanaṃ sandhāya vuttanti vadanti ekassa pamuṭṭhaṃ itaresaṃ paguṇaṃ bhaveyyāti āha ti ti nāmarūpaparicchede ti mahāvaggato heṭṭhā sagāthāvaggo nidānavaggo khandhavaggoti ime tayo vaggā dhammapadampi saha vatthunā uggahetuṃ vaṭṭatīti mahāpaccariyaṃ vuttattā jātakabhāṇakena sāṭṭhakathaṃ jātakaṃ uggahetvāpi dhammapadaṃ saha vatthunā uggahetabbameva
tiādīsu ti upajjhāyena hutvā sāmaṇero na upaṭṭhāpetabbo ti asekkhassa sīlakkhandhopi asekkho sīlakkhandho nāma asekkhassa ayanti hi asekkho sīlakkhandho evaṃ sabbattha evañca katvā vimuttiñāṇadassanasaṅkhātassa paccavekkhaṇañāṇassapi vasena apekkhitvā uppannā ayaṃ kathā nti ca na aggaphalasīlameva adhippetaṃ atha kho yaṃ kiñci asekkhasantāne pavattasīlaṃ lokiyalokuttaramissakassa sīlassa idhādhippetattā samādhikkhandhādīsupi vimuttikkhandhapariyosānesu ayameva nayo tasmā yathā sīlasamādhipaññākkhandhā lokiyamissakā kathitā evaṃ vimuttikkhandhopīti tadaṅgavimuttiādayopi veditabbā na paṭippassaddhivimutti eva vimuttiñāṇadassanaṃ pana lokiyameva teneva saṃyuttanikāyaṭṭhakathāyaṃ  vuttaṃ purimehi  catūhi padehi lokiyalokuttarasīlasamādhipaññāvimuttiyo  kathitā vimuttiñāṇadassanaṃ paccavekkhaṇañāṇaṃ hoti taṃ lokiyamevāti
tiādīsu tīsu vatthūsu saddhā etassa natthīti  natthi etassa hirīti  akusalasamāpattiyā ajigucchamānassetaṃ adhivacanaṃ na ottappatīti  akusalasamāpattiyā na bhāyatīti vuttaṃ hoti kucchitaṃ sīdatīti  hīnavīriyassetaṃ adhivacanaṃ āraddhaṃ vīriyaṃ etassāti  sammappadhānayuttassetaṃ adhivacanaṃ muṭṭhā sati etassāti  naṭṭhassatīti vuttaṃ hoti upaṭṭhitā sati etassāti  niccaṃ ārammaṇābhimukhapavattasatissetaṃ adhivacanaṃ
adhisīle sīlavipanno ca ajjhācāre ācāravipanno ca āpajjitvā avuṭṭhito adhippeto sassatucchedasaṅkhātaṃ antaṃ gaṇhāti gāhayatīti vā  micchādiṭṭhi ti na paṭibalo hoti antevāsiṃ vā saddhivihāriṃ vā gilānaṃ upaṭṭhātuṃ vā upaṭṭhāpetuṃ vā anabhirataṃ vūpakāsetuṃ vā vūpakāsāpetuṃ vāti imāni dve padāni
abhi visiṭṭho uttamo samācāro abhisamācāro abhisamācārova ābhisamācārikoti ca sikkhitabbato sikkhāti ca  abhisamācāraṃ vā ārabbha paññattā sikkhā  khandhakavattapariyāpannasikkhāyetaṃ adhivacanaṃ maggabrahmacariyassa ādibhūtāti  ubhatovibhaṅgapariyāpannasikkhāyetaṃ adhivacanaṃ teneva ubhatovibhaṅgapariyāpannaṃ  vā ādibrahmacariyakaṃ khandhakavattapariyāpannaṃ ābhisamācārikanti visuddhimagge  vuttaṃ tasmā nti ettha sikkhitabbato sekkhā bhagavatā paññattattā paṇṇatti sabbāpi ubhatovibhaṅgapariyāpannā sikkhāpadapaṇṇatti sekkhapaṇṇattīti vuttāti gahetabbaṃ teneva gaṇṭhipadepi vuttaṃ nti pārājikamādiṃ katvā sikkhitabbasikkhāpadapaññattiyanti sesamettha uttānatthamevāti sāratthadīpaniyaṃ 
upajjhāyācariyakathāyaṃ ti upajjhāyena hutvā na pabbājetabbo asekkhassa  ayanti  lokiyalokuttaro sīlakkhandho ti sassatucchedakoṭṭhāsaggāhikāya ti appassuto hoti duppañño hotīti imāni dve aṅgāni ti na paṭibalo uppannaṃ kukkuccaṃ dhammato vinodetuṃ āpattiṃ na jānāti āpattivuṭṭhānaṃ na jānātīti imāni tīṇi kukkuccassa hi pāḷiaṭṭhakathānayasaṅkhātadhammato vinodetuṃ appaṭibalatā nāma abyattattā eva hotīti sāpi āpattiaṅgameva vuttā
abhi visiṭṭho uttamo samācāro abhisamācāro vattappaṭipattisīlaṃ taṃ ārabbha paññattā khandhakasikkhāpadasaṅkhātā sikkhā  sikkhāpadampi hi tadatthaparipūraṇatthikehi uggahaṇādivasena sikkhitabbato sikkhāti vuccati maggabrahmacariyassa ādibhūtā kāraṇabhūtā  ubhatovibhaṅgapariyāpannasikkhāpadaṃ tenevettha visuddhimaggepi  ubhatovibhaṅgapariyāpannaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ ādibrahmacariyakaṃ khandhakavattapariyāpannaṃ ābhisamācārikanti vuttaṃ tasmā nti ettha sikkhitabbato sekkhā bhagavatāva  paññattattā paññatti sabbāpi ubhatovibhaṅgapariyāpannā sikkhāpadapaṇṇatti sekkhapaṇṇattīti vuttāti gahetabbaṃ ti ettha kusalattikādīhi vuttaṃ jātibhūmipuggalasampayogavatthārammaṇakammadvāralakkhaṇarasādibhedehi vedanākkhandhādicatubbidhaṃ sanibbānaṃ nāmaṃ bhūtopādāyabhedaṃ rūpañca paricchinditvā jānanapaññā tappakāsako ca gantho nāmarūpaparicchedo nāma iminā abhidhammatthakusalena bhavitabbanti dasseti nti uggaṇhāpetunti vimativinodaniyaṃ  vuttaṃ
ti ettha ti upayogatthe sāmivacanaṃ āyasmantaṃ nissāya vasissāmīti attho yaṃ pana vuttaṃ samantapāsādikāyaṃ  byatto vuttalakkhaṇoyevāti taṃ parisūpaṭṭhākabahussutaṃ sandhāya vadati nti pabbajjācariyaupasampadācariyadhammācariyānaṃ etehi yathāvuttavattaṃ kātabbaṃ tattha yena sikkhāpadāni dinnāni ayaṃ  yena upasampadakammavācā vuttā ayaṃ  yo uddesaṃ vā paripucchaṃ vā deti ayaṃ ti veditabbaṃ sesamettha uttānatthameva
 nissayapaṭippassaddhikathāyaṃ ti puna āgantukāmo anāgantukāmo vā hutvā vāsatthāya kañci disaṃ gato nti pesalabhāvaṃ ti upaparikkhitvā nti ettha sace kenaci karaṇīyena tadaheva gantuṃ asakkonto katipāhena gamissāmīti gamane saussāho hoti rakkhatīti vadanti ti mā  idha pavisi nti tattheva nissayaṃ gahetvā vasitabbaṃ ti ettha upajjhāyena pariccattattā upajjhāyasamodhānaparihāro natthi tasmā upajjhāyasamodhānagatassapi ācariyassa santike gahitanissayo na paṭippassambhati
ti ettha pakkantoti disaṃ gatotiādinā upajjhāyassa pakkamane yo vinicchayo vutto so tattha vuttanayeneva idhāpi sakkā viññātunti taṃ avatvā tiādinā aññoyeva nayo āraddho ayañca nayo upajjhāyapakkamanepi veditabboyeva īdisesu hi ṭhānesu ekatthavuttalakkhaṇaṃ aññatthāpi daṭṭhabbaṃ ti ettha ettāvatā disāpakkanto nāma hotīti antevāsike anikkhittadhurepi nissayo paṭippassambhati ti bahiupacārasīmāyaṃ antevāsikasaddhivihārikānaṃ vasanaṭṭhānato dve leḍḍupāte atikkamma aññasmiṃ senāsane vasanti antoupacārasīmāyaṃ pana dve leḍḍupāte atikkamitvāpi vasato nissayo na paṭippassambhati sacepi ācariyo muñcitukāmova hutvā nissayapaṇāmanāya paṇāmetītiādi sabbaṃ upajjhāyassa āṇattiyampi veditabbanti sāratthadīpaniyaṃ  vuttaṃ
ti sādhu sundaraṃ ti agaru subharatāti attho nti upāyapaṭisaṃyuttaṃ evaṃ paṭipajjanaṃ nittharaṇupāyoti attho nti sāmīcikammamidanti attho  ti pasādāvahena kāyavacīpayogena sampādehīti attho ti etadatthaviññāpakaṃ hatthamuddādiṃ dassento kāyena viññāpeti ti upajjhāyena sāhūtiādīsu vuttesu saddhivihārikassa sādhūti sampaṭicchanavacanaṃ sandhāya kāyena viññāpetītiādi vuttanti adhippāyo ti  saddhivihārikassa paṭhamaṃ āyācanamattena tato upajjhāyassa ca sāhūtiādinā vacanamattenāti attho ācariyassa santike nissayaggahaṇe ti āyasmantaṃ nissāya vasissāmīti attho
 nissayapaṭippassaddhikathāyaṃ ti iminā lajjīsu eva nissayaggahaṇaṃ niyojeti alajjīsu paṭikkhittattā ettha ca paribhogasaddena ekakammādiko saṃvāso gahito paccayaparibhogassa sambhogasaddena gahitattā etena ca sambhogasaṃvāsānaṃ alajjīhi saddhiṃ na kattabbataṃ dasseti ti āpattiparihāro natthi ti yattha nissayo gahitapubbo yo ca ekasambhogaparibhogo tādiso nti lahuṃ āgamissatīti vuttañceti attho ti idaṃ upalakkhaṇamattaṃ yadi ekāhadvīhena sabhāgatā paññāyati ñātadivase gahetabbova athāpi catupañcāhenapi na paññāyati yattakehi divasehi paññāyati tattakāni atikkāmetabbāni sabhāgataṃ olokemīti pana leso na kātabbo ti ettha asutvāpi āgamissati kenaci antarāyena cirāyantīti saññāya satipi labbhateva parihāro  tenāha ti ti gamane nirussāhaṃ sandhāya vuttaṃ saussāhassa pana senāsanapaṭisāmanādivasena katipāhe gatepi na doso
nti tatra sabhāgaṭṭhāne eva nissayaṃ gahetvā vasitabbaṃ ti iminā upajjhāye saṅgaṇhanteyeva taṃsamodhāne nissayapaṭippassaddhi vuttā tasmiṃ pana kodhena vā gaṇanirapekkhatāya vā asaṅgaṇhante aññesu gahito nissayo na paṭippassambhatīti dasseti
