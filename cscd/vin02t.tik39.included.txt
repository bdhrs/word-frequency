ti vinayalakkhaṇaṃ vinā kevalaṃ dhammadesanāmattavasenevāti attho ti pāḷimuttakavinicchayameva sandhāya vuttaṃ ti khandhakaparivārato ānītasuttena ti paññāpenti
nti pubbe kataukkhepanīyādikiccaṃ nissāya uppajjanakakiccānaṃ kīdisānaṃ yāvatatiyasamanubhāsanādīnaṃ
ti taṃ vivādādhikaraṇaṃ
nti yadā adhikaraṇehi sammanti tadā kiccādhikaraṇeneva sammanti na aññehi adhikaraṇehīti dassanatthaṃ vuttanti adhippāyo
