(9) 4. Sativaggo
1-2. Satisampajaññasuttavaṇṇanā
81-82 . saddho No cupasaṅkamitā No  ca paripucchitā Samannāgato  Ekantapaṭibhānā tathāgataṃ dhammadesanā hotī
3. Mūlakasuttavaṇṇanā
83 . sabbe dhammā Chandamūlakā manasikārasambhavā phassasamudayā vedanāsamosaraṇā samādhippamukhā satādhipateyyā, paññuttarā vimuttisārā
4. Corasuttavaṇṇanā
84 .  mahācoro Pariyāpajjatī Na ciraṭṭhitiko hotī Appaharantassa paharatī Anavasesaṃ ādiyatī Accāsanne kammaṃ karotī Na ca nidhānakusalo hotī
5. Samaṇasuttavaṇṇanā
85 .   yaṃ samaṇenā Vusīmatā Mutto mocemi bandhanā Paramadanto Parinibbuto
6. Yasasuttavaṇṇanā
86 . mā ca mayā yaso Akasiralābhī Sīlapaññāṇa Saṅgammā Samāgammā Saṅgaṇikavihāra Na hi nūname Tathā hi paname Aṅgulipatodakehī Sañjagghante Saṃkīḷante
7. Pattanikujjanasuttavaṇṇanā
87 .  nikkujjeyyā Alābhāyā  Anatthāyā Ukkujjeyyā
8. Appasādapavedanīyasuttavaṇṇanā
88 . appasādaṃ pavedeyyu Agocare
9. Paṭisāraṇīyasuttavaṇṇanā
89 .  dhammikañca gihipaṭissava Na saccāpetī
10. Sammāvattanasuttavaṇṇanā
90 . paccekaṭṭhāne Na ca tena mūlena vuṭṭhāpetabbo
Sativaggo navamo.
