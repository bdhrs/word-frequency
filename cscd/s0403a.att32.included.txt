

 sattamassa paṭhame nti assaddhabhāvaṃ nti nippaññabhāvaṃ dutiye nti sativippavāsaṃ tatiye nti uttamasamācārabhūtaṃ vattavasena paṇṇattisīlaṃ nti sekhapaṇṇattisīlaṃ ti cattāri mahāsīlāni

 catutthe ti gaṇasaṅgaṇikārāmo suttantikagaṇādīsu pana gaṇesu attano vā parisāsaṅkhāte gaṇe ramatīti ti kāyaviveke nti samādhivipassanācittassa nimittaṃ samādhivipassanākāraṃ nti vipassanāsammādiṭṭhiṃ nti maggasamādhiñceva phalasamādhiñca ti dasa saṃyojanāni nti apaccayaparinibbānaṃ

 pañcame ti subbacabhāvo ti sucimittatā ti satthari gāravayutto esa nayo sabbattha

 chaṭṭhe ti paccanīkakilesehi avūpasantena ti na atappakena ti kilesappaṭippassaddhiyā aladdhena appattena ti na ekaggabhāvaṃ upagatena

 sattame ti tasmiṃ tasmiṃ visese nti paccakkhabhāvaṃ ti kāraṇe dayo visuddhimagge (visuddhi 139) saṃvaṇṇitā ti na sukatakārī na ādarakārī ti na sappāyakārī na upakārabhūtadhammakārī

 aṭṭhame nti balabhāvaṃ thāmabhāvaṃ ti na satatakārī sesaṃ heṭṭhā vuttanayameva

 navame ti vatthukāmakilesakāmesu ādīnavo na yathāsabhāvato jhānapaññāya sudiṭṭho hoti dasamaṃ uttānatthamevāti
