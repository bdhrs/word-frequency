


ratanattayappaṇāma

 namatthu jana santāna tama santāna bhedino
dhammujjalanta rucino munindodāta rocino
nimitta

 piṅgalācariyādīhi chandaṃ ya muditaṃ purā
suddhamāgadhikānaṃ taṃ na sādheti yathicchitaṃ 
ganthaparimāṇa

 tato māgadhabhāsāya mattāvaṇṇavibhedanaṃ
lakkhya lakkhaṇa saṃyuttaṃ pasannatthapadakkamaṃ
abhidhānādi

 idaṃ  nāma lokiyacchandanissitaṃ
ārabhissamahaṃ dāni tesaṃ sukhavibuddhiyā
gaṇasaṅketasaññā

 sabbaglā mnādigalahū majjhanta garū 
majjhantalā teṭṭha gaṇā go garulo lahu
gaṇaniyama

 bhajasā  sabbagalahū pañcime saṇṭhitā gaṇā
 viññeyyā gaṇo idha catukkalo
garulahusarūpa

 saṃyogādi ca dīgho ca niggahītaparo ca yo
 vaṅko pādantovā rassoñño mattiko ju

 pare pādādisaṃyoge yo pubbo garukakkharo
 sa kvaci viññeyyo tadudāharaṇaṃ yathā

 dassanarasānubhāvane nibaddhagedhā jinassayaṃ janatā
vimhayajananī saññayā nu kaṃ nānurañjayati

 viññeyyā lokato saññā dinaṃ
ñeyyo catutthaṃso padacchedo  bhave

 vuttaṃ  cāparaṃ tidhā
samā lakkhaṇato pādā cattāro yassa taṃ 

 yassantimena dutiyo tatiyenādimo samo
ta tu bhinna lakkhaṇa pādikaṃ

 pādamekakkharārabbha yāva chabbīsatakkharā
bhave pādehi taṃ chandaṃ nānānāmoditaṃ tato

 caṇḍavuṭṭhyādi pādehi chahi tīhi tu
ti ca paratthevaṃ chando saññā pakāsitā

 anantaroditaṃ cañña metaṃ sāmañña nāmato
icceva niddiṭṭhaṃ munindavacane pana

 visesanāmato kiñci gahetvā sabbathocitaṃ
dassayissāmahaṃ tettha nāmānāvi bhavissare
