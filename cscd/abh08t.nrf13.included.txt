

nti  hitāvahaṃ nti suṭṭhu pavattamānasañca madhurā atthavaṇṇanā etassāti nti manussānaṃ lokiyadhammato uttaribhūtañāṇadassanalokuttaraṃ ti saṅkāya kaṅkhāya saritabbasamācāre acchiddaṃ akkhaṇḍaṃ akammāsaṃ ti attano hitakāmena

nti taṃ nimittaṃ

nti upacārappanāsamādhīnaṃ
