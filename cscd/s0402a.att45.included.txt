

 pañcamassa  paṭhame ti asurasadiso bībhaccho ti devasadiso guṇavasena abhirūpo pāsādiko

 dutiye ti niyakajjhatte appanācittasamādhissa ti saṅkhārapariggāhakavipassanāñāṇassa tañhi adhipaññāsaṅkhātañca pañcakkhandhasaṅkhātesu ca dhammesu vipassanābhūtaṃ tasmā adhipaññādhammavipassanāti vuccatīti

 tatiye ti yuttappayuttatā kattabbā ti kattukamyatāchando ti payogo ti tato adhimattataraṃ vīriyaṃ ti paṅkalaggasakaṭauddharaṇasadisaṃ mahāvīriyaṃ ti anivattanatā

 catutthe tiādīsu āvuso saṅkhārā nāma aniccato daṭṭhabbā aniccato sammasitabbā aniccato passitabbā tathā dukkhato anattatoti evaṃ attho daṭṭhabbo ntiādīsupi paṭhamajjhānavasena āvuso cittaṃ saṇṭhapetabbaṃ paṭhamajjhānavasena sannisādetabbaṃ paṭhamajjhānavasena  ekodi kātabbaṃ paṭhamajjhānavasena samādahitabbaṃ tathā dutiyajjhānādivasenāti evaṃ attho daṭṭhabbo imesu tīsupi suttesu samathavipassanā lokiyalokuttarāva kathitā

 pañcame nti susāne alātaṃ nti majjhaṭṭhāne gūthamakkhitaṃ ti kūṭagopānasithambhasopānādīnaṃ atthāya anupaneyyatāya gāme na kaṭṭhatthaṃ sādheti khettakuṭipādaṃ vā mañcapādaṃ vā kātuṃ anupaneyyatāya na araññe kaṭṭhatthaṃ sādheti dvīsu koṭīsu gayhamānaṃ hatthaṃ ḍahati majjhe gayhamānaṃ gūthena makkheti nti taṃsarikkhakaṃ ti sundarataro ti uttamataro nti gāvito khīraṃ tiādīsu paraṃ paraṃ purimato purimato aggaṃ sappimaṇḍo pana tesu sabbesupi aggameva  tiādīsu guṇehi aggo ceva seṭṭho ca pamukho ca uttamo ca pavaro cāti veditabbo chavālātūpamāya na dussīlo puggalo kathito appassuto pana vissaṭṭhakammanto goṇasadiso puggalo kathitoti veditabbo chaṭṭhe sabbaṃ uttānatthameva

 sattame ti khippanisāmano sīghaṃ jānituṃ samattho nti sutappaguṇānaṃ tantidhammānaṃ ti atthaṃ upaparikkhako ti aṭṭhakathañca pāḷiñca jānitvā ti navalokuttaradhammānaṃ  anurūpadhammabhūtaṃ sasīlakaṃ pubbabhāgappaṭipadaṃ paṭipanno hoti ti na sundaravacano ti na sundaravacanaghoso hoti tiādīhi saddhiṃ no-kāro yojetabboyeva guṇaparipuṇṇāya apalibuddhāya adosāya agaḷitapadabyañjanāya atthaṃ viññāpetuṃ samatthāya vācāya samannāgato na hotīti attho iminā upāyena sabbattha attho veditabbo

 aṭṭhamaṃ  puggalajjhāsayavasenāpi dasabalassa desanāñāṇavilāsenāpi kathitaṃ navamaṃ pañcaveravasena

 dasame ti yuttappattakālena ti ruccati ti yā esā tattha tattha kālaṃ jānanā taṃ taṃ kālaṃ ñatvā hi avaṇṇārahassa avaṇṇakathanaṃ vaṇṇārahassa ca vaṇṇakathanaṃ paṇḍitānaṃ pakatīti dasseti
