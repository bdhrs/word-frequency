

 tatiyapaṇṇāsakassa  paṭhame ti meghā ti idañcidañca karissāmīti kevalaṃ bhāsatiyeva na karoti ti akathetvāva idañcidañca mayā kātuṃ vaṭṭatīti kattā hoti evaṃ sabbattha attho veditabbo dutiyaṃ uttānatthameva

 tatiye ti ghaṭā ti rittako pihitamukho ti udakapuṇṇo apārutamukho sesadvayepi eseva nayo

 catutthe tiādīsu purāṇapaṇṇarasasambhinnavaṇṇo kāḷaudako  nāma acchavippasannamaṇivaṇṇaudako  nāma

 pañcame ti āmakaṃ hutvā olokentānaṃ pakkasadisaṃ khāyati evaṃ sabbapadāni daṭṭhabbāni chaṭṭhaṃ uttānatthameva

 sattame  yo āvāṭaṃ khaṇati na ca tattha vasati so ti vuccati khantātipi pāṭho iminā nayena sabbapadāni veditabbāni

 aṭṭhame  yo attano gogaṇaṃ maddati na paragogaṇaṃ ayaṃ ti evaṃ sabbapadāni veditabbāni ti ghaṭṭetvā vijjhitvā ubbegapattaṃ karoti

 navame ti nissāro pheggurukkho pheggurukkheheva parivuto ti  khadirādīhi sārarukkheheva parivuto esa nayo sabbattha

 dasame ti yassa visaṃ āgacchati ghoraṃ pana na hoti cirakālaṃ na pīḷeti sesapadesupi eseva nayoti
