
 tatiyassa  paṭhame nti samathavipassanābhāvanāvasena na bhāvitaṃ tathā abhāvitattā tañhi avaḍḍhitanti vuccati paṭipakkhābhibhavena paribrūhanābhāvato tenāha bhagavā  ti
 dutiye vuttavipariyāyena attho veditabbo ti tatiyavaggassa paṭhamasutte ti vipākavaṭṭavasena nti tebhūmakavipākavaṭṭaṃ nti vipākavaṭṭassa paṭilābhāya upanissayabhūtaṃ kammaṃ tassa sahāyabhūtaṃ kilesavaṭṭampi kammaggahaṇeneva saṅgahitanti daṭṭhabbaṃ nti vivaṭṭādhigamassa upanissayabhūtaṃ kammaṃ yaṃ pana carimabhavanibbattakaṃ kammaṃ taṃ vivaṭṭappaṭilābhāya kammaṃ hoti na hotīti na hoti vaṭṭapādakabhāvato carimabhavapaṭisandhi  viya pana vivaṭṭūpanissayoti sakkā viññātuṃ na hi kadāci tihetukapaṭisandhiyā vinā visesādhigamo sambhavati ti imesu pana paṭhamadutiyasuttesu yathākkamaṃ vaṭṭavivaṭṭameva kathitaṃ
 tatiye nti ettha bhāvanā nāma samādhibhāvanā sā yattha āsaṅkitabbā taṃ kāmāvacarapaṭhamamahākusalacittādiabhāvitanti adhippetanti āha  tiādi
 catutthe yasmā nti vivaṭṭavaseneva uppannacittaṃ adhippetaṃ tasmā jātijarābyādhimaraṇasokādidukkhassa anibbattanato mahato atthāya saṃvattatīti yojanā veditabbā
 pañcamachaṭṭhesu nti avigatuppādādikhaṇattayampi abhāvitaṃ bhāvanārahitaṃ apātubhūtameva paṇḍitasammatassa uppannakiccassa asādhanato yathā aputtoti so hi samattho hutvā pitu puttakiccaṃ asādhento aputtoti loke vuccati evaṃ sampadamidaṃ tenāha  tiādi ti lokuttarapādakajjhānādīsu thero pana matthakappattameva bhāvitaṃ cittaṃ dassento ti āha
 sattamaṭṭhamesu nti bhāvanābahulīkāravasena punappunaṃ na kataṃ ti imesu dvīsu suttesu āgatāni imānipi dve cittāni
