ti jiṇṇabhāvena javaparakkamahāniyā aparibhogo na bhuñjitabbo ti yavasaṃ adadantā tato nīharanti nāma ti vuḍḍho
ti na vacanakarā ti patissayarahitā ti na mayhaṃ vasena vattanakā ti upatthambhakāribhāvena sundarataro
ti upatthambhakabhāvena purato katvā  tattha patiṭṭhato thirapatiṭṭhabhāvato addhapatiṭṭho hi daṇḍo daṇḍadharapurisassa patiṭṭhaṃ labhāpeti
ti attano brāhmaṇiyo nti asukassa gehe asukassa geheti evaṃ uddesikaṃ katvā visuṃ visuṃ ti tava puttānaṃ gehesu yaṃ amhākaṃ ruccanaṭṭhānaṃ tatthameva gamissāmāti sabbesampi etesaṃ anuggahaṃ kātukāmo bhagavā evamāha
ti sotāpattiphalapaṭilābhato paṭṭhāya yadatthaṃ mayaṃ imassa brāhmaṇassa mahāsampattidānādinā  anuggaho kato so cassa puttaparijanassapi attho siddhoti satthā na sabbakālaṃ tassa brāhmaṇassa puttānaṃ gehaṃ agamāsi te eva pana kālena kālaṃ satthu santikaṃ gantvā yathāvibhavaṃ sakkārasammānaṃ akaṃsūti adhippāyo


ti ayaṃ khotiādinā paggahitena mānena thaddho bajjhitacitto thaddhaayosalāko viya kassacipi anonato  loke paṭisanthāramattassapi ajānanato
ti sañjātaabbhutavittā vittaṃ vittīti ca tuṭṭhipariyāyā ti yā tassā parisāya vitti tadā bhūtā sā ito pubbe abhūtā tenāha ti ti anena puggalena ti paramanipaccena pūjanīyasāmaññato tattha attano pakkhipanaṃ idha desanākosallaṃ


nti kenaci  tassa  brāhmaṇassa ti vināsetvā  suṇātīti kiriyāpadaṃ āharitvā gāthāya attho veditabbo jaññā subhāsitanti vuttattā na hi asutvā desitaṃ jānituṃ sakkonti
paccanīkasuttavaṇṇanā niṭṭhitā

 vanakamme niyutto kiriyamānena vanakammaṃ etassa atthīti  ti imasmiṃ evaṃmahante vanasaṇḍe na me vanasmiṃ karaṇīyamatthi yathā brāhmaṇa tuyhanti adhippāyo itaraṃ pana yaṃ mahākilesavanaṃ taṃ maggañāṇapharasunā samādhimayasilāyaṃ  sunisitena sabbaso  ucchinnamūlaṃ tato eva  nikkilesagahano vivekābhiratiyā ekako abhirato paṭipakkhavigamena tenāha ti


ti kiñcipi dhanaṃ adatvā kevalaṃ antevāsikā tenāha ti ti samantato dūrato gahanasacchannavipulatararukkhagacchalatāya ca tadā himapiṇḍasinnabhāvena mayūrayānehi patiṭṭhātuṃ asakkuṇeyyatāya ca gambhīrabhāve
ti bahubhayānake ti itthambhūtatthe karaṇavacanaṃ ti evaṃ sante nāma araññe evaṃ niccalakāyo nisinno jhāyanto ativiya sundarañca jhānaṃ jhāyasīti vadati
nti acchariyabhāvaṃ ti seṭṭhabhāvappattiyā
lokādhipatisahabyataṃ ākaṅkhamānoti iminā brāhmaṇo lokādhipatisahayogaṃ pucchati kasmā bhavantiādinā pana tadaññavisesākaṅkhaṃ pucchatīti āha ti
 abhikkhaṇavasena pavattā ti rūpādivasena ajjhattikādivasena evaṃ nānāsabhāvesu ārammaṇesu assādarāgo lobho abhijjhāti  diṭṭhimānadosādayo  sattānaṃ avassayabhāvattā ti pakārehi taṇhāyanavasena ti antarahitā niravasesā
ti anupādāno  tassa hi vasena eva nānāsabhāvesu  bhagavā samantacakkhunā passatīti ti  vuccati  tañhi nippariyāyato anuttaranti vuccati


ti manussattabhāvato ti abhigantvā sagge hi nibbattanto kammaphalena dhammatāvasena paṭisandhikālato paṭṭhāya abhirativasena abhiramati eva nāmāti


 virūpaṃ gandhaṃ pasavatīti  duggandho ti attano santānato bahikatvā nīharitvā taṃ pana anavasesato pajahananti āha ti saṅkhāyati sammadeva paricchindati etāyāti  ñāṇanti āha ti ti niruttinayena bhikkhusaddasiddhimāha


ti pattiyāyati saddahati tathābhūto ca tamatthaṃ nikāmento nāma hotīti vuttaṃ ti ti ettha -saddo āyācanattho na abhinandanatthoti ti vatvā āyācane kāraṇaṃ dassento tiādimāha udakasuddhiko na ekaṃsena apāyūpago laddhiyā pana  sāvajjakilesabhūtabhāvato āyācati aññaṃ kāraṇaṃ apadisanto tiādimāha phalabhūto attho etassāti  kāraṇanti āha nti  pana phaleneva araṇīyato attho kāraṇanti katvā attho eva atthavaso tasmā ti dve atthe dve kāraṇānīti vuttaṃ


 evaṃladdhanāmaṃ  piṇḍāya pāvisīti yojanā nti kenaci animantitena sayameva upasaṅkamante nti ayuttarūpaṃ nti sabhāya caritabbaṃ cārittadhammaṃ ti āsanato avuṭṭhāpetvā  lokagganāyako bhagavā sabbasattuttamo attano uttaritarassa abhāvato lokassa apacitiṃ na dasseti ye sabbaso saṃkilese pajahanti teva paṇḍite santoti dasseti tiādinā
