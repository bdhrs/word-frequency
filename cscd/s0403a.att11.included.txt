

 dutiyassa  paṭhame ti attanā saddhiṃ santhavaṃ akarontesu vissāsaṃ anāpajjantesuyeva vissāsaṃ karoti ti  anissarova samāno imaṃ detha imaṃ gaṇhathāti issaro viya vikappeti ti vissaṭṭhāni bhinnakulāni ghaṭanatthāya upasevati ti kaṇṇamūle mantaṃ gaṇhāti sukkapakkho vuttavipariyāyena veditabbo

 dutiye ti upajjhāye nivattitvā ṭhite attano tucchapattaṃ datvā tassa pattaṃ na gaṇhāti tato vā dīyamānaṃ na gaṇhāti ti idaṃ vacanaṃ āpattivītikkamavacanaṃ nāmāti  na jānāti ñatvā vāpi bhante evarūpaṃ nāma vattuṃ na vaṭṭatīti na nivāreti ti tassa kathaṃ bhinditvā attano kathaṃ paveseti ti jaḍo ti paggharitakheḷamukho tatiyaṃ uttānameva

 catutthe ti sīlavantā hotha ti dvārarakkhikāya satiyā samannāgatā ti dvārarakkhanakeneva ñāṇena samannāgatassatino ti satārakkhena cittena samannāgatā ti appakathā ti kammassakatajjhāna-vipassanāmagga-phalavasena pañcavidhāya sammādiṭṭhiyā samannāgatā apica paccavekkhaṇañāṇampi sammādiṭṭhiyevāti veditabbā

 pañcame ti āvāsaṃ maccharāyati tattha aññesaṃ vāsaṃ na sahati ti upaṭṭhākakulaṃ maccharāyati aññesaṃ tattha upasaṅkamanaṃ na sahati ti lābhaṃ maccharāyati aññesaṃ taṃ uppajjantaṃ na sahati ti guṇaṃ maccharāyati aññesaṃ guṇakathaṃ na sahati ti  pariyattidhammaṃ maccharāyati aññesaṃ dātuṃ na icchati

 chaṭṭhe ti parehi saddhāya dinnapiṇḍapātato aggaṃ aggahetvā parassa deti sattame ti issāya samannāgatā sesaṃ sabbattha uttānamevāti
