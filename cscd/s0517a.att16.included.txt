parato  pana ti kammassakā sattāti evaṃ kammassakatāya pavattacittena ca ñāṇena ca ti saccapaṭivedhassa anukūlattā saccānulomikasaṅkhātena vipassanāsampayuttacittena ca vipassanāñāṇena ca nti yathā abhisamayo hoti tathā kathetukamyatā pucchā nti yasmā cittassa uppāde asati cetasikānaṃ uppādo natthi ārammaṇaggahaṇañhi cittaṃ tena saha uppajjamānā cetasikā kathaṃ ārammaṇe aggahite uppajjissanti abhidhammepi cittuppādeneva cetasikā vibhattā tasmā maggañāṇassa uppāde adhipatibhūtaṃ cittanti attho ti maggañāṇassa ti janako ca upatthambhako  ca nti tena ñāṇena sampayuttaṃ nti nibbānārammaṇaṃ nti sesānaṃ dassanakiccābhāvā nibbānadassane adhipatibhūtaṃ ti maggasampayuttacittassa nti tena cittena sampayuttaṃ

 yasmā etampi pariyāyaṃ na kevalaṃ cittañāṇehiyeva abhisamayo atha kho sabbepi maggasampayuttacittacetasikā dhammā saccābhisamayakiccasādhanavasena  abhisamayo nāma honti tasmā tampi pariyāyaṃ dassetukāmo ti pucchitvā ti taṃ vacanaṃ paṭikkhipitvā tiādimāha ti dassanabhūto abhisamayo esa nayo sesesupi ti saccañāṇāni maggañāṇameva nibbānānupassanaṭṭhena ti maggavimokkho ti maggañāṇameva ti samucchedavimutti nibbānaṃ abhisamīyatīti  sesā abhisamenti etehīti 

 puna maggaphalavasena abhisamayaṃ bhinditvā dassetuṃ tiādimāha phalakkhaṇe panettha yasmā samucchedanaṭṭhena khaye ñāṇaṃ na labbhati tasmā paṭippassaddhaṭṭhena nti vuttaṃ sesaṃ pana yathānurūpaṃ veditabbanti idāni yasmā kilesappahāne sati abhisamayo hoti abhisamaye ca sati kilesappahānaṃ hoti tasmā codanāpubbaṅgamaṃ kilesappahānaṃ dassetukāmo ntiādimāha tattha nti yo ayaṃ maggaṭṭho ariyapuggalo evamādikāni panettha cattāri vacanāni codakassa pucchā puna ti idaṃ codanāya okāsadānatthaṃ vissajjanaṃ nti bhaṅgavasena khīṇaṃ nti santānavasena punappunaṃ anuppattiyā niruddhaṃ nti vattamānakkhaṇato apagataṃ ti apagamayati nti abhāvaṃ gataṃ ti abhāvaṃ gamayati tattha dosaṃ dassetvā ti paṭikkhittaṃ anāgatacodanāya nti jātiṃ appattaṃ nti sabhāvaṃ appattaṃ nti uppādato pabhuti uddhaṃ na paṭipannaṃ nti paccuppannabhāvena cittassa apātubhūtaṃ atītānāgate pajahato pahātabbānaṃ natthitāya aphalo vāyāmo āpajjatīti tadubhayampi paṭikkhittaṃ ti vattamānena rāgena ratto tameva rāgaṃ pajahati vattamānakilesesupi eseva nayo ti thirasabhāvaṃ gato ti akusalā ca kusalā ca dhammā ti āpajjatīti attho ti evaṃ saṃkilesānaṃ sampayuttabhāve sati saṃkilese niyuttā ti āpajjatīti attho evaṃ paccuppanne pajahato vāyāmena saddhiṃ pahātabbānaṃ  atthitāya saṃkilesikā ca maggabhāvanā hoti vāyāmo ca aphalo hoti na hi paccuppannānaṃ kilesānaṃ cittavippayuttatā nāma atthīti
