bhavati ettha phalaṃ tadāyattavuttitāyāti  kāraṇanti āha tiādi ti pariṇato vuddhibhāvaṃ pattoti attho ti anto thirakaraṇānaṃ dhammānaṃ abhāvena tucchajiṇṇo nāma bāladārakopi daharoti vuccatīti tato visesanatthaṃ ti vuttaṃ atikkantapaṭhamavayā eva sattā sabhāvena palitasirā hontīti paṭhamavaye ṭhitabhāvaṃ dassetuṃ ti vuttaṃ ti laddhakena ekacco hi daharopi samāno kāṇo vā hoti kuṇiādīnaṃ vā aññataro so na bhadrena yobbanena samannāgato nāma hoti yo pana abhirūpo hoti dassanīyo pāsādiko sabbasampattisampanno yaṃ yadeva alaṅkāraparihāraṃ icchati tena tena alaṅkato devaputto viya carati ayaṃ bhadrena yobbanena samannāgato nāma hoti tenevāha tiādi

 aṭṭhame ti padaṃ uddharitvā yehi kāraṇehi te balavanto honti tesaṃ sabbhāvaṃ dassento tiādimāha tattha  giriduggādisabbhāvato nti anto yātuṃ gantuṃ pavisitunti attho taṃ pana antopavisanaṃ kenaci kāraṇena bahigatassa hotīti āha tiādi nti bahi nikkhamituṃ tañca bahinikkhamanaṃ bahiddhākaraṇīye sati sambhavatīti āha tiādi nti anusañcarituṃ sesamettha uttānameva
