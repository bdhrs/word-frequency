

 cammakkhandhake nti ubhato lomāni uṭṭhāpetvā kataṃ uṇṇamayaṃ pāvāraṇaṃ ubhato kappāsapicuṃ uṭṭhāpetvā vītapāvāropi atthi tato nivattanatthaṃ uṇṇapāvāraṇanti vuttaṃ
ti sopānamūle upaḍḍhaṃ anto pavesetvā ṭhapite aḍḍhapāsāṇe pāḷiyaṃ nti vihārapaccante chāyāya vihārassa vaḍḍhamānacchāyāyantipi vadanti
ti upayogatthe paccattavacanaṃ ti atiāyatā kharamucchanā ti madhurasarasaṃyuttā  mandamucchanā nti vīriyasampayuttasamathaṃ ti tasmiñca samabhāve sati yaṃ ādāse mukhanimittaṃ viya nimittaṃ uppajjati taṃ samathanimittaṃ vipassanānimittaṃ magganimittaṃ phalanimittañca gaṇhāhi nibbattehīti evamassa arahattapariyosānaṃ kammaṭṭhānaṃ kathitaṃ
ti cha kāraṇāni ti paṭivijjhitvā ṭhito tiādi sabbaṃ arahattavasena vuttaṃ arahattañhi sabbakilesehi nikkhantattā  teheva ca pavivittattā  byāpajjābhāvato  upādānassa khayante uppannattā  taṇhakkhayante uppannattā  sammohābhāvato ti ca vuccati
nti kevalaṃ paṭivedhapaññāya asammissaṃ saddhāmattakaṃ nti punappunaṃ  karaṇena vaḍḍhiṃ ti maggapaṭivedhena rāgassa vigatattā eva nekkhammasaṅkhātaṃ arahattaṃ paṭivijjhitvā sacchikatvā ṭhito hoti sesapadesupi eseva nayo ti paviveke adhimutto ahanti evaṃ arahattaṃ byākarotīti attho
nti sīlañca vatañca parāmasitvā gahitaggahaṇamattaṃ ti sārabhāvena jānanto ti abyāpajjaṃ arahattaṃ byākaroti
nti  amissakataṃ kilesā hi ārammaṇena saddhiṃ cittaṃ missaṃ karonti tesaṃ abhāvā amissīkataṃ ti balavavātakkhandho
ti upayogatthe sāmivacanaṃ nti cakkhādiāyatanānaṃ uppādañca vayañca disvā ti imāya vipassanāpaṭipattiyā phalasamāpattivasena cittaṃ vimuccati


ti dvīhi sakaṭehi paricchinne vāhe ti bahuvacanassa hiraññavisesanattepi sāmaññāpekkhāya hiraññanti ekavacanaṃ kataṃ
ti allāriṭṭhaphalavaṇṇā tintakākapakkhavaṇṇātipi vadanti nti upalittaṃ nīlādivaṇṇaṃ sandhāya vuttaṃ tenāha ti tañhi tathā puñchite vigacchati yaṃ pana cammassa duggandhāpanayanatthaṃ kāḷarattādirajanehi rañjitattā kāḷarattādivaṇṇaṃ hoti taṃ coḷādīhi apanetuṃ na sakkā cammagatikameva tasmā taṃ vaṭṭatīti daṭṭhabbaṃ
nti sabbapaṇhipidhānacammaṃ aparigaḷanatthaṃ paṇhiuparibhāge apidhāya āropanabandhanamattaṃ vaṭṭati ti saṇṭhānato vicitrapaṭā adhippetā na vaṇṇato sabbaso apanetabbesu  khallakādīsu paviṭṭhattā biḷālasadisamukhattā mahāulūkā ti vuccati tesaṃ cammaṃ nāma pakkhalomameva


ti ettha uṇṇāmayakambalehi katā pādukā saṅgayhanti
ti gaṅgāmahe kīḷikāya tattha hi itthipurisā yānehi udakakīḷaṃ gacchanti nti phalakādinā kataṃ pīṭhakayānaṃ  andolikā sabbampi yānaṃ upāhanenapi gantuṃ asamatthassa gilānassa anuññātaṃ
ti āharimāni vāḷarūpāni ti uddalomīekantalomīhi visesadassanaṃ caturaṅgulato hi ūnāni kira uddalomīādīsu pavisanti ti nānāvaṇṇehi uṇṇāmayasuttehi bhitticchedādivasena vāyitvā katacittattharaṇo ti bahalarāgo ti tūlapuṇṇā bhisi ti sīharūpādivasena vānacitrāva gayhati ti ubhatodasaṃ uṇṇāmayattharaṇanti  vuttaṃ nti kosiyasuttānaṃ antarā suvaṇṇamayasuttāni pavesetvā vītaṃ suvaṇṇasuttaṃ kira kaṭṭissaṃ kasaṭanti ca vuccati teneva koseyyakasaṭamayanti  vuttanti vadanti nti suvaṇṇalittaṃ nti ratanaparisibbanarahitaṃ
ajinamigacammānaṃ atisukhumattā dupaṭṭatipaṭṭāni katvā sibbantīti vuttaṃ ti ti sabbarattena vitānena yaṃ pana nānāvaṇṇaṃ vānacittaṃ vā lepacittaṃ vā taṃ vaṭṭati pi eseva nayo ti idaṃ pana sabbathā kappiyattā vuttaṃ na pana ubhatoupadhānesu akappiyattā na hi lohitaka-saddo citte vattati paṭaliggahaṇeneva cittakassapi attharaṇassa saṅgahetabbappasaṅgato kāsāvaṃ pana lohitakavohāraṃ na gacchati tasmā vitānepi ubhatoupadhānepi vaṭṭati ntiādi aññappamāṇātikkantassa bibbohanassa paṭikkhittabhāvadassanatthaṃ vuttaṃ na pana  uccāsayanamahāsayanabhāvadassanatthaṃ tathā avuttattā taṃ pana upadhānaṃ uposathikānaṃ  gahaṭṭhānaṃ vaṭṭati uccāsayanamahāsayanameva hi tadā tesaṃ na vaṭṭati dīsu kiñcāpi ṭhapetvā tūlikaṃ sabbāneva gonakādīni ratanaparisibbitāni vaṭṭantītiādi vuttaṃ vinayaṭṭhakathāyeva kappiyākappiyabhāve pamāṇanti gahetabbaṃ


ti apassāya nti paṭipakkhabhūtānaṃ diṭṭhicittavipphanditānanti attho
nti manindriyavasena saññatindriyaṃ
 pāḷiyaṃ ti   aṭṭhakavaggabhūtāni soḷasa suttāni ti evaṃ cirakālaṃ pabbajjaṃ anupagantvā agāramajjhe kena kāraṇena vāsamakāsīti attho so kira majjhimavaye pabbajito tena bhagavā evamāha ti kāmesu diṭṭhādīnavā cirāyitvāpi gharāvāsena pakkhandantīti etamatthaṃ sabbākārato viditvā
ti saṅkhāraloke aniccatādiādīnavaṃ nti nibbānaṃ ariyo na ramatī pāpeti imassa hetumāha ti tattha ti visuddhapuggalo
ti kāḷamukhavānarajāti ti nisīdanattharaṇaṃ kātuṃ na vaṭṭati bhūmattharaṇādivasena senāsanaparibhogo vaṭṭateva
