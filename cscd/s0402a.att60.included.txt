

 pañcamassa  paṭhame nti vivādādhikaraṇādīsu aññataraṃ adhikaraṇaṃ bhikkhusaṅghassa uppajji satthā tassa vūpasantabhāvaṃ pucchanto evamāha ti bhante kuto kinti kena kāraṇena taṃ adhikaraṇaṃ vūpasamissatīti vadati nti sakalaṃ samantato ti saṅghena saddhiṃ vādatthāya kathitaṃ paṭikathentova ṭhito ti tasmiṃ evaṃ ṭhite āyasmā anuruddho ti mā āvuso saṅghena saddhiṃ evaṃ avacāti ekavacanampi vattabbaṃ na maññati ti anuyuñjati anuyogaṃ āpajjati ti kāraṇavase ti  uposathappavāraṇaṃ upagantuṃ adatvā nikkaḍḍhissanti sesaṃ pāḷivaseneva veditabbaṃ

 dutiye ti pañca sikhaṇḍake ṭhapetvā khurena muṇḍaṃ karitvā ti kakkhaḷasaddena ti vajjhabheriyā ti ekamante ṭhitassa nti sīsacchedārahaṃ ti yaṃ nāma nti so vata ahaṃ assaṃ yaṃ evarūpaṃ pāpaṃ na kareyyanti attho ti dhammānurūpaṃ paṭikarissati sāmaṇerabhūmiyaṃ ṭhassatīti attho ti kāḷapilotikaṃ nivāsetvā nti musalābhipātārahaṃ nti idha āpattito vuṭṭhāya suddhante patiṭṭhahanto yathādhammaṃ karoti nāma nti chārikābhaṇḍikaṃ nti garahitabbachārikāpuṭena matthake abhighātārahaṃ nti idha āpattiṃ desento yathādhammaṃ paṭikaroti nāma nti upavādārahaṃ ti paṭidesetabbesu iminā  sabbāpi  sesāpattiyo saṅgahitā ti bhikkhave imāni cattāri āpattiṃ nissāya uppajjanakabhayāni nāmāti

 tatiye sikkhā ānisaṃsā etthāti  paññā uttarā etthāti  vimutti sāro etthāti  sati ādhipateyyā etthāti  etesaṃ hi sikkhādisaṅkhātānaṃ ānisaṃsādīnaṃ atthāya vussatīti vuttaṃ hoti ti uttamasamācārikā vattavasena paññattasīlassetaṃ adhivacanaṃ ti tathā tathā so sikkhākāmo bhikkhu tasmiṃ sikkhāpade
ti maggabrahmacariyassa ādibhūtānaṃ catunnaṃ mahāsīlānametaṃ adhivacanaṃ ti sabbākārena ti catusaccadhammā ti sahavipassanāya maggapaññāya sudiṭṭhā honti ti arahattaphalavimuttiyā ñāṇaphassena phuṭṭhā honti ti niyakajjhatteyeva sati suṭṭhu upaṭṭhitā hoti ti vipassanāpaññāya  anuggahessāmi ti idhāpi vipassanāpaññā adhippetā ti ettha pana maggapaññāva adhippetā

 catutthe ti kālakatā vuccanti ti te yebhuyyena uttānakāva sayanti atha vā pettivisaye nibbattā  nāma te appamaṃsalohitattā aṭṭhisaṅghātajaṭitā ekena passena sayituṃ na sakkonti uttānāva senti ti tejussadattā sīho migarājā dve purimapāde ekasmiṃ pacchimapāde ekasmiṃ ṭhāne ṭhapetvā naṅguṭṭhaṃ antarasatthimhi pakkhipitvā purimapādapacchimapādanaṅguṭṭhānaṃ ṭhitokāsaṃ sallakkhetvā dvinnaṃ purimapādānaṃ matthake sīsaṃ ṭhapetvā sayati divasampi sayitvā pabujjhamāno na uttasanto pabujjhati sīsaṃ pana ukkhipitvā purimapādādīnaṃ ṭhitokāsaṃ sallakkhetvā sace kiñci ṭhānaṃ  vijahitvā ṭhitaṃ hoti nayidaṃ tuyhaṃ jātiyā na sūrabhāvassa anurūpanti anattamano hutvā tattheva sayati na gocarāya pakkamati idaṃ sandhāya vuttaṃ  anattamano hotīti avijahitvā ṭhite pana tuyhaṃ jātiyā ca sūrabhāvassa ca anurūpamidanti haṭṭhatuṭṭho uṭṭhāya sīhavijambhanaṃ vijambhitvā kesarabhāraṃ vidhunitvā tikkhattuṃ sīhanādaṃ  naditvā gocarāya pakkamati tena vuttaṃ  attamano hotīti

 pañcame ti ettha kasmā bhagavā agāramajjhe vasitvā kālakatassa rañño thūpakaraṇaṃ anujānāti na sīlavato puthujjanabhikkhussāti anacchariyattā puthujjanabhikkhūnañhi thūpe anuññāyamāne tambapaṇṇidīpe tāva thūpānaṃ okāso na bhaveyya tathā aññesu ṭhānesu tasmā anacchariyā te bhavissantīti nānujānāti cakkavattī rājā ekova nibbattati tenassa thūpo acchariyo hoti puthujjanasīlavato pana parinibbutabhikkhuno viya mahantampi sakkāraṃ kātuṃ vaṭṭatiyeva chaṭṭhasattamāni uttānatthāneva

 aṭṭhame ti anariyānaṃ kathā sesesupi eseva nayo
