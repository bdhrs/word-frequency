4. Yassaṃdisaṃsuttavaṇṇanā
134 . ‘‘ubhato sujāto’’   ‘‘mātito ca pitito cā’’ ‘‘saṃsuddhaggahaṇiko’’ gahaṇī, ‘‘saṃsuddhāya mātukucchiyā samannāgato’’ gahaṇī, yāva sattamā pitāmahayugā, ‘‘tato uddhaṃ sabbepi pubbapurisā pitāmahaggahaṇeneva gahitā’’
Akkhitto Anavakkhitto ‘‘kena kāraṇenā’’
‘‘yo koci attano santakena vibhavena aḍḍho hotī’’ ‘‘mahatā aparimāṇasaṅkhena dhanena samannāgato’’ ‘‘pañcakāmaguṇavasenā’’ koṭṭhāgāraṃ ‘‘dhaññena ca paripuṇṇakoṭṭhāgāro’’ ‘‘atha vā’’  ‘‘catubbidho koso hatthī assā rathā pattī’’ ‘‘tividhaṃ koṭṭhāgāra’’
 ‘‘paṇḍiccena samannāgato’’ veyyattiyaṃ medhāvī ṭhānuppatti, Vaḍḍhiatthe
Yassaṃdisaṃsuttavaṇṇanā niṭṭhitā.
