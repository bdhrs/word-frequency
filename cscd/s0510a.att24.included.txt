

chabbīsatime  vagge paṭhamāpadānaṃ uttānameva
 dutiyāpadāne nti devatā sarīraṃ vijahitvā chaḍḍetvā manussasarīraṃ nimminitvāti attho ti adhikakiriyaṃ puññasambhāraṃ kattukāmo devaro nāma ahaṃ devarājā bhariyāya saha buddhaseṭṭhassa sāsane sādaratāya  imasmiṃ manussaloke  āgatoti attho ti padumuttarassa bhagavato yo nāmena  nāma sāvako ahosi tassa sāvakassa mayā vippasannena cetasā  piṇḍapāto dinnoti attho
 tatiyāpadāne ti ānandaṃ tuṭṭhiṃ jananato ānando nāma paccekabuddhoti attho ti amanussapariggahe kānane mahāaraññe  anupādisesanibbānadhātuyā  antaradhāyi adassanaṃ agamāsīti attho nti ahaṃ devalokā idhāgantvā tassa bhagavato sarīraṃ tattha araññe jhāpesiṃ dahanaṃ akāsinti attho
catutthapañcamāpadānāni uttānāneva
 chaṭṭhāpadāne ti nāmena paṇṇattivasena candano nāma ti paccekasambuddhabhūtato pubbe tassa atrajo putto ahaṃ ti ekaṃ upāhanayugaṃ mayā dinnaṃ nti tena upāhanayugena me mayhaṃ sāvakabodhiṃ tuvaṃ sampajja nipphādehīti attho
 sattamāpadāne ti mañjeṭṭhipupphaṃ haritacaṅkoṭakaṃ gahetvā  vīthiyā paṭipajjiṃ ahaṃ tathā paṭipannova  bhikkhusaṅghena parivutaṃ  samaṇānaṃ bhikkhūnaṃ aggaṃ seṭṭhaṃ sammāsambuddhaṃ addasanti sambandho nti disvā ca pana taṃ pupphaṃ ubhohi hatthehi  ukkhipitvā buddhassa phussassa bhagavato abhiropayiṃ pūjesinti attho
 aṭṭhamāpadāne ti khīrapaṇṇādīni uñchācariyāya āharitvā loṇavirahitāni paṇṇāni pacitvā bhakkhāmi aloṇapaṇṇabhakkho  amhi bhavāmīti attho ti niyamasaññitesu pāṇātipātāveramaṇiādīsu niccapañcasīlesu saṃvuto pihitoti attho ti purebhattakāle anuppatte nti mama samīpaṃ siddhattho bhagavā upagañchi sampāpuṇi nti ahaṃ taṃ aloṇapaṇṇaṃ tassa buddhassa adāsinti attho
navamāpadānaṃ uttānameva
 dasamāpadāne ti sarīrato nikkhantachabbaṇṇaraṃsīhi  jalantaṃ sikhīnaṃ sikhībhagavantaṃ sikhīnaṃ yathā jalamānaaggikkhandhaṃ viya ti aggajanāmakaṃ pupphaṃ gahetvā  sikhissa bhagavato  pūjesinti attho
