ti ayaṃ idāni vuccamānā tassa ācariyassa laddhi ti yena vaṃso namati tena kāyaṃ anāmento tathā namanto hi patitvā cuṇṇavicuṇṇaṃ hoti nti kāyaṃ  vaṃsaṃ vā ti namitaṭṭhānato parabhāgena ākaḍḍhento viya ti yathāvuttaṃ satiṃ sūpaṭṭhitaṃ  katvā onataṃ viya katvā tathā karaṇaṃ pana tathā vātūpatthambhagāhāpanenāti āha  ti tañca satiyā tadatthaṃ upaṭṭhānenāti vuttaṃ ti  antevāsī  ettakaṃ ācariyassa laddhivasena vuttaṃ tiādi sabbaṃ heṭṭhā vuttanayameva
ti idaṃ attano rakkhaṇaṃ padhānaṃ katvā vuttaṃ nti avadhāraṇaphalaṃ attarakkhāya panettha sijjhamānāya antevāsikarakkhāpi siddhā eva hotīti dutiyapakkhepi eseva nayo ti yā attano eva rakkhā sā atthato pararakkhāpi hotīti ayamettha ñāyo yuttappayogo ti yathāvaḍḍhitassa anuanuvaḍḍhiyā etena paṭiladdhasampayuttapamodanākāro dassitoti āha  ti tiādīni padāni anudayatāpariyosānāni (yasmā satipaṭṭhānaṃ sevantassa siddhaṃ attano ca parassa ca rakkhaṇaṃ pakāsenti tasmā)  attānaṃ bhikkhave rakkhissāmīti satipaṭṭhānaṃ sevitabbantiādi vuttaṃ

ti sakalajanapade bhaddā rūpasampattiyā sikkhāsampattiyā ca sundarā  seṭṭhā rūpasampatti ca nāma sabbaso rūpadosābhāvena rūpaguṇapāripūriyā hotīti tadubhayaṃ dassetuṃ ti vuttaṃ taṃ duvidhampi vivaranto tiādimāha ti pañcavidhasarīraguṇasampadāhi samannāgatā ti pamāṇamajjhimā dīghatarappamāṇā na hoti na atirassā lakuṇḍakarūpā na hoti ti ativiya kisathaddhamaṃsalohitā dissamānā aṭṭhisarīrā jālasarīrā na hoti ti bhāriyamaṃsā mahodarā na hoti ti ativiya kāḷavaṇṇā jhāmaṅgāro viya  dadhitakkādīhi pamajjitamattakaṃsalohavaṇṇā na hoti manussaloke tādisiyā rūpasampattiyā abhāvato  yathā pamāṇayuttā evaṃ ārohapariṇāhayogato ca paresaṃ pasādāvahā nātidīghatādayo evaṃ manussānaṃ dibbarūpatāsampattīpīti vuttaṃ nti ettha ca nātidīghanātirassatāvacanena ārohasampatti vuttā ubbedhena pāsādikabhāvato kisathūladosābhāvavacanena pariṇāhasampatti vuttā ubhayenapi saṇṭhānasampadā vibhāvitā nātikāḷatāvacanena vaṇṇasampatti vuttā vivaṇṇatābhāvato ti pariṇatapiyaṅgupupphasadisasarīranibhāsā nti adharoṭṭhamāha  yathāvuttā sarīravaṇṇasampatti ti janapadakalyāṇiyā  chavisampattihetukattā tassā esa nayo sesesupi nakhā eva pattasadisatāya 
( sarīrāvayavena iriyananti āha  ti pasāvo yathāparitameva kanatanti na sabhāvasandhānaṃ yathāvibhāvasena  te vā vīsatiyāsūtiraṃ dhānappattiyā pavattiyā pavattimakatamandatā vibhāvasuṭatassa uttamameva gītañca gāyatīti attho) [etthantare pāṭho asuddho dussodhanīyo ca suddhapāṭho gavesitabbo] ti mukhavattisamaṃ telānaṃ pūritattā samatittikamukhaṃ telabhājanaṃ nti janapadakalyāṇiyā tassā ca naccagītaṃ pekkhituṃ sannipatitamahājanasamūhassa majjhato  netabbo nti telaṃ ti āpajjeyya tatridaṃ opammasaṃsandanaṃ  telapattaṃ viya kāyagatāsati tassa pariharaṇapuggalo viya vipassako janakāyā viya puthuttārammaṇāni asipuriso viya mano telassa cajanaṃ viya kilesuppādanaṃ sīsapātanaṃ viya ariyamaggañāṇasīsānuppatti kāyagatā sati no bhāvitā sikkhitabbanti vuttattā ti vuttaṃ



ti  bahuvacanaṃ anekavidhattā sīlassa tañhi sīlanaṭṭhena ekavidhampi cārittādivasena anekavidhaṃ tenāha  ti ti ñātuṃ icchitassa atthassa vīmaṃsanaṃ tenāha  nti

 mahāvisayattā mahatiyo abhiññā etassāti  tassa bhāvoti sabbaṃ vattabbaṃ nti vatvā tamatthaṃ pākaṭaṃ kātuṃ tiādi vuttaṃ ti therassa nti ekāvajjanasseva gamanavīthiṃ  cakkavāḷānaṃ sahassaṃ ekāvajjaneneva sahassalokadhātuyā icchitamatthaṃ jānituṃ samatthoti dasseti


ti yā tebhūmakavedanā sammasitvā ti tā tebhūmakavedanā eva assa bhikkhuno sadisavasena cetaṃ vuttaṃ aniccādisammasanavasena  pubbabhāge sammasanakāle ti parijānanavasena paricchijja gahitesu  taṇhāpapañcassa  mānapapañcassa  diṭṭhipapañcassa mūlavasena sammasanaṃ vuttāti vadanti vedanāvitakkasaññā taṇhāmānadiṭṭhipapañcānaṃ mūladassanavasenāti apare



ti ārammaṇalakkhitā satipaṭṭhānā kathitā na satilakkhaṇā

 visuṃ visuṃ katvā tathā ca vuttaṃ pāḷiyaṃ kāye vā bhikkhūti

nti padhānakasīlaṃ sīlaggahaṇañhi pāḷiyaṃ pātimokkhasaṃvaravaseneva āgataṃ tenāha tiādi tattha nti avadhāraṇaṃ itaresaṃ tiṇṇaṃ ekadesena pātimokkhantogadhabhāvaṃ dīpeti tathā hi anolokiyolokane ājīvahetu ca chasikkhāpadavītikkamane gilānapaccayassa appaccavekkhitaparibhoge  ca āpatti vihitāti  indriyasaṃvarādīni ti sīlapariyāyena tesaṃ katthaci sutte gahitaṭṭhānaṃ nāma natthīti nippariyāyasīlataṃ tesaṃ  paṭikkhipati ti tassa sallahukatamāha cittādhiṭṭhānamattena paṭipākatikabhāvappattito itaradvayepi eseva nayo nti phalena hetuṃ dasseti uppādanahetukā hi paccayānaṃ uppatti nti idaṃ payojanaṃ imassa paccayassa paribhuñjaneti adhippāyo ti iminā indriyasaṃvarādīni tīṇi padhānasīlassa paripālanaparisodhanavasena pavattiyā pariyāyasīlāni nāmāti dasseti idāni pātimokkhasīlasseva padhānabhāvaṃ byatirekato anvayato ca upamāya vibhāvetuṃ tiādimāha tattha ti pātimokkhasaṃvaro  indriyasaṃvarādīni pātimokkhasaddassa attho pana visuddhimaggasaṃvaṇṇanādīsu vitthārito tasmā tattha vuttanayeneva veditabbo
ti kāyikacetasikaavītikkamasaṅkhātena ācārena ca navesiyādigocaratādisaṅkhātena gocarena ca sampanno sampannaācāragocaroti attho ti atiparittakesu anāpattigamanīyesu dukkaṭadubbhāsitamattesūti apare ti akaraṇīyesu gārayhesu te pana ekantato akusalā hontīti āha  ti ti bhayato dassanasīlo paramāṇumattampi vajjaṃ sineruppamāṇaṃ viya katvā dassanasīlo ti sammadeva sakkaccaṃ sabbaso ca ādiyitvā nti samudāyato avayavaniddhāraṇaṃ dassento ntiādimāha sikkhāpadaṃ samādātabbaṃ sikkhitabbañcāti adhippāyo ti adhisīlasikkhā pubbe -saddo adhiṭṭhānaṭṭho idha bhāgatthoti daṭṭhabbanti āha  ti mūlapaññattianupaññattiādibhedaṃ yaṃkiñci sikkhitabbaṃ pūretabbaṃ sīlaṃ taṃ pana dvāravasena duvidhamevāti āha  ti imasmiṃ atthavikappe ti ādhāre bhummaṃ sikkhābhāgesu kassaci visuṃ aggahaṇato tenāha  nti tato tvaṃ bhikkhu sīlaṃ nissāyāti vacanato anabhijjhā abyāpādasammādiṭṭhiyopi sīlanti vuttā tasmā imasmiṃ sutte nti vuttaṃ

 etthāpi  manosucaritaṃ sīlaṃ nāmāti dassetīti katvā vuttaṃ ti anabhijjhāabyāpādasammādiṭṭhidhammā sīlaṃ hotīti veditabbā kāyavacīsucaritehi saddhiṃ  manosucaritampi vatvā tato tvaṃ bhikkhu sīlaṃ nissāya sīle patiṭṭhāyāti vuttattā  vuttanayameva ti chaṭṭhasattamavaggesu apubbaṃ natthi tena vuttaṃ ti vaggapeyyālato pana imasmiṃ satipaṭṭhānasaṃyutte katipayavaggā saṅgahaṃ ārūḷhā tathāpi tesaṃ atthavisesābhāvato ekaccesu potthakesu mukhamattaṃ dassetvā saṃkhittā ekaccesu atisaṃkhittāva te saṅkhepavasena dve katvā ti vuttaṃ
