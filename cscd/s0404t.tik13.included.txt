

 dasakanipātassa  paṭhame ti avippaṭisārappayojanāni ti avippaṭisārudayāni etena avippaṭisāro nāma sīlasssa udayamattaṃ saṃvaddhitassa rukkhassa chāyāpupphasadisaṃ añño eva panānena nipphādetabbo samādhiādiguṇoti dasseti yāva maggāmaggañāṇadassanavisuddhi tāva taruṇavipassanāti hi vacanato upakkilesavimuttaudayabbayañāṇato paraṃ ayañca vipassanā virajjati yogāvacaro viratto puriso viya bhariyāya saṅkhārato etenāti virāgo


 dutiye  saṃsāramahoghassa paratīrabhāvato yo naṃ adhigacchati taṃ pāreti gametīti  nibbānaṃ tabbidūratāya natthi ettha pāranti  saṃsāro tenāha tiādi tatiyādīsu natthi vattabbaṃ
