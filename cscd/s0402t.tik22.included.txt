

 pañcamassa paṭhame sammukho bhavati yena so sammukhībhāvo purato vijjamānatā tasmā nti dānasaṅkhātaṃ puññakammaṃ  bāhirattā yathāsakaṃ paccayasamavāyena labbhanato  pacurajanassa anavaṭṭhitakiccattā tenevāha tiādi


 dutiye macchariyameva malaṃ macchariyamalaṃ cittassa malīnasabhāvāpādanato vigataṃ macchariyamalaṃ etthāti  gedhābhāvena koci kiñci dentopi tattha āsattiṃ na vissajjeti ayaṃ pana na tādisoti āha ti  cittavisuddhiyā  abhāvato  dhotahatthena kātabbakiccasādhanato tena vuttaṃ  citte suddhe visujjhanti iti vuttaṃ mahesināti  yācakānaṃ manorathapūraṇato  payogāsahehi yācakehi suṭṭhu yuttabhāvato taṃsamaṅgī eva tattha rato nāma na cittamattenevāti āha ti
ti vatvā ayaṃ maṃ uccato vadatīti cinteyyāti puna ti āha sarīrenāti adhippāyo ti nīlobhāsassa ti bhikkhū pucchi ti paṭhamaṃ  mañce nipajjitvā uṭṭhāya bhūmiyaṃ lekhaṃ likhanto acchi akhīṇāsavoti maññanā hotūti tathā hi rājā khīṇāsavassa nāma nivatti ti paggahitadhajāva nti thūpārāmassa ca mahācetiyassa ca antare silāya katacetiyaṭṭhānaṃ  sāvakassa taṃ kūṭāgāranti katvā
ti dhammakathikassa therassa piṭṭhipassena ti maṇḍalasappo dhammassavanantarāya bahūnaṃ saggamaggappaṭilābhantarāyo bhaveyyāti adhippāyena ti cintesi ti vipassanātejena visavegaṃ vikkhambhetvā nti gāthāsahassavantaṃ ti paṭṭhāpanagāthāya ādigāthāyāti attho  na dvinnaṃ antare vuttaṃ kilantakāyattā  sāyanhadhammakathā ti paccakkhaṃ karontī gaṇhāti sakkaccaṃ suṇātīti attho dhammakathanadivase dhammakathikānaṃ akilamanatthaṃ saddhā upāsakā siniddhabhojanaṃ madhupānakañca denti sarassa madhurabhāvāya sappimadhukatelādiñca bhesajjaṃ tenāha tiādi ti daḷhadaṭṭhabhāvadassanaṃ ti sammānessāmi sakkaccaṃ suṇissāmīti attho ti nimmadditvā apanetvāti attho


 tatiye attho nāma phalaṃ taṃ etassa vasoti  hetu attho etassa atthīti attho so evāti āha ti dhammadesanā nāma ukkaṭṭhaniddesena  catunnaṃ ariyasaccānaṃ pakāsanāti āha ti ti pāḷipadānaṃ atthaṃ vivaraṇañāṇena paṭi paṭi saṃvedanasīlo ayaṃ imassa bhāsitassa atthoti etena atthapaṭisambhidābyāpāramāha ti pāḷigatiṃ pāḷiṃ padavivaraṇaṃ paṭi paṭi saṃvedanasīlo etena dhammapaṭisambhidābyāpāramāha


 catutthe ti yāva adhippetatthanigamanā avicchedena pavattinī paṭighātābhāvena  sappāṭihīrakā


 pañcame ti paṇḍitehi paṭhamaṃ paññattāni ti seyyaso kathitāni ti buddhabodhisattehi ti karuṇācetovimutti vuttā ti tassa upacāro  manacchaṭṭhānaṃ indriyānaṃ damananti katvā nti cittadamanaṃ   sakkhissasi kho tvaṃ puṇṇa iminā damūpasamenāti āgatattā  āḷavakasutte  saccā damā cāgāti evaṃ  imasmiṃ sutte damoti vattuṃ vaṭṭati nti mātāpitūnaṃ manussāmanussakatūpaddavato rakkhanaṃ byādhiādianatthato gopanaṃ ghāsacchādanādīhi veyyāvaccakaraṇena paṭijagganaṃ  sabbakilesadarathapariḷāhūpasamena upasantakāyavacīsamācāratāya ca nti matteyyatādīhi seṭṭhaṭṭhena santānaṃ
ti imasmiṃ sutte āgatānaṃ tiṇṇaṃ ṭhānānaṃ karaṇena nibbattanena ti mātupaṭṭhānaṃ pitupaṭṭhānanti etāni dve uttamapurisānaṃ  kāraṇāni uttamakiccakaraṇena hi mātāpituupaṭṭhākā uttamapurisāti vuttā tenāha ti anupaddavabhāvena 


 chaṭṭhe nti ettha manussā puññaṃ pasavantīti avisesena vuttattā bhāvanāmayassapi puññassa saṅgaṇhanato lokuttarassapi sambhavo daṭṭhabbo


 sattame samecca sambhūya paccayehi kataṃ ti sañjānanassa nimittāni hetupaccayasamavāye uppajjanaṃ  attalābho ti bhaṅgo ti uppādakkhaṇato uddhaṃ ṭhitikkhaṇapattassa sā panassa avatthā uppādāvatthāya bhinnāti katvā aññathattaṃ jarāti ca vuttā yasmā dhammo uppajjamāno eva bhijjati tathā sati uppādabhaṅgā samānakkhaṇā siyuṃ na ca taṃ yujjati tasmā uppādāvatthāya bhinnā bhaṅgābhimukhāvatthā jarāti veditabbā ye pana saṅkhārānaṃ ṭhiti natthīti vadanti tesaṃ taṃ micchā yathā hi tasseva dhammassa uppādāvatthāya bhinnā bhaṅgāvatthā icchitā aññathā aññaṃ uppajjati aññaṃ nirujjhatīti āpajjati evaṃ uppajjamānassa bhaṅgābhimukhā dhammā icchitabbā sā ca ṭhitikkhaṇo na hi uppajjamāno bhijjatīti sakkā viññātunti
 paccayasamuppannattā yadi evaṃ maggaphaladhammā kathanti āha tiādi lakkhaṇakathā hi yāvadeva sammasanatthā  na ṭhānabhaṅgakkhaṇesu kasmā uppādauppādakkhaṇānaṃ aññamaññaṃ paricchinnattā yathā hi uppādasaṅkhātena vikārena uppādakkhaṇo paricchinno evaṃ uppādakkhaṇenapi uppādo paricchinno sesadvayepi eseva nayo dhammappavattimattatāyapi kālassa lokasamaññāvaseneva vuttaṃ nti nesaṃ bhedadassanaṃ avatthāvato hi avatthā bhinnāvāti  nti ettha uppādavayehi tāva saṅkhataṃ paricchinnaṃ hotu jarāya pana taṃ kathaṃ paricchinnanti vuccati na vuccati paricchedo pubbantāparantamattena atha kho sabhāvabhedenāti nāyaṃ doso  dhammajātaṃ  tabbantaṃ dhammajātaṃ  saṅkhatanti paññāyati evaṃ tesaṃ abhāvena nibbānametanti lakkhitabbato sañjānitabbato idāni tiādinā yathāvuttamattaṃ upamāhi vibhāveti


 aṭṭhame ti vuttanayena na saṅkhatassa tenāha tiādi yathā uppādādīnaṃ bhāvena saṅkhatadhammajātaṃ saṅkhatanti paññāyati evaṃ tesaṃ abhāvena nibbānaṃ asaṅkhatanti paññāyati


 navame idha -saddo rukkhasāmaññapariyāyo na rukkhavisesapariyāyoti āha ti nti tasmiṃ kule jeṭṭhabhūtaṃ sāmibhūtaṃ  na paṃsumayo missako ca gāmaṃ gāmūpacārañca ṭhapetvā sabbaṃ araññanti āha ti mahanto pabbato seloti yojanā


 dasame nti sarīre sambhūtānaṃ nti aniṭṭhānaṃ nti nirantarappavattiyā aviraḷānaṃ ti dukkhāpanavasena nti kurūrānaṃ tāsaṃ yathāvuttānaṃ adhivāsanāya pahātabbadukkhavedanānaṃ pajahanaṃ nāma khamanamevāti āha ti  ti ātappaṃ karaṇīyanti buddhāṇaṃ vidhāya  sasambhārānaṃ maggadhammānaṃ kathitattā


 ekādasame  mahābalavacoro ti āha ti ti ca mahāthāmatāya mahāparivāratāya mahācoriyakammasamatthatāya ca veditabbo mahataṃ gāmanigamānaṃ viluppanaṃ ti taṃ taṃ akaraṇameva kāraṇaṃ katvā tappaṭibaddhāya kathāya pakkhipitvā ti tassa tassa atthañca kathayissanti ti apanentā pariharantā ti sassato lokoti  ādidasavatthusannissitāya ti tameva sassatādiantaṃ gahetvā avissajjetvā ṭhitadiṭṭhiyā
