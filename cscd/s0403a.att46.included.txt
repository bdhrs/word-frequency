

 aṭṭhamassa paṭhame ti āpattiṃyeva āpattīti jānāti sesapadesupi eseva nayo

 dutiye ti suāgatāni suppaguṇāni ti koṭṭhāsato suṭṭhu vibhattāni  ti āvajjitāvajjitaṭṭhāne suṭṭhu pavattāni daḷhappaguṇāni ti suṭṭhu vinicchitāni ti vibhaṅgato ti khandhakaparivārato

 tatiye ti vinayalakkhaṇe patiṭṭhito hoti ti na sakkā hoti gahitaggahaṇaṃ vissajjāpetuṃ

 navame ti adutiyo ti kāyena gaṇato cittena kilesehi vūpakaṭṭho vivekaṭṭho dūrībhūto ti  satiavippavāse ṭhito ti pesitatto ti vaṭṭe ukkaṇṭhanatthāya ti rāgādīnaṃ virajjanatthāya ti appavattikaraṇatthāya ti kilesavūpasamāya appavattiyā ti tilakkhaṇaṃ āropetvā abhijānanatthāya ti maggasaṅkhātassa sambodhassa atthāya ti nibbānassa sacchikaraṇatthāya

 dasame adhikaraṇāni samenti vūpasamentīti nti uppannānaṃ uppannānaṃ nti vivādādhikaraṇaṃ anuvādādhikaraṇaṃ āpattādhikaraṇaṃ kiccādhikaraṇanti imesaṃ catunnaṃ ti samathatthañceva vūpasamanatthañca ti ime satta samathā dātabbā tesaṃ vinicchayo vinayasaṃvaṇṇanato (cūḷava aṭṭha 186-187 ādayo) gahetabbo apica dīghanikāye saṅgītisuttavaṇṇanāyampi  (dī ni aṭṭha 3331 adhikaraṇasamathasattakavaṇṇanā) vitthāritoyeva tathā majjhimanikāye sāmagāmasuttavaṇṇanāyāti (ma ni aṭṭha 346)

ito  parāni satta suttāni uttānatthāneva na hettha kiñci heṭṭhā avuttanayaṃ nāma atthīti
