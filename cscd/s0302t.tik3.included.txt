


 upasaggo  samāsavisaye sasādhanaṃ kiriyaṃ dassetīti vuttaṃ ti nti nidassanamattametaṃ tato bhiyyopi anugantvā anamataggo eva saṃsāro saddo idha mariyādavacano anuddesikañcetaṃ vacananti āha ti aññathā antimabhavikaparicchinnakatavimuttiparipācanīyadhammādīnaṃ vasena aparicchinnapubbāparakoṭi na sakkā vattuṃ saṃsaraṇaṃ  andhabālānaṃ vasenāti adhippāyo tenāha bhagavā dīgho bālāna saṃsāroti   pubbāparakoṭīnaṃ alabbhanīyattā  yathābhūtāvabodhābhāvato ti sāsaneti attho  yathābhūtāvabodhisambhavato atthassa vipulatāya taṃsadisā upamā natthīti parittaṃyeva upamaṃ āharantīti adhippāyo idāni vuttamevatthaṃ tiādinā samattheti ti mātu mātāmahiyo ti dukkhasseva nti dukkhapariyāyoti


nti avisesena anavasesapariyādāyinīti āha nti parikappavacanañcetaṃ


 kandanaṃ  sasaddaṃ rodanaṃ pana kevalamevāti āha nti nti sandanavasena pavattaṃ sinerurasmīhi paricchinnesūti saṅkhepena vuttamatthaṃ vivaranto tiādimāha nti indanīlamaṇimayaṃ sinerussa pubbadakkhiṇakoṇasamapadesā pubbadakkhiṇapassāti adhippetā tehi nikkhantarajatarasmiyo indanīlarasmiyo ca ti tāsaṃ catūhi koṇehi nikkhantarasmīnaṃ catūsu antaresu ti dakkhiṇādibhedā cattāro mahāsamuddā honti nti visesena khepanaṃ kiṃ pana tanti āha ti


nti pītaṃ mātuyā thanato nibbattakhīraṃ bahutaranti veditabbaṃ


 anamataggassa saṃsārassa dīghatamattā na sukaraṃ nasukaranti aṭṭhakathāpāṭho ti kathaṃ na pariyosāpeti kāyacipi gahaṇatāyāti adhippāyo ti tayo ekakappāsaṃsū yehi naṃ phuṭṭhaṃ tatopi sukhumataraṃ  khīyeyya pabbataṃ sabbabhāgehi aticiravelaṃ parimajjante


nti  nagarasaṅkhepena pākārena parikkhittataṃ sandhāya vuttaṃ anto pana sabbaso vicittasāsapehi eva puṇṇaṃ evaṃ cuṇṇikābaddhaṃ tenāha nti


ti bhikkhuno anussaritvā ṭhitaṭṭhānato tena anussaritassa satasahassakappassa anantarakappato paṭṭhāyāti attho nti vuttappakārena  bhikkhū abhiññālābhino  divase divase nti parikappanavasena vadanti


ti etasmiṃ pabhavasamuddapadesaparicchinne āyāmato pañcayojanasatike atirekayojanasatike vā ṭhāne


 navame ti punappunaṃ khitto ekavārañhi khitto mūlādīsu ekeneva nipateyya tathā sati adhippeto pātassa aniyamo na nidassito siyā tattha ca dhammaṃ suṇantā bhikkhū manussaloke te sandhāya ti āha tadaññaṃ sandhāya nti tassa tassa vā puggalassa yathādhippeto ayaṃ loko tadañño paraloko


ti  samena ākārena laddhabbaaṭṭhikālo ti pañcahi girīhi parikkhittattā giriparikkhepoti laddhanāme rājagahe




nti  kicchajīvikattā sabbathā dukkhaṃ gataṃ upagataṃ tathābhūto pana daliddo varāko nāma hotīti vuttaṃ nti ti nidassanamattaṃ aññehipi sarīrāvayavehi dussaṇṭhānehi upeto durupeto evāti


nti sañjātasukhaṃ tenāha ntiādi nti sukhumupakaraṇehi sabbathā sajjitaṃ


 na araññavāsādimattena  sabbaso saṃyojanānaṃ appahīnattā na puthujjanabhāvato ti etena mahiṃsādīnaṃ rassadīghapiṅgalādīsu ekekāneva gahetvā dasseti


ti purisānañhi mātugāmakālo mātugāmānañca purisakāloti yathā sattasantāne liṅganiyamo natthi evaṃ kadāci imasmiṃ cakkavāḷe nibbattanti kadāci aññatarasminti cakkavāḷaniyamopi natthi evameva ṭhite
cakkavāḷe  mātugāmakāle namātābhūtapubbo natthītiādinā liṅganiyamena cakkavāḷaniyamo ca veditabbo tenāha tiādi


 evaṃ saṃvegaṃ janetvā bhikkhū visesaṃ pāpessāmīti  catuyojanubbedhattā nti kakusandhassa koṇāgamanassa cāti imesaṃ dvinnaṃ buddhānaṃ tivarā rohitassā suppiyāti manussānaṃ tasmiṃ tasmiṃ kāle samaññā tattha desanāmavasena jātāti veditabbā yathā etarahi māgadhāti
nti paṭhamavassasatato uparivassasataṃ jīvanako nāma manusso natthi  desanāya ti dasavassāyukabhāvato paṭṭhāya yāva asaṅkhyeyyāyukabhāvā vaḍḍhitvā parihīnanti vatvā taṃ parihīnabhāvaṃ dassento ntiādimāha ti yattakaṃ āyuppamāṇesūti
