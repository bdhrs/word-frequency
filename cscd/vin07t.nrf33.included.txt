

 saṅghabhedakakkhandhake  pāḷiyaṃ ti anupiyā nāma ti pāsādato heṭṭhā heṭṭhimatalaṃ heṭṭhāpāsādantipi pāṭho nti vapitakhettesu pavesetabbaṃ nti tato nīharitabbaṃ nti sassadūsakatiṇādīni uddharitabbaṃ nti puñjaṃ kārāpetabbaṃ ayameva vā pāṭho
ti parehi dinnapaccayehi pavattamāno ti katthaci alaggatāya migassa viya jātena cittena
nti jhānamanena nibbattaṃ brahmakāyaṃ kiṃ nu kho ahaṃ pasādeyyaṃ yasmiṃ me pasanne bahulābhasakkāro uppajjeyyāti paṭhamaṃ uppannaparivitakkassa mandapariyuṭṭhānatāya devadattassa tasmiṃ khaṇe jhānaparihāni nāhosi pacchā eva ahosīti daṭṭhabbaṃ tenāha tiādi ti ettha magadharaṭṭhe khuddakaṃ gāmakhettaṃ gāvutamattaṃ majjhimaṃ pana diyaḍḍhagāvutamattaṃ mahantaṃ anekayojanampi hoti tesu majjhimena gāmakhettena dve vā khuddakena tīṇi vā gāmakhettāni tassa sarīraṃ tigāvutappamāṇo attabhāvoti vuttaṃ hoti
ti gaṇasatthāro ti na etassa bhaveyya nti satthāraṃ ti amanāpena ti cīvarādinā amhākaṃ sammānaṃ karoti parehi vā ayaṃ satthā sammānīyatīti attho
nti acchapittaṃ vā macchapittaṃ vā nāsāpuṭe pakkhipeyyuṃ ti vaḷavāya kucchismiṃ gadrabhassa jātā tassā hi gahitagabbhāya vijāyitumasakkontiyā udaraṃ phāletvā potakaṃ nīharanti tenāha ti
nti churikaṃ kharantipi vuccati
ti he kuñjara buddhanāgaṃ vadhakacittena mā upagaccha nti dukkhakāraṇattā dukkhaṃ ti ito jātito ti yasmā yantassa vā gacchantassāti attho ti mado tayā na kātabboti attho
nti tīhi bhuñjitabbaṃ bhojanaṃ tato adhikehi ekato paṭiggahetvā bhuñjituṃ na vaṭṭanakaṃ gaṇabhojanapaṭipakkhaṃ bhojananti attho tiādīni devadattaparisāya gaṇapāmokkhānaṃ nāmāni nti mahāniraye āyukappaṃ taṃ antarakappanti keci keci pana asaṅkhyeyyakappanti


 parassa cittaṃ ñatvā kathanaṃ  kevalaṃ dhammadesanā  tadubhayampi  nāma tāya thero ovadi iddhividhaṃ  nāma tena sahitā anusāsanī eva  tāya thero ovadi
ti idaṃ bhedapurekkhārassa uposathādikaraṇe thullaccayassa uposathakkhandhakādīsu paṭhamameva paññattattā vuttaṃ itarathā etesaṃ ādikammikattā anāpattiyeva siyā
ti saro ti mahiṃ dantehi vilikhantassa idañca hatthīnaṃ sabhāvadassanaṃ ti saresu bhisaṃ ghasamānassāti yojanā ti yūthaṃ pālentassa
nti dūtakammaṃ pattuṃ dūtakammaṃ kātunti attho ti yuttāyuttassa yaṃ vattuṃ kātuñca yuttaṃ tattha kusalo atha vā adhippetānādhippetassa vacanassa kusalo byañjanamatte na tiṭṭhati adhippetatthameva ārocetīti attho
 gāthāsu ti ekaṃsena  mā hotūti attho ti pāpicchānaṃ puggalānaṃ yādisī gati abhisamparāyo  atthajātaṃ  kāraṇena ti devadattassa paṇḍitotiādinā upari vakkhamānākāraṃ dasseti
ti pamādaṃ āpanno nti avassaṃbhāvīatthasiddhiyaṃ sā hi idha āsīsāti adhippetā na patthanā īdise anāgatatthe atītavacanaṃ saddavidū icchanti
ti dubbheyya ti ekena visapuṇṇakumbhena ti so puggalo  visamissaṃ kātuṃ na sakkotīti attho ti vipulagambhīrabhāvena bhayānako tenāpi dūsetuṃ na sakkuṇeyyataṃ dasseti ti dosakathanena ti bādhati


ti salākaggāhāpanamattena saṅghabhedānibbattito vuttaṃ uposathādisaṅghakamme kate eva hi saṅgho bhinno hoti tattha ca uposathapavāraṇāsu ñattiniṭṭhānena sesakammesu apalokanādikammapariyosānena saṅghabhedo samatthoti daṭṭhabbo
ti idaṃ bhikkhave devadattena paṭhamaṃ ānantariyakammaṃ upacitantiādinā ānantariyattaṃ vadatā bhagavatā tassa abhabbatāsaṅkhātā pārājikatā na paññattā etena āpatti viya abhabbatāpi paññattianantarameva hoti na tato pureti dasseti idha pana ādikammikassapi anāpattiyā avuttattā devadattādayopi na muttāti daṭṭhabbaṃ
tiādīsu tayo eva satipaṭṭhānā na tato paranti ekassa satipaṭṭhānassa  paṭikkhepova idha  na pana tiṇṇaṃ satipaṭṭhānattavidhānaṃ  tassa dhammattā evaṃ sesesupi hāpanakoṭṭhāsesu vaḍḍhanesu pana ti anindriyassapi ekassa indriyattavidhānameva  evaṃ sesesupi na kevalañca eteva cattāro khandhā terasāyatanānītiādinā yattha katthaci viparītato pakāsanaṃ sabbaṃ  yāthāvato pakāsanañca sabbaṃ ti daṭṭhabbaṃ nti cettha tathā tathā kāyavacīpayogasamuṭṭhāpikā arūpakkhandhāva adhippetā evamettha dasakusalakammapathādīsu anavajjaṭṭhena sarūpato dhammesu akusalakammapathādīsu sāvajjaṭṭhena sarūpato adhammesu ca tadaññesu ca abyākatesu yassa kassaci koṭṭhāsassa bhagavatā paññattakkameneva pakāsanaṃ dhammoti ca viparītato pakāsanaṃ adhammoti ca dassitanti daṭṭhabbaṃ kāmañcettha vinayādayopi yathābhūtato ayathābhūtato ca pakāsanavasena dhammādhammesu eva pavisanti vinayādināmena pana visesetvā visuṃ gahitattā tadavasesameva dhammādhammakoṭṭhāse pavisatīti daṭṭhabbaṃ
tiādi dhammañca adhammañca yāthāvato ñatvāva pāpicchaṃ nissāya viparītato pakāsentasseva saṅghabhedo hoti na pana tathāsaññāya pakāsentassāti dassanatthaṃ vuttaṃ esa nayo avinayaṃ vinayoti dīpentītiādīsupi tattha nti ukkaṭṭhanti attho ti iminā evaṃ amhākaṃ ācariyakulantiādinā vuttamatthaṃ ākaḍḍhati
ti saṃvaravinayo pahānavinayo paṭisaṅkhāvinayo ca vutto tenāha ti paññattaṃ apaññattanti dukaṃ bhāsitaṃ abhāsitanti dukena atthato samānameva tathā duṭṭhulladukaṃ garukadukena teneva tesaṃ tiādinā sadisaniddeso kato nti avasesasīlehi sahitāpattiṃ natthi etissaṃ āpannāyaṃ sīlāvasesāti 
 pāḷiyaṃ ti yathā samaggānaṃ sāmaggī na bhijjati evaṃ anuggahaṇaṃ anubalappadānaṃ
ti sambhaveyya nu kho ti attano adhammaṃ dhammoti etasmiṃ dīpane ayuttadiṭṭhi ti adhammaṃ dhammoti dīpetvā anussāvanasalākaggāhāpanādinā attānaṃ muñcitvā catuvaggādikaṃ saṅghaṃ ekasīmāyameva ṭhitato  catuvaggādisaṅghato viyojetvā ekakammādinipphādanavasena saṅghabhedakaraṇe adhammadiṭṭhiko hutvāti attho nti yā tasmiṃ adhammaṃ dhammoti dīpane attano adhammadiṭṭhi uppajjati taṃ vinidhāya paṭicchādetvā dhammo evāyanti viparītato pakāsetvāti attho evaṃ sabbattha attho veditabbo
ti yathāvuttanayena saṅghabhedane doso natthīti laddhiko ayaṃ pana adhammaṃ dhammoti dīpane adhammadiṭṭhiko hutvāpi taṃ diṭṭhiṃ vinidhāya karaṇena saṅghabhedako atekiccho jāto ti imassa pana bhede vematikadiṭṭhiyā vinidhānampi atthi sesaṃ samameva ti ayaṃ pana bhede adhammadiṭṭhiṃ vinidhāya katattā saṅghabhedako atekiccho jāto sukkapakkhe pana sabbattha adhammaṃ dhammotiādidīpane vā bhede vā dhammadiṭṭhitāya diṭṭhiṃ avinidhāyeva katattā saṅghabhedakopi satekiccho jāto tasmā adhammaṃ dhammotiādidīpane vā saṅghabhede vā ubhosupi vā adhammadiṭṭhi vā vematiko vā hutvā taṃ diṭṭhiṃ vimatiñca vinidhāya dhammoti pakāsetvā vuttanayena saṅghabhedaṃ karontasseva ānantariyaṃ hotīti veditabbaṃ
