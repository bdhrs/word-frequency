
1 veḷudvāravaggo

ti anuggaṇhanagarahatthajotako nipāto kimettha anuggaṇhāti kiṃ vā garahatīti āha ntiādi tattha  anucchavikaṃ katvā gaṇhanto  nindanto issarasīlo issaro tassa bhāvo  pabhutā adhīnaṃ pati adhipati tassa bhāvo  sāmibhāvoti āha  ntiādi ti antarahitāni
ti vatthuttayassa guṇe yāthāvato avecca pavisitvā pasādo so pana kenaci calanarahitoti āha ti ti ariyamaggena  tassa adhigamena laddhappasādo nti ekajjhaṃ ariyasāvakānañhi ariyamaggo uppajjantova tīsu vatthūsu aveccappasādaṃ āvahanto eva uppajjati nti visayabhūtānaṃ tiṇṇaṃ vatthūnaṃ vasena tidhā vutto  atthato  pavattaṭṭhānabhede satipi tiādinā nayena tamatthaṃ vivarati  okappanā  bhatti  garukaraṇaṃ  uḷāraṃ  vibhāgena  sabbattha samānattā
nti bhavantarepi akopanīyatāya ti aññehi akhaṇḍādīhi sadisavasena tenāha tiādi khaṇḍā etissā atthīti  esa nayo sesesupi pātimokkhe āgatānukkamena sīlassa ādimajjhavibhāgo veditabbo ti sīlakoṭṭhāsānaṃ nti abhinnena ekantaraṃ hutvā bhinnaṃ  khaṇḍādīnaṃ bhujissehīti uttarapadalopena niddesoti āha ti nti idampi sīlaṃ vītikkantaṃ ayaṃ sīlassa vītikkamoti 

nti  aniyamato saddhādīnamādhārabhūtapuggaladassanaṃ  so hi itarehi paṭhamaṃ gahetabboti  sotāpannassa sīlānaṃ adhippetattā  dhammappasādassa anantaraṃ vuccamānattā  aveccappasādabhāvato sotaṃ ariyamaggaṃ ādito patti sotāpatti tassā aṅgāni ti pajāyanti adhigacchanti tenāha ti nti maggabrahmacariyaṃ  vattamānoti adhippāyo ti maggasampayutto pasādo ti adhigatamaggassa ti tasmā ubhopi therā paṇḍitā bahussutā

 yadaggena tasmiṃ upāsake yathāvuttāni sotāpattiyaṅgāni saṃvijjanti tadaggena so tesu vattissatīti āha ti vijjaṃ bhajantīti  tesu koṭṭhāsesu pariyāpannāti vuttaṃ ti

 sotāpattīti paṭhamamaggo adhippeto tassa adhigamūpāyo  tenāha nti ti sotāpattimaggatthāya nti kāraṇaṃ  ratanattayappasādādayo pubbabhāgiyāya  kāraṇanti

 (vitānaṃ kāyānaṃ kālanakāya kāraṇassa upagatānaṃ sātapariyā sijjhati tattha  kāyaparipajjhāyamukhena tantiṃ ṭhapesi bhagavā na hi passatha namatthehi karaṇe niratthako piti vattati ti vuttaṃ tameva niyamaṃ ti avadhāraṇena vibhāveti  mahāmaṇḍalacārikaṃ carantopi majjhimapadesassa antanteneva carati tattha ca vineyyajanassa samosaraṇatā mattapanavācamahatthasupananti) [etthantare pāṭho asuddho dussodhanīyo ca suddhapāṭho gavesitabbo] aruṇuṭṭhāpanampi tattheva hoti paccantapadese pana dūre vineyyajanā honti tattha gantvā maggaphalesu patiṭṭhāpetvā tato paccāgantvā majjhimapadese eva vāsaṃ upagacchati tattha manussehi katānaṃ kārānaṃ mahapphalabhāvaniyamanatthanti daṭṭhabbaṃ āsanne no bhagavāti idaṃ nidassanamattanti dassento nti ādimāha
ti ettha kiñcanaṃ palibodho asamāpitakiccatā tadubhayassa atthibhāvenāti attho ti mahāgehe
ti isidattapurāṇā  nāma mandahasitaṃ  nāma vissaṭṭhahasitaṃ tiādīsu yaṃ vattabbaṃ taṃ  (visuddhi mahāṭī 1160) vuttanayena veditabbaṃ ti parassa dānavasena saṃvibhajane nti deyyadhammavasena na katavibhāgaṃ puggalavasena pana sīlavantehīti vuttattā katavibhāgameva mahapphalatākaraṇena tenāha nti
(etthantare pāṭho asuddho dussodhanīyo ca suddhapāṭho gavesitabbo)

ti anekapurisayugavasena paramparāgatassa nti attani netvā parasmiṃ upanetabbaṃ tenāha tiādi ti samphassa samphappalāpassa amantāya mantārahitabhāsanena

 dvinnaṃ ñātīnaṃ gāmāti katvā ti evaṃladdhanāme ekasmiṃ  gāmake ti giñjakā vuccanti iṭṭhakā giñjakāhi eva kato āvasatho tasmiṃ so kira āvāso yathā  sudhāhi parikammena payojanaṃ natthi evaṃ iṭṭhakāhi eva cinitvā kato tena vuttaṃ ti tulāthambhadvārabandhakavāṭaphalakāni pana dārumayā eva oraṃ vuccati kāmadhātu paccayabhāvena taṃ oraṃ bhajantīti  orambhāgassa vā hitāni  tenāha ntiādi ti heṭṭhimehi tīhi maggehi tehi pahātabbatāya hi tesaṃ saññojanānaṃ orambhāgiyatā oraṃ bhañjiyāni vā  vuttāni niruttinayena idāni byatirekamukhena nesaṃ orambhāgiyabhāvaṃ vibhāvetuṃ tiādi vuttaṃ vikkhambhitāni samatthatāvighātena puthujjanānaṃ samucchinnāni sabbaso abhāvena ariyānaṃ rūpārūpabhavūpapattiyā vibandhanāya na hontīti vuttaṃ ti ti paṭisandhiggahaṇavasena gantuṃ na denti mahaggatabhavagāmikammāyūhanassa vibandhanato  saññojanāni kāmacchandabyāpādā viya mahaggatabhavūpapattiyā visesapaccayattā  mahaggatabhave  tannibbattihetukammaparikkhaye kāmabhavūpapattipaccayatāya mahaggatabhavato  kāmabhave eva  tasmā  pañcapi saṃyojanāni ti paṭisandhiggahaṇavasena tasmā lokā idha na āgamanasabhāvo buddhadassana-theradassana-dhammassavanānaṃ pana atthāya assa āgamanaṃ anivāritaṃ
kadāci uppattiyā viraḷākāratā pariyuṭṭhānamandatāya abahalatāti nti bahuso ti tibbatibbā yattha uppajjanti taṃ santānaṃ maddantā pharantā sādhentā andhakāraṃ karontā uppajjanti dvīhi pana maggehi pahīnattā tanukatanukā mandamandā uppajjanti ti idaṃ akāraṇaṃ tathā hi aṅgapaccaṅgaparāmasanamattenapi te honti nti rāgadosamohānaṃ tanuttāti idaṃ vacanaṃ ti appakabhavavasena nti mahāsīvattherassa vacanaṃ paṭikkhittanti sambandho ye bhavā ariyānaṃ labbhanti te paripuṇṇalakkhaṇabhavā eva ye na labbhanti tattha kīdisaṃ taṃ bhavatanukaṃ tasmā ubhayathāpi bhavatanukassa asambhavo evāti dassetuṃ tiādi vuttaṃ  aṭṭhamasseva bhavassa sabbasseva abhāvato sesesupi eseva nayo
 itarassa lokassa vasena tathā vattuṃ asakkuṇeyyattā yo hi  sakadāgāmī devamanussalokesu vomissakavasena nibbattati sopi kāmabhavavaseneva paricchinditabbo bhagavatā ca kāmaloke ṭhatvā  sakideva imaṃ lokaṃ āgantvāti vuttaṃ ti ca iminā pañcasu sakadāgāmīsu cattāro vajjetvā ekova gahito ekacco hi idha sakadāgāmiphalaṃ patvā idheva parinibbāyati ekacco idha patvā devaloke parinibbāyati ekacco devaloke patvā tattheva parinibbāyati ekacco devaloke patvā idhūpapajjitvā parinibbāyati ime cattāro idha na labbhanti yo pana idha patvā devaloke yāvatāyukaṃ vasitvā puna idhūpapajjitvā parinibbāyati ayamidha adhippeto aṭṭhakathāyaṃ pana imaṃ lokanti kāmabhavo adhippetoti imamatthaṃ vibhāvetuṃ tiādinā aññaṃyeva catukkaṃ dassitaṃ
 apāyagamanīyānaṃ pāpadhammānaṃ sabbaso pahīnattā ti maggadhammaniyāmena  uparimaggādhigamassa avassaṃbhāvibhāvato tenāha sambodhiparāyaṇoti nti tesaṃ tesaṃ sattānaṃ asuko sotāpanno asuko sakadāgāmītiādinā taṃtaṃñāṇādhigamanaṃ ñāṇūpapattiṃ nti tato parampi  niyato sambodhiparāyaṇo sakideva imaṃ lokaṃ āgantvā dukkhassantaṃ karissatītiādinā ñāṇasahitaṃ upapattipaccayabhavaṃ  ñāṇacakkhunā apekkhantassa kevalaṃ  na tena kāci paresaṃ atthasiddhīti adhippāyo  cittakhedo sā kilesūpasaṃhitattā 
ādissati ālokīyati attā etenāti ādāsaṃ dhammabhūtaṃ ādāsaṃ  ariyamaggañāṇassetaṃ adhivacanaṃ tena hi ariyasāvako catūsu ariyasaccesu viddhastasammohattā attānaṃ yāthāvato ñatvā yāthāvato byākareyya tappakāsanato pana dhammapariyāyassa suttassa dhammādāsatā veditabbāti ti idha pana maggadhammameva vadati sesaṃ uttānatthattā suviññeyyamevāti

2 rājakārāmavaggo

 dhammikattā  desavāsīhi anukampito rājāti rājako tassa ārāmo  tasmiṃ  seṭṭhappadeso yattha vasanto lābhaggayasaggappatto hotīti tesaṃ adhippāyo
ti paṭisedhehi nti kalahaṃ kārāpetuṃ ti aparasmiṃ saṃvacchare āgacchantā ti mahatī vīciyo ubbaṭṭetvā yathā tassa sakalameva raṭṭhaṃ ekodakībhūtaṃ hoti evaṃ katvā samuddameva jātaṃ tena vuttaṃ ti
ti isīnaṃ kāraṇaṃ katvā isīnaṃ hetūti attho isīnaṃ vā antarabhedaṃ katvā tathā loke kolāhalassa patthaṭataṃ vibhāvento bhagavā nti āha paccakkhato jānampi ti kulacchedena ucchinno na kevalaṃ sayameva atha kho nti vināsaṃ upagato
nti taṃ yathāvuttaṃ pasenadinā kosalarājena kāritaṃ vihāraṃ sandhāya etaṃ rājakārāmeti vuttaṃ

nti ārambhato paṭṭhāya sampattiāvahaṃ

 duṭṭhā gati nipphatti duggati duggatabhāvo dāliddiyaṃ tadeva bhayaṃ taṃ sabbaṃ anavasesaṃ vā  daliddabhayaṃ sammadeva atikkantoti 

ti  taṃtaṃdānaggahaṇavasena vohārakā mittā ti ālāpasallāpagamananisajjādiatthasaṃvidhānādīsu  ti saha pavattakattabbā amā saha bhavantīti  amhākaṃ imeti ñāyantīti  āvāhavivāhasambaddhā tenāha ti yonisambandhā vā  tenāha ti

 kismiñci kassaci ca tathā tathā uppannassa pasādassa aññathābhāvo  bhūtasaṅghātassa ghanaādikassa aññathābhāvo  nirayādigatiantaraupapatti  sabhāvadhammānaṃ kakkhaḷaphusanādilakkhaṇassa aññathābhāvo  ṭhitassa aññathattaṃ paññāyatīti evaṃ vuttaṃ aññathattaṃ  lakkhaṇaññathattaṃ na labbhati tenāha ti sesaṃ labbhatīti ti sasambhārapathavīdhātuyā ti etthāpi eseva nayo ti ghanakaṭhinabhāvo  rasaññathattasabhāvo  uggatūpapatti tenāha ti  ariyasāvakassa

3 saraṇānivaggo

nti sampuṇṇaṃ nti upasobhitatāya supupphitasadisattā byūhā nāma yehi eva pavisanti tehi eva nikkhamanti tenāha ti ti yaṭṭhantarā

ti  ratanattaye uppannehi tīhi pasādadhammehi ti tehi eva saddhiṃ sīlena tiādi parikappavasena vuttaṃ bhagavati  attano saddhāya uḷāratamabhāvadassanatthaṃ tenāha tiādi ti vivādadhammauppattihetu tadeva hi sandhāyāha nti nti nānākāraṇaṃ nti akalyāṇaṃ akusalaṃ tadidaṃ yathāvuttaappasādanena apanītaatthadassanatthaṃ ti mahānāmasakkassa ti catūsu dhammesu ekenapi samannāgato sotāpanno hotīti anujānitvā catūhipi samannāgatena ācikkhitabbanti yāthāvato anavajjanadosoti attho

ti ekena pamāṇena na sabbaso ti dassanamaggena catusaccadhammā paccattaṃ passitabbā paṭivijjhitabbā paṭhamamaggakkhaṇe hi catusaccadhammā ekadesatova diṭṭhā nāma honti  paṭivijjhanakiriyāya vattamānattā  apāyesu anuppattirahattā tenāha ti na uppajjatīti attho   yo koci viññujātiko mama ce gocaraṃ gacchati ekassa āgamanaṃ avañjhaṃ amoghanti dassetuṃ

 nirojakaṃ tāya eva dukkhettatāya visamaṃ hotīti āha nti nti jātasabhāvena loṇena ūsarena upahataṃ ti khaṇḍitāni ti temitattā ti cirakālaṃ vātena ceva ātapena ca upahatāni ābādhitāni

nti ṭhānaso tenāha ti niyyānikanti pavattaṃ ñāṇapaṭirūpakaṃ  pakatipurisantarajānanādimicchāñāṇaṃ taṃ pana aniyyānikaṃ niyyānikanti paccavekkhaṇavasena pavatteyyāti āha ti guṇaviyuttassa attano sakattani avaṭṭhānasaṅkhātā vimutti 

 yathākammaṃ  samparetabbato samparāyo peccabhavo samparāyahetukaṃ  maraṇabhayaṃ

4 puññābhisandavaggo

 avicchedena niccappavattiyamānāni puññāni abhisandanaṭṭhena ti vuttā tena puññanadiyoti attho vutto sukhassa āharaṇato ānayanato 

nti visuddhidevānaṃ ti tesaṃ padāni devapadāni devoti vā sammāsambuddho ti paṭivedhañāṇena ceva desanāñāṇena ca akkantapadāni  jātidevā tesampi devaṭṭhena devoti devadevo sambuddho

 saṃsāramahoghassa paratīrabhāvato tenāha  tiṇṇo pāraṅgato thale tiṭṭhati brāhmaṇo (saṃ ni 4238 itivu 69 pu pa 188) ye  janā pāragāminoti (dha pa 85) ca atha vā pāti rakkhatīti  nibbānaṃ yo paṭivijjhati taṃ vaṭṭadukkhato pāti rakkhati accantahitena ca vimuttisukhena ca rameti tasmā nti vuccati ti pāraṃ nibbānaṃ gacchamānā eva te dhammā  sacchikiriyāpahānapaṭivedhānaṃ samakālattā

ti  pavivekasukhatthāya ti bahiddhā nānārammaṇato cittaṃ paṭinivattetvā kammaṭṭhāne sammadeva līnatthāya

5 sagāthakapuññābhisandavaggo

 heṭṭhā mahāpathaviyā upari ākāsena parito cakkavāḷapabbatena majjhe tattha tattha ṭhitehi dīpapabbatapariyantehi paricchinnattā jānantena yojanato saṅkhātuṃ sakkāti adhippāyo mahāsarīramaccha-kumbhīla-yakkha-rakkhasa-mahānāgadānavādīnaṃ saviññāṇakānaṃ baḷavāmukhapātālādīnaṃ aviññāṇakānaṃ bheravārammaṇānaṃ vasena 

ti sambhedaṃ samodhānaṃ gataṭṭhāne ti parikappavacanametaṃ tādisāsu hi mahānadīsu kāci puratthimasamuddaṃ paviṭṭhā kāci pacchimaṃ

 kusale  patiṭṭhitoti ettha yaṃ accantikaṃ kusale patiṭṭhānaṃ taṃ dassento ti āha heṭṭhimamaggakusaleti adhippāyo tenāha nti ariyaphalaṃyeva  kilesā khīyanti etthāti kilesakkhayo nibbānaṃ tasmiṃ 

 ariyānaṃ  buddhānaṃ dhanantipi ariyadhanaṃ nibbānanti keci anayatopi visuddhaṭṭhena ariyañca taṃ dhanañca dhanāyitaṭṭhenāti ariyadhanaṃ tena ti ariyadhanabhogena

6 sappaññavaggo

ti yehi indriyādīhi bhāviyamānehi sotāpattimaggo anuppatto tāneva dhammasamānatāya cetaṃ vuttaṃ aññāneva hi atthato taṃ taṃ maggaṃ sādhakāni indriyādīni ti yaṃ kañci vineyyapuggalaṃ anapekkhitvā kevalaṃ tantivasena ṭhiti kathitā

ti sattasu kittiyamānesu upāsakajanesu tiādīsu ti pāḷigatiyā gambhīrā tathā ca  heṭṭhā pakāsitameva tiādinā (a ni 663 kathā 539) āgataṃ  tattha cetanāsahajātaṃ nānākkhaṇikantiādinā paṭṭhāne āgatanayena suttesu (a ni 3101) ca diṭṭhadhammavedanīyantiādinā āgatanayena gambhīrabhāvo veditabbo nibbānassa ceva ariyamaggassa ca pakāsanato  lokuttaratthadīpakatā atītaṃpāhaṃ rūpena khajjiṃ etarahi khajjāmītiādinā pañcannaṃ khandhānaṃ khādakabhāvassa puggalassa khāditabbatāya vibhāvanena  (saṃ ni 379) visesato nissattanijjīvatā dīpitāti vuttaṃ ti ti ye tesu suttesu vuttapaṭipadaṃ sammadeva paripūrenti te tesu upasampajja viharanti nāma  ti na kho netanti ettha na-kāro aññamaññanti ettha ma-kāro viya byañjanasandhimattameva nāssa koci attho

nti na kho etaṃ noti ca amhehīti atthoti āha tiādi assasantīti ti āha ti marissatīti  ekantabhāvimaraṇo so pana maraṇādhīnavuttikoti vuttaṃ ti ti adhimuttiṃ uppādehi taṃ pana tathā cittassa paṇidhānaṃ ṭhapananti āha ti ti pubbabhāgaguṇesu  anantāparimāṇattā  vimuttiyā ninnānattā

ti maggaphalapaññāya paṭilābhatthaṃ tenāha tiādi
