

 pañcamassa  paṭhame nti evaṃnāmake nagare ti mucalindavane ti ayaṃ kira thero tasmiṃyeva nagare seṭṭhiputto satthu santike pabbajitvā pubbenivāsañāṇaṃ paṭilabhi so attanā nivutthaṃ khandhasantānaṃ anussaranto kassapadasabalassa sāsanosakkanakāle pabbajitvā catūsu parisāsu sāsane agāravaṃ karontīsu nisseṇiṃ bandhitvā pabbataṃ āruyha tattha samaṇadhammaṃ katvā attano nivutthabhāvaṃ addasa so satthāraṃ upasaṅkamitvā taṃ kāraṇaṃ pucchissāmīti etaṃ ko nu kho bhantetiādivacanaṃ avoca
ti satthari gāravañceva jeṭṭhakabhāvañca anupaṭṭhapetvā viharanti sesesupi eseva nayo tattha cetiyaṅgaṇādīsu chattaṃ dhāretvā upāhanā āruyha vicaranto nānappakārañca niratthakakathaṃ kathento satthari agāravo viharati nāma dhammassavanagge pana nisīditvā niddāyanto ceva nānappakārañca niratthakakathaṃ kathento dhamme agāravo viharati nāma saṅghamajjhe bāhāvikkhepakaṃ nānattakathaṃ kathento theranavamajjhimesu ca cittīkāraṃ akaronto saṅghe agāravo viharati nāma sikkhaṃ aparipūrento sikkhāya agāravo viharati nāma aññamaññaṃ kalahabhaṇḍanādīni karonto aññamaññaṃ agāravo viharati nāma dutiyaṃ uttānatthameva

 tatiye ti ujubhāvena avaṅkagamanena ti  padajavena ti sarīramudutāya ti adhivāsanakkhantiyā ti sucisīlatāya bhikkhuvāre nti ñāṇassa ujukagamanaṃ ti sūraṃ hutvā ñāṇassa gamanabhāvo nti  sīlamaddavaṃ ti adhivāsanakkhantiyeva  sucisīlatāyeva catutthe pañca balāni missakāni kathitāni

 pañcame ti cittassa thaddhabhāvā kacavarabhāvā khāṇukabhāvā ti satthu sarīre vā guṇe vā kaṅkhati sarīre kaṅkhamāno dvattiṃsavarapurisalakkhaṇapaṭimaṇḍitaṃ nāma sarīraṃ atthi nu kho natthīti kaṅkhati guṇe kaṅkhamāno atītānāgatapaccuppannajānanasamatthaṃ sabbaññutaññāṇaṃ atthi nu kho natthīti kaṅkhati ti vicinanto kicchati dukkhaṃ āpajjati vinicchetuṃ na sakkoti ti evametanti adhimokkhaṃ na paṭilabhati ti guṇesu otaritvā nibbicikicchabhāvena pasīdituṃ anāvilo bhavituṃ na sakkoti ti kilesasantāpakavīriyakaraṇatthāya ti punappunaṃ yogāya ti satatakiriyāya ti padahanatthāya ti ayaṃ satthari vicikicchāsaṅkhāto  paṭhamo cittassa thaddhabhāvo evametassa bhikkhuno appahīno hoti
ti pariyattidhamme ca paṭivedhadhamme ca pariyattidhamme kaṅkhamāno tepiṭakaṃ buddhavacanaṃ caturāsītidhammakkhandhasahassānīti vadanti atthi nu kho etaṃ natthīti kaṅkhati paṭivedhadhamme kaṅkhamāno vipassanānissando maggo nāma magganissandaṃ phalaṃ nāma sabbasaṅkhārapaṭinissaggo nibbānaṃ nāmāti vadanti taṃ atthi nu kho natthīti kaṅkhati ti ujuppaṭipannotiādīnaṃ padānaṃ vasena evarūpaṃ paṭipadaṃ paṭipanno cattāro maggaṭṭhā cattāro phalaṭṭhāti aṭṭhannaṃ puggalānaṃ samūhabhūto saṅgho nāma atthi nu kho natthīti kaṅkhati  kaṅkhamāno adhisīlasikkhā nāma adhicittaadhipaññāsikkhā nāmāti vadanti sā atthi nu kho  natthīti kaṅkhati ti ayaṃ sabrahmacārīsu kopasaṅkhāto pañcamo cittassa thaddhabhāvo kacavarabhāvo khāṇukabhāvo

 chaṭṭhe ti cittaṃ vinibandhitvā muṭṭhiyaṃ katvā viya gaṇhantīti cetasovinibandhā ti vatthukāmepi kilesakāmepi ti  attano kāye ti bahiddhārūpe nti yattakaṃ icchati tattakaṃ nti udarapūraṃ tañhi udaraṃ avadehanato udarāvadehakanti vuccati nti mañcapīṭhasukhaṃ utusukhaṃ vā nti  yathā samparivattakaṃ sayantassa dakkhiṇapassa vāmapassānaṃ sukhaṃ hoti evaṃ uppannasukhaṃ nti niddāsukhaṃ ti yuttappayutto viharati ti patthayitvā tiādīsu nti catupārisuddhisīlaṃ nti vatasamādānaṃ ti tapacaraṇaṃ nti methunavirati ti mahesakkhadevo vā bhavissāmi ti appesakkhadevesu vā aññataroti

 sattame ti vātaṃ anulometvā harati ti dhamaniyo suddhā karoti ti sace āmāvasesakaṃ hoti taṃ pāceti aṭṭhame nti na cakkhūnaṃ hitaṃ cakkhuṃ visuddhaṃ na karoti

 navame ti dīghena paripuṇṇapadabyañjanakaṃ gāthāvattañca vināsetvā pavattena ti evaṃ gītassaro kātabboti sarakiriyaṃ patthayamānassa ti samathavipassanācittassa vināso hoti

 dasame ti nānāvidhaṃ supinaṃ passanto dukkhaṃ supati ti paṭibujjhantopi uttasitvā salomahaṃso paṭibujjhati imasmiṃ sutte satisampajaññaṃ missakaṃ kathitaṃ
