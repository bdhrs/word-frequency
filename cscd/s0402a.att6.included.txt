
 dutiyassa  paṭhame nti gihīnaṃ sabbakāmanipphattimūlakaṃ sukhaṃ nti pabbajitānaṃ pabbajjāmūlakaṃ sukhaṃ
 dutiye nti kāme ārabbha uppajjanakasukhaṃ nti nekkhammaṃ vuccati pabbajjā taṃ ārabbha uppajjanakasukhaṃ
 tatiye nti tebhūmakasukhaṃ nti lokuttarasukhaṃ
 catutthe nti āsavānaṃ paccayabhūtaṃ vaṭṭasukhaṃ nti tesaṃ apaccayabhūtaṃ vivaṭṭasukhaṃ
 pañcame nti saṃkilesaṃ vaṭṭagāmisukhaṃ nti nikkilesaṃ vivaṭṭagāmisukhaṃ
 chaṭṭhe nti aputhujjanasukhaṃ nti puthujjanasukhaṃ
 sattame nti kāyaviññāṇasahajātaṃ nti manodvārikasukhaṃ taṃ lokiyalokuttaramissakaṃ kathitaṃ
 aṭṭhame nti paṭhamadutiyajjhānasukhaṃ nti tatiyacatutthajjhānasukhaṃ tattha lokiyasappītikato lokiyanippītikaṃ lokuttarasappītikato ca lokuttaranippītikaṃ agganti evaṃ bhummantaraṃ abhinditvā aggabhāvo veditabbo
 navame nti tīsu jhānesu sukhaṃ nti catutthajjhānasukhaṃ
 dasame nti appanaṃ vā upacāraṃ vā pattasukhaṃ nti tadubhayaṃ appattasukhaṃ
 ekādasame nti sappītikaṃ jhānadvayaṃ paccavekkhantassa uppannasukhaṃ pi eseva nayo dvādasamepi imināva upāyena attho veditabbo
 terasame nti rūpāvacaracatutthajjhānārammaṇaṃ yaṃkiñci rūpaṃ ārabbha uppajjanakaṃ vā nti arūpāvacarajjhānārammaṇaṃ yaṃkiñci arūpaṃ ārabbha uppajjanakaṃ vāti
