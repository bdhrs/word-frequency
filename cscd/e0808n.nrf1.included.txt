


 chaṭṭhokhilalahujo  vā
gayutāññechaggaṇāna jo visame
ariyāyantaḍḍhe lo chaṭṭhonte gogaṇā chaññe


 paṭhamaḍḍhe chaṭṭho ce
sabbalahetthādilahuni bhavatiyati
tapparakontepi sace carimepi bhavati catutthonte
 ce pubbodita lakkhaṇaṃ bhave yassā
 ādimamatha pādayugaṃ yassā tyaṃsehi sā 

 yattha gaṇattaya mullaṅghi
yobhayatthādimo bhave 
 garumajjhago jakāro catutthako dutiyako 

 capalāgatākhilaṃ ce dalādimaṃ lakkhaṇaṃ bhajati yassā
pathyālakkhaṇamaññaṃ  nāma sā bhavati

 pathyāya lakkhaṇaṃ ce paṭhamaḍḍhe lakkhaṇaṃ tu capalāya
dutiye daletha yassā pakittitā sā 


 sabbaṃpaṭhamadale yadi lakkhaṇamariyāya vuttamubhayesu
yassā dalesu yuttaṃ
vuttā sā  vutta yati lalitā

 ariyāyaṃ dutiyaḍḍhe gaditākhilalakkhaṇaṃ yaṃ taṃ
bhavati dalesubhayesupi
yadi yassā sāya 

 ariyāyaḍḍhadvitayaṃ pubbodita lakkhaṇopetaṃ
vipariyayenābhihitaṃ
yassā sambhavati ceha so

 ariyāpubbaḍḍhaṃ  yadi garunekenādhikena nidhane yuttaṃ
yadi pubbaḍḍhasamānaṃ dala mitaraṃ coditāya


 visame cha siyuṃ kalā mukhe
same tvaṭṭha ralagā tatopari
 ta muccate lahu chakkaṃ na nirantaraṃ same

 vetālīyopamaṃ mukhe taṃ
 rayā yadante
 kathitāyaṃ bhagagānte yadi pubbamivaññaṃ

 yadādito 
ṭhitettha pādesvākhilesu jo

ti vuccate
jo cādo visamesu saṇṭhito
 pubbattha samesu ce ga jā  ruditāti saṇṭhitā

 samāsamātrādinaṃ samā
saṃyutā bhavati taṃ 
 assa sā sama katā 
 tadaññajā 

 dvika vihata vasu lahu  riha
 navamo lgante
 jo nlā thavāṇṇavā 
 tadvayato khyā
 pañcaṭṭhanavasu yadi lo 
 galyāṭṭhahi cesā vu

 yamatīta lakkhaṇa visesa yutaṃ 
mattā samādi pādābhihitaṃ 
aniyata vutta parimāṇa sahitaṃ 
pathitaṃ janesu  


 vinā  vaṇṇehi mattā gā vinā vaṇṇā garūhi tu
vinā lahūhi garavo dale pathyādino matā
