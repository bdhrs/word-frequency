catubyūhena  hārena vuttehi nibbacanādhippāyanidānehi saddhiṃ sutte padatthānaṃ suttantarasaṃsandanasaṅkhāte pubbāparavicāre dassite tesaṃ suttapadatthānaṃ sabhāgavisabhāgadhammantarāvaṭṭanaṃ sukhena sakkā dassetunti catubyūhahārānantaraṃ āvaṭṭo hāro suttantarasaṃsandanassa hi sabhāgavisabhāgadhammantarāvaṭṭanayassa upāyabhāvato ārambhatha nikkamathātiādigāthāya  ārambhananikkamanabuddhasāsanayogadhunanehi vīriyasamādhipaññindriyāni niddhāretvā tesu ārambhananikkamanabuddhasāsanayogadhunanesu ananuyogassa mūlaṃ pamādoti suttantare dassito pamādo āvaṭṭitoti
