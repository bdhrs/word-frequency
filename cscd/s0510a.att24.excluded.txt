26. Thomakavaggo
1-10. Thomakattheraapadānādivaṇṇanā
.
5-6.vijahitvā devavaṇṇa,.Adhikāraṃ kattukāmoidhaāgamiṃ.Tassa bhikkhā mayā dinnādevalo,bhikkhā dinnā.
9-10.ānando nāma sambuddho.Amanussamhi kānaneparinibbāyi,.Sarīraṃ tattha jhāpesi.
.
20.ahosiṃ candano nāmā.Sambuddhassatrajo.Ekopāhano mayā dinno.Bodhiṃ sampajja me tuva.
23-24.mañjarikaṃ karitvānārathiyaṃbhikkhusaṅghapurakkhataṃsamaṇānaggaṃ.Buddhassa abhiropayipaggayha.
28-29.aloṇapaṇṇabhakkhomhī,.Niyamesu ca saṃvuto.Pātarāse anuppatte.Siddhattho upagacchi ma.Tāhaṃ buddhassa pādāsi.
.
37-38.sikhinaṃ sikhinaṃ yathāobhāsayantaṃ.Aggajaṃ pupphamādāyābuddhassaabhiropayiṃ.
Chabbīsatimavaggavaṇṇanā samattā.
