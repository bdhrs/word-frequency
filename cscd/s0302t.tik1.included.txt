

ti  taruṇā parittā kesaggamattabhāvato yathā kesā dīghaso dvaṅgulamattāya sabbasmiṃ kāle etappamāṇāva na tacchindanaṃ evaṃ nakhaggāpi kesaggamattāva na tesaṃ chindanaṃ avaḍḍhanato ti sahassimaṃ satasahassimanti vuttaṭṭhāne ti paṭivijjhitvā tasmā abhisametāvino paṭividdhasaccassāti attho kāmaṃ purimapadaṃ dukkhakkhandhassa atītabhāvaṃ upādāyapi vattuṃ yuttaṃ puretaraṃyeva pana vuttabhāvaṃ upādāya vuttanti dassetuṃ ntiādi vuttaṃ  nāma pacchimaṃ apekkhitvā purimapacchimatā hi taṃ taṃ upādāya vuccatīti idhādhippetaṃ purimaṃ nīharitvā dassetuṃ tiādi vuttaṃ atītampi parikkhīṇanti idhādhippetaṃ parikkhīṇameva vibhāvetuṃ ntiādi vuttaṃ sotāpannassa dukkhakkhayo idha coditoti taṃ dassetuṃ ti vatvā idāni taṃ sarūpato dassetuṃ puna ntiādi vuttaṃ  apāyesu cāti yaṃ dukkhaṃ uppajjeyya taṃ sabbaṃ parikkhīṇanti daṭṭhabbaṃ ti sotāpannassa yaṃ parimāṇaṃ tato uddhañca upapātaṃ atthīti adhippāyo mahā attho guṇo mahattho so etassa atthīti  kakārassa yakāraṃ katvā tenāha ti


ti avavedhena adhodisatāya tenāha ti


ti  sambhijjasamodhānagataṭṭhāne  sametā honti tenāha ti pāḷiyaṃ vibhattilopena niddesoti tamatthaṃ dassento ti āha sambhijjati missībhāvaṃ gacchati etthāti sambhejjaṃ missitaṭṭhānaṃ tattha udakaṃ  tenāha nti


ti cakkavāḷapabbatassa antogadhāya
ti idha chaṭṭhasuttādīsu paṭhamasuttādīsu vuttanayenevāti attho veditabbo visesābhāvato
ti imassa abhisamayasaṃyuttassa osānaṭṭhāne nti aññatitthiyānaṃ ti jhānābhiññāsahito guṇādhigamo  saccapaṭivedhassa mahānubhāvattā tenāha bhagavā paccakkhasabbadhammo evaṃ mahādhigamo bhikkhave diṭṭhisampanno puggalo evaṃ mahābhiññoti
