


 lābhasakkārasaṃyuttassa  paṭhame ti thaddho ti ettha  nāma catupaccayalābho ti tesaṃyeva sukatānaṃ susaṅkhatānaṃ lābho ti vaṇṇaghoso ti tikhiṇo ti kharo ti antarāyakaro paṭhamaṃ

 dutiye ti baḷisaṃ gahetvā caramāno macchaghātako nti āmisamakkhitaṃ ti āmise cakkhu dassanaṃ assāti āmisacakkhu ti gilitabaḷiso ti dukkhaṃ patto ti vināsaṃ patto ti yathākāmena yathāruciyā yatheva naṃ bāḷisiko icchati tathevassa kattabboti attho ti yathā kilesamārassa kāmo evaṃ kattabbo nirayaṃ vā tiracchānayoniṃ vā pettivisayaṃ vā pāpetabbo dutiyaṃ

 tatiye nti mahantaṃ aṭṭhikacchapakulaṃ ti ettha addhā kiñci khāditabbaṃ atthi taṃ maccharāyanto maṃ esa nivāretīti saññāya agamāsi ti  papatā vuccati dīgharajjukabaddho ayakantakosake daṇḍakaṃ pavesetvā gahito  kaṇṇikasallasaṇṭhāno ayakaṇṭako yasmiṃ vegena patitvā kaṭāhe laggamatte daṇḍako nikkhamati rajjuko ekābaddho gacchateva ti so  viddhakummo ti udakasaddaṃ sutvā sāsaṅkaṭṭhānaṃ bhavissatīti nivattitvā yena so atthakāmo kummo nti idāni tvaṃ amittahatthaṃ gato na amhākaṃ santakoti attho evaṃ sallapantānaṃyeva ca nesaṃ nāvāya ṭhito luddo rajjukaṃ ākaḍḍhitvā kummaṃ gahetvā yathākāmaṃ akāsi sesamettha ito anantarasutte ca uttānameva tatiyacatutthāni

 pañcame ti gūthapāṇakā ti gūthabhakkhā ti anto gūthena bharitā ti idaṃ purimasseva atthadīpanaṃ ti pacchimapāde bhūmiyaṃ ṭhapetvā purimapāde gūthassa upari āropetvā ṭhitā ahamhi gūthādīti bhaṇantī atimaññeyya ti aparopissa pattapūro paṇītapiṇḍapāto bhaveyya pañcamaṃ

 chaṭṭhe nti bhikkhave kaṃ puggalaṃ matthake patitvā maddamānaṃ sukkāsanicakkaṃ āgacchatu  nti anadhigatārahattaṃ iti bhagavā na sattānaṃ dukkhakāmatāya ādīnavaṃ pana dassetuṃ evamāha asanicakkañhi matthake patitaṃ ekameva attabhāvaṃ nāseti lābhasakkārasilokena pariyādiṇṇacitto nirayādīsu anantadukkhaṃ anubhoti chaṭṭhaṃ

 sattame ti gatadiddhena ti visamakkhitena ti sattiyā sattamaṃ

 aṭṭhame ti jarasiṅgālo yathā hi suvaṇṇavaṇṇopi kāyo pūtikāyo tveva taṃkhaṇaṃ gaḷitampi ca muttaṃ pūtimuttantveva vuccati evaṃ tadahujātopi siṅgālo jarasiṅgālotveva vuccati ti evaṃnāmakena rogena so kira sītakāle uppajjati tasmiṃ uppanne sakalasarīrato lomāni patanti sakalasarīraṃ nillomaṃ hutvā samantato phuṭati vātabbhāhatā vaṇā rujjanti yathā ummattakasunakhena daṭṭho puriso anavaṭṭhitova bhamati evaṃ tasmiṃ uppanne bhamitabbo hoti asukaṭṭhāne sotthi bhavissatīti na paññāyati aṭṭhamaṃ

 navame ti evaṃnāmakā mahāvātā kīdise pana ṭhāne te vātā vāyantīti yattha ṭhitassa cattāro  dīpā uppalinipattamattā hutvā paññāyanti ti navavuṭṭhe deve viravanto vātasakuṇo tattha gacchati taṃ sandhāyetaṃ vuttaṃ tiādīsu hatthapāde kīḷāpento khandhaṭṭhiṃ vā nāmento kāyaṃ na rakkhati nāma nānāvidhaṃ duṭṭhullakathaṃ kathento vācaṃ na rakkhati nāma kāmavitakkādayo vitakkento cittaṃ na rakkhati nāma ti kāyagatāsatiṃ anupaṭṭhapetvā navamaṃ

 dasame nti asakkārena ca ubhayena ti arahattaphalasamādhi so hi tena na vikampati ti appamāṇena phalasamādhinā viharantassa nti satatakāriṃ nti arahattamaggadiṭṭhiyā sukhumadiṭṭhiphalasamāpattiatthāya vipassanaṃ paṭṭhapetvā āgatattā vipassakaṃ nti upādānakkhayasaṅkhāte nibbāne rataṃ ti sappurisoti kathentīti dasamaṃ



 dutiyavaggassa  paṭhame nti appamattakenapi kāraṇena sampajānameva musā bhāsantaṃ sīlaṃ pūressāmīti saṃvihitabhikkhuṃ sinerumattopi paccayarāsi cāletuṃ na sakkoti yadā pana sīlaṃ pahāya sakkāranissito hoti tadā kuṇḍakamuṭṭhihetupi musā bhāsati aññaṃ vā akiccaṃ karoti dutiyaṃ uttānamevāti paṭhamadutiyāni

 tatiyādīsu ti ekassa kañcananikkhassa ti siṅgīsuvaṇṇanikkhassa ti cakkavāḷabbhantarāya mahāpathaviyā ti kassacideva āmisassa hetu antamaso kuṇḍakamuṭṭhinopi ti aṭaviyaṃ corehi gahetvā jīvite voropiyamāne tassapi hetu ti janapade uttamitthiyā tatiyādīni



 tatiyavaggassa paṭhame ti na tassa raho ekakassa nisinnassa tena dhammena atthikopi mātugāmo cittaṃ pariyādātuṃ sakkoti yassa lābhasakkārasiloko  cittaṃ pariyādātuṃ sakkotīti attho dutiyaṃ uttānamevāti paṭhamadutiyāni

 tatiye ti sotāpannā sesamettha uttānameva tathā catutthe pañcame chaṭṭhe ca tatiyādīni

 sattame ntiādīsu saha pubbakammena attabhāvo kolaputtiyaṃ vaṇṇapokkharatā kalyāṇavākkaraṇatā dhutaguṇāvīkaraṇaṃ cīvaradhāraṇaṃ parivārasampattīti evamādi lābhasakkārassa samudayo nāma taṃ samudayasaccavasena nappajānāti nirodho ca paṭipadā ca nirodhasaccamaggasaccavaseneva veditabbā sattamaṃ

 aṭṭhame  yasmā lābhasakkārasiloko narakādīsu nibbattento sakalampi imaṃ attabhāvaṃ nāseti idhāpi maraṇampi maraṇamattampi dukkhaṃ āvahati tasmā tiādi vuttaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ

 navame ti suttādimayā rajju mudukā hoti vāḷarajju kharā pharusā tasmā ayameva gahitā navamaṃ

 dasame ti phalasamāpattisukhavihārā ti tesaṃ ahaṃ assa  khīṇāsavo hi lābhī puññasampanno yāgukhajjakādīni gahetvā āgatāgatānaṃ anumodanaṃ karonto dhammaṃ desento pañhaṃ vissajjento phalasamāpattiṃ appetvā nisīdituṃ okāsaṃ na labhati taṃ sandhāya vuttanti dasamaṃ



 catutthavaggassa paṭhamaṃ uttānameva dutiyādīsu nti alobhāditividhakusaladhammo ti tasseva pariyāyadesanā  ayaṃ panettha saṅkhepattho  yassa kusalamūlādisaṅkhātassa anavajjadhammassa asamucchinnattā devadatto sagge vā nibbatteyya maggaphalāni vā adhigaccheyya svāssa  sabbaso samucchinno vinaṭṭho paṭhamādīni

 pañcame ti avaḍḍhiyā vināsāya ti vaḷavāya kucchismiṃ gadrabhassa jātā ti taṃ assena saddhiṃ sampayojenti sā  gabbhaṃ gaṇhitvā kāle sampatte vijāyituṃ na sakkoti pādehi bhūmiyaṃ paharantī tiṭṭhati athassā cattāro pāde catūsu khāṇukesu bandhitvā kucchiṃ phāletvā potaṃ nīharanti sā tattheva marati tenetaṃ vuttaṃ pañcamaṃ

 chaṭṭhe ti abhiharitabbaṃ bhattaṃ tassa pana pamāṇaṃ dassetuṃ ti  vuttaṃ tattha eko thālipāko dasannaṃ purisānaṃ bhattaṃ gaṇhāti nti acchapittaṃ vā macchapittaṃ vāssa nāsapuṭe pakkhipeyyaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ

 sattame ti sace musā bhaṇasi mātaraṃ te vissajjessāma no ce bhaṇasi na vissajjessāmāti evaṃ corehi aṭaviyaṃ pucchamāno tassā corahatthagatāya mātuyāpi hetu sampajānamusā na bhāseyyāti attho ito paresupi eseva nayoti sattamādīni
