


 asaṅkhatasaṃyutte nti akataṃ ti hitaṃ esantena ti anukampamānena ti anukampaṃ cittena pariggahetvā paṭiccātipi vuttaṃ hoti ti taṃ mayā imaṃ asaṅkhatañca asaṅkhatamaggañca desentena tumhākaṃ kataṃ ettakameva hi anukampakassa satthu kiccaṃ yadidaṃ aviparītadhammadesanā ito paraṃ pana paṭipatti nāma sāvakānaṃ kiccaṃ tenāha ti iminā rukkhamūlasenāsanaṃ dasseti ti iminā janavivittaṃ ṭhānaṃ ubhayena ca yogānurūpaṃ senāsanaṃ ācikkhati dāyajjaṃ niyyāteti
ti ārammaṇūpanijjhānena aṭṭhatiṃsārammaṇāni lakkhaṇūpanijjhānena ca aniccādito khandhāyatanādīni upanijjhāyatha samathañca vipassanañca vaḍḍhethāti vuttaṃ hoti ti mā pamajjittha ti ye hi pubbe daharakāle arogakāle sattasappāyādisampattikāle satthu sammukhībhāvakāle ca yonisomanasikārarahitā rattindivaṃ maṅkulabhattaṃ hutvā seyyasukhaṃ middhasukhaṃ anubhontā pamajjanti te pacchā jarākāle rogakāle maraṇakāle vipattikāle satthu parinibbutakāle ca taṃ pubbe pamādavihāraṃ anussarantā sappaṭisandhikālakiriyañca bhāriyaṃ sampassamānā vippaṭisārino honti tumhe pana tādisā mā ahuvatthāti dassento āha  mā pacchā vippaṭisārino ahuvatthāti
ti ayaṃ amhākaṃ santikā jhāyatha mā pamādatthāti tumhākaṃ anusāsanī ovādoti vuttaṃ hoti


tiādīsu yaṃ vattabbaṃ taṃ parato vakkhāma
ntiādīsu taṇhānatiyā abhāvena anataṃ catunnaṃ āsavānaṃ abhāvena  paramatthasaccatāya  vaṭṭassa parabhāgaṭṭhena  saṇhaṭṭhena  suṭṭhu duddasatāya  jarāya ajaritattā  thiraṭṭhena  apalujjanatāya  cakkhuviññāṇena apassitabbattā  taṇhāmānadiṭṭhipapañcānaṃ abhāvena 
santabhāvaṭṭhena  maraṇābhāvena  uttamaṭṭhena  sassirikaṭṭhena  nirupaddavatāya  taṇhākkhayassa paccayattā 
vimhāpanīyaṭṭhena accharaṃ paharitabbayuttakanti  abhūtameva bhūtaṃ ajātaṃ hutvā atthīti vā  niddukkhattā  niddukkhasabhāvattā  vānābhāvena  byābajjhābhāveneva  virāgādhigamassa paccayato  paramatthasuddhitāya  tīhi bhavehi muttatāya  kāmālayānaṃ abhāvena  patiṭṭhaṭṭhena  allīyitabbayuttaṭṭhena  tāyanaṭṭhena  bhayasaraṇaṭṭhena  bhayanāsananti attho paraṃ ayanaṃ gati patiṭṭhāti  sesamettha vuttanayamevāti
