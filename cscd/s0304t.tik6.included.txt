

nti  bhoguppattigāmaṃ ti appaṭihataṃ hutvā pavattati paṭisambhidappattiyā


ti pañhaṃ byākātuṃ veyyattiyābhāvena asamatthattā upāsako theresu byākātuṃ asakkontesu sayaṃ byākātukāmo pucchati na vihesādhippāyo paṭhamavacanena abyākaronte disvāva punappunaṃ pucchitaṃ viheso viya hotīti katvā vuttaṃ ti


ti avantiraṭṭhe taṃ pana majjhimapadesato dakkhiṇadisāyaṃ tenāha ti ti tassa vacanassa anumodanādhippāyena vadati na pana tato kiñci gahetukāmatādhippāyena


ti tesaṃ dāsakammakarānaṃ sesake yathāruci vicāraṇaṃ anujānanto nti balavatā sūriyasantāpena saṅkuthitaṃ  tenāha tiādi nti ativiya tikkhadhātukaṃ nti  aññattha anāgatattā tipiṭake avomissakapadaṃ idheva āgatapadanti attho ti paṭikaṃsena visesanena vilīyamānena kāyena
ti etasmiṃ adhiṭṭhāniddhikaraṇe ti samantato chādanavasena maṇḍapaṃ viya meghapaṭalaṃ uppādetvā ti megho  phusitakaṃ  jālavinaddhaṃ viya vassatu evaṃ vuttappakārena nānāparikammaṃ nānādhiṭṭhānaṃ ekato parikammaṃ ekato adhiṭṭhānanti catukkamettha sambhavatīti dasseti tiādinā ti yathāvuttesu catūsu pakāresu yena vā tena vā pakārena ti evaṃ vā evaṃ vā hotūti pavattitaparikammacittassa ti adhiṭṭhānacittuppādanatthaṃ samāpannapādakajjhānato vuṭṭhāya adhiṭṭhānacittassa ekāvajjanavīthiyaṃ pavattaparikammaṃ sandhāya vuttanti vadanti


 elaṃ vuccati doso taṃ etassa natthīti nelaṃ taṃ aṅgaṃ etassāti  suvisuddhasīlaguṇo tenāha nelaṅganti kho bhante sīlānametaṃ adhivacananti aṭṭhakathāyaṃ pana dosābhāvameva dassetuṃ ti vuttaṃ nti mahākappinattheraṃ sandhāya vuttaṃ ti attano sabbaññutaññāṇena paccakkhato upalakkhitena atthena kathesi ti ayaṃ pana gahapati asutvā kevalaṃ nayaggāhena āha


 anāgāmibhāvato ti ime kāyasaṅkhāro vacīsaṅkhāro cittasaṅkhāroti vuccamānā tayo saṅkhārā saddatopi atthatopi  tato eva  ākulā  dubbibhāvanā  asaṅkarato dīpetuṃ dukkarā tathā hi kusalacetanā eva kāyasaṅkhārotipi  vuccati vacīsaṅkhārotipi cittasaṅkhārotipi assāsapassāsāpi katthaci kāyasaṅkhāroti vitakkavicārāpi vacīsaṅkhāroti vuccanti saññāvedanāpi cittasaṅkhāroti vuccanti tena vuttaṃ tiādi tattha ākaḍḍhitvā gahaṇaṃ  sampattassa hatthe karaṇaṃ  gahitassa vissajjanaṃ  phandanaṃ  nipphādetvā nti kāyaviññattivasena pubbabhāge hanusañcopanaṃ katvā evañhi vacībhedakaraṇaṃ tiādi yathāvuttassa atthassa nigamanaṃ
 itarasaṅkhārehi padatthato visesaṃ kathāpessāmīti ti ettha kāyanissitatā ca nesaṃ tabbhāvabhāvitāya veditabbā na kāyassa nissayapaccayatāvasenāti dassento ti āha ti cettha karajakāyo daṭṭhabbo ti cittaṃ nissāya taṃ nissayapaccayabhūtaṃ labhitvā uppannā
samāpajjāmīti padassa samīpe vuccamānaṃ samāpajjissanti padaṃ āsannānāgatakālavācī eva bhavituṃ yuttaṃ na itaranti āha  ti tayidaṃ tassa tathā vattabbatāya vuttaṃ na pana tassa tathā cittapavattisambhavato samāpannepi eseva nayo ti samāpajjissaṃ samāpajjāmīti dvīhi padehi ti samāpannoti padena
 uppāditaṃ hoti nirodhasamāpannatthāya anupubbasamāpattisamāpajjanasaṅkhātāya nirodhabhāvanāya taṃ cittaṃ bhāvitaṃ hoti tenāha ntiādi ti dutiyajjhānakkhaṇe  tattha anuppajjanato anuppādanato heṭṭhā nirodhoti adhippeto catunnaṃ arūpakkhandhānaṃ tajjā parikammasiddhā yā appavatti  tattha nirodhasamāpattisaññāti yā nesaṃ tathā appavatti sā ti vuttā
cittasaṅkhāro niruddhoti vacanato tadaññesaṃ anirodhaṃ icchantānaṃ vādaṃ dassento tiādiṃ vatvā tattha atippasaṅgadassanamukhena taṃ vādaṃ nisedhetuṃ tiādimāha ti yathāgate byañjanamatte abhinivesaṃ akatvā ti ācariyānaṃ paramparāgate dhammanaye dhammanettiyaṃ 
nti  parittabhūmakakusalākusaladhammapabandhe  tasmiñhi vattamāne kāyika-vācasika-kiriyasampavatti hoti dassana-savanādivasena ārammaṇaggahaṇe pavattamāneti attho tenāha  ti ti puñchitāni viya honti ghaṭṭanāya vibādhitattā etenāyaṃ ghaṭṭanā pañcadvārikaviññāṇānaṃ vegasā uppajjanāya na ārammaṇanti dasseti tenevāha  ti
ti idaṃ atthato āpannaṃ gahetvā vuttaṃ  ettakaṃ kālaṃ acittako bhavissāmīti eteneva tassa atthassa siddhattā nti ettha visuṃ cittassa bhāvanā nāma natthi addhānaparicchedaṃ pana katvā nirodhasamāpattatthāya kātabbaparikammabhāvanāya eva tassa sijjhanato
 nirodhakathā ti samathavipassanābalehi nti tiṇṇaṃ kāyavacīcittasaṅkhārānaṃ paṭippassaddhiyā ti aniccānupassanādīnaṃ aṭṭhannaṃ anupassanāñāṇānaṃ aṭṭhannañca maggaphalañāṇānaṃ vasena imāhi soḷasahi ñāṇappavattīhi ti aṭṭha samāpattiyo aṭṭha samādhicariyā tāsaṃ upacārasamādhi samādhibhāvasāmaññena ekā samādhicariyāti evaṃ navahi samādhicariyāhi ti vasībhāvatāsaṅkhātā paññā  te tāva ākārā  tiṭṭhantu sarūpamattassa panassa vattabbanti āha  ti yadi khandhānaṃ appavatti  jhānasukhādiṃ viya ti āha ntiādi
nti arahattaṃ anāgāmiphalacittaṃ tato paraṃ bhavaṅgasamayeti vuttattā āha ti  rūpasamuppādakattā ti nirodhaṃ samāpannabhikkhuno ti sā catutthajjhānikā phalasamāpatti na samuṭṭhāpeti assāsapassāse phalasamāpattiyā catutthajjhānikabhāvo kathaṃ nicchitoti āha  tiādi vakkhamānākārenapi parihāro hotīti ti nirodhasamāpattimeva sandhāya vadati  atisukhumasabhāvattā sañjīvattherassa pubbe samāpattikkhaṇe assāsapassāsā abbohārikabhāvaṃ gacchanti tena vuttaṃ nti
ti kasmā vuttaṃ nanu bhavaṅguppattikālampi vitakkavicārā uppajjantevāti kiñcāpi uppajjanti vacīsaṅkhāralakkhaṇappattā pana na hontīti imamatthaṃ dassetuṃ ntiādi vuttaṃ
ti ārammaṇabhūtametaṃ  niccasukhaattasabhāvato suññattā  vuttanayena suññasabhāvattā  rāganimittādīnaṃ abhāvato  rāgapaṇidhiādīnamabhāvato sesaṃ vuttanayameva tenāha ti nti ettha -saddena saṅkhāranimittassapi saṅgaho daṭṭhabbo yadaggena phalasamāpattisamphasso suññato nāma tadaggena phalasamāpattipi suññatā nāma phassasīsena pana desanā āgatāti tathā vuttaṃ animittappaṇihitesupi eseva nayo
āgamanaṃ ettha etāyāti vā āgamanikā sā eva  ka-kārassa ya-kāraṃ katvā  nimittato maggassa uppādanena  niccanimittassa ugghāṭanato ettha ca vuṭṭhānameva pamāṇaṃ na  pariggahadassanāni  sukhapaṇidhiyā paṭipakkhato  attadiṭṭhiyā ujupaṭipakkhattā sattasuññatāya sudiṭṭhattā  vipassanāgamanato  maggāgamanato  āgamanassa vavatthānassa abhāvena vipassanāya animittādināmalābho avavatthitoti adhippāyo tena vuttaṃ tiādi yasmā pana sā maggavuṭṭhānakāle evarūpāpi hotīti tassa vasena maggaphalānaṃ viya phassassapi pavattirūpattā yathāvutto vikappo anavasaroti daṭṭhabbaṃ ti puggalabhedavasena vuttaṃ na hi ekaṃyeva puggalaṃ etasmiṃ khaṇe tayo phassā phusanti tividho phasso phusatīti vā bhavitabbaṃ yasmā pana nirodhaphalasamāpattiyā vuṭṭhitassātiādi yassa yathāvuttā tayo eva phassā sambhavanti tassa anavasesaggahaṇavaseneva vuttaṃ ti
 sabbasaṅkhāravivittabhāvato tasmiṃ viveke ekanteneva ninnapoṇattā eva hi te paṭippassaddhasabbussukkā uttamapurisā catunnaṃ khandhānaṃ appavattiṃ anavasesaggahaṇalakkhaṇaṃ nirodhasamāpattiṃ samāpajjantīti


nti  appamāṇacetovimutti-ākiñcaññacetovimuttisaññitānaṃ jhānānaṃ atthopi nānāti ānetvā yojanā pharaṇaappamāṇatāya ti laddhanāmaṃ brahmavihārajjhānanti āha tiādi ti ākiñcaññāyatanajjhānanti āha tiādi ti aniccānupassanā sabbāpi vā  nāma yassa sayaṃ uppajjati tassa guṇābhāvadassanavasena pamāṇakaraṇato
ti  pharaṇavasena appamāṇagocaratāya  pamāṇagocarānaṃ kilesānaṃ ārammaṇabhāvassapi anāgamanato nti khale pasāritasālisīsādibhaṇḍaṃ ti maddassu tenāha ti ārammaṇabhūtaṃ palibuddhakaṃ vā natthi etassa kiñcananti akiñcanaṃ akiñcanameva 
ti kasiṇarūpanimittassa ti sarūpato na gahitā atthato pana gahitā eva tenāha  ti
ti saddavasena ti ārammaṇavasena ārammaṇabhāvena ekasabhāvā tenāha ti ti ārammaṇassa vasena cattāro hi maggā cattāri phalāni ārammaṇavasena nibbānapaviṭṭhatāya ekatthā ekārammaṇā ti idaṃ visuṃ visuṃ gahetvā vuttaṃ appamāṇādi pariyāyavuttaṃ nibbānaṃ ārabbha pavattanato tasmā nti idaṃ tena tena pariyāyena tattha tattha āgatabhāvaṃ sandhāya vuttaṃ


ti vācuggatapariyattidhammo ti paṭividdhasatthusāsano tenāha tiādi nti avasanabhāvena naggaṃ kuṭilajjhāsayatāya bhoggaṃ tato eva  naggatāya hi so rūpena nissiriko bhoggatāya cittena ti bhagavati saddhāya tasmiṃ saddahanā okappanā tassa saddhātipi vattabbataṃ labhati ti āgacchāmi bujjhāmīti attho etaṃ nigaṇṭhena pucchitamatthamāha
ti kāyaṃ abbhunnāmetvā ti piṭṭhiyā ninnamanena kucchiṃ purato nīharitvā  pasāraṇavasena  paggahetvā  sabbamidaṃ nigaṇṭhassa pahaṭṭhākāradassanatthaṃ idāni samaṇassa gotamassa upari vādaṃ āropetuṃ labbhatīti tenāha tiādi ti yuttisahitā ti pañhasaṅkhāto vīmaṃsā evaṃ bhavitabbanti citteneva parivīmaṃsā pañhā ti ekaṃ uddisanaṃ atthassa saṃkhittavacanaṃ nti niddisanaṃ atthassa vicāretvā kathanaṃ nti iminā nayena ti sabbesu pañhuddesaveyyākaraṇesu attho vitthārato veditabbo


 alaṃ samattho ariyabhāvāyāti  ñeyyajānanaṭṭhena ñāṇameva paccakkhato dassanaṭṭhena ñāṇadassanaṃ soyeva atisayaṭṭhena  pāvaḷā vuccati ānisadapadeso taṃ pāvaḷaṃ rajoharaṇatthaṃ nipphoṭīyati etāyāti  morapiñchavatti


 mattarājā nāma eko bhummadevo bhūtādhipati surāpotalarukkhanivāsī tena vuttaṃ ti ti vatvā te yathākkamaṃ dassento ti āha  cittaṃ ti yathādhippāyaṃ samijjhissati ti yasmā taṃ devāpi āsannamaraṇaṃ maññanti tasmā sā varameva bhavissati taṃ tumhākaṃ dīgharattaṃ hitāya sukhāya bhavissatīti adhippāyo
