 navamanaye sampayogavisiṭṭhā sampayuttā uddhaṭā dvādasamanaye ca sampayogavisiṭṭhā saṅgahitāsaṅgahitāti ubhayatthāpi sampayogavisiṭṭhāva gahitāti āha ti


ti  rūpakkhandhadhammāyatanādīhi  khandhāyatanādisaṅgahehi puna ti arūpabhavādīhi  āyatanadhātusaṅgahehipi asaṅgahitattā ti catuvokārabhavādayo ti rūpakkhandhadhammāyatanādīhi avasiṭṭhā satipi asaṅgāhakatte tehi asaṅgahitānaṃ sampayogo na sambhavatīti  terasamanaye  nārohanti tenāha aṭṭhakathāyaṃ vedanākkhandhena hi khandhādivasena rūpārūpadhammā asaṅgahitā honti tesañca sampayogo nāma natthīti   avikalapañcakkhandhādisamudāyabhāvato
ti dukkhasaccādīti ādisaddena vuttadhammā  avitakkāvicārādidhammā viya  tabbinimuttassa cittuppādassa sambhavato  anārammaṇamissakattā na vippayogassa atthitāyāti vuttaṃ tathā cāha rūpārūpadhammā asaṅgahitāti ti sampayogavippayogābhāvato  acetasikacetasikalokiyapadādīnaṃ vasena veditabbā

samucchijjati etenāti  saṅgahādivicārapariniṭṭhānabhūto cuddasamanayo tenāha ti vissajjetabbadhammavivittā pucchā  sā tathābhūtāpi vissajjetabbadhammābhāvassa ñāpikā hotīti āha ti moghā pucchā etassāti  tattha sabbapucchānaṃ moghattā  ti tena aṭṭhamanayena saddhiṃ imasmiṃ osānanaye aṭṭhamanaye ca osānanaye ca  dhammāyatanādayo sabbappakārena na labbhanti ti tattha kāraṇamāha
