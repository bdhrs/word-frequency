

ti  sukhadukkhavedakassa nti tesaṃ āyattā paṭibaddhā yesaṃ vipākānaṃ te tadāyattavuttino tesaṃ  imasmiṃ ṭhāne ācariyena parihāro vuccate ti tattha tasmiṃ aññassa attakārakassa abhāvepi sattusaṅkhātassa attano bhāvepi ayaṃ tava anurodho anunayo kuto kena kāraṇena  imasmiṃ asati kattari kusalādīnaṃ atthibhāve kammavirodho kuto kena kāraṇena ti aparo nayopi ti  etissaṃ codanāyaṃ  vattabbaṃ atthi avasiṭṭhaṃ tāva brūhi aparisamattā te codanā ti attano ti kārakavedakattassa abhāvo siyā
ti attano anāse cetanāpi anāso yadi na bhavati ti cetanāya anañño attāti paṭiññā hīnā ti attano anāse cetanāya nāso na bhavatiiti vuttappakārato  nti attano anāse cetanāya anāsoiti viparītaggahaṇaṃ siyā ti attā nassatu cetanā tiṭṭhatu evaṃ gahaṇaṃ pana na bhavati anaññattapakkhaṃ  pariccajāhi cetanāya anañño attāti paṭiññā hīnā yassa so 
 cetanattānaṃ  ekadesabhāvo natthi ti evaṃ ubhinnaṃ ekadesassa abhāve sati ko doso iti tvaṃ ce vadeyyāsi nti yaṃ vacanaṃ pana tayā vuttaṃ  vacanaṃ  nānurūpanti attho ti ekadese vattamānānaṃ cetanattānaṃ lakkhaṇato aññattanti paṭiññā hīnā
ti etaṃ cetanāya aññabhāve sati ko doso iti ce bhavaṃ vadeyya ti vacanaṃ ti pākāratarupāsāṇatiṇasadiso siyāti pubbe vuttadosato
ti opapātikā sattā atthi
nti bhāraggahaṇaṃ nti bhāroropanaṃ
 ekassa puggalassa  aṭṭhisamūho ekena kappena pabbatasamo rāsi siyā
 bho  tvaṃ satto iti paccesi kaṃ saddahasi sattoti gahaṇaṃ  tava diṭṭhigataṃ suddhasaṅkhārapuñjo ayaṃ  loke satto na upalabbhati
 bhikkhu imaṃ ganthaṃ accantaṃ satatampi cinteti  bhikkhuno paramā paññā  vipulabhāvaṃ gacchati
 yo bhikkhu  abhidhammāvatāraṃ  uttamaṃ abhiññāṇakaraṃ  kaṅkhacchedakaraṃ  pemakaraṃ sadā paṭhati suṇāti tassa bhikkhuno mati  sāsane ṭhite  paṭiphullati
