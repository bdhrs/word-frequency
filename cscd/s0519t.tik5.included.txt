ti ca mūlapadāni eva sandhāya vadati  taṃtaṃmūlapadānuppavesato kusale vā viññāte akusalo paṭipakkho akusale vā kusalo paṭipakkho  saṃvaṇṇiyamānasuttapadānurūpato upaparikkhitabbo anvesanā upaparikkhā disālokananti vuccati ti tathā anvesitvā tehi dhammehi disā vavatthapetvā so so nayo niddhāretvā yojetabbo yathā mūlapadesu mūlapadānaṃ anuppaveso saṃvaṇṇito imināva nayena mūlapadato mūlapadānaṃ niddhāraṇāti veditabbāti dassento ti āha tiādi kāraṇavacanaṃ
tattha ti ekekasmiṃ naye ti taṇhādike ekasmiṃ mūlapadadhamme sarūpato niddhāraṇavasena vā viññāte ti tadaññamūlapadabhūtā sabbe lobhādayo viññātā nayassa bhūmicaraṇāyogyatāya pakāsā pākaṭā honti  ntiādi nayattayadisābhūtadhammānaṃ matthakapāpanena tiṇṇaṃ nayānaṃ kūṭaggahaṇaṃ taṃ heṭṭhā vuttanayameva
puna tiādi kammanayadvayassa vibhāgavibhāvanaṃ taṃ viññeyyameva
