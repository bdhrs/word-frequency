16. Sahaseyyavinicchayakathā
79 .  ‘‘duvidhaṃ sahaseyyaka’’ duvidhaṃ, sahaseyyakaṃ Dirattatiratta Sahaseyyaṃ Seyya seyyaṃ kappeyyā Diyaḍḍhahatthubbedhenā Pañcahi chadanehī Vācuggatavasenā
Ekūpacāro   ‘‘ekūpacāro Tesaṃ payoge payoge bhikkhussa āpattī
80 . Uparimatalena saddhiṃ asambaddhabhittikassā ‘‘heṭṭhāpāsāde’’ Nānūpacāre ‘‘uparimatalepī’’ ‘‘chadanabbhantare’’ Sabhāsaṅkhepenā Aḍḍhakuṭṭake senāsane  ‘‘sabhāsaṅkhepenā aḍḍhakuṭṭake Vāḷasaṅghāṭo
Parikkhepassa bahi gate  ‘‘parikkhepassa bahi gate Aparicchinnagabbhūpacāre paricchinnagabbhūpacāraṃ Sabbagabbhepi pavisantī  ‘‘gabbhaparikkhepoyeva hissa parikkhepo’’
81 . ekasālasanniveso dvisālādisannivesā Aṭṭha pācittiyānī ‘‘Satta pācittiyānī’’ cūḷakaṃ, upaḍḍhaṃ, ‘‘mātugāmena…pe… ayameva vinicchayo’’  
Iti vinayasaṅgahasaṃvaṇṇanābhūte vinayālaṅkāre
Sahaseyyavinicchayakathālaṅkāro nāma
Soḷasamo paricchedo.
