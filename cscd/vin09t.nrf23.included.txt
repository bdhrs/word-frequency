


paṭhamaṃ  uttānatthameva


ti yaṃ cīvaraṃ bhājāpitāya attanā laddhaṃ tameva nissaggiyaṃ hoti taṃ vinayakammaṃ katvāpi attanā na labbhati tenāha ntiādi tattha ti ayyāya dammīti evaṃ nissaṭṭhaṃ paṭilabhitvāpi nti yathā dāyakehi dinnaṃ tatheva upanetabbaṃ akālacīvarapakkheyeva ṭhapetabbanti vuttaṃ hoti


nti me etaṃ sakasaññāya gahitattā pācittiyañceva dukkaṭañca vuttaṃ itarathā bhaṇḍagghena kāretabbaṃ


ti  paṭhamaviññattito ūnatarassāpi aññassa paṭilābhena
ti yaṃ paṭhamaṃ viññattaṃ thokattā tasmiṃ appahonte puna taññeva viññāpentiyāti attho pi atthe  satī ti paṭhamaṃ viññattito aññenapi atthe sati ti yaṃ paṭhamaṃ viññattaṃ tena saddhiṃ aññañca kiṃ vuttaṃ hoti sace paṭhamaṃ sappi viññattaṃ yamakaṃ pacitabbanti ca vejjena vuttattā telenāpi attho hoti tato telenāpi me atthoti evamādinā aññañca viññāpentiyāti vuttaṃ hoti ti sace kahāpaṇassa sappi ābhataṃ hoti iminā mūlena dviguṇaṃ telaṃ labbhati telenāpi ca idaṃ kiccaṃ nippajjati tasmā telaṃ āharāti evaṃ ānisaṃsaṃ dassetvā


ti paṭhamaṃ cetāpitato yaṃ kiñci aññaṃ cetāpeyya


ti yaṃ cetāpeti tato aññassatthāya dinnena ti purimassevatthadīpanaṃ
ti yadatthāya dinno taṃ cetāpetvā avasesaṃ aññassatthāya upanentiyā ti dāyake āpucchitvā nti evarūpesu upaddavesu aññassa yassa kassaci atthāya upanentīnaṃ


na  kevalaṃ teneva parikkhārenāti āha ti vuttañhetaṃ pāḷiyaṃ bhikkhuniyo tena ca parikkhārena sayampi yācitvā bhesajjaṃ cetāpetvā paribhuñjiṃsūti  mātikāyaṃ pana gamyamānattā pisaddo nappayutto gamyamānatthassa hi saddassa payogaṃ pati kāmacāroti yutti


aṭṭhamanavamadasamāni uttānatthāneva




yasmā pavāritaṭṭhāne viññatti nāma na paṭisedhetabbā tasmā bhagavā dhammanimantanavasena pavāritaṭṭhāne vadethāyye yenatthoti  vuttāya catukkaṃsaparamaṃ viññāpetabbanti paricchedaṃ dassetīti veditabbaṃ teneva nti vuttaṃ nti pasenadikosalarājānaṃ


dutiyaṃ  uttānatthameva
