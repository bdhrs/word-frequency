5. Indriyavibhaṅgo
1. Abhidhammabhājanīyavaṇṇanā
219 . Cakkhudvāre   indaṭṭhaṃ kāretī
Katthaci dve 
‘‘khīṇāsavasseva uppajjanato’’
indaliṅgaṭṭho,  indaliṅgaṭṭho indasiṭṭhaṃ Juṭṭhaṃ ‘‘yathāyoga’’ Tenā Etthā Ulliṅgenti ‘‘kānici gocarāsevanāyā’’ ‘‘kānici bhāvanāsevanāyā’’ Ādhipaccaṃ
Heṭṭhā Amoho eva, Sesāni sarūpeneva āgatāni 
 ‘‘ajjhattadhammaṃ pariññāyā’’ ñāpanatthaṃ Tato anantaraṃ bhāvetabbattā
‘‘kicca’’ vasavattāpanaṃ anuvidhānaṃ ‘‘yathāsakaṃ oḷārikākārānupāpana’’ Ādi ‘‘amatābhimukhabhāvapaccayatā cā’’
220 . Evaṃ   santepī sabbasaṅgāhakāni,
Abhidhammabhājanīyavaṇṇanā niṭṭhitā.
2. Pañhapucchakavaṇṇanā
223 . Idha anābhaṭṭha ‘‘Rūpamissakattā anārammaṇesu rūpadhammesu saṅgahita’’  
Pañhapucchakavaṇṇanā niṭṭhitā.
Indriyavibhaṅgavaṇṇanā niṭṭhitā.
