

ti na āgato anāgato na tāva sampattoti attho anāgate aṃsā koṭṭhāsā tesaṃ ñāṇaṃ anāgataṃsañāṇaṃ yaṃ yaṃ kammaṃ yathākammaṃ taṃ upagacchati jānātīti  vipākacetanājānanakañāṇaṃ
 tesu ārammaṇattikesu ti taṃ kāyaṃ cittaṃ sannissitaṃ karitvā tameva kāyacittacetasikavasena mahaggate cittasmiṃ samāropeti ñāṇaṃ iddhividhañāṇaṃ tato kāyārammaṇato kāyassa parittattā kāmāvacarattā parittārammaṇaṃ ñāṇaṃ siyā
 yo yogī dissamānena kāyena gantukāmo yadā bhaveyya so yogī tadā cittaṃ  rūpakāye sannissitaṃ katvā taṃ pādakajjhānacittaṃ kāyavasena kāye āropeti tadā jhānārammaṇato taṃ ñāṇaṃ mahaggatagocaraṃ hoti yaṃ ñāṇaṃ yadā  dhammajātaṃ  dhammajātaṃ  ārammaṇaṃ karoti tadā taṃ ñāṇaṃ atītārammaṇaṃ hoti tadā anāgatārammaṇaṃ ārammaṇakaraṇakāle anāgatagocaraṃ hoti dissamānena kāyena tassa bhikkhuno gamane pana sati  yogino ñāṇassa gocaro paccuppanno nāma hoti iti viniddise
 kāyaṃ cittassa vasena citte āropeti cittaṃ kāyassa vasena kāye āropeti iti pariṇāmanakālasmiṃ tassa yogino ñāṇaṃ ajjhattārammaṇaṃ siyā ti parittamahaggataatītaanāgatapaccuppannaajjhattabahiddhārammaṇasaṅkhātesu  ārammaṇesu sampavattati
nti mahaggatānaṃ jānane
 cetopariyañāṇassa maggārammaṇatā pariyāyena eva  ñātā

 tesu paccuppannesu yaṃ paccuppannaṃ khaṇādikaṃ  uppādaṭṭhitibhaṅgakkhaṇasampattaṃ  khaṇāyeva taṃ khaṇāvayavaṃ paccuppannaṃ nāma nti ekacittadvicittavīthivārapariyāpannaṃ santatipaccuppannaṃ nāma nti addhāpaccuppannanti attho
 yena  āvajjanacittena dhamme āvajjati yena cetasā jānāti  dvinnaṃ āvajjanajavanacittānaṃ sahaṭṭhānassa abhāvā  khaṇādikattayaṃ paccuppannantiādikaṃ ekassa cittamekena avassaṃ pana vijjhatiiti pariyosānaṃ vacanaṃ na yujjati āvajjanajavanānaṃ nānārammaṇabhāvaṃ pattito āpajjanato ca na yujjati  tesaṃ āvajjanajavanānaṃ ekārammaṇabhāve anicchite tasmiṃ ṭhāne na yujjati aññasmiṃ pana ṭhāne maggavīthiyaṃ āvajjanaparikammajavanānaṃ kasiṇādikaṃ ārammaṇaṃ hoti gotrabhulokuttaracittānañca nibbānaṃ ārammaṇaṃ hoti ettha ārammaṇajavanānaṃ ekārammaṇatā anicchatā
 ekabbhavaparicchinnaṃ paccuppannanti pacchimanti vatvā puna aññena pariyāyena taṃ paccuppannaṃ dassetuṃ idaṃ  addhānāma paccuppannaṃ javanavārato dīpetabbaṃ  vacanaṃ niddiṭṭhaṃ yadā  yogī parassa cittaṃ ñātuṃ āvajjati tassa āvajjanamano  paccuppannaṃ manodvārāvajjanacittaṃ ārammaṇaṃ katvā  cittena saddhiṃ nirujjhati tesu niruddhesu tassa yogino cattāri pañca vā javanāni jāyanti  catunnaṃ pañcannaṃ javanānaṃ yaṃ pacchimaṃ cittaṃ iddhicittamudīritaṃ iddhicittato  kāmāvacaracittāni  kāmāvacare iddhijavanānaṃ tadeva ca paracittaṃ ārammaṇaṃ hoti yasmā  javanāni ekārammaṇataṃ yanti tesaṃ javanānaṃ nānārammaṇatā addhāvasā na bhaveyya paccuppannārammaṇato tesaṃ javanānaṃ ekārammaṇabhāvepi iddhimānasameva ca parassa cittaṃ jānāti  kāmāvacarāni pana cittāni parassa cittaṃ na jānanti nti cakkhuviññāṇaṃ rūpaṃ passati tathā itaraṃ cittaṃ na passati
