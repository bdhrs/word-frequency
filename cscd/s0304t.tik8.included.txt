
1 paṭhamavaggo

nti  na saṅkhataṃ hetupaccayeti tenāha nti ti mettāyantena ti karuṇāyantena ti ādiyitvāti ayamatthotiāha ti ti aviparītadhammassa desanā paṭipattimpi sāvakā viya garuko bhagavā  attano adhiṭṭhitaṃ niyyāteti
bhikkhāsampattikālādīnaṃ sattannaṃ sappāyānaṃ sampattiyā labbhanakāle vipattikāle pana ettha vuttavipariyāyena attho veditabbo nti dukkhabahulatāya dāruṇaṃ  laddhabbā ti tumhākaṃ dātabbā anusāsanī

2 dutiyavaggo

 tattha ca natthi ettha taṇhāsaṅkhātaṃ nataṃ natthi etasmiṃ vā adhigate puggalabhāvoti nti etthāpi eseva nayo saccadhammatāya  vaṭṭadukkhato pārametīti ti sukhumaṭṭhena nipuṇaṃ tato eva  niccasabhāvattā natthi etassa nidassananti vā  etasmiṃ adhigate natthi saṃsāre papañcanti vā 
etasmiṃ  adhigate puggalassa maraṇaṃ natthīti vā  atappakaṭṭhena vā  sukhahetutāya vā  taṇhā khīyanti etthāti 
aññassa tādisassa abhāvato vimhāpanīyatāya ti kutoci paccayato anibbattameva hutvā  vijjamānaṃ tenāha ti natthi ettha dukkhanti niddukkhaṃ tassa bhāvo  tasmā  ītirahitaṃ vānaṃ vuccati taṇhā tadabhāvena  byābajjhaṃ vuccati dukkhaṃ tadabhāvena  paramatthato saccato suddhibhāvena kāmā eva puthujjanehi allīyitabbato  esa nayo sesesupi ti patiṭṭhābhāvena vaṭṭadukkhato muccitukāmānaṃ dīpasadisaṃ oghehi anajjhottharaṇīyattā ti allīyituṃ arahabhāvato ti saparatāyanaṭṭhena ti bhayassa hiṃsanaṭṭhena  uttamaṃ ti gandhabbaṭṭhānaṃ
