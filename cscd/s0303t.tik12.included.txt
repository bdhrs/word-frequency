

ti  samādhismiṃ kusalo tayidaṃ samādhikosallattaṃ saha jhānaṅgayogena catubbidho jhānasamādhi tasmā taṃ taṃ vibhāgaṃ jānantassa siddhaṃ hotīti āha  ntiādi tattha vitakkavicārapītisukhekaggatāvasena paṭhamaṃ pañcaṅgikaṃ pītisukhekaggatāvasena dutiyaṃ tivaṅgikaṃ sukhekaggatāvasena tatiyaṃ duvaṅgikaṃ upekkhekaggatāvasena catutthaṃ duvaṅgikamevāti evaṃ tasmiṃ tasmiṃ jhāne taṃtaṃaṅgānaṃ vavatthāne kusalo ti samāpajjane kusalo ti tosetvā ti samādhānassa paṭipakkhadhammānaṃ dūrīkaraṇena sahakārīkāraṇañca samādhānena samāpajjane cittaṃ samatthaṃ katvā ti sesā tayo koṭṭhāsā tatiyādīsu nayesu akusalopi jhānatthāya paṭipannattā jhāyītevāti vutto


 dutiyādisuttesu ti ettha antogadhahetuattho saddo tasmiñca pana kusaloti atthoti āha  ti nti sattaṭṭhaaccharāmattaṃ khaṇaṃ jhānaṃ ṭhapetuṃ na sakkoti adhiṭṭhānavasībhāvassa anipphāditattā  kālena  vuṭṭhānavasībhāvassa anipphāditattā kallaṃ jātaṃ assāti  tasmiṃ kallite kallitabhāvena kasiṇārammaṇesu idaṃ nāma asukassāti visayavasena samāpajjituṃ asakkonto ti samādhismiṃ nipphāditabbe tassa gocare kammaṭṭhānasaññite pavattiṭṭhāne  satisampajaññavirahito  keci pana kammaṭṭhānagocaro paṭhamajjhānādikaṃ evaṃ samāpajjitabbaṃ evaṃ bahulīkātabbanti ajānanto tattha akusalo nāmāti vadanti nti kammaṭṭhānaṃ visesabhāgiyatāya  abhinīharituṃ ti cittīkārī ti niyatakārī ti appanākosallā ti saddena sakkaccakāripadādīnaṃyeva saṅgaho daṭṭhabbo catukkānaṃ vuttattā tenāha ti  samādhikusalotiādinā nayena desanāya pavattattā na hi lokuttaradhammesu akosallaṃ nāma labbhati yadi akosallaṃ na kusalasaddena visesitabbatā siyāti
