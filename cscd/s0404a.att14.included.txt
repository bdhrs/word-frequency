

 pañcamassa paṭhame ti kāḷudāyitthero ti aññāsi ti mahāpañño ti yo jhānaṃ abujjhi ti ekībhāvavasena paṭilīno ceva uttamaṭṭhena ca nisabho ti buddhamuni ti ekena kāraṇena kāmasambādhassa hi abhāvamatteneva paṭhamajjhānaṃ okāsādhigamo nāma na sabbathā sabbaṃ ti tasmimpi paṭhamajjhāne sambādho  paṭipīḷanaṃ atthiyeva tatrāpitthītipi pāṭho ti tasmiṃ pana jhāne kiṃ sambādho nāma ti ayaṃ vitakkavicārānaṃ aniruddhabhāvo sambādho saṃpīḷā nāma iminā upāyena sabbavāresu attho veditabbo ti na ekena kāraṇena atha kho āsavakkhayo nāma sabbasambādhānaṃ pahīnattā sabbena sabbaṃ okāsādhigamo nāmāti

 dutiye nti yena yena kāraṇena yena yenākārena taṃ paṭhamajjhānasaṅkhātaṃ āyatanaṃ hoti ti tena tena kāraṇena tena tenākārena taṃ samāpattiṃ sahajātanāmakāyena phusitvā viharati samāpajjatīti attho ti yasmā tena nāmākāyena paṭhamajjhānaṃ sacchikataṃ tasmā iminā pariyāyena kāyasakkhi vutto ti yattakaṃ kāyena sacchikātabbaṃ sabbassa katattā ayaṃ nippariyāyena kāyasakkhi nāma

 tatiye ti taṃ paṭhamajjhānavipassanāpaññāya jānāti idhāpi pariyāyanippariyāyā purimanayeneva veditabbā yathā ca idha evaṃ ito paresupi

 catutthaṃ  ubhayena veditabbaṃ ettha ca ti ubhatobhāgehi samathavipassanānaṃ paccanīkakilesehi vimutto pariyosāne pana samāpattiyā rūpakāyato ariyamaggena nāmakāyato vimuttoyeva ubhatobhāgavimuttoti veditabbo

 pañcamādīsu ti sayaṃ passitabbako nti kilesanibbānaṃ nti tasseva vevacanaṃ nti paṭhamajjhānādinā tena tena aṅgena nibbānaṃ nti ismiṃyeva attabhāve nibbānaṃ sesaṃ sabbattha uttānatthamevāti



 ito  paresu nti nirupaddavaṃ ti khemabhāvaṃ patto ti sikkhāya dubbalabhāvakaraṇāni sesaṃ sabbattha uttānatthamevāti
