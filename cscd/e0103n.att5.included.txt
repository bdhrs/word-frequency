

 yathāvitthāritassa  atthassa atidesopi vitthāraṭṭhāneyeva tiṭṭhatīti āha ti nti āpokasiṇajjhānaṃ āpokasiṇakammaṭṭhānaṃ vā nti pathavīkasiṇakammaṭṭhāne vuttanayena vitthāretabbaṃ ti kate vā akate vāti sabbaṃ vitthāretabbanti ettakampi avatvā sāmuddikena loṇudakena bharito jalāsayo  nīlādivaṇṇasaṅkarapariharaṇatthaṃ nti vuttaṃ nti bhūmiṃ sampattaṃ nti yādisaṃ ākāsajaṃ udakaṃ tādisaṃ tenāha nti yaṃ pana udakanti sambandho tiādīsu yaṃ vattabbaṃ taṃ pathavīkasiṇakathāyaṃ vuttameva  pana idha paggharaṇalakkhaṇaṃ veditabbaṃ
ti pathavīkasiṇabhāvanāyaṃ vuttanayeneva taraṅguṭṭhānādi pheṇamissatādi ca idha ti yogāvacaro ti paṭibhāganimittassa

 sinehavantāni  na pheggudārūni jālāya ciraṭṭhitatthaṃ siniddhasāradāruggahaṇaṃ ti khandhaso karitvā ti jāletvā ti aviraḷavasena pavattaaggijālāyaṃ
nti patamānasadisaṃ ti vuttanayena yathā kasiṇamaṇḍalaṃ paññāyati evaṃ anabhisaṅkhate kevale tejasmiṃ nimittaṃ gaṇhantassa

nti  ettha saddo hetuattho yasmāti attho tassa tasmāti iminā sambandho nti ucchukhette yathāṭhitānaṃ aggaṃ nti  vātena calitaṃ nti sabbaso calitaṃ ti yasmā vāyokasiṇaṃ upalakkhetīti evaṃ vuttaṃ aṭṭhakathāyaṃ tasmā nti upari pattānaṃ vasena samasīsaṃ hutvā ṭhitaṃ ti etthāpi samasīsaṃ ṭhitaṃ ghanapattaveḷuṃ vā ghanapattarukkhaṃ vāti ānetvā yojetabbaṃ ekaṅgulādippamāṇesu kesesu rassabhāvato dīghataresu olambanato viraḷesu anuppavesato vātappahāro na paññāyatīti ggahaṇaṃ ggahaṇañca kataṃ ti ucchaggādīnaṃ pacalanākāraggahaṇamukhena tesaṃ pahārake vātasaṅghāte satiṃ upaṭṭhapetvā ti tasmiṃ kāyapadesassa saṅghaṭṭanavasena pavatte vāyupiṇḍe saṅghaṭṭanākāraggahaṇamukhena satiṃ upaṭṭhapetvā usumavaṭṭisadisanti etena purimakasiṇassa viya imassāpi nimittassa saṃviggahataṃ dasseti niccalanti iminā niccalabhāvoyeva uggahanimittato imassa visesoti paṭibhāganimittassāpi usumavaṭṭisadisatāva vibhāvitā hoti

 añjanarājivaṭṭādi nti aviraḷavikasitanīlavaṇṇapupphasañchannaṃ pupphagacchaṃ ti akatādhikārena ggahanena nīlaṃ girikaṇṇikamāha  samuggapidhānaṃ ti nīluppalādīnaṃ kesaravaṇṭāni apanetvā kevalehi pattehiyeva nti nīlavaṇṇaṃ vatthaṃ gahetvā bhaṇḍikaṃ viya bandhitvā yathā nīlamaṇḍalaṃ hutvā paññāyati tathā ti assa caṅkoṭakassa karaṇḍapaṭalassa vā mukhavaṭṭiyaṃ  indanīlamaṇimayaṃ tālavaṇṭaṃ

 pītakasiṇe nti ikkaṭādimālāgacchaṃ haritālaṃ manosilā vā ti  pattaṅganāmikā pītavaṇṇapupphā ekā gacchajāti tassa pupphehi nti cetiyaṅgaṇe kataṃ āsanapūjaṃ  ti saddena ākulikiṅkirātapupphādīnaṃ saṅgaho daṭṭhabbo

 lohitaṅgamaṇiādi  gerukajātihiṅgulikādi

 odātakasiṇe nti nandiyāvattādimālāgacchaṃ  kakkaṭimuttasetikādi

ti yathā sūriyālokādivasena obhāsanimittuppādanaṃ vuttaṃ tassa obhāsamaṇḍalassa na ciraṭṭhititāya tathā nimittuppādanaṃ kātuṃ asakkontena ghaṭamukhena niggacchanakaobhāsassa mahantabhāvato ti vuttaṃ nti bhittiabhimukhaṃ nti bhittiādīsu uṭṭhitapākatikaālokamaṇḍalasadisaṃ nti bhagavato byāmappabhā viya bahalo vippasanno ca hutvā puñjabhūto āloko atthi ce taṃsadisoti attho

ti yehi bhittipariyantādīhi paricchinnaṃ taṃ chiddaṃ taṃsadisaṃ tenavākārena uggahanimittaṃ upaṭṭhātīti attho ti uggahanimittassa avaḍḍhanīyataṃ dassetuṃ vuttaṃ sabbampi hi uggahanimittaṃ vaḍḍhiyamānaṃ na vaḍḍhatiyeva satipi ca vaḍḍhetukāmatāyaṃ vaḍḍhanā na sambhavati bhāvanāya paridubbalattā bhāvanāvasena hi nimittavaḍḍhanā  paṭibhāganimittaṃ pana tasmiṃ uppanne bhāvanā thirāti katvā ti vuttaṃ
kiñcāpi  pathavīkasiṇādīni rūpajhānārammaṇāni aṭṭheva kasiṇāni sarūpato āgatāni odātakasiṇe pana ālokakasiṇaṃ ākāsakasiṇe ca paricchinnākāsakasiṇaṃ antogadhaṃ katvā desanā katāti adhippāyenāha ti ti pubbe viya asādhāraṇaṃ tasmiṃ tasmiṃ kasiṇe paṭiniyatameva atthaṃ aggahetvā asādhāraṇato sādhāraṇato ca tattha tattha pakiṇṇakaṃ visaṭaṃ atthaṃ gahetvā pavattā pakiṇṇakakathāpi vijānitabbā

ti ettha saddena yassa kassaci pathavīpakkhiyassa vatthuno nimmānādiṃ saṅgaṇhāti ti etthāpi ākāse vā udake vāti ānetvā sambandhitabbaṃ ti nīlādivaṇṇaṃ anāmasitvā parittaappamāṇanayeneva ti saddena sarīrato udakadhārānimmānādiṃ saṅgaṇhāti
ti bahūsu kappāsapicusāradāruādīsu ekajjhaṃ rāsibhūtesu ṭhitesu yaṃ yadeva icchati tassa tasseva ḍahanasamatthatā idha saddena andhakāravidhamanādiṃ saṅgaṇhāti
vāyugatiyā gamanaṃ  atisīghagamanaṃ idha saddena yadicchitadesantaraṃ pāpuṇanādiṃ saṅgaṇhāti
 suvaṇṇabhāvādhiṭṭhānaṃ seyyathāpi āyasmā pilindavaccho  tiṇaṇḍupagapāsādādīnaṃ ti suvaṇṇadubbaṇṇanayena
vaṇṇakasiṇesu  tattha tattha saddena nīlobhāsanimmānādīnaṃ saṅgaho daṭṭhabbo ti saddena samuddādīnaṃ saṅgaho daṭṭhabbo
 dasapi kasiṇāni ti imaṃ vaḍḍhanādivisesaṃ pāpuṇanti ti ekacco ti bhāvanāpaññāya sañjānāti saddena āpokasiṇantiādipāḷiṃ saṅgaṇhāti
 pathavīkasiṇameko sañjānātīti pāḷipadāni ānetvā sambandhitabbaṃ tañca kho vaḍḍhanavasena tenāha ti ntiādīsupi eseva nayo pubbe vaḍḍhanakāle payojanaṃ anapekkhitvā vaḍḍhentānaṃ vasena vuttattā idāni ti āha kasiṇaṃ vaḍḍhetīti attho yathā kinti āha ti uddhañce rūpaṃ daṭṭhukāmo uddhaṃ ālokaṃ pasāreti adho ce rūpaṃ daṭṭhukāmo adho ālokaṃ pasāreti samantato ce rūpaṃ daṭṭhukāmo samantato ālokaṃ pasāreti evamayaṃ kasiṇanti attho
ti pathavīkasiṇādīsu ekekassa nti aññakasiṇabhāvānupagamanadīpanatthaṃ na aññaṃ pathavīādi na hi udakena ṭhitaṭṭhāne sasambhārapathavī atthi ti āpokasiṇādinā saṅkaro ti sabbesu āpokasiṇādīsu sesakasiṇesu ekadese aṭṭhatvā anavasesena pharaṇappamāṇassa aggahaṇato pharaṇaṃ  teneva hi nesaṃ kasiṇasamaññā tathā cāha tiādi tattha ti bhāvanācittena ālambanaṃ karonto bhāvanācittañhi kasiṇaṃ parittaṃ vā vipulaṃ vā ekakkhaṇe sakalameva manasi karoti na ekadesanti
ti pañcasu ānantariyakammesu yena kenaci samannāgatā ti ahetukadiṭṭhi akiriyadiṭṭhi natthikadiṭṭhīti tīsu micchādiṭṭhīsu yāya kāyaci niyatāya micchādiṭṭhiyā samannāgatā ti ubhatobyañjanakā paṇḍakā ca kāmañcete ahetukapaṭisandhikattā vipākāvaraṇena samannāgatā honti tathāpi tibbakilesattā kilesāvaraṇena samannāgatā vuttā ti ahetukapaṭisandhikā  dvihetukapaṭisandhikā ca duhetukapaṭisandhikānampi hi ariyamaggapaṭivedho jhānapaṭilābho  ca natthi tasmā tepi vipākāvaraṇena samannāgatā eva
nti maggassa anulomapaṭipadāyaṃ saccānulomikāyaṃ vipassanāyaṃ ti kattukamyatāchandarahitāti  vuttaṃ tampi nibbānādhigamatthameva kattukamyatāchandaṃ sandhāya vuttanti daṭṭhabbaṃ uttarakurukāpi manussā mārādayo viya acchandikaṭṭhānaṃ paviṭṭhā nibbutichandarahitattā ti bhavaṅgapaññāya parihīnā bhavaṅgapaññāya paripuṇṇāyapi yassa bhavaṅgaṃ lokuttarassa pādakaṃ na hoti sopi duppaññoyevāti  vuttaṃ yasmiṃ hi bhavaṅge vattamāne taṃsantatiyaṃ lokuttaraṃ nibbattati taṃ tassa pādakaṃ nāma hoti
ti anavajjadhammesu sukhavipākadhammesu vā nti adhigantuṃ ti kasiṇakammaṭṭhāneyeva nti kammāvaraṇasamannāgatādīnaṃ ti yasmā ete vipākantarāyādayo evaṃ atthajānikarā anatthahetubhūtā ca tasmā tiṇṇameva cettha antarāyānaṃ gahaṇaṃ itarassa sappaṭikārattā kammantarāyapakkhikattā vāti daṭṭhabbaṃ ti saddena tajjaṃ yonisomanasikārādiṃ saṅgaṇhāti nti kammaphalasaddhaṃ ratanattayasaddhañca nti bhāvanānuyoge tibbakattukamyatāsaṅkhātaṃ kusalacchandaṃ nti pārihāriyapaññaṃ ti yathā bhāvanā ijjhati tathā paribrūhetvā yaṃ panettha atthato na vibhattaṃ taṃ suviññeyyattā heṭṭhā vuttanayattā ca na vibhattaṃ
