 chayime  bhikkhave dhammā diṭṭhisampannassa puggalassa pahīnā katame cha sakkāyadiṭṭhi vicikicchā sīlabbataparāmāso apāyagamanīyo rāgo apāyagamanīyo doso apāyagamanīyo moho ime kho bhikkhave cha dhammā diṭṭhisampannassa puggalassa pahīnāti chaṭṭhaṃ
