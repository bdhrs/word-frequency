

 dutiyassa  paṭhame pāṭiyekkaṃ pucchā ca vissajjanā ca katā dutiye ekatova

 tatiye ti mātikaṃ nikkhipitvā ti pañcasu ṭhānesu etadagge ṭhapentena satthārā saṃvaṇṇito ti guṇasambhāvanāya sambhāvito ti sakkoti ti atikkamitvā ti jānitabbakaṃ  jānāti ti passitabbakaṃ passati ti cakkhu viya bhūto jāto nibbatto ti ñāṇasabhāvo ti dhammasabhāvo ti seṭṭhasabhāvo ti vattuṃ samattho ti pavattetuṃ samattho ti atthaṃ nīharitvā dassetā ti yathā amhākaṃ bhagavā byākareyya

 catutthe ti evaṃnāmako ti cittuppādasatāni ti yehi cittaṭṭhānasatehi anuyuñjiyamānā ti viraddhā niggahitā evaṃ jānanti viraddhā mayaṃ niggahitā mayaṃ āropito no dosoti ti paṇḍitabhāvatthāya kāraṇāni

 pañcame nti lokiyaṃ orimatīraṃ nti lokuttaraṃ pārimatīraṃ ti nibbānagāmino ti sakkāyadiṭṭhitīraṃyeva anudhāvati ti sammā akkhāte navavidhe lokuttaradhamme anudhammavattino tassa dhammassānucchavikāya sahasīlāya pubbabhāgapaṭipattiyā pavattamānā nti maccuno ṭhānabhūtaṃ tebhūmakavaṭṭaṃ suduttaraṃ taritvā ti nibbānaṃ pāpuṇissanti
ti vaṭṭato vivaṭṭaṃ āgamma nti yasmiṃ kāyacittaupadhiviveke durabhiramaṃ tatrābhiratimiccheyya ti duvidhepi kāme pahāya ti nippalibodho ti gahaṇapaṭinissaggasaṅkhāte nibbāne ti catūhi upādānehi kiñcipi anupādiyitvā ye abhiratā ti te apaccayaparinibbānena parinibbutā nāmāti veditabbā chaṭṭhaṃ bhikkhūnaṃ desitaṃ

 sattame ti pāpassa paccorohaṇaṃ ti santharitvā nti vālikārāsissa ca aggiagārassa ca antare aṭṭhamaṃ bhikkhusaṅghassa desitaṃ sesaṃ sabbattha uttānatthamevāti
