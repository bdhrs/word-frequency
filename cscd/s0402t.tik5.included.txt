
 dutiyapaṇṇāsakassa  paṭhame hitaggahaṇena mettā vuttā hoti na karuṇā anukampāgahaṇena pana karuṇāti  nippariyāyato lokānukampā nāma sammāsambuddhādhīnāti ti manussadevasampattivasena dve sampattiyo tā dve nibbānasampatti cāti 
 dutiye bahuso loke na ciṇṇā na pavattā manussāti  kadācideva hi nesaṃ loke nibbatti abhūtapubbā bhūtāti 
 tatiye  tassa ekabuddhakhettabhāvato
 catutthe ti dhātuyo pakkhipitvā thūpakaraṇassa yuttā
 pañcame ti sayambhuñāṇena ti buddhavanto
 chaṭṭhe ti attasinehābhāvato na bhāyati sakkāyadiṭṭhiggahaṇañcettha nidassanamattaṃ attasinehassa paṭighassa tadekaṭṭhasammohassa ca vasena bhāyanaṃ hotīti tesampi pahīnattā na bhāyati aññathā sotāpannādīnaṃ abhayena bhavitabbaṃ siyā ti ettha ahaṃkārasammohanatādīnampi balavattāti vattabbaṃ
 sattame ti likhanti ti pana pāṭhoti
 navame ti antarāpaṇe ti dvīsu kinnaresu eko ambilikāphalañca addasāti ānetvā sambandho nti tintiṇīphalanti  vadanti caturambilanti apare ti pāḷiyaṃ vutte dve atthe
 dasame  yathāāraddhe kicce vattamāne antarā eva paṭigamanaṃ paṭivānaṃ natthi etassa paṭivānanti  tattha asaṃkocappatto tenāha tiādi
 ekādasame nti sahavāsaṃ yathā asappurisā saha vasantā aññamaññaṃ agāravena anādariyaṃ karonti tappaṭikkhepena sappurisānaṃ sagāravappaṭipattidassanaparamidaṃ suttaṃ daṭṭhabbaṃ
 dvādasame ti vivādāpannānaṃ bhikkhūnaṃ dvīsupi pakkhesu ti pavattamānā ti diṭṭhisannissayo paḷāso yugaggāho ti āhananto bādhento ti yassa uppajjati tassa tadaññesañca atthassa anabhirādhanavasena nti vacīsaṃsāroti sabbampetaṃ attano citte parisāya ca citteti ānetvā sambandho
