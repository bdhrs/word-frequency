
punapi rājā vāsimhi jimhamānase  pasanno hutvā tassatthāya mahāvihārasīmabbhantare  jetavanavihāraṃ kāretumārabhi mahāvihāravāsino bhikkhū taṃ nivāretuṃ asakkontā punapi tato apakkamiṃsu tadāpi mahāvihāro nava māsāni bhikkhūhi suñño ahosi rājā pana attano ajjhāsayavaseneva tattha  kāretvā tassa kuhakatissattherassa adāsiyeva tattha dakkhiṇagirivihārato  bhikkhū āgantvā vasiṃsu pacchā ca te rañño kāle (1067-1080-bu-va) vetullavādino ahesuṃ 
evaṃ ācariyabuddhaghosattherassa sīhaḷadīpamāgamanakālato (965-bu-va) pubbeyeva visuddhattheravādīhi mahāvihāravāsīhi viruddhasamayā  (455-bu-va)  (797-810-bu-va)  (829-845-bu-va) cāti tayo nikāyā uppannā ahesuṃ tesu pana abhayagirivāsinoyeva visesato pākaṭā ceva honti balavanto ca tathā hi te visuddhattheravādapiṭakañca vajjiputtakapariyāpannadhammarucinikāyapiṭakañca mahisāsakādinikāyapiṭakañca mahāyānapiṭakañca sampaṭicchanti tesu dhammarucinikāyapiṭakassa sampaṭicchitabhāvo pākaṭoyeva mahisāsakādinikāyapiṭakassa sampaṭicchitabhāvo pana nnāmassa cinabhikkhuno addhānakkamasallakkhaṇakathāya ceva aṭṭhakathāsu paṭikkhittavaṇṇapiṭakādināmavasena ca veditabbo tathā mahāyānapiṭakassa sampaṭicchitabhāvopi
