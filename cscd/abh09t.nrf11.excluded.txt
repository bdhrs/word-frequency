puggalo Upalabbhatī Saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā saccikaṭṭho Paramattho 
‘‘yo saccikaṭṭho’’  Na hevaṃ vattabbe
Ājānāhi niggaha ‘‘hañci puggalo’’ hañcī anulomaṭṭhapanā Tena vata re anulomapāpanā tenā Vatā Re Yaṃ tattha vadesī anulomaropanā   No ce pana vattabbe paṭilomaṭṭhapanā No ca vata re paṭilomapāpanā yaṃ tattha vadesī paṭilomaropanā
idaṃ te micchā
anulomapañcakaṃ
 puggalo nupalabbhatī ‘‘āmantā’’ ‘‘yo saccikaṭṭho’’ ‘‘na heva’’  ‘‘ājānāhi paṭikamma’’ ‘‘hañci puggalo’’ paṭikammacatukkaṃ
‘‘tvaṃ ce pana maññasī’’ tvaṃ ce pana maññasī ‘‘Vattabbe  kho’’ ‘‘no ca vattabbe’’ Tena tava tatthā hevaṃ paṭijāna Hevaṃ niggahetabbe Atha taṃ niggaṇhāmā suniggahito ca hosī ‘‘hañcī’’ idaṃ te micchā niggahacatukkaṃ
‘‘ese ce dunniggahite’’ ese ce dunniggahite Hevamevaṃ tattha dakkhā  ‘‘vattabbe kho’’ ‘‘no ca mayaṃ tayā’’ no ca mayaṃ tayā tattha hetāya paṭiññāyā  ‘‘hañci puggalo…pe… idaṃ te micchā’’ upanayanacatukkaṃ
niggamanacatukkaṃ na hevaṃ niggahetabbe Tena hī Hañci puggalo…pe… idaṃ te micchā Tena hi ye kate niggahe anulomapaccanīkaṃ
‘‘puggalo nupalabbhatī’’  ‘‘puggalo nupalabbhatī’’ ‘‘na heva’’ ‘‘ājānāhi niggaha’’
