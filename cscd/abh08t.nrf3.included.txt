




ahaṃ  ito paricchedato paraṃ ekavidhādikaṃ nayaṃ ābhidhammikabhikkhūnaṃ buddhiyā vuddhitthāya  desessāmi

 saccaṃ  kāmāvacare kusalākusalavipākā dasa pañca ca kiriyā tisso iti sabbe  cittuppādā ahetukā
 ahetukato cittuppādato  ekasattati cittuppādā  sahetukā nāmāti mahesinā  aviparītasabhāvena  paccayavādinā niddiṭṭhā
 savatthukaavatthukavasena tathā ubhayavasena savatthukavasena avatthukavasena ca sabbaṃ vuttappakārena ca mānasaṃ tividhaṃ hoti
 cittuppādā  paṭhamamaggo hasituppādo manodhātukiriyāpi ca domanassadvayañcāpi tecattālīsa mānasā  vatthuṃ vajjetvā na uppajjanti ekantena savatthukā nāma arūpāvacaravipākā ca ekantena  avatthukā mayā vuttato cittuppādato  dvecattālīsa cittāni  savatthukāvatthukavasena siyuṃ
 cittaṃ chaḷārammaṇakañceti  iminā mayā vuttappakārenāpi tividhaṃ cittaṃ siyā
 abhiññāmānasaṃ ṭhapetvā sabbaṃ mahaggatañcevāti  cittuppādā pana ekekārammaṇā viññeyyā dhīrena  citte manodhātuttayaṃ pañcārammaṇaṃ īritaṃ bhagavatā mayā vuttacittato  cittāni chaḷārammaṇikāni matāni satthunā tathā evaṃ cittaṃ kusalākusalādito tividhaṃ -saddena abyākataṃ gahetabbaṃ
 ahetukaṃ cittaṃ ekahetukañca cittaṃ dvihetukañca cittaṃ tihetukañca cittanti  iminā pakārena cittaṃ catubbidhaṃ vibhāvinā viññātabbaṃ
 heṭṭhā mayāpi niddiṭṭhā  cittuppādā ahetukā  cittaṃ ekahetukaṃ evaṃ udīritaṃ bhagavatā  kāmāvacare  kusalavasena ca vipākavasena ca kiriyāvasena ca  cittuppādā akusalā ca dasadhā cāti bāvīsati cittuppādā duhetukā  kāmāvacare  puññavasena ca vipākavasena ca kiriyāvasena ca  cittuppādā sabbaṃ mahaggatañceva  lokuttaracittañca tihetukaṃ
ti rūpairiyāpathaviññattijanakavasena rūpairiyāpathajanakavasena ca rūpajanakavasena ca tikiccājanakavasena cātiādīhi pakārehi sabbaṃ cittaṃ catubbidhaṃ hoti  tasmiṃ citte dvādasākusalā kāmadhātuyā kusalā tathā kāme dasa kiriyā abhiññāmānasaṃ dvayaṃ ime bāttiṃsa mānasā rūpāni samuṭṭhāpenti iriyāpathaṃ kappenti viññattiṃ janayanti kusalā mahaggatamānasā kiriyā ca mahaggatamānasā  anāsavacittāni chabbīsati ca mānasā rūpāni samuṭṭhāpenti iriyāpathaṃ  kappenti  viññattiṃ na janayantīti attho ime cittuppādā dukiccaniyatā  dasa viññāṇe ṭhapetvā dvīsu bhūmīsu  manodhātudvayasantīraṇattayamahāvipākarūpavipākānaṃ vasena aṭṭhārasa pākā manodhātu kiriyā cevāti  cittāni rūpāni samuṭṭhāpenti itaradvayaṃ na karonti  paṭhamaṃ dasa viññāṇeti vuttattā punāti vuttā arūpīsu vipākā ca  sattānaṃ paṭisandhicittañca arahato cuticittañca ime soḷasa mānasā tikiccāni na karonti
 ekakiccadvikiccatikiccacatukiccapañcakiccabhedena  pañcapakāraṃ cittaṃ  buddho  kathesi



 cittuppādā  javanakiccavaseneva  ekakicce  taṇhāmānadiṭṭhivirahitena satthunā  kathitā
 cittuppādā dassane savane tathā ghāyane sāyanaṭṭhāne phusane paṭipāṭiyā satthunā niyāmitā
 manodhātuttikaṃ āvajjane paṭicchane  cittuppādā  ekakiccabhāvaṃ  pattā bhavanti
 cittadvayaṃ dviṭṭhānikaṃ nāma udīritaṃ bhagavatā somanassayutaṃ cittaṃ pañcadvāre santīraṇaṃ siyā chadvāre tadālambaṇañca  atimahantārammaṇe sati siyā  tathā voṭṭhabbanaṃ pañcadvāresu voṭṭhabbanaṃ hoti manodvāresu pana  ārammaṇānaṃ āvajjanaṃ hoti idaṃ cittadvayaṃ dviṭṭhānikaṃ nāma hoti
 paṭisandhiyā ṭhānato paṭisandhiyā kiccavasena bhavaṅgassa ṭhānato bhavaṅgassa kiccavasena cutiyā ṭhānato  cutiyā kiccavasena te mahaggatavipākā nava  tikiccāti muninā matā
 paṭisandhibhavaṅgavasena  tadārammaṇavasena ca eva cutiṭṭhānavasena ca  cittāni catuṭṭhānikacittāni honti  vacanaṃ dhīro niddise kusalākusalapākopekkhāsahagatadvayaṃ pañcadvāre santīraṇaṃ bhave chadvārikesupi balavārammaṇe sati tadārammaṇatā siyā paṭisandhibhavaṅgānaṃ ṭhānavasena ca cutiṭṭhānavasena ca idaṃ cittadvayaṃ pañcaṭṭhānikacittaṃ nāmāti udīritaṃ muninā

 manodhātumanoviññāṇadhātudvayaṃ manodhātuṃ kusalavipākaakusalavipākakiriyāvasena  manoviññāṇadhātuñca kusalākusalābyākatavasena  cakkhusotaghānajivhākāyaviññāṇehi saha cittaṃ ekādasavidhaṃ hoti  idaṃ vacanaṃ paṇḍito paridīpaye
 manoviññāṇadhātumpi  kusalākusalavipākakiriyāvasena catudhā vibhajitvāna taṃ  cittaṃ cakkhusotaghānajivhākāyaviññāṇehi saha tippakārāya manodhātuyā ca saha dvādasadhāpi dhīro vade
 cittaṃ cuddasaṭṭhānabhedena cuddasadhā bhave paṭisandhiyā vasena ca bhavaṅgavasena ca cutiyā vasena ca āvajjanassa vasena ca pañcannaṃ  kiccānaṃ vasena ca sampaṭicchanacetaso vasena ca santīraṇassa vasena ca voṭṭhabbanajavanānaṃ vasena ca yathā evaṃ tadārammaṇacittassa vasena  cāti  iminā pakārena  kiccabhedena cuddasadhā cittaṃ hoti  idaṃ vacanaṃ dhīro  paridīpeyya
