

 ekādasamassa  paṭhame ti asubhakammaṭṭhānaṃ ti mettākammaṭṭhānaṃ ti sahavipassanā maggapaññā

 chaṭṭhe ti sassatadiṭṭhi ti attānaṃ anugatā vīsativatthukā sakkāyadiṭṭhi ti dvāsaṭṭhividhāpi diṭṭhi ti maggasammādiṭṭhi natthi dinnantiādikā vā micchādiṭṭhi kammassakatañāṇaṃ sammādiṭṭhi

 sattame ti dasa akusalakammapathā

 dasame ti anadhivāsakabhāvo sesaṃ sabbattha uttānamevāti
