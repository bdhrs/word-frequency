

 catutthassa  paṭhame ti samaṇākappena sampanno ti vaḍḍhanīyo na hoti dutiyaṃ uttānameva

 tatiye ti kāyabalena ca ñāṇabalena ca samannāgatattā paṭibalo

 catutthe nti patitaṭṭhānañca bhinnaṭṭhānañca ti paṭipākatikaṃ karoti ti idaṃ pavāritakulānaṃ vasena vuttaṃ

 pañcame ti pañcasu sīlesu ti catusaccadhammadassane patiṭṭhāpeti nti sabbasakkārānaṃ arahe ratanattayeva gataṃ tīsu vatthūsu garucittīkāraṃ upaṭṭhapethāti attho chaṭṭhaṃ uttānameva

 sattame ti āvāsaṃ balavagiddhivasena gilitvā viya ṭhito sesaṃ sabbaṃ uttānamevāti
