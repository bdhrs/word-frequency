

 catutthassa paṭhame ti hitaññū hitapariyāyo hettha attha-saddo attattho paratthotiādīsu (mahāni 69 cūḷani mogharājamāṇavapucchāniddeso 85 paṭi ma 35) viya yasmā cesa paresaṃ hitaṃ jānanto te attani rañjeti tasmā vuttaṃ ti ti aparādhānurūpe daṇḍane ti balakāye ti attattho parattho ubhayattho diṭṭhadhammiko attho samparāyiko atthoti evaṃ pañcappabhede atthe ti catusaccadhamme kāmarūpārūpalokuttarabhede vā cattāro dhamme paṭiggahaṇaparibhogamattaññutāya eva pariyesanavissajjanamattaññutāpi bodhitā hontīti icceva vuttaṃ
uttarati atikkamati abhibhavatīti vā uttaraṃ natthi ettha uttaranti  anatisayaṃ appaṭibhāgaṃ vā anekāsu devamanussaparisāsu anekasatakkhattuṃ tesaṃ ariyasaccappaṭivedhasampādanavasena pavattā bhagavato dhammadesanā  apica sabbapaṭhamaṃ aññātakoṇḍaññappamukhāya aṭṭhārasaparisagaṇāya brahmakoṭiyā catusaccassa paṭivedhavidhāyinī yā dhammadesanā tassā sātisayā dhammacakkasamaññā tattha satipaṭṭhānātidhammo eva pavattanaṭṭhena cakkanti nti vā āṇā dhammato anapetattā dhammañca taṃ cakkañcāti  dhammena ñāyena cakkantipi  yathāha dhammañca pavatteti cakkañcāti dhammacakkaṃ cakkañca pavatteti dhammañcāti dhammacakkaṃ dhammena pavattetīti dhammacakkantiādi (paṭi ma 240-41) nti dhammissarassa bhagavato sammāsambuddhassa dhammacakkassa  anuttarabhāvato appaṭisedhanīyaṃ kehi pana appaṭivattiyanti āha  tiādi tattha ti pabbajjaṃ upagatena ti jātibrāhmaṇena sāsanaparamatthasamaṇabrāhmaṇānañhi paṭilomacittaṃyeva natthi ti kāmāvacaradevena ti yena  kenaci avasiṭṭhapārisajjena ettāvatā aṭṭhannampi parisānaṃ anavasesapariyādānaṃ daṭṭhabbaṃ nti sattaloke


 dutiye nti dasavidhaṃ dvādasavidhaṃ vā cakkavattibhāvāvahaṃ vattaṃ tattha antojanasmiṃ balakāye dhammikāyeva rakkhāvaraṇaguttiyā saṃvidhānaṃ khattiyesu anuyantesu brāhmaṇagahapatikesu negamajānapadesu samaṇabrāhmaṇesu migapakkhīsu adhammikapaṭikkhepo adhanānaṃ dhanānuppadānaṃ samaṇabrāhmaṇe upasaṅkamitvā pañhapucchananti idaṃ dasavidhaṃ cakkavattivattaṃ idameva ca gahapatike pakkhijāte ca visuṃ katvā gahaṇavasena dvādasavidhaṃ ti dasavidhaṃ vā dvādasavidhaṃ vā cakkavattivattaṃ pūretvā nisinnassa puttassa aññaṃ pātubhavati so taṃ pavatteti ratanamayattā pana sadisaṭṭhena tadevetanti katvā pitarā pavattitanti vuttaṃ yasmā vā so appossukko tvaṃ deva hohi ahamanusāsissāmīti āha tasmā pitarā pavattitaṃ āṇācakkaṃ anuppavatteti nāmāti evamettha attho daṭṭhabbo yañhi attano puññānubhāvasiddhaṃ cakkaratanaṃ taṃ nippariyāyato tena pavattitaṃ nāma netaranti paṭhamanayo vutto yasmā pavattitasseva anuvattanaṃ paṭhamanayo ca taṃsadise tabbohāravasena vuttoti taṃ anādiyitvā dutiyanayo vutto


 catutthe ti ettake vutte yehi kehici dvīhi bhāgehi sujātatā paññāpeyya sujāta-saddo ca sujāto cārudassanotiādīsu (ma ni 2399 su ni 553 theragā 818) ārohasampattipariyāyoti  jātivasena  sujātataṃ vibhāvetuṃ ti vuttaṃ anorasaputtavasenapi loke mātupitusamaññā dissati idha pana sā orasaputtavasena icchitāti dassetuṃ ti vuttaṃ gabbhaṃ gaṇhāti dhāretīti  gabbhāsayasaññito mātukucchippadeso tenāha ti yathābhuttassa āhārassa vipācanavasena gaṇhanato achaḍḍanato  tejodhātu pitā ca mātā ca pitaro pitūnaṃ pitaro pitāmahā tesaṃ yugo pitāmahayugo tasmā  pitāmahadvandāti evamettha attho daṭṭhabbo evañhi pitāmahaggahaṇeneva mātāmahopi gahitoti so aṭṭhakathāyaṃ visuṃ na uddhato yuga-saddo cettha ekasesanayena daṭṭhabbo yugo ca yugo ca yugoti evañhi tattha tattha dvinnaṃ gahitameva hoti tenāha ti purisaggahaṇañcettha ukkaṭṭhaniddesavasena katanti daṭṭhabbaṃ evañhi mātitoti pāḷivacanaṃ samatthitaṃ hoti
ti appattakkhepo ti sampattavivādādīsu na avakkhitto na chaḍḍito jātivādenāti hetumhi karaṇavacananti dassetuṃ tiādi vuttaṃ ettha ca ubhato pitāmahayugāti etena khattiyassa yonidosābhāvo dassito saṃsuddhaggahaṇikabhāvakittanato akkhittoti iminā kiriyāparādhābhāvo kiriyāparādhena hi sattā khepaṃ pāpuṇanti anupakkuṭṭhoti iminā ayuttasaṃsaggābhāvo ayuttasaṃsaggañhi paṭicca sattā akkosaṃ labhanti
aḍḍhatā nāma vibhavasampannatā sā taṃ taṃ upādāyupādāya vuccatīti āha ti tathā mahaddhanatāpīti taṃ ukkaṃsagataṃ dassetuṃ ti vuttaṃ bhuñjitabbato paribhuñjitabbato visesato kāmā bhogā nāmāti āha ti koṭṭhaṃ vuccati dhaññassa āvasanaṭṭhānaṃ koṭṭhabhūtaṃ agāraṃ  tenāha ti evaṃ sāragabbhaṃ koso dhaññapariṭṭhapanaṭṭhānañca koṭṭhāgāranti dassetvā idāni tato aññathā taṃ dassetuṃ tiādi vuttaṃ tattha yathā asino tikkhabhāvaparihārako  paṭicchado kosoti vuccati evaṃ rañño tikkhabhāvaparihāraṃ katvā caturaṅginī senā kosoti āha ti vatthakoṭṭhāgāraggahaṇeneva sabbassapi bhaṇḍaṭṭhapanaṭṭhānassa gahitattā nti vuttaṃ
yassā  paññāya vasena puriso paṇḍitoti vuccati taṃ paṇḍiccanti āha ti taṃtaṃitikattabbatāsu chekabhāvo byattabhāvo  sammohaṃ hiṃsati vidhamatīti medhā sā etassa atthīti  ṭhāne ṭhāne uppatti etissā atthīti  ṭhānaso uppajjanapaññā ti vaḍḍhisaṅkhāte atthe


 pañcame nti hatthisippe hatthīti hi hatthivisayattā hatthisannissitattā ca hatthisippaṃ gahitaṃ sesapadesupi eseva nayo vayatīti vayo sobhanesu katthaci apakkhalanto avitthāyanto tāni sandhāretuṃ sakkotīti attho na vayo avayo tāni atthato saddato ca sandhāretuṃ na sakkoti avayo na hotīti  dve paṭisedhā pakatiṃ gamentīti āha ti chaṭṭhādīni uttānatthāneva


 dasame nti tikkhānaṃ nti kakkasānaṃ nti dāruṇānaṃ nti nasātānaṃ appiyānaṃ na tāsu mano appeti na tā manaṃ appāyanti vaḍḍhentīti
