

 dutiyassa  paṭhame āvaraṇavasena  nīvaraṇavasena  ceto ajjhāruhantīti  vipassanāpaññañca maggapaññañca uppattinivāraṇaṭṭhena dubbalaṃ karontīti  yā vā etehi saddhiṃ vokiṇṇā paññā uppajjati taṃ dubbalaṃ karontītipi ti pañcanīvaraṇapariyonaddhattā apagatabalāya nti dasakusalakammapathasaṅkhātā manussadhammā uttari ariyabhāvaṃ kātuṃ samatthaṃ ñāṇadassanavisesaṃ ti haritabbaṃ harituṃ samatthā ti mātikāmukhāni tāni hi naṅgalasarikkhakattā naṅgalehi ca khatattā naṅgalamukhānīti vuccanti
ti ettha sotaṃ viya vipassanāñāṇaṃ daṭṭhabbaṃ ubhato naṅgalamukhānaṃ vivaraṇakālo viya chasu dvāresu saṃvarassa vissaṭṭhakālo majjhenadiyā rukkhapāde koṭṭetvā palālatiṇamattikāhi āvaraṇe kate udakassa vikkhittavisaṭabyādiṇṇakālo viya pañcahi nīvaraṇehi pariyonaddhakālo evaṃ āvaraṇe kate vihatavegassa udakassa tiṇapalālādīni  parikaḍḍhitvā samuddaṃ pāpuṇituṃ asamatthakālo viya vipassanāñāṇena sabbākusale viddhaṃsetvā nibbānasāgaraṃ pāpuṇituṃ asamatthakālo veditabbo sukkapakkhe vuttavipallāsena yojanā kātabbā imasmiṃ sutte vaṭṭavivaṭṭaṃ kathitaṃ dutiyaṃ uttānatthameva

 tatiye ti padhānaṃ vuccati padahanabhāvo padhānamassa atthīti padhāniyo padhāniyassa bhikkhuno aṅgānīti padhāniyaṅgāni ti saddhāya samannāgato saddhā panesā āgamasaddhā adhigamasaddhā okappanasaddhā pasādasaddhāti catubbidhā tattha sabbaññubodhisattānaṃ saddhā abhinīhārato paṭṭhāya āgatattā  nāma ariyasāvakānaṃ  paṭivedhena adhigatattā  nāma buddho dhammo saṅghoti vutte acalabhāvena okappanaṃ  nāma pasāduppatti  nāma idha okappanasaddhā adhippetā nti catumaggañāṇaṃ taṃ suppaṭividdhaṃ tathāgatenāti saddahati desanāsīsameva cetaṃ iminā pana aṅgena tīsupi ratanesu saddhā adhippetā yassa hi buddhādīsu pasādo balavā tassa padhānavīriyaṃ ijjhati
ti arogo ti niddukkho ti samavipākiniyā ti kammajatejodhātuyā ti atisītalaggahaṇiko hi sītabhīruko hoti accuṇhaggahaṇiko uṇhabhīruko tesaṃ padhānaṃ na ijjhati majjhimaggahaṇikassa ijjhati tenāha  ti ti yathābhūtaṃ attano aguṇaṃ pakāsetā ti udayañca atthañca gantuṃ paricchindituṃ samatthāya etena paññāsalakkhaṇapariggāhakaṃ udayabbayañāṇaṃ vuttaṃ ti parisuddhāya ti anibbiddhapubbe lobhakkhandhādayo nibbijjhituṃ samatthāya ti tadaṅgavasena kilesānaṃ pahīnattā yaṃ dukkhaṃ khīyati tassa dukkhassa khayagāminiyā iti sabbehipi imehi padehi vipassanāpaññāva kathitā duppaññassa hi padhānaṃ na ijjhati

 catutthe ti vīriyakaraṇatthāya nti na sakkā hoti pattaṃ gahetvā uñchācariyāya yāpetuṃ imasmimpi sutte vaṭṭavivaṭṭameva kathitaṃ

 pañcame ti pariyādiyitvā gahetvā khepetvā tiṭṭhati ti uddhumātā
ti sīsacchedanatthāya asiṃ ādāya āgatenāpi ti khādituṃ āgatayakkhenāpi ti ghaṭṭeyya ti mudukena nti kāmoghena vuḷhānaṃ kaḍḍhitānaṃ nti  vaṭṭakālaṃ gatiñca punappunabbhave ca ti purecārikā purato gatāyeva ti ye paṇḍitā duvidhepi kāme tīhi pariññāhi parijānitvā ti khīṇāsavānaṃ kutoci bhayaṃ nāma natthi tasmā te akutobhayā hutvā caranti ti pāraṃ vuccati nibbānaṃ taṃ upagatā sacchikatvā ṭhitāti attho nti arahattaṃ imasmiṃ sutte vaṭṭameva kathetvā gāthāsu vaṭṭavivaṭṭaṃ kathitaṃ

 chaṭṭhe ti sañjātagarubhāvo ti catasso disā ca anudisā ca mayhaṃ na upaṭṭhahantīti vadati ti samathavipassanādhammāpi me na upaṭṭhahanti ti ukkaṇṭhito hutvā brahmacariyavāsaṃ vasāmi ti tassa kathaṃ sutvā buddhaveneyyapuggalo ayanti taṃ kāraṇaṃ bhagavato ārocetuṃ upasaṅkami nti kusaladhamme avipassantassa anesantassa agavesantassāti attho nti satipaṭṭhānādīnaṃ sattatiṃsadhammānaṃ

 sattame ti jarāsabhāvo amhi ti jaraṃ anatikkanto antojarāya eva carāmi sesapadesupi eseva nayo tiādīsu  kammaṃ mayhaṃ sakaṃ  attano santakanti kammassako amhi kammassa dāyādoti  kammaṃ mayhaṃ dāyajjaṃ santakanti attho kammaṃ mayhaṃ yoni kāraṇanti  kammaṃ mayhaṃ bandhūti  kammañātakoti attho kammaṃ mayhaṃ paṭisaraṇaṃ patiṭṭhāti ti tassa kammassa dāyādo tena dinnaphalapaṭiggāhako bhavissāmīti attho ti yobbanaṃ ārabbha uppannamado sesesupi eseva nayo ti lokuttaramaggo sañjāyati ti dasa saṃyojanāni sabbaso pahīyanti ti satta anusayā vigatantā paricchinnā parivaṭumā  honti evamettha heṭṭhā pañcasu ṭhānesu vipassanā kathitā imesu pañcasu lokuttaramaggo
idāni gāthāhi kūṭaṃ gaṇhanto tiādimāha tattha nti upadhirahitaṃ arahattamaggaṃ ñatvā ti sabbe ime tayopi made adhibhaviṃ atikkamma ṭhitosmīti attho ti pabbajjaṃ khemato disvā ti tassa mayhaṃ nibbānaṃ abhipassantassa vāyāmo ahosi ti pabbajjato anivattiko bhavissāmi brahmacariyavāsato  anivattiko sabbaññutaññāṇato anivattiko bhavissāmi ti maggabrahmacariyaparāyaṇo iminā lokuttaro aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo kathitoti

 aṭṭhame ti sajiyāni āropitadhanūni ti addasaṃsu ti vaḍḍhissanti vajjirājāno ti avaḍḍhinissitā mānathaddhā ti pacchato gantvā piṭṭhiṃ pādena paharanti tiādīsu raṭṭhaṃ bhuñjatīti  pitarā dattaṃ sāpateyyaṃ bhuñjatīti  senāya pati jeṭṭhakoti ti gāmānaṃ gāmaṇikassa gāmasāmikassāti attho ti gaṇajeṭṭhakassa ti tesu tesu kulesu ti paccekaṃ jeṭṭhakaṭṭhānaṃ kārenti ti sundarena cittena anuggaṇhanti ti ye ca attano khettakammantānaṃ sāmantā anantarakkhettasāmino te ca rajjudaṇḍehi bhūmippamāṇaggāhake sabbohāre ca ti kulappaveṇiyā āgatā ārakkhadevatā ti tā devatā aggayāgubhattādīhi sakkaroti
ti uppannānaṃ kiccānaṃ kārako ti ye ca etaṃ upanissāya jīvanti ti ubhinnampi hitatthāya paṭipanno hotīti attho nti paralokagatānaṃ nti ye ca diṭṭhe dhamme  jīvanti iti padadvayenāpi atītapaccuppanne ñātayo dasseti ti tuṭṭhijanano nti gharāvāsaṃ vasanto ti pūjetabbo ca pasaṃsitabbo ca hotīti

 navame ti saṇho sukhumakāraṇaññū ti samaṇākappena sampanno dasame ti dinnovādaṃ padakkhiṇato gaṇhanto sesaṃ sabbattha uttānamevāti
