kathañca  bhikkhave bhikkhu sampajjhāyati idha bhikkhave bhikkhuno asmimāno pahīno na hoti ucchinnamūlo tālāvatthukato anabhāvaṃkato āyatiṃ anuppādadhammo evaṃ kho bhikkhave bhikkhu  sampajjhāyati
