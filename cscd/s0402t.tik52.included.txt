

 catutthassa paṭhamādīni uttānatthāneva

 catutthe ti citte uppannabalavasokena socati cittasantāpena anto nijjhāyatīti attho ti tasseva sokassa vasena kāye ca uppannadukkhena kilamati ti sokuddehakavasena taṃ taṃ vippalapanto vācāya paridevati nti urattāḷaṃ uraṃ tāḷetvāti attho tenāha ti ti ettha ekamevidaṃ pañcamaṃ nīvaraṇaṃ kiṃ nu kho idanti  ārammaṇaṃ kaṅkhanato  idameva varanti nicchetuṃ asamatthabhāvato ti vuccati  dhammasabhāvaṃ vicinanto kicchati vigatā cikicchāti vā katvā


 pañcame ti etasmiṃ suññatānupassanādhikāre ti katthaci ṭhāne kāle dhamme vā atha vā ti ajjhattaṃ bahiddhā vā nti sakattānaṃ ayaṃ kho bho brahmā mahābrahmā vasī pitā bhūtabhabyānantiādinā  parehi parikappitaṃ attānañca kassaci kiñcanabhūtaṃ na passatīti dassento tiādimāha tattha ti parajāti paro purisoti vā evaṃ gahitassa ti ettha saddo aṭṭhānappayuttoti āha ti ti attano aññassa paro puriso nāma atthi mamatthāya ṭhito tassa vasena mayhaṃ sabbaṃ ijjhatīti ekaccadiṭṭhigatikaparikappitavasena paraṃ attānaṃ tañca attano kiñcanabhūtaṃ na passatīti dassento tiādimāha ettha ca ti sakaattano sabbhāvaṃ na passati nti sakaattano eva kassaci attaniyataṃ na passati ti etaṃ dvayaṃ yathārahaṃ sambandhitabbaṃ atthipadaṃ paccekaṃ ti parassa attano abhāvaṃ passati ti parassa attano anattaniyataṃ passati evaṃ ajjhattaṃ bahiddhā khandhānaṃ attattaniyasuññatāsuddhasaṅkhārapuñjatācatukoṭikasuññatāpariggaṇhanena niṭṭhā hoti sesamettha suviññeyyameva


 chaṭṭhe ti cittavasiko loko tattha tattha upakaḍḍhīyati nti ye apariññātavatthukā cittassa vasaṃ gacchanti tesaṃyeva gahaṇaṃ na hi pariññātakkhandhā pahīnakilesā cittassa vasaṃ gacchanti cittaṃ pana attano vase vattenti ti cārittavārittasīlena saddhiṃ nti dhutaṅgamādiṃ katvā yāva gotrabhuñāṇaṃ tāva pavattetabbaṃ samathavipassanāsaṅkhātaṃ sammāpaṭipadaṃ sesaṃ suviññeyyameva


 sattame ti ettha ti tassa brāhmaṇassa nāmaṃ mahatiyā issariyamattāya samannāgato mahāmatto ti ca issariyappamāṇenāti attho issariyena ceva vittūpakaraṇena cāti evaṃ vā ettha attho daṭṭhabbo upabhogūpakaraṇānipi hi loke mattāti vuccanti magadharañño magadharaṭṭhe vā mahāmattoti  tudisaññito gāmo nivāso etassāti  tenevāha ti tudigāmavāsitā cassa tattha adhipatibhāvenāti daṭṭhabbā so hi taṃ gāmaṃ soṇadaṇḍo viya campaṃ kūṭadanto viya khāṇumataṃ adhipatibhāvena ajjhāvasati  pañcacattālīsakoṭivibhavo ti parisāyaṃ janasamūheti attho tenāha ti ti ettha abhikkamanattho saddoti āha ti pasādanīyaṃ ṭhānaṃ atikkamitvā appasādanīyaṭṭhāne pasannoti adhippāyo taṃ taṃ kiccaṃ atthaṃ passatīti atthadasā alaṃ samatthā paṭibalā atthadasā alamatthadasā alamatthadase atisayantīti  tenāha tiādi  sappurisadhammānaṃ asappurisadhammānañca  yāthāvato ajānanato sesamettha uttānameva


 aṭṭhame nibbattivasena nirayaṃ arahati nirayasaṃvattaniyena vā kammunā niraye niyuttoti  avīcimhi uppajjitvā tattha āyukappasaññitaṃ antarakappaṃ tiṭṭhatīti  nirayūpapattiṃ pariharaṇavasena tikicchituṃ asakkuṇeyyoti ti aparikkhīṇāyeva sace hi eko bhikkhu kāyānupassanaṃ pucchati añño vedanānupassanaṃ cittānupassanaṃ iminā puṭṭhe ahaṃ pucchāmīti eko ekaṃ na oloketi evaṃ santepi tesaṃ vāro paññāyati eva buddhānaṃ pana vāro na paññāyati vidatthicaturaṅgulaṃ chāyaṃ atikkamanato puretaraṃyeva bhagavā cuddasavidhena kāyānupassanaṃ navavidhena vedanānupassanaṃ soḷasavidhena cittānupassanaṃ pañcavidhena dhammānupassanaṃ katheti tiṭṭhantu vā evaṃ cattāro sace hi aññe cattāro sammappadhānesu aññe iddhipādesu aññe pañcindriyesu  aññe pañcabalesu aññe sattabojjhaṅgesu aññe aṭṭhamaggaṅgesu pañhe puccheyyuṃ tampi bhagavā katheyya tiṭṭhantu vā ete aṭṭha sace aññe sattatiṃsa janā bodhipakkhiyesu pañhe puccheyyuṃ tampi bhagavā tāvadeva katheyya kasmā yāvatā hi lokiyamahājane ekaṃ padaṃ kathente tāva ānandatthero aṭṭha padāni katheti ānandatthere pana ekaṃ padaṃ kathenteyeva bhagavā soḷasa padāni katheti kasmā bhagavato hi jivhā mudu dantāvaraṇaṃ suphusitaṃ vacanaṃ agaḷitaṃ bhavaṅgaparivāso lahuko tena vuttaṃ  nti
tattha ti tantiṭṭhapanā nti ettha ti pāḷi pajjati attho etenāti  vākyañca padeneva saṅgahitaṃ atthaṃ byañjetīti  akkharaṃ tañhi padavākyaṃ paricchijjamānaṃ taṃ taṃ atthaṃ byañjeti pakāseti etena aparāparehi padabyañjanehi sucirampi kālaṃ kathentassa  tathāgatassa na kadāci tesaṃ pariyādānaṃ atthīti dasseti ti guṇadhaṃsanasīlo ti vācāpāgabbiyena samannāgato


 navame ti sahajātanāmakāyena sesamettha uttānameva


 dasame nti āmeḍitavacanaṃ byāpanicchāvasena vuttanti āha ti ti ettha saddopi byāpanicchāvacanamevāti anu anu viloketvāti attho pañcapasādappaṭimaṇḍitāni akkhīni ummīletvā tato tato viloketvāti vuttaṃ hoti nti yuttaṃ opāyikanti attho alameva nibbindituntiādīsu  viya puṭabandhanena pariharitvā asitabbaṃ  sambalaṃ akārassa okāraṃ katvā tenāha nti sesamettha suviññeyyameva
