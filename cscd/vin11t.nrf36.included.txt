
ganthārambhakathāvaṇṇanā

 devātidevaṃ  sugataṃ devabrhmindavanditaṃ
dhammañca vaṭṭupacchedaṃ natvā vaṭṭātitaṃ gaṇaṃ

 vandanāmayapuññena kammena ratanattaye
chetvā upaddave sabbe ārabhissaṃ samāhito

 therena  racitassa samāsato
masaṃkiṇṇaṃ uttarassa yathābalaṃ
 athāyamācariyo attano viracite vinaye tassupanissaye vinayapiṭake ca bhikkhūnaṃ nānappakārakosallajananatthaṃ parivāraṭṭhakathāyañca āgatavinicchayaṃ saṅgahetvā uttarapakaraṇaṃ vaṇṇayitukāmo  paṭhamaṃ tāva antarāyanivāraṇena yathādhippetasādhanatthaṃ ratanattayaṃ vandanto āha ntiādi
pakaraṇārambhe ratanattayavandanāpayojanaṃ tattha tatthācariyehi bahudhā papañcitaṃ amhehi ca vinayavinicchayavaṇṇanāyaṃ samāsato dassitanti na taṃ idha vaṇṇayissāma pakaraṇābhidheyya karaṇappakārapayojanānipi  tattha dassitanayānusārena idhāpi veditabbāni sambandhādidassanamukhena anuttānapadavaṇṇanamevettha karissāmi
jinaṃ dhammañca gaṇañca vanditvā uttaraṃ dāni karissāmīti sambandho kiṃvisiṭṭhaṃ jinaṃ dhammaṃ gaṇañca vanditvāti āha ntiādi tattha nti pañcasu kāmaguṇesu sattā āsattā visattā laggitāti  paramatthato sattapaññattiyā upādānabhūtā upādānakkhandhā vohārato khandhasantatiṃ upādāya paññattā sammuti sattāti vuccanti te pana kāmāvacarādibhūmivasena nirayādipadesavasena ahetukādipaṭisandhivasenāti evamādīhi anantapabhedā tesu khīṇāsavānaṃ yathāvuttanibbacanatthena sattavohāro na labbhati tathāpi te bhūtapubbagatiyā vā taṃsadisattā vā sattāti vuccanti sabbe ca te sattā cā ti  uddhaṭatamattā uggatatamattā seṭṭhattā ca  sabbasattānaṃ lokiyalokuttarehi rūpārūpaguṇehi uttamo sabbasattesu vā uttamo pavaro seṭṭhoti nti etassa visesanaṃ
punapi kiṃvisiṭṭhanti āha nti  vuccati paññā tāya īrati vattatīti  taṃ tādibhāvena indakhīlasineruādayo viya aṭṭhalokadhammasaṅkhātena bhusavātena akampiyaṭṭhena catuvesārajjavasena sadevake loke kenaci akampanīyaṭṭhena ca  dhitisampannanti attho idampi tasseva visesanaṃ
ti kāyavacīmanodvārehi abhivādetvāti attho yathābhuccaguṇasaṃkittanena thometvā ti bhattibhāvanatuttamaṅgena karaṇabhūtena iminā visesato  kāyapaṇāmo dassito guṇasaṃkittanena vacīpaṇāmo ubhayapaṇāmehi nānantariyakatāya manopaṇāmopi dassito ca hoti
nti  devaputtakilesābhisaṅkhāramaccukhandhamārasaṅkhāte pañcavidhe māre balavidhamanasamaucchedapahānasahāyavekallanidānopacchedavisayātikkamavasena pañcahi ākārehi jitavāti  taṃ
nti etassa nibbacanādivasena atthavinicchayo heṭṭhā dassitova nti dhammasaṅkhātassa kusalassa paṭipakkhattā  vuccati akusaladhammo taṃ akusalasaṅkhātaṃ adhammaṃ viddhaṃseti vināseti pajahati tadaṅgavikkhambhanasamucchedapaṭippassaddhinissaraṇappahānenāti  sapariyattiko navalokuttaro dhammo pariyatti hi pañcannaṃ pahānānaṃ mūlakāraṇattā phalūpacārena tathā vuccati taṃ adhammaviddhaṃsaṃ dhammanti etassa visesanaṃ
nti aṭṭhannaṃ ariyapuggalānaṃ samūhaṃ saṅghanti attho nti attano nissayaṃ aṅganti matthentīti  kilesā rāgadosamohā te aṅgaṇe nāseti yathāyogaṃ tadaṅgavikkhambhanasamucchedapaṭippassaddhinissaraṇappahānehi pajahatīti  taṃ gaṇanti etassa visesanaṃ
 mayā vinayassa yo sāro vinicchayo racito  vinicchayassāti yojanā natthi tassa uttaroti  sabbesu vinicchayesu sabbesaṃ vā vinicchayānaṃ anuttaro uttamo vinicchayoti  taṃ  pakaraṇaṃ idāni karissāmīti yojanā
ti bhaṇantassa paguṇaṃ vācuggataṃ karontassa ti paṭhantassa vācuggataṃ sajjhāyantassa ti tattha  pakārena yuñjantassa taṃ aññesaṃ vācentassa vā ti parehi vuccamānaṃ suṇantassa ti yathāsutaṃ atthato saddato ca cintentassa abuddhassa buddhivaḍḍhananti vattabbe gāthābandhavasena vibhattilopo  bālassa vinaye appakataññuno bhikkhubhikkhunijanassa  vinayavinicchaye paññāvuddhinipphādakaṃ atha vā  vinicchaye kataparicayattā paññavato janassa  buddhiyā paññāya tikkhavisadabhāvāpādanena bhiyyobhāvasādhakaṃ  uttamaṃ  nāma  pakaraṇaṃ  kathentassa  mama santikā  vinicchaye tīsu sikkhāsu vā visesena ratā  jānātha sutamayañāṇaṃ abhinipphādethāti sotujanaṃ savane niyojeti
