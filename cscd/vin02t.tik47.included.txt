 ṭhānakaraṇāni sithilāni katvā uccāretabbaṃ akkharaṃ  tāniyeva dhanitāni asithilāni katvā uccāretabbaṃ akkharaṃ  dvimattakālaṃ  ekamattakālaṃ ti evaṃ sithilādivasena byañjanabuddhiyā akkharuppādakacittassa dasappakārena pabhedo sabbāni hi akkharāni cittasamuṭṭhānāni yathādhippetatthabyañjanato byañjanāni ca saṃyogo paro etasmāti saṃyogaparo na saṃyogaparo  ti ettha kāra kārasahitākāro asaṃyogaparo ti kaṇṭhādīni
 anukkhittā  pārājikaṃ anāpannā ca pakatattāti āha tiādi tattha ti purimapadasseva vevacanaṃ ti pārājikaṃ anāpannā ti tīsu dvīsu vā nisinnesu ekassa vā dvinnaṃ vā chandapārisuddhi āhaṭāpi anāhaṭāva hotīti adhippāyo

ti sahaseyyapaṭiggahaṇādi ti subhe rato suṭṭhu oratoti vā ti nīcavutti ti paṭisaṅkhāya ñāṇena upaparikkhitvā yaṃ taṃ avandiyakammaṃ anuññātanti sambandho ti evampi apalokanakammaṃ pavattatīti attho kammameva lakkhaṇanti  osāraṇanissāraṇabhaṇḍukammādayo viya kammañca hutvā aññañca nāmaṃ na labhati kammameva hutvā upalakkhīyatīti kammalakkhaṇanti vuccati ti vuttakammalakkhaṇaṃ dassetuṃ ntiādi vuttaṃ ti iṇameva palibodho iṇapalibodho tampi dātuṃ vaṭṭati sace tādisaṃ bhikkhuṃ iṇāyikā palibundhanti tatruppādatopi tassa iṇaṃ sodhetuṃ vaṭṭatīti adhippāyo
ti ettha ti cetiyassa upari caturassacayo vuccati nti tato uddhaṃ valayāni dassetvā kato aggacayo vuccati ti cetiye navakammattāya upanikkhittato cetiyasantakatoti vuttaṃ hoti ti purimakatikāya asaṅgahitattā vuttaṃ ti yesaṃ puggalikaṭṭhāne tiṭṭhanti tehi nti dasamabhāgaṃ ti tasmiṃ vihāre ti pubbe ti vacaneneva yathāsukhaṃ paribhogo paṭikkhitto hotīti āha ti

paccayaṃ apaccāsīsantena nti vuttamevatthaṃ vibhāveti nti ti tato uppannaāyena  katvā ti jaggitānaṃ pupphaphalabharitakāle na vāretabbā ti jaggituṃ āraddhakāle ti ñattikammassa ṭhānabhede


 satta  āpattikkhandhā paññattaṃ nāmāti sambandho kakusandhakoṇāgamanakassapā eva satta āpattikkhandhe paññapesuṃ vipassīādayo pana ovādapātimokkhaṃ uddisiṃsu na sikkhāpadaṃ paññapesunti āha ti sesamettha suviññeyyameva



avasānagāthāsu  pana ayamattho nti ubhatovibhaṅgakhandhakaparivārehi vibhattadesanaṃ vinayapiṭakanti yojetabbaṃ ti tassa vinayassa
ntiādi paṭhamapārājikavaṇṇanāyaṃ vuttanayameva
ti satthunā paribhuttamahābodhivibhūsito maṇḍito tassa mahāvihārassa dakkhiṇabhāge uttamaṃ yaṃ padhānagharanti sambandho tattha nti taṃnāmakaṃ pariveṇaṃ nti idampi padhānagharavisesanaṃ
ti tasmiṃ padhānaghare nti manāpena pākārena parikkhittaṃ nti ghananicitapattasañchannasākhāpasākhatāya sītacchāyehi rukkhehi upetaṃ vikasitakamalakuvalayapuṇḍarīkasogandhikādipupphasañchannamadhurasītaludakapuṇṇatāya sampannā salilāsayā assāti ti buddhasiriṃ nāma theraṃ nissāya tassa ajjhesanaṃ nissāyāti vuttaṃ hoti ti atthavinicchayādīhi iddhā phītā paripuṇṇā
ti siriyā nivāsaṭṭhānabhūtassa ti jayappattasaṃvacchare nti theraṃ buddhasiriṃ uddissa yā vinayavaṇṇanā āraddhā ayaṃ ti kusalasannissitā idāni sadevakassa lokassa accantasukhādhigamāya attano puññaṃ pariṇāmento tiādimāha  tattha nti upacitaṃ ti sabbassa tassa puññassa tejena ti kāmāvacarādibhedā sabbe sattā ti yathārahaṃ bodhittayādhigamavasena saddhammarasasevino bhavantu sesamettha suviññeyyameva
