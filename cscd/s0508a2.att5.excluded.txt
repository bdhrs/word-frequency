 dukkhassāvahani Visattika  Papañcasaṅghātadukhādhivāhini pahantvānā
Siva  dvecaturaṅgagāminaṃ, magguttamaṃ sabbakilesasodhanaṃ. Paññāya passitvā
asokaṃ virajaṃ asaṅkhataṃ santaṃ padaṃ sabbakilesasodhanaṃ. Bhāvetī saṃyojanabandhanacchidaṃ 
‘‘yadā nabhe’’ nabhe meghadundubhi dhārākulā vihagapathe Tato
Kusumākulāna  Vicittavāneyyavaṭaṃsakāna  sumano
Nisīthe Rahitamhī Deve Gaḷantamhī Dāṭhino nadanti dāṭhino
Vitakke uparundhiyattano Nagantare Nagavivara Samassito Vītaddaro Vītakhilo
Sukhī Malakhilasokanāsano Niraggaḷo Nibbanatho Visallo Sabbāsave Byantikato
