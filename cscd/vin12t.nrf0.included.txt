 yaṃ mūlaṃ vā yā sākhā vā gacchatīti yojanā ti rukkhassa khandhe ti uccārapassāvo nikkhamati nti tiṇena kataṃ tiṇamayaṃ vā aṇḍupakaṃ ti uccārapassāvakheḷesu
 adhippetaudakaṃ dassetuṃ vuttaṃ paribhogaudakamevāti sattamo vaggo
ti sekhiyesu ti sūpabyañjane odanena paṭicchādeti samattā sekhiyā

 saṅkhyaṃ  paricchijjatīti nti catubbidhānamadhikaraṇānaṃ ti tesaṃ khandhakaparivārānaṃ ti tesu eva khandhakaparivāresu ti sabbesu sikkhāpadesūti
