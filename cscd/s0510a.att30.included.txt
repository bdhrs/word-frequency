

bāttiṃsatimavagge  paṭhamadutiyatatiyāpadānāni suviññeyyāneva
 catutthāpadāne nti jalajehi uttamehi uppalapadumādīhi pupphehi okiriṃ pūjesinti attho
pañcamāpadānaṃ uttānameva
 chaṭṭhāpadāne ti sakalalokattayassa bandhuno ñātakassa sikhissa bhagavato uttamaṃ cetiyaṃ  janasañcaravirahite  manussānaṃ kolāhalavirahite mahāaraññe ahosīti sambandho ti tasmiṃ kāle vane maggamūḷhabhāvena andho na cakkhunā  andho ahaṃ āhiṇḍāmi maggaṃ pariyesāmīti attho ti mahāvanato nikkhamantena mayā  uttamāsanaṃ sīhassa vā bhagavato āsanaṃ diṭṭhanti attho ti ekaṃsaṃ uttarāsaṅgaṃ katvā sirasi añjaliṃ ṭhapetvāti attho nti sakalalokattayanayaṃ taṃ nibbānaṃ pāpentaṃ thomitaṃ thutiṃ akāsinti attho
 sattamāpadāne ti sundaradassano dvattiṃsamahāpurisalakkhaṇasampannasarīrattā manoharadassano mahāvīriyo siddhattho bhagavāti sambandho ti uttame vihāre vasatīti attho
aṭṭhamanavamadasamāpadānāni uttānānevāti
