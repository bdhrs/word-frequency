
nātho  bhikkhunīnaṃ hitatthāya yaṃ pātimokkhaṃ pakāsayīti sambandho tattha ti desayi paññāpayīti attho


nti vacanamattaṃ na atthoti adhippāyo nti purisaliṅgaṃ itthiliṅganti visesamattaṃ ti nānāttaṃ ti pemena ceva sinehena cevāti attho  ti icchāya ca ti dūsitāya paripuṇṇā upasampadā yassā sā  ubhatosaṅghena upasampannāti attho



nti dutiyamahāsandhito uddhaṃ nti hatthassa vā kāyassa vā tiriyaṃ ito cito ca sañcaraṇaṃ
ti (pāci aṭṭha 662) bhikkhuniyā avassave bhikkhuniyā kāyasaṃsaggarāgena avassutabhāve satīti attho bhikkhuniyā vaseneva ca ekatoavassutabhāvo gahetabbo vuttañhetaṃ  (pāci aṭṭha 662) 
ekatoavassuteti  ettha kiñcāpi ekatoti avisesena vuttaṃ tathāpi bhikkhuniyā eva avassute sati ayaṃ āpattibhedo vuttoti veditabboti
nti purisassa yaṃ kañci kāyaṃ ti bhikkhuniyā ceva purisassa ca kāyasaṃsaggarāgena avassutabhāve satipi ti yathāparicchinnena attano kāyena nti purisassa kāyappaṭibaddhaṃ ti yathāparicchinnakāyato avasesena kāyena ubbhakkhakaadhojāṇumaṇḍalaadhokapparasaṅkhātena kāyenāti vuttaṃ hoti nti avassutassa tassa purisassa yaṃ kañci kāyaṃ yathā cettha sayaṃ āmasantiyā thullaccayaṃ evaṃ tassa āmasanaṃ sādiyantiyāpīti daṭṭhabbaṃ ti purisassa methunarāgo gehasitapemaṃ suddhacittaṃ vā ti pārājikakhettato avasese ti kāyappaṭibaddhena kāyappaṭibaddhaṃ āmasati āpatti dukkaṭassātiādike  (pāci 659 662) sabbavāre tiādīsu virajjhitvā vā āmasantiyā aññavihitāya vā ayaṃ purisoti vā itthīti vā ajānantiyā vā tena phuṭṭhāyapi taṃ phassaṃ assādayantiyā vā āmasanepi sati anāpatti


nti bhikkhūhi sādhāraṇānaṃ catunnaṃ asādhāraṇānaṃ catunnañcāti aṭṭhannamaññataraṃ ajjhāpannaṃ idañca pārājikaṃ (pāci aṭṭha 666) pacchā paññattaṃ saṅgītikārakācariyehi pana purimena saddhiṃ yugaḷaṃ katvā imasmiṃ okāse ṭhapitanti veditabbaṃ aṭṭhannaṃ pārājikānaṃ aññataranti (pāci 666) vacanato pana vajjappaṭicchādikaṃ yā paṭicchādeti sāpi vajjappaṭicchādikā evāti daṭṭhabbaṃ kiñcāpi vajjappaṭicchādanaṃ pemavasena hoti tathāpi  sikkhāpadavītikkamacittaṃ domanassameva hotīti katvā dukkhavedanaṃ hotīti ti vuttaṃ


ti ñattisampadāya ceva anussāvanasampadāya ca idha pana codanāsāraṇāpubbakameva ñattianussāvanaṃ satthusāsananti āha tiādi nti ñattiṭṭhapanañceva anussāvanāvacanañca nti puggale ceva dhamme ca ādaravirahitaṃ tenāha tiādi ti tasmiṃ saṅghe pariyāpanne sambahulapuggalasaṅkhāte gaṇe vā ti iminā adhippāyattho vutto ti ekakammaekuddesasamasikkhātāya ti saha vattanabhāvena samāno saṃvāso etesanti 
 yena saṃvāsena te samānasaṃvāsakāti (pāci aṭṭha 669) vuttā so saṃvāso  tassa ukkhittakassa tehi saddhiṃ natthi yehi ca saddhiṃ tassa so saṃvāso natthi na tena te bhikkhū attano sahāyā katā honti tasmā so  nāmāti imamatthaṃ dassetuṃ tiādi vuttaṃ evaṃ padavaṇṇanaṃ katvā idāni atthamattaṃ dassetuṃ ti vuttaṃ kiṃ taṃ anuvattananti kassaci āsaṅkā siyāti taṃ sarūpato dassento tiādimāha tattha ti yo ukkhittako so


ti (sārattha ṭī pācittiya 3675) lokassādasaṅkhātassa mittasanthavassa vasena kiṃ tanti āha ti kāyasaṃsaggarāgeneva  tintā idha avassutā nāma na methunarāgenāti kathametaṃ viññāyatīti āha tiādi kiṃ panettha kāraṇanti āha ti tatthāyaṃ vicāraṇā  ettha ca ti kāyasaṃsaggova veditabbo na methunadhammo na hi methunassa sāmantā thullaccayaṃ hoti viññū paṭibalo kāyasaṃsaggaṃ samāpajjitunti (pāci 676) vacanampi cettha sādhakanti yaṃ yena kataṃ taṃ tasseva hotīti ti sāmivacananti dassetuṃ tiādi vuttaṃ tattha ti attano hattho na kevalañcettha nti hattho nāma kapparaṃ upādāya yāva agganakhāti (pāci 676) vuttassa hatthasseva gahaṇaṃ atha kho tassa ca aññassapi apārājikakhettassa gahaṇaṃ ekajjhaṃ katvā hatthaggahaṇanti vuttaṃ tathā nti na kevalaṃ saṅghāṭikaṇṇasseva gahaṇaṃ atha kho tassa ca aññassapi yassa kassaci cīvarappadesassa gahaṇaṃ vuttanti dassetuṃ tiādi vuttaṃ nti evaṃnāmakaṃ ṭhānaṃ ti idaṃ nidassanamattaṃ tasmā paṭipāṭiyā vā uppaṭipāṭiyā vā ekantarikāya vā yena tena nayena pūretvāti attho
ti tā āpannā āpattiyo yathā cekekasmiṃ vatthusmiṃ evaṃ visuṃ visuṃ sattasu vatthūsu satakkhattumpi vītikkantesu tā āpattiyo desetvā muccati ti desitagaṇanaṃ  upagacchati ti aññaṃ vatthuṃ āpajjissāmīti ussāhaṃ ṭhapetvā
