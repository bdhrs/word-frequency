

 catutthassa  paṭhame ti rāgassa paccayesu ārammaṇesu sesesupi eseva nayo

 dutiye ti guṇamakkhako ti yugaggāhalakkhaṇena paḷāsena samannāgato

 tatiye ti tīhi kuhanavatthūhi samannāgato ti lābhasannissitāya lapanāya samannāgato ti nimittakiriyakārako ti nippesanakatāya samannāgato ti lābhena lābhagavesako sukkapakkho vuttavipallāsavasena veditabbo catutthaṃ uttānameva

 pañcame nti rūpārammaṇānaṃ anadhivāsako hoti tadārammaṇehi rāgādīhi abhibhuyyati eseva nayo sabbattha

 chaṭṭhe ti pañcasu atthesu pabhedagataṃ ñāṇaṃ patto ti catubbidhe dhamme pabhedagataṃ ñāṇaṃ patto ti dhammaniruttīsu pabhedagataṃ ñāṇaṃ patto ti tesu tīsu ñāṇesu pabhedagataṃ ñāṇaṃ patto so pana tāni tīṇi ñāṇāneva jānāti na tesaṃ kiccaṃ karoti ti mahantakhuddakāni ti iti kattabbāni

 sattamaṃ  uttānatthameva sīlaṃ panettha khīṇāsavasīlameva bāhusaccampi khīṇāsavabāhusaccameva vācāpi khīṇāsavassa kalyāṇavācāva jhānānipi kiriyajjhānāneva kathitānīti veditabbāni

 aṭṭhame ti thirabhāvappatto ti pabbajitadivasato paṭṭhāya atikkantānaṃ bahūnaṃ rattīnaṃ ñātā ti paññāto pākaṭo ti yasanissito ti ayāthāvadiṭṭhiko ti dasakusalakammapathadhammato vuṭṭhāpetvā ti akusalakammapathesu patiṭṭhāpeti

 navame ti sikkhakassa sakaraṇīyassa ti upariguṇehi parihānatthāya ti navakamme ramanakabhāvo ti  ālāpasallāpe ramanakabhāvo ti niddāyane ramanakabhāvo ti gaṇasaṅgaṇikāya ramanakabhāvo ti yathā yaṃ cittaṃ vimuttaṃ ye ca dosā pahīnā guṇā ca paṭiladdhā te paccavekkhitvā upariguṇapaṭilābhāya vāyāmaṃ na karotīti attho iti imasmiṃ sutte sattannaṃ sekhānaṃ upariguṇehi parihānikāraṇañca vuddhikāraṇañca kathitaṃ yañca nāma sekhassa parihānakāraṇaṃ taṃ puthujjanassa paṭhamameva hotīti

 dasame ti byatto cheko ti iti kattabbesu nti samādhikammaṭṭhānaṃ ti sāsanassa ananucchavikena ti atipātova ti divā vuccati majjhanhiko taṃ atikkamitvā ti ativiya kilesasallekhikā ti cittavivaraṇasaṅkhātānaṃ samathavipassanānaṃ sappāyā ti appicchā hothāti  kathanakathā ti catūhi paccayehi santuṭṭhā hothāti kathanakathā ti tīhi vivekehi vivittā hothāti kathanakathā ti pañcavidhena saṃsaggena asaṃsaṭṭhā hothāti kathanakathā ti duvidhaṃ vīriyaṃ ārabhathāti kathanakathā dīsu sīlaṃ ārabbha kathā sīlakathā samādhiṃ ārabbha paññaṃ ārabbha pañcavidhaṃ  vimuttiṃ ārabbha ekūnavīsatipaccavekkhaṇasaṅkhātaṃ vimuttiñāṇadassanaṃ ārabbha kathā vimuttiñāṇadassanakathā tiādīsu na icchiticchitalābhī dukkhalābhī na vipulalābhīti attho sesaṃ uttānatthamevāti
