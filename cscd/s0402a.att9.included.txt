
 pañcamassa  paṭhame ti sammajjanī padīpo ca udakaṃ āsanena ca chandapārisuddhiutukkhānaṃ bhikkhugaṇanā ca ovādo pātimokkhaṃ therabhāroti vuccatīti imaṃ dasavidhaṃ therabhāraṃ navako hutvā  therena anajjhiṭṭho karonto anāgataṃ bhāraṃ vahati nāma ti thero samāno tameva dasavidhaṃ bhāraṃ attanā vā akaronto paraṃ vā asamādapento āgataṃ bhāraṃ na vahati nāma dutiyasuttepi imināva nayena attho veditabbo
 tatiye ti akappiye sīhamaṃsādimhi kappiyaṃ idanti evaṃsaññī ti kumbhīlamaṃsabiḷāramaṃsādimhi kappiye akappiyaṃ idanti evaṃsaññī catutthaṃ vuttanayeneva veditabbaṃ
 pañcame ti āpucchitvā bhaṇḍakaṃ dhovantassa pattaṃ pacantassa kese chindantassa gāmaṃ pavisantassātiādīsu anāpatti tattha āpatti ayanti evaṃsaññī ti tesaññeva vatthūnaṃ anāpucchākaraṇe āpatti tattha anāpattīti evaṃsaññī chaṭṭhepi  vuttanayeneva attho veditabbo sattamādīni uttānatthāneva
 ekādasame ti kilesā nti saṅghabhogassa apaṭṭhapanaṃ avicāraṇaṃ na kukkuccāyitabbaṃ nāma taṃ kukkuccāyati nti tasseva paṭṭhapanaṃ vicāraṇaṃ taṃ na kukkuccāyati dvādasamādīni heṭṭhā vuttanayeneva veditabbānīti
