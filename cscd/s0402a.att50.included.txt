

 pañcamassa  paṭhame ābhāsanavasena candova  sesapadesupi eseva nayo

 dutiyādīsupi pabhāsanavasena candova  ālokanavasena candova  obhāsanavasena candova  pajjotanavasena candova ti evaṃ sabbapadesupi attho veditabbo

 chaṭṭhe ti yuttappayuttakālā nti yuttappayuttakāle dhammassavanaṃ ti pañhapucchanavissajjanavasena pavattā saṃsandanakathā

 sattame ti tasmiṃ tasmiṃ kāle dhammassavanādivasena pavattānaṃ kusaladhammānaṃ etaṃ adhivacanaṃ te bhāviyanti ceva anuparivattiyanti ca nti arahattaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ uttānatthameva

 navame ti mudukavācā ti mantasaṅkhātāya paññāya paricchinditvā kathitakathā dasame ti sārasampāpakaṃ sīlaṃ sesesupi eseva nayo
