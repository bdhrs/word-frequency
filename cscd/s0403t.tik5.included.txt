

 dutiyassa  paṭhame āvarantīti  nīvārayantīti  ettha ca ti kusaladhammuppattiṃ ādito parivārenti ti niravasesato vārayantīti attho tasmā ti ādito kusaluppattivāraṇavasena ti niravasesato vāraṇavasenāti evamettha attho daṭṭhabbo yasmā pañca nīvaraṇā uppajjamānā anuppannāya lokiyalokuttarāya paññāya uppajjituṃ na denti uppannāpi aṭṭha samāpattiyo pañca vā abhiññā upacchinditvā pātenti tasmā ti vuccanti upacchindanaṃ pātanañcettha tāsaṃ paññānaṃ anuppannānaṃ uppajjituṃ appadānameva iti mahaggatānuttarapaññānaṃ ekaccāya ca parittapaññāya anuppattihetubhūtā nīvaraṇadhammā itarāsaṃ samatthataṃ vihanantiyevāti paññāya dubbalīkaraṇā vuttā bhāvanāmanasikārena vinā pakatiyā manussehi nibbattetabbo dhammoti  manussattabhāvāvaho vā dhammo  anuḷāraṃ parittakusalaṃ yaṃ asatipi buddhuppāde vattati yañca sandhāyāha hīnena brahmacariyena khattiye upapajjatīti  alaṃ ariyāya ariyabhāvāyāti alamariyo ariyabhāvāya samatthoti vuttaṃ hoti ñāṇadassanameva ñāṇadassanaviseso alamariyo ca so ñāṇadassanaviseso cāti 
nti ca dibbacakkhupi vipassanāpi maggopi phalampi paccavekkhaṇañāṇampi sabbaññutaññāṇampi vuccati appamatto samāno ñāṇadassanaṃ ārādhetīti  hi ettha dibbacakkhu ñāṇadassanaṃ nāma ñāṇadassanāya cittaṃ abhinīharati abhininnāmetīti  ettha vipassanāñāṇaṃ abhabbā te ñāṇāya dassanāya anuttarāya sambodhāyāti  ettha maggo ayamañño  uttarimanussadhammā alamariyañāṇadassanaviseso adhigato phāsuvihāroti  ettha  phalaṃ ñāṇañca pana me dassanaṃ udapādi akuppā me vimutti ayamantimā jāti natthi dāni punabbhavoti  ettha paccavekkhaṇañāṇaṃ ñāṇañca pana me dassanaṃ udapādi sattāhakālakato āḷāro kālāmoti  ettha sabbaññutaññāṇaṃ idha pana lokuttaradhammo adhippeto ettha ca rūpāyatanaṃ jānāti cakkhuviññāṇaṃ viya passati cāti  dibbacakkhu sammasanūpacāre ca dhammalakkhaṇattayañca tathā jānāti passati cāti  vipassanā nibbānaṃ cattāri vā saccāni asammohappaṭivedhato jānāti passati cāti  maggo phalaṃ pana nibbānavaseneva yojetabbaṃ paccavekkhaṇā maggādhigatassa atthassa sabbaso jotanaṭṭhena  sabbaññutā anāvaraṇatāya samantacakkhutāya ca ti pariyādinnakālo dutiyaṃ uttānameva


 tatiye padahatīti padahano bhāvanamanuyutto yogī tassa bhāvo bhāvanānuyogo  padhānamassa atthīti padhāniko ka-kārassa ya-kāraṃ katvā ti vuttaṃ abhinīhārato paṭṭhāya āgatattāti vuttattā paccekabodhisattasāvakabodhisattānampi paṇidhānato pabhuti āgatasaddhā  eva ukkaṭṭhaniddesena pana nti vuttaṃ adhigamato samudāgatattā aggamaggaphalasampayuttā cāpi  yā sotāpannassa aṅgabhāvena vuttā ti paṭipakkhena anadhibhavanīyattā niccalabhāvena nti okkanditvā adhimuccanaṃ pasāduppattiyā pasādanīyavatthusmiṃ pasīdanameva nti suṭṭhu paṭividdhaṃ yathā tena paṭividdhena sabbaññutaññāṇaṃ hatthagataṃ ahosi tathā paṭividdhaṃ yassa buddhasubuddhatāya saddhā acalā asampavedhi tassa dhammasudhammatāya saṅghasuppaṭipannatāya  tena paṭivedhena saddhā na tathāti aṭṭhānametaṃ anavakāso tenāha bhagavā  yo bhikkhave buddhe pasanno dhamme pasanno saṅghe pasannotiādi  addhā imāya paṭipadāya jarāmaraṇato muccissāmīti sakkaccaṃ padahanato
-saddo  abhāvattho appasaddassa kho panātiādīsu viyāti āha ti ti yathābhuttamāhāraṃ samākāreneva pacanasīlāya daḷhaṃ katvā pacantī hi gahaṇī ghorabhāvena pittavikārādivasena rogaṃ janeti sithilaṃ katvā pacantī mandabhāvena vātavikārādivasena tenāha ti gahaṇitejassa mandapaṭutāvasena sattānaṃ yathākkamaṃ sītuṇhasahatāti āha tiādi yāthāvato accayadesanā attano āvikaraṇaṃ nāmāti āha ti ti saṅkhārānaṃ udayañca vayañca paṭivijjhantiyāti ayamettha atthoti āha tiādi ti nirupakkilesāya ti tadaṅgavasena savisesaṃ pajahituṃ samatthāya ti yaṃ dukkhaṃ imasmiṃ ñāṇe anadhigate pavattirahaṃ adhigate na pavatti taṃ sandhāya vadati tathāhesa yogāvacaro cūḷasotāpannoti vuccati catutthaṃ uttānameva


 pañcame ti visacchāyasantāno bhāvo ti tattha cittassa anuppaveso ti attano eva okāsaṃ gahetvā ti kusalavāraṃ khepetvā
ti akkamanādivasena bādheyya ti ñātatīraṇappahānasaṅkhātāhi tīhi pariññāhi natthi etesaṃ kutoci bhayanti  nibbhayāti attho catunnaṃ oghānaṃ saṃsāramahoghasseva vā pāraṃ pariyantaṃ gatā tenāha ntiādi


 chaṭṭhe  madhurakabhāvo nāma sarīrassa thambhitattaṃ taṃ pana garubhāvapubbakanti āha ti  ti nappakāsenti nānākāraṇato na upaṭṭhahanti tenāha ti sesamettha uttānameva


 sattame ti -saddo asammosadhammo nibbānantiādīsu  viya pakatipariyāyo tasmā jarāpakatiko jiṇṇasabhāvoti attho tenāha tiādi sesapadesupi eseva nayo kammunā dātabbaṃ ādiyatīti  attanā yathūpacitakammaphalabhāgīti attho taṃ pana dāyajjaṃ kāraṇūpacārena vadanto ti āha yathā kusalānaṃ bhikkhave dhammānaṃ samādānahetu evamidaṃ puññaṃ vaḍḍhatīti  yonīhi phalaṃ sabhāvato bhinnampi abhinnaṃ viya missitaṃ hoti tenāha nti mamattavasena bajjhantīti bandhū ñāti sālohito ca kammaṃ pana ekantasambandhavāti āha ti ti avassayo kammasadiso hi sattānaṃ avassayo natthi
ti mahallakakāle puññaṃ karissāma daharamha tāvāti yobbanaṃ apassāya mānakaraṇaṃ ahaṃ nirogo saṭṭhi vā sattati vā vassāni atikkantāni na me harītakakhaṇḍampi khāditabbaṃ ime panaññe asukaṃ no ṭhānaṃ rujjati bhesajjaṃ khādāmāti vicaranti ko añño mayā sadiso nirogo nāmāti evaṃ mānakaraṇaṃ  sabbesampi jīvitaṃ nāma pabhaṅguraṃ dukkhānubandhañca tadubhayaṃ anoloketvā pabandhaṭṭhitiṃ paccayasulabhatañca nissāya ciraṃ jīviṃ ciraṃ jīvāmi ciraṃ jīvissāmi sukhaṃ jīviṃ sukhaṃ jīvāmi sukhaṃ jīvissāmīti evaṃ mānakaraṇaṃ 
nti  kāmūpadhirahitaṃ cattāro hi upadhī  kāmūpadhi khandhūpadhi kilesūpadhi abhisaṅkhārūpadhīti kāmāpi yaṃ pañca kāmaguṇe paṭicca uppajjati sukhaṃ somanassaṃ ayaṃ kāmānaṃ assādoti  evaṃ vuttassa sukhassa adhiṭṭhānabhāvato upadhiyati ettha sukhanti iminā vacanatthena upadhīti vuccati khandhāpi khandhamūlakassa dukkhassa  adhiṭṭhānabhāvato kilesāpi apāyadukkhassa adhiṭṭhānabhāvato abhisaṅkhārāpi bhavadukkhassa adhiṭṭhānabhāvato sesaṃ suviññeyyameva


 aṭṭhame nti ettha saṃ vuccati dhanaṃ tassa patīti sapati dhanasāmiko tassa hitāvahattā sāpateyyaṃ drabyaṃ dhananti attho attano rucivasena gāmakiccaṃ netīti gāmaniyo gāmaniyoyeva 
anvāya upanissāya jīvanasīlā ti āha ti ekaṃ mahākulaṃ nissāya paṇṇāsampi saṭṭhipi kulāni jīvanti te manusse sandhāyetaṃ vuttaṃ sesaṃ suviññeyyameva navamādīni uttānatthāneva
