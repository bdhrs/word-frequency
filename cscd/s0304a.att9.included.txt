

 abyākatasaṃyuttassa  paṭhame ti gihikāle bimbisārassa upāsikā saddhāpabbajitā mahātherī etadaggaṃ bhikkhave mama sāvikānaṃ bhikkhunīnaṃ mahāpaññānaṃ yadidaṃ khemāti evaṃ bhagavatā mahāpaññatāya etadagge ṭhapitā ti paṇḍiccena samannāgatā ti veyyattiyena samannāgatā ti medhāya paññāya samannāgatā ti pariyattibāhusaccenapi paṭivedhabāhusaccenapi samannāgatā
ti acchiddakagaṇanāya kusalo ti aṅgulimuddāya gaṇanāya kusalo ti piṇḍagaṇanāya kusalo ti caturāsītiyojanasahassagambhīro ti āḷhakagaṇanāya appameyyo ti āḷhakagaṇanāya pamāṇagahaṇatthaṃ durogāho nti yena rūpena dīgho rasso sāmo odātoti sattasaṅkhātaṃ tathāgataṃ paññapeyya nti taṃ vuttappakārarūpaṃ samudayappahānena sabbaññutathāgatassa pahīnaṃ ti āyatiṃ rūpassa anuppattiyā rūpārūpakoṭṭhāsenapi evarūpo nāma bhavissatīti vohārassapi paṭipassaddhattā rūpapaṇṇattiyāpi vimutto ti ajjhāsaya gambhīratāya ca guṇagambhīratāya ca gambhīro tassa evaṃ guṇagambhīrassa sato sabbaññutathāgatassa yaṃ upādāya sattasaṅkhāto tathāgatoti paññatti hoti tadabhāvena tassā paññattiyā abhāvaṃ passantassa ayaṃ sattasaṅkhāto ti idaṃ vacanaṃ na upeti na yujjati tiādivacanampi na upeti na yujjatīti attho
ti  ekaṃ bhavissati ti nirantaraṃ bhavissati ti  na viruddhaṃ padaṃ bhavissati nti desanāya desanā hi idha aggapadanti adhippetā

 dutiyaṃ  vitthāritameva abyākatādhikārato pana idha vuttaṃ

 tatiye nti rūpamattametaṃ ettha rūpato añño koci satto nāma na upalabbhati rūpe pana sati nāmamattaṃ etaṃ hotīti dasseti ntiādīsupi eseva nayo ti ayaṃ rūpādīni muñcitvā anupalabbhiyasabhāvo hetu yenetaṃ abyākataṃ bhagavatāti catutthādīni uttānatthāneva

 navame nti kutūhalasālā nāma paccekasālā natthi yattha pana nānātitthiyā samaṇabrāhmaṇā nānāvidhaṃ kathaṃ pavattenti sā bahūnaṃ ayaṃ kiṃ vadati ayaṃ kiṃ vadatīti kutūhaluppavattiṭṭhānato kutūhalasālāti vuccati ti yāva ābhassarabrahmalokā gacchati ti cuticittena nikkhipati ti cutikkhaṇeyeva paṭisandhicittassa anuppannattā anupapanno hoti

 dasame ti tesaṃ laddhiyā saddhiṃ etaṃ abhavissati yaṃ etaṃ sabbe dhammā anattāti vipassanāñāṇaṃ uppajjati api nu me tassa anulomaṃ abhavissāti attho

 ekādasame ti āvuso yassāpi etaṃ ettakena kālena hetumhi sati rūpītiādi paññāpanā hoti asati na hotīti byākaraṇaṃ bhaveyya tassa ettakameva bahu ti atikkante pana atimanāpe dhammadesanānaye vādoyeva ko natthi vādo chinnā kathāti
