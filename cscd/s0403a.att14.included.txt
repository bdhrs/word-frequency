

 pañcamassa  paṭhame ti ekatovāsena ti ādiyanamukho gahaṇamukhoti attho ti ayaṃ dinnaṃ paṭiggahetumeva jānātīti evaṃ avamaññati ti appamattake kismiñcideva kujjhitvā jānāmahaṃ tayā katakammaṃ ettakaṃ addhānaṃ ahaṃ kiṃ karonto vasiṃ nanu tuyhameva katākataṃ vīmaṃsantotiādīni vattā hoti atha itaro addhā koci mayhaṃ doso bhavissatīti kiñci paṭippharituṃ na sakkoti ti taṃ vaṇṇaṃ vā avaṇṇaṃ vā sīghameva  saddahati saddahanaṭṭhena hi ādānena esa ādiyanamukhoti vutto ti pāḷiyā  pana ṭhapitamukhoti attho magge khaṭaāvāṭo viya āgatāgataṃ udakaṃ vaṇṇaṃ vā avaṇṇaṃ vā saddahanavasena sampaṭicchituṃ ṭhapitamukhoti vuttaṃ hoti
ti parittakasaddho tiādīsu kusale dhamme ime kusalāti na jānāti akusale dhamme ime akusalāti na jānāti tathā sāvajje sadosadhamme ime sāvajjāti anavajje ca niddosadhamme ime anavajjāti hīne hīnāti paṇīte paṇītāti ti ime kaṇhā sukke paṭibāhetvā ṭhitattā sappaṭibhāgā nāma ime ca sukkā kaṇhe paṭibāhitvā ṭhitattā sappaṭibhāgāti na jānāti

 dutiye ti āpattivītikkamanavasena ārabhati ceva tappaccayā ca vippaṭisārī hoti nti arahattasamādhiñceva arahattaphalañāṇañca ti anadhigatattā na jānāti ti āpattiṃ āpajjati vuṭṭhitattā pana na vippaṭisārī hoti ti sakiṃ āpattiṃ āpajjitvā tato vuṭṭhāya pacchā kiñcāpi nāpajjati vippaṭisāraṃ pana vinodetuṃ na sakkoti ti na ceva āpattiṃ āpajjati na ca vippaṭisārī hoti ti arahattaṃ pana appatto hoti pañcamanayena khīṇāsavo kathito
ti āpattivītikkamasambhavā ti vippaṭisārato jātā ti punappunaṃ uppajjanena vaḍḍhanti ti vītikkamasambhave āsave āpattidesanāya vā āpattivuṭṭhānena vā pajahitvā ti suddhante ṭhitabhāvapaccavekkhaṇena nīharitvā ti vipassanācittañca taṃsampayuttaṃ paññañca bhāvetu sesaṃ iminā upāyeneva veditabbanti

 tatiye nti vatthukāmakilesakāmesu adhimuttānaṃ ti navalokuttaradhammatthāya  sahasīlakaṃ pubbabhāgappaṭipadaṃ paṭipanno paṭipattipūrako puggalo dullabho lokasmiṃ

 catutthe ti appaṭikūlārammaṇe ti paṭikūlanti evaṃsaññī esa nayo sabbattha kathaṃ panāyaṃ evaṃ viharatīti iṭṭhasmiṃ vatthusmiṃ pana asubhāya vā pharati aniccato vā upasaṃharati evaṃ tāva appaṭikūle paṭikūlasaññī viharati aniṭṭhasmiṃ vatthusmiṃ mettāya vā pharati dhātuto vā upasaṃharati evaṃ paṭikūle appaṭikūlasaññī viharati ubhayasmiṃ pana purimanayassa ca pacchimanayassa ca vasena tatiyacatutthavārā vuttā chaḷaṅgupekkhāvasena pañcamo chaḷaṅgupekkhā cesā khīṇāsavassa upekkhāsadisā na pana khīṇāsavupekkhā tattha ti majjhattabhāve ṭhito vihareyya ti kismiñci ārammaṇe ti kismiñci padese ti koci appamattakopi iti imasmiṃ sutte pañcasu ṭhānesu vipassanāva kathitā taṃ āraddhavipassako bhikkhu kātuṃ sakkoti ñāṇavā paññuttaro bahussutasamaṇopi kātuṃ sakkoti sotāpannasakadāgāmianāgāmino kātuṃ sakkontiyeva khīṇāsave vattabbameva natthīti pañcamaṃ uttānameva

 chaṭṭhe ti khettādikammantaṃ kāreti ti cattāri adhikaraṇāni ādiyati ti disāpāmokkhesu bhikkhūsu ti paccanīkaggāhitāya viruddho hoti nti anavatthānacārikaṃ

 sattame ti na sakkaritvā suciṃ katvā deti ti acittīkārena agāravavasena deti ti na nirantaraṃ deti atha vā chaḍḍetukāmo viya deti ti katassa nāma phalaṃ āgamissatīti na evaṃ āgamanadiṭṭhiṃ na uppādetvā deti
sukkapakkhe ti deyyadhamme ca dakkhiṇeyyesu ca cittīkāraṃ upaṭṭhapetvā deti tattha deyyadhammaṃ paṇītaṃ ojavantaṃ katvā dento deyyadhamme cittīkāraṃ upaṭṭhapeti nāma puggalaṃ vicinitvā dento dakkhiṇeyyesu cittīkāraṃ upaṭṭhapeti nāma ti āṇattiyā parahatthena adatvā anamatagge saṃsāre vicarantena me hatthapādānaṃ aladdhakālassa pamāṇaṃ nāma natthi vaṭṭamokkhaṃ bhavanissaraṇaṃ karissāmīti sahattheneva deti ti anāgatabhavassa paccayo bhavissatīti kammañca vipākañca saddahitvā detīti

 aṭṭhame ti dānañca dānaphalañca saddahitvā ti yuttappattakālena ti aggahitacitto muttacāgo hutvā ti anupaghātetvā guṇe amakkhetvā ti atthā āgacchamānā vayovuḍḍhakāle  anāgantvā yuttappattakāle paṭhamavayasmiṃyeva āgacchanti ceva bahū ca honti

 navame ti appitappitakkhaṇeyeva vikkhambhitehi kilesehi vimuttattā samayavimuttisaṅkhātāya lokiyavimuttiyā vimuttacittassa dasamaṃ uttānatthameva
