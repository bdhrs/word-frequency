

 dutiyassa paṭhame ti vipākaphalena mahapphalā vipākānisaṃseneva ti nibbānapatiṭṭhā ti sabbasmiṃ tedhātusannivese loke ukkaṇṭhitassa uppajjanakasaññā dutiyaṃ uttānatthameva

 tatiye ti uttamassa varassa ādāyako sesamettha catutthe ca uttānatthamevāti

 pañcame ti sākacchāya yutto nti pucchitaṃ pañhaṃ ti vissajjitā hoti

 chaṭṭhe ti sājīvāya yutto ti pañhapucchanañceva pañhavissajjanañca sabbepi hi sabrahmacārino pañhaṃ upajīvanti tenetaṃ pañhapucchanavissajjanaṃ samānājīvatāya sājīvoti vuttaṃ nti abhisaṅkhataṃ pañhaṃ

 sattame ti adhimattavīriyaṃ aṭṭhame attano bodhimaṇḍe paṭividdhe āgamanaiddhipāde kathetvā upari attanova cha abhiññā kathesīti navamadasamesu vipassanā kathitā sesaṃ sabbattha uttānamevāti
