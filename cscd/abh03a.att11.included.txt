

 idāni  jahatikathā nāma hoti tattha yesaṃ jhānalābhī puthujjano saha saccābhisamayā anāgāmī nāma hoti tassa puthujjanakāleyeva kāmarāgabyāpādā pahīnāti laddhi seyyathāpi etarahi sammitiyānaṃ tesaṃ taṃ laddhiṃ bhindituṃ ti pucchā sakavādissa jhānavikkhambhitānaṃ pana tesaṃ pariyuṭṭhānaṃ apassantassa paṭiññā paravādissa yasmā pana tesaṃ jhānavikkhambhitānampi anāgāmimaggeneva accantaṃ  pahānaṃ hoti tasmā puna ntiādianuyogo sakavādissa tathārūpassa pahānassa abhāvato paṭikkhepo itarassa vikkhambhetīti accantavikkhambhanameva sandhāya pucchā sakavādissa tato paraṃ anāgāmimaggaṭṭhena saddhiṃ puthujjanasaṃsandanaṃ hoti taṃ uttānatthameva
 tato paraṃ ti puṭṭho jhānānāgāmitaṃ sandhāya paṭijānāti ti puṭṭho dassanamaggena uddhambhāgiyānaṃ pahānābhāvato paṭikkhipati
ti puṭṭho tathārūpāya bhāvanāya abhāvā paṭikkhipati puna puṭṭho tiṇṇaṃ maggānaṃ kiccasabbhāvaṃ sandhāya paṭijānāti sāmaññaphalapucchāsupi eseva nayo ti puṭṭho ti jhānānāgāmitaṃ sandhāya vadati puna saṃyojanappahānaṃ puṭṭho tiṇṇaṃ anāgāmimaggena tesaṃ kilesānaṃ appaheyyattā paṭikkhipati dutiyaṃ puṭṭho paṭhamamaggasseva  jhānānāgāmimaggabhāvaṃ sandhāya paṭijānāti sesamettha uttānatthamevāti
