
vivādādhikaraṇamhāti adhammaṃ dhammoti dīpetītiādinayappavattā aṭṭhārasabhedakaravatthunissitā vivādādhikaraṇamhā
 upasampannaṃ omasato bhikkhussa pācitti hotīti yojanā
anuvādādhikaraṇapaccayāti  codanāparanāmadheyyaṃ anuvādādhikaraṇameva paccayo tasmā anuvādanādhikaraṇahetūti attho
tathāti iminā amūlakattaṃ atidisati
āpattipaccayāti āpattādhikaraṇapaccayā
kiccādhikaraṇapaccayāti apalokanādicatubbidhakammasaṅkhātakiccādhikaraṇahetu
accajantīvāti attano laddhiṃ apariccajantī eva
pāpikāya diṭṭhiyā pariccajanatthāya katāya yāvatatiyakaṃ samanubhāsanāya taṃ diṭṭhiṃ accajantiyā tassā bhikkhuniyā tassa bhikkhussa ca accajato pācitti hotīti yojanā
iti uttare līnatthapakāsaniyā
adhikaraṇapaccayakathāvaṇṇanā niṭṭhitā
