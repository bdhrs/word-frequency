 lobhābhibhūtatāya  nibbatti lobhavasikassa hi duggati pāṭikaṅkhā  pubbaparicayena  subhaṃ māṇavaṃ anuggaṇhitukāmo  katokāsassa kammassa paṭibāhituṃ asakkuṇeyyabhāvato
brāhmaṇacārittassa bhāvitataṃ sandhāya tathā pitaraṃ ukkaṃsento ca ti āha ti sutametaṃ mayā mayhaṃ pitā sunakho hutvā nibbattoti tumhehi vuttaṃ kimidaṃ saccanti pucchi ti yathā pubbe sunakhassa vuttaṃ tatheva vatvā nti saccāpanatthaṃ todeyyabrāhmaṇo sunakho hutvā nibbattoti attano vacanassa avisaṃvādanatthaṃ avisaṃvādabhāvassa dassanatthanti attho ti  buddhānubhāvena so sunakho taṃ sabbaṃ netvā dassesi na jātissaratāya bhagavantaṃ disvā bhukkaraṇaṃ pana purimajātisiddhavāsanāvasena ti dissanti hi bho  gotama manussā appāyukā dissanti dīghāyukā dissanti bavhābādhā dissanti appābādhā dissanti dubbaṇṇā dissanti vaṇṇavanto dissanti appesakkhā dissanti mahesakkhā dissanti appabhogā dissanti mahābhogā dissanti nīcakulīnā dissanti uccākulīnā dissanti duppaññā dissanti paññāvantoti  ime cuddasa pañhe pucchitvā aṅgasubhatāya kiresa ti nāmaṃ labhi
ti iminā upari pucchiyamānassa pañhassa pageva tena abhisaṅkhatabhāvaṃ dasseti ti dukkhavedanā sā hi kusalakammanibbatte attabhāve uppajjanakasukhavedanāpaṭipakkhabhāvato visabhāgavedanāti kāyaṃ gāḷhā hutvā bādhati pīḷetīti ābādhoti ca vuccati ti sarīrassa ekadese uṭṭhitāpi  aparivattabhāvakaraṇato etena balavarogo ābādho nāmāti dasseti ti asukhajīvitāvaho etena dubbalo appamattako rogo ātaṅkoti dasseti nti sayananisajjādito uṭṭhahanaṃ tena yathā tathā aparāparaṃ sarīrassa parivattanaṃ vadati nti bhāriyaṃ kicchasiddhikaṃ  ti etthāpi gilānassevāti padaṃ ānetvā sambandhitabbaṃ heṭṭhā catūhi padehi aphāsuvihārābhāvaṃ pucchitvā idāni phāsuvihārasabbhāvaṃ pucchati tena saviseso phāsuvihāro pucchitoti daṭṭhabbo asatipi atisayatthajotane sadde atisayatthassa labbhanato yathā abhirūpāya deyyaṃ dātabbanti
ti ettha  nāma upasaṅkamanassa yuttapattakālo  nāma tasseva paccayasāmaggī atthato tajjaṃ sarīrabalañceva tappaccayaparissayābhāvo ca  nāma ñāṇena tesaṃ gahaṇaṃ sallakkhaṇanti dassetuṃ tiādi vuttaṃ ti vaḍḍhissati
ti cetiraṭṭhe ya-kārena hi padaṃ vaḍḍhetvā vuttaṃ cetiraṭṭhato aññaṃ visuṃyevekaṃ raṭṭhanti ca vadanti nti maraṇaṃ  nāma tādisānaṃ roga vaseneva hotīti yena rogena taṃ jātaṃ tassa sarūpapucchā kāraṇapucchā maraṇahetukacittasantāpapucchā tassa ca santāpassa sabbalokasādhāraṇatā tathā maraṇassa ca appatikāratāti evaṃ ādinā maraṇapaṭisaṃyuttaṃ  sammodanīyaṃ kathaṃ kathesīti dassetuṃ tiādi vuttaṃ  māro viya  uttaramāṇavo viyāti adhippāyo ti vimokkhupāyesu niyyānadhammesu ti tiṭṭhanti pavattantīti attho
 atthappayuttatāya saddapayogassa saddappabandhalakkhaṇāni tīṇi piṭakāni tadatthabhūtehi sīlādīhi dhammakkhandhehi saṅgayhantīti vuttaṃ ti nti tiṇṇaṃ khandhānanti evaṃ gahaṇato sāmaññato cāti saṅkhepeneva kathitaṃ nti  ayaṃ adiṭṭhajotanā pucchā na kathetukamyatā pucchāti vuttaṃ ti kathetukamyatābhāve panassa therassa vacanatā siyā

ti ettha -saddo ādiattho pakārattho vā tena ariyassa samādhikkhandhassa patiṭṭhāpesīti ayaṃ ettako pāṭho dassitoti daṭṭhabbaṃ tenāha ti uddesavasenāti adhippāyo ti sāmaññaphaladesanādīsu bhagavatā desitanayeneva tenassa suttassa satthubhāsitabhāvaṃ jinavacanabhāvaṃ dasseti ti ariyamaggasāre bhagavato sāsane yathā dassitaṃ sīlaṃ sāro eva na hoti sāravato mahato rukkhassa papaṭikaṭṭhāniyattā yadi evaṃ kasmā idha gahitanti āha ti jhānādiuttarimanussadhamme adhigantukāmassa adhiṭṭhānamattaṃ tattha appatiṭṭhitassa tesaṃ asambhavato atha vā ti kāmañcettha sāsane maggasīlaṃ phalasīlanti idaṃ lokuttarasīlampi sārameva tathāpi na sīlakkhandho eva sāro atha kho samādhikkhandhopi paññākkhandhopi  sāro evāti evamettha attho daṭṭhabbo purimo eva sāro tenāha tiādi

 kasmā panettha thero samādhikkhandhaṃ puṭṭho indriyasaṃvarādike vissajjesi nanu evaṃ sante aññaṃ puṭṭho aññaṃ byākaronto ambaṃ puṭṭho labujaṃ byākaronto viya hotīti īdisī codanā idha anokāsāti dassento ti āha tenettha indriyasaṃvarādayopi samādhiupakārataṃ upādāya samādhikkhandhapakkhikāni uddiṭṭhānīti dasseti  rūpāvacaracatutthajjhānadesanānantaraṃ abhiññādesanāya avasaroti katvā rūpāvacaracatutthajjhānapādikā hi saparibhaṇḍā chapi abhiññāyo lokiyā abhiññā  pana sijjhamānā yasmā aṭṭhasu samāpattīsu cuddasavidhena cittaparidamanena vinā na ijjhanti tasmā abhiññāsu desiyamānāsu arūpajjhānānipi desitāneva honti nānantariyabhāvato tenāha ti vuttanayena desitāneva katvā saṃvaṇṇakehi pakāsetabbānīti attho aṭṭhakathāyaṃ pana catutthajjhānaṃ upasampajja viharatīti imināva arūpajjhānampi saṅgahitanti dassetuṃ tiādi vuttaṃ catutthajjhānañhi rūpavirāgabhāvanāvasena pavattaṃ arūpajjhānanti vuccatīti
ti ettha heṭṭhā vuttanayānusārena attho veditabbo lokiyassa samādhikkhandhassa adhippetattā ti vuttaṃ ariya-saddo cettha suddhapariyāyo na lokuttarapariyāyo tathā heṭṭhāpi lokiyābhiññāpaṭisambhidāhi vināva arahatte adhigate nattheva uttariṃkaraṇīyanti sakkā vattuṃ yadatthaṃ bhagavati brahmacariyaṃ vussati tassa siddhattā idha pana lokiyābhiññāpi āgatā eva sesaṃ suviññeyyameva
