

 tatiyassa paṭhamadutiyesu natthi vattabbaṃ tatiye hatthagoti vattabbe ti vuttaṃ so hi rājapurisānaṃ hatthato  yakkhassa hatthaṃ yakkhassa hatthato bhagavato hatthaṃ  bhagavato hatthato puna rājapurisānaṃ hatthaṃ gatattā nāmato ti jāto tenāha ti catutthādīni uttānatthāneva


 aṭṭhame khīṇāsavassa sabbesaṃ saṅkhārānaṃ aniccatā asammohavasena kiccato maggapaññāya suppaṭividdhā vipassanāya ārammaṇakaraṇavasenapīti dassento āha ti ti dvepi kāme vadati kilesavasena uppajjamāno hi pariḷāho vatthukāmasannissayo vatthukāmavisayo vāti dvepi sapariḷāhaṭṭhena aṅgārakāsu viyāti ti vuttā anto vuccati lāmakaṭṭhena taṇhā byantaṃ vigatantaṃ bhūtanti nti āha nti nittaṇhanti attho


 navame yasmā mahiddhikapetā devāsurānaṃ āvāhaṃ gacchanti vivāhaṃ na gacchanti tasmā pettivisayeneva asurakāyo gahitoti veditabbo petāsurā pana petā evāti tesaṃ petehi saṅgaho avuttasiddhova


 dasame ti na icchassa abhāvattho hettha saddo appaḍaṃsamakasavātātapātiādīsu (a ni 1011) viya paccayesu appiccho  cīvarādipaccayesu icchārahito ti jhānādiadhigamavibhāvane icchārahito  ti pariyattiyaṃ bāhusaccavibhāvane icchārahito ti dhutaṅgesu appiccho dhutaṅgabhāvavibhāvane icchārahito ti attani saṃvijjamānānaṃ jhānādiguṇānañceva bāhusaccaguṇassa dhutaṅgaguṇassa ca niguhanena chādanena ti nipphajjati sijjhati ti mahatiyā icchāya samannāgatassa no sampajjati anudhammassapi anipphajjanato ti pakārehi vivittassa tenāha ti ti bhāvanābhiyogavasena ekībhāvova kāyavivekoti adhippeto na gaṇasaṅgaṇikābhāvamattanti dasseti nti yogakammaṃ
sattehi kilesehi ca saṅgaṇanaṃ samodhānaṃ saṅgaṇikā sā āramitabbaṭṭhena ārāmo etassāti  tassa tenāha tiādi ti paggahitavīriyassa tañca kho upadhiviveke ninnatāvasena ayaṃ dhammoti vacanato esa nayo itaresupi vivaṭṭanissitaṃyeva hi samādhānaṃ idhādhippetaṃ tathā paññāpi kammassa-katapaññāya hi ṭhito kammavasena bhavesu nānappakāro anatthoti jānanto kammakkhayakaraṃ ñāṇaṃ abhipattheti tadatthañca ussāhaṃ karoti mānādayo sattasantānaṃ saṃsāre papañcenti vitthārentīti ti āha tiādi sesamettha suviññeyyameva
