

 catutthassa  paṭhame nti kusalesu dhammesu sammattabhūtaṃ magganiyāmaṃ okkamituṃ abhabbo abhājanaṃ tiādīsu kiṃ kathā nāma esāti vadanto  nāma kiṃ nāmesa katheti kiṃ ayaṃ jānātīti vadanto  nāma mayaṃ kiṃ jānāma kuto amhākaṃ etaṃ sotuṃ balanti vadanto  nāma vipariyāyena sukkapakkho veditabbo

 dutiye ti aviññātasmiṃyeva viññātamidaṃ mayāti evaṃmānī

 tatiye ti makkhī hutvā guṇamakkhanacittena dhammaṃ suṇāti ti niggahāropanacitto ti guṇarandhaṃ guṇacchiddaṃ gavesanto

 catutthe ti ohitasotā sukatakārino hutvā na suṇanti  ti yathāsutaṃ dhammaṃ vaḷañjantāpi sakkaccaṃ na vaḷañjenti pañcamaṃ uttānameva

 chaṭṭhe ti appatiṭṭho ācariyā hi suttantassa paṭisaraṇaṃ nāma tesaṃ abhāvā appaṭisaraṇo hoti sesamettha heṭṭhā vuttanayameva

 sattame ti taṃ taṃ puggalaṃ upanikkhipitvā sakkhiṃ katvāti attho ti kathiyamānāya sesamettha aṭṭhamañca uttānatthamevāti

 navame ti dānānantaraṃ sīlaṃ sīlānantaraṃ sagganti evaṃ desanānupubbiṃ kathaṃ vā yaṃ yaṃ suttapadaṃ vā gāthāpadaṃ vā nikkhittaṃ hoti tassa tassa anurūpakathaṃ kathessāmīti cittaṃ upaṭṭhapetvā paresaṃ dhammo desetabbo ti tassa tassa atthassa taṃ taṃ kāraṇaṃ dassento kāraṇañhi idha pariyāyoti vuttaṃ ti mahāsambādhappatte satte sambādhato mocessāmīti anukampaṃ āgamma ti na āmisahetuko attano catupaccayalābhaṃ anāsīsantoti attho ti attukkaṃsanaparavambhanādivasena attānañca parañca guṇupaghātena anupahantvā

 dasame ti yāni hassādīni kiccāni nipphādetuṃ  ṭhānāni uppannāni honti tesu matthakaṃ asampattesu antarāyeva dunnīhārā duvikkhambhayā honti nti  kathetukāmatā vuccati imāni pañca duppaṭivinodayāni na suppaṭivinodayāni upāyena pana kāraṇena anurūpāhi paccavekkhaṇaanusāsanādīhi sakkā paṭivinodetunti
